Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is used in to obtain the [book
of Flash](/wiki/Book_Of_Flash "wikilink"). It [unlocks](Unlock "wikilink") [a
door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") to the [Kzinti war
wizard](/wiki/Kzinti_War_Wizard "wikilink").
