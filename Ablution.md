Syntax: cast 'ablution' \[CAST ON SELF ONLY\]

Prerequisites: None

*With a prayer, you may attempt to cleanse your body and soul. It
will*weaken you, but upon success, all unnatural affects will be cleared
from ''you.

''It requires equal hp and movement in addition to its mana cost.

[Sorcerers](:Category:Sorcerers.md "wikilink") sometimes have problems
due to being tainted.
