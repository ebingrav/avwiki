*With this [ability](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), a
[Priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") may grant a heathen
forgiveness for past sins. The [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
clears [curses](/wiki/Curse "wikilink") from
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"), but not
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), and raises
[alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [via pax](/wiki/Via_Pax "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'absolve'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
