*A downpour of acidic rain will dissolve the flesh of the
[opponents](:Category:_Mobs "wikilink") in the
[room](:Category:_Rooms "wikilink") under the onus of this spell. It is
said to be a deadly beauty as colorful green droplets fall from the sky,
create rainbows in the right light, then quickly dissolve your flesh.*

Prerequisite(s): [Acid Blast](Acid_Blast "wikilink"), [Control
Weather](Control_Weather "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'acid rain'.

[Hero](:Category:_Hero "wikilink") [mage](:Category:_Mages "wikilink")
[hitters](Hitters "wikilink") may find this
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink") handy when their
[tanks](Tanks "wikilink") are fighting multiple
[mobs](:Category:_Mobs "wikilink") simultaneously.
[Mage](:Category:_Mages "wikilink") [tanks](Tanks "wikilink") or soloers
may also find it handy on occastion.
