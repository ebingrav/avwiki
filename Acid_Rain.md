*A downpour of acidic rain will dissolve the flesh of the
[opponents](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") under the onus of this spell. It is
said to be a deadly beauty as colorful green droplets fall from the sky,
create rainbows in the right light, then quickly dissolve your flesh.*

Prerequisite(s): [Acid Blast](/wiki/Acid_Blast "wikilink"), [Control
Weather](/wiki/Control_Weather "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'acid rain'.

[Hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") [mage](Category%3A_Mages "wikilink")
[hitters](/wiki/Hitters "wikilink") may find this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") handy when their
[tanks](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink") are fighting multiple
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") simultaneously.
[Mage](Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") [tanks](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink") or
soloers may also find it handy on occastion.
