Comments
--------

Give [void essence](/wiki/Void_Essence "wikilink") to Acolyte of the Void to
complete the [Citadel Of
Arcanists](/wiki/Category%3A_Citadel_Of_Arcanists "wikilink")' [Staff of Void
Quest](/wiki/Staff_Of_Void_Quest "wikilink").

The Acolyte of Void will give you the [mystical trapdoor
key](/wiki/Mystical_Trapdoor_Key "wikilink") if you give him [ sheaf of strong
evidence](/wiki/Sheaf_of_Strong_Evidence "wikilink").
