*By controlling the production and release of adrenaline, the
[caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") of this psionic spell can gain a temporary
boost in [dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink"). This control cannot be
initiated during battle because that's a little too exciting. ADRENALINE
PUMP is a [BIOCENTRIC Psionic
Spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Biocentric_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). It can only
be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on others if the caster first masters the art
of [BIO EMPATHY](/wiki/Bio_Empathy "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'adrenaline pump'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This is a rather useful
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") for all
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") (who can) to
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"). It increases its target
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")'s
[dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink") by 4, though not more than 3 above its
target's maximum [trainable](/wiki/Train "wikilink")
[dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink"). It is included in
[Fortitudes](/wiki/Fortitudes "wikilink").
