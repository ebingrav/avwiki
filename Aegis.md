*Named after mythical armor from Heaven, the Aegis
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") grants
[invincibility](/wiki/Invincibility "wikilink"), [holy
aura](/wiki/Holy_Aura "wikilink"), [holy armor](Holy_Armor "wikilink"),
[armor](/wiki/Armor_(spell) "wikilink"), [bless](Bless "wikilink") and
[protection evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink") all at once for an
increased [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") cost.*

Prerequisite(s): [Invincibility](/wiki/Invincibility "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'aegis'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") will find this
[priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") "macro"
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") (or its variant
[Awen](/wiki/Awen "wikilink")), like both [Foci](Foci "wikilink") and
[Fortitudes](/wiki/Fortitudes "wikilink"), highly valuable for
[buffing](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")
either themselves or others.
