Comments
--------

This item drops off a [Stinkin' Human](/wiki/Stinkin'_Human "wikilink"). It
can not be worn or wielded and is listed as a key. This item is used in
all the [Fae ambassador](/wiki/Aeldiskaa "wikilink") [DC
quests](/wiki/Diplomatic_Corps "wikilink") hence mob and area will change.
