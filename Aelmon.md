*Aelmon is a pious old man. He now works in the Temple Square healing
wounded adventurers. Those who can afford it, [buy](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") his
special vials. Just [ask](/wiki/List "wikilink") to see what he has got. He
has his followers working at all the [fountains](/wiki/Fountains "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Aelmon, Healer.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink"),
[healer](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").

Comments
--------

Aelmon. The guy who stands at
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink")'s heart healing injured
[newbies](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_Levels_1-10 "wikilink"). He is the
[recall](/wiki/Recall "wikilink") reset location both for
[heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") and for
[lowmorts](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink") over
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 15. He is also [portable](Portal "wikilink").
Lots of directions start out from him. Various
[shops](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink") and a
[bank](/wiki/Category%3A_Banks "wikilink") and such are within easy walking
distance of him. A bit to his west is an entrance to the [Temple of
AVATAR](/wiki/Category%3A_Temple_Of_AVATAR "wikilink"), home of the lowmort
favorite statues. [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink")'s
[Jail](/wiki/Jail_Rooms "wikilink") is due south of him, as is [The
Executioner](/wiki/Executioner "wikilink").

Aelmon will buy most [trash](/wiki/Category%3A_Trash "wikilink").
