Aggressive [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") are those
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") that will attack players without
provocation. These can be very dangerous, especially at higher
[levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink") where many of them can [detect
invisible](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink") and/or [detect
hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink").

Note that there are two different subtypes of aggressive
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"); one subtype will attack any players
it sees, while another subtype will attack only players it sees who are
up to five levels larger than it is.

If an aggressive [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") is also
[wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink"), then it will act aggressively only
toward sleeping players.

See also [Assistive Mobs](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink").
