*Even with her [Orken](Orcs.md "wikilink") features and lithe muscular
body, this is easily one of the most beautiful women a person could
find, and her flowing brown hair has been twisted into a tight braid
that hangs behind her almost to the ground. She wears only a thick brown
cloak and sandals, though a large pouch of healing herbs hangs around
her waist.*
