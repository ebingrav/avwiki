*With magic and air, Henfield the Port was able to form basic
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") to replace those missing. Here
is the result. Nothing fancy, but lightweight! Learn the
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") and enjoy turning air into
something useful besides breathing.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'air armor'.
