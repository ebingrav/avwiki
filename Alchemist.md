*This is an alchemist, a mixer of
[potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink") and
[pills](/wiki/Category%3A_Pills "wikilink") of magic and healing. He has
plenty of ingredients for you or can sell you some of his own special
brands. Just type [list](/wiki/List "wikilink") to see what he can offer.*

**Keywords:** alchemist, small, man.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
