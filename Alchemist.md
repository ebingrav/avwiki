*This is an alchemist, a mixer of
[potions](:Category:_Potions "wikilink") and
[pills](:Category:_Pills "wikilink") of magic and healing. He has plenty
of ingredients for you or can sell you some of his own special brands.
Just type [list](List "wikilink") to see what he can offer.*

**Keywords:** alchemist, small, man.  
**[Level](Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](Detect_Invis "wikilink").
