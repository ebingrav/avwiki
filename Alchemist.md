*This is an alchemist, a mixer of
[potions](:Category:_Potions.md "wikilink") and
[pills](:Category:_Pills.md "wikilink") of magic and healing. He has
plenty of ingredients for you or can sell you some of his own special
brands. Just type [list](List.md "wikilink") to see what he can offer.*

**Keywords:** alchemist, small, man.  
**[Level](Level.md "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](Alignment.md "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs.md "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](Detect_Hidden.md "wikilink"), [detect
invis](Detect_Invis.md "wikilink").
