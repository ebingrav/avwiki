*A good [thief](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") is always on his toes,
always scanning his surroundings for [hidden
goodies](/wiki/Hide_Flag "wikilink"), making mental notes of the best escape
routes should a fight break out. Alertness represents this state of
focused perception. While utilizing this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), a
[Rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") will be able to see
[hidden](/wiki/Hide_Flag "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), people, hidden or secret
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink"), and even
[sneaking](/wiki/Sneak_Flag "wikilink") people.
[Rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") must use this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") in order to make use
of the [withdraw](/wiki/Withdraw_(skill) "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). The downside is that
it will cost more [movement points](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink") each time
the [Rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") moves, because of the added
notice he takes of everything in each new
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").*

Prerequisite: [Lore](/wiki/Lore "wikilink"),
[Nightvision](/wiki/Nightvision "wikilink").

Syntax: alertness.
