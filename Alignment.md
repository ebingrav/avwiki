*Alignment is one of the primary concerns of mid-to-high-level players.
Align can be anywhere from -1000 (Satanic) to +1000 (Angelic). Align can
apply either to [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or to
[equipment](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink"). For example, if an angelic
player attempts to wield an [anti-good](/wiki/Anti-Good_Flag "wikilink")
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink"), it will repel the player and
fall off immediately. Enchanting changes the alignment of an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") to favor the alignment of the
enchanter. It does not normally remove alignments that oppose the
alignment of the enchanter. A player's alignment is changed by
[killing](/wiki/Kill "wikilink") [mobs](Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink").
[Killing](/wiki/Kill "wikilink") a good [mob](Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")
will move your alignment towards evil, whereas killing a bad one will
move your alignment towards good.*
