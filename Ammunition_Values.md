[Ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have four
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **Shot Number:** This value determines how many times this
    [ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink") may be
    [shot](/wiki/Shoot "wikilink") before it is gone. When
    [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") put more than one
    item of [ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink") in their
    [inventory](/wiki/Inventory "wikilink"), all those items whose other three
    values (see below) are identical will automatically combine into one
    or more items of up to 500 shots each.

<!-- -->

-   **[Projectile Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Projectile_Types "wikilink"):** This
    value determines whether this
    [ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink") consists of
    [arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink"),
    [bolts](/wiki/Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink"),
    [bullets](/wiki/Category%3A_Bullets "wikilink"),
    [darts](/wiki/Category%3A_Darts "wikilink"), or
    [stones](/wiki/Category%3A_Stones "wikilink"); indirectly, it also
    determines which type(s) of [missile
    weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink") a
    [character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must
    [wield](Wield "wikilink") in order to [shoot](/wiki/Shoot "wikilink") it.
    Follow these links for more information about each [projectile
    type](/wiki/Category%3A_Projectile_Types "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **[Warhead Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Warhead_Types "wikilink"):** This value
    determines whether this
    [ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink") is tipped with
    [standard](/wiki/Category%3A_Standard_Warheads "wikilink"),
    [steel](/wiki/Category%3A_Steel_Warheads "wikilink"),
    [barbed](/wiki/Category%3A_Barbed_Warheads "wikilink"),
    [poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poison_Warheads "wikilink"),
    [flaming](/wiki/Category%3A_Flaming_Warheads "wikilink"),
    [piercing](/wiki/Category%3A_Piercing_Warheads "wikilink"),
    [splinter](/wiki/Category%3A_Splinter_Warheads "wikilink"), one of four
    types of [mystic](/wiki/Category%3A_Mystic_Warheads "wikilink"), or
    [sableroix](/wiki/Category%3A_Sableroix_Warheads "wikilink") warheads (or
    some other [type](/wiki/Category%3A_Warhead_Types "wikilink") not found
    in Midgaardia). Follow these links for more information about each
    [warhead type](/wiki/Category%3A_Warhead_Types "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **[Poison Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink"):** If this
    [ammuniton](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink") has a [poison
    warhead](/wiki/Category%3A_Poison_Warheads "wikilink"), then this value
    determines which one (and only one) [type of
    poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") it carries, which may be
    either [poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poison "wikilink"),
    [toxin](/wiki/Category%3A_Toxin "wikilink"),
    [biotoxin](/wiki/Category%3A_Biotoxin "wikilink"),
    [virus](/wiki/Category%3A_Virus "wikilink"),
    [venom](/wiki/Category%3A_Venom "wikilink"), or
    [plague](/wiki/Category%3A_Plague "wikilink") (or some other
    [type](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") not found in Midgaardia).
    Follow these links for more information about each [poison
    type](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink").

See also [Ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink").
