## Comments

Necklace](Carved_Bone_Necklace "wikilink") it is
[treasure](:Category:_Treasure "wikilink") so it lacks the base [armor
class](Armor_Class "wikilink") and cannot be
[enchanted](Enchant_Armor "wikilink"). But it does have a nice [save vs
spell](Saving_Throw "wikilink").
