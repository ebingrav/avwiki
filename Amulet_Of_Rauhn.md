[draconian](Draconian_Language.md "wikilink") rune has been carved. The
carving has then been lined with melted silver. If you remember your
[Dragon](Dragons.md "wikilink") Lore correctly, this is the rune of the
Ancient Silver [Dragon](Dragons.md "wikilink"), Rauhn.*
