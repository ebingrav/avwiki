## Comments

While this has the same AC bonus as [Carved Bone
Necklace](Carved_Bone_Necklace "wikilink") it is
[treasure](:Category:_Treasure.md "wikilink") so it lacks the base
[armor class](Armor_Class.md "wikilink") and cannot be
[enchanted](Enchant_Armor.md "wikilink"). But it does have a nice [save
vs spell](Saving_Throw.md "wikilink").
