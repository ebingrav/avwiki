Comments
--------

To reach this mob, one needs either [Pass Door](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink") or
the [Strange Key](/wiki/Strange_Key "wikilink").  
This mob may be difficult to kill since it is affected by
[Sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink"), and has two
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") cronies in the same room.
