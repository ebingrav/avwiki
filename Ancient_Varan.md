*Older than history, these beautiful large lizard-like creatures seem
slow, but when threatened they become fierce and almost as deadly as [a
dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink"). The varan's large tail can be used with
deadly precision.*

**Keywords:** ancient, varan.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 35.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** none.  
**Affects:** [sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
