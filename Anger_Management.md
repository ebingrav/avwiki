*As an alternative to diminishing an enemy's aggressiveness with the
'[awe](Awe "wikilink")' [spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink"), a Psi can
redirect that anger to another target. This causes the
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink")'s victim to stop attacking the
[Psi](:Category:_Psionicists "wikilink"), and start attacking another,
random person in the [Psi](:Category:_Psionicists "wikilink")'s group.
There is a chance that, as a side effect, the target will receive a
bonus or penalty to attack, as their anger level is manipulated. It is
suggested that [Psions](:Category:_Psionicists "wikilink") obtain their
group's leader's permission before using this
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Awe](Awe "wikilink"),
[Overconfidence](Overconfidence "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'anger management'
\<[mob](:Category:_Mobs "wikilink")\>.
