*As an alternative to diminishing an enemy's aggressiveness with the
'[awe](/wiki/Awe "wikilink")' [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), a Psi
can redirect that anger to another target. This causes the
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")'s victim to stop attacking the
[Psi](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink"), and start attacking another,
random person in the [Psi](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink")'s group.
There is a chance that, as a side effect, the target will receive a
bonus or penalty to attack, as their anger level is manipulated. It is
suggested that [Psions](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") obtain their
group's leader's permission before using this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Awe](/wiki/Awe "wikilink"),
[Overconfidence](/wiki/Overconfidence "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'anger management'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
