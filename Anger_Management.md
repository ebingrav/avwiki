*As an alternative to diminishing an enemy's aggressiveness with the
'[awe](Awe.md "wikilink")' [spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink"), a
Psi can redirect that anger to another target. This causes the
[spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink")'s victim to stop attacking the
[Psi](:Category:_Psionicists.md "wikilink"), and start attacking
another, random person in the
[Psi](:Category:_Psionicists.md "wikilink")'s group. There is a chance
that, as a side effect, the target will receive a bonus or penalty to
attack, as their anger level is manipulated. It is suggested that
[Psions](:Category:_Psionicists.md "wikilink") obtain their group's
leader's permission before using this
[spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Awe](Awe.md "wikilink"),
[Overconfidence](Overconfidence.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast.md "wikilink") 'anger management'
\<[mob](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink")\>.
