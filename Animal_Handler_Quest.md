This quest confers the [Eragora - Animal
Handler](/wiki/Eragora_-_Animal_Handler "wikilink") to the entire group
completing it, and is located within
[Rapture](/wiki/Category%3ARapture "wikilink") on
[Eragora](/wiki/Category%3AEragora "wikilink").

Simply find a proper docile mob and decide to open a butcher shop. If
you survive, you get the insignia and its bonus.
