*A holy person may anoint a corpse with a blessing, preserving it a bit
longer than naturally possible. Anoint achieves the same goal as
[embalm](/wiki/Embalm "wikilink") but does not require
[bandages](/wiki/Some_Bandages "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Embalm](/wiki/Embalm "wikilink"), [Bless](Bless "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'anoint' <corpse>.
