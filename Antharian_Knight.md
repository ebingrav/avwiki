*These Antharians are impressive. The only information that
non-Antharians have of the knights are that they are very good, and very
mysterious. It is said that they are knighted in a very intense ceremony
in the Temple of Anthar.*

**Keywords:** antharian, knight.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 72.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** none.  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[protection evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink").
