This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") prevents a neutral
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
([alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") between -300 and 300) from
[wearing](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") an [object](Category%3A_Objects "wikilink").
This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") may be added via
[enchanting](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink").
