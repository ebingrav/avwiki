*Through deep attention to the details of your surroundings, you can
learn to anticipate the motions and tactics of your enemies. For the
duration of this [Psionic](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), your mind will be aware enough
of your opponents that you will have a better chance of hitting them.
ANTICIPATE is a [BIOCENTRIC
spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Biocentric_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). It cannot
be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on others until the caster masters the art of
[BIO EMPATHY](/wiki/Bio_Empathy "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'anticipate'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in
[Fortitudes](/wiki/Fortitudes "wikilink").
