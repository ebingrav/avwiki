A ticket in the [Orosca's Enchanted Wrist
Guard](/wiki/Orosca's_Enchanted_Wrist_Guard "wikilink") quest.  
pp [Broo Shaman](/wiki/Broo_Shaman "wikilink"), the mob which carries the
brooch. Note that this item only repops if all the shamen were killed
(like the [Flaming Pants](/wiki/Flaming_Pants "wikilink")), so if you kill the
shamen and you don't get a brooch, then you'll need to wait for the
repop.
