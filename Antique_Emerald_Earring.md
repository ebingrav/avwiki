Guard](/wiki/Orosca's_Enchanted_Wrist_Guard "wikilink")  
pp [Diana](/wiki/Diana "wikilink"), go 9e, n, \[d\], n, d, 2w, and kill the
[Silk Producer](/wiki/Silk_Producer "wikilink"), a level 20-25 mob which
carries the earring.
