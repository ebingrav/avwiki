A ticket in the [Orosca's Enchanted Wrist
Guard](/wiki/Orosca's_Enchanted_Wrist_Guard "wikilink") quest  
This item is located within the House of quests, another part of the
[Secrets Of Avatar](/wiki/Secrets_Of_Avatar "wikilink").  
To get there, pp [stat beho](/wiki/Statue_Of_The_Mighty_Beholder "wikilink")
and go 7s, e, 2s, 6w, s, 2w, 2n. From there, head north, killing mobs of
increasing difficulty and collecting keys. The mob which carries the
tiara is the [Quest Sword](/wiki/Quest_Sword "wikilink"), which is the
toughest mob you have to fight to get [Orosca's Enchanted Wrist
Guard](/wiki/Orosca's_Enchanted_Wrist_Guard "wikilink"), but it's still weak
compared to most hero gear mobs.
