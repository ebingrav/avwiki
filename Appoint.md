*With this [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") a group leader can
pass his group to someone else. Everyone in the group will automatically
stop [following](/wiki/Follow "wikilink") you, and will instead
[follow](/wiki/Follow "wikilink") the new appointed leader. The new leader has
to be in the group, and has to be [able to
lead](/wiki/Leading_Ability "wikilink") that many people.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: appoint &lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
