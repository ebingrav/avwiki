*Like [banks](/wiki/Category%3A_Banks "wikilink"), you need to be in a certain
place to use this [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"). Appraising
an [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") while in the same
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") as a
[Smith](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink") will give you a price on how much
the [Smith](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink") will
[repair](/wiki/Repair "wikilink") your
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") for you. Remember! A
[Smith](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink") will [repair](/wiki/Repair "wikilink")
your [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") perfectly, which is better
than your own. This is also a good
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") to use for testing for the
existence of a [Smith](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink") in your
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").*

Syntax: appraise &lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.

See also [Smiths](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink").
