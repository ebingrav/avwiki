*There are [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") of magic in our
mysterious world that can be used to [cast
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellcasting_Gear "wikilink"). These
[wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink") and
[staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") do not simply work for anybody
who waves them around... the [wielder](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") of these
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") must know how to focus and
trigger the energy within these
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). Arcane Knowledge is a general
knowledge of how to utilize magic [wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink")
and [staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink"). Without it, they are simply
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") to show off at the nearest Inn.*

Prerequisite(s): [Magic Lore](/wiki/Magic_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is often highly
useful to [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of
spellcasting [classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") (like
[psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink"),
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink"), and
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink")). It enables
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") to
[brandish](Brandish "wikilink") [wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink")
and [staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink"), which allows them to
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") [spells](Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") like
[Heal](/wiki/Heal_(spell) "wikilink") or [Sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink") on
many [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") simultaneously
while consuming [brandishes](/wiki/Category%3A_Brandishes "wikilink") rather
than [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink").
