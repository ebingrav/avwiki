*With this [skill](:Category:_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
[practiced](Practice "wikilink"), an
[archer](:Category:_Archers "wikilink") can effectively identify a
[bow](:Category:_Missile_Weapons "wikilink") or
[arrow](:Category:_Arrows "wikilink") item by
[examining](Examine "wikilink") it. Many
[armor](:Category:_Armor "wikilink") items worn on the wrists, hands or
arms can influence archery in many ways. [Examining](Examine "wikilink")
them with archery lore [practiced](Practice "wikilink") will reveal
these influences.*

*Additionally, an archer examining foes will learn a little about their
defenses against arrows.*

Prerequisite(s): [Lore](Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: [examine](Examine "wikilink")
\<[object](:Category:_Objects "wikilink")\>.
