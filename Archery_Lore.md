*With this [skill](:Category:_Skills_And_Spells.md "wikilink")
[practiced](Practice.md "wikilink"), an
[archer](:Category:_Archers.md "wikilink") can effectively identify a
[bow](:Category:_Missile_Weapons.md "wikilink") or
[arrow](:Category:_Arrows.md "wikilink") item by
[examining](Examine.md "wikilink") it. Many
[armor](:Category:_Armor.md "wikilink") items worn on the wrists, hands
or arms can influence archery in many ways.
[Examining](Examine.md "wikilink") them with archery lore
[practiced](Practice.md "wikilink") will reveal these influences.*

*Additionally, an archer examining foes will learn a little about their
defenses against arrows.*

Prerequisite(s): [Lore](Lore.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [examine](Examine.md "wikilink")
\<[object](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink")\>.
