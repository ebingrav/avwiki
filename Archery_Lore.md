*With this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"), an
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") can effectively identify a
[bow](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink") or
[arrow](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink") item by
[examining](/wiki/Examine "wikilink") it. Many
[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink") items worn on the wrists, hands or
arms can influence archery in many ways. [Examining](/wiki/Examine "wikilink")
them with archery lore [practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") will reveal
these influences.*

Prerequisite(s): [Lore](/wiki/Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: [examine](/wiki/Examine "wikilink")
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.
