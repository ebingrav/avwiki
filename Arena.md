*This [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") is similar to the
[recall](Recall "wikilink") [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"),
except that it takes you to a [special
place](/wiki/Category%3A_Special_Areas "wikilink"), and is not settable. ARENA
takes you to the middle of the [Players'
arena](/wiki/Category%3A_Avatar_Arena "wikilink").*

See also [Avatar Arena](/wiki/Category%3A_Avatar_Arena "wikilink") (area).
