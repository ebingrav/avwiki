[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink") are revealed to the person who has
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") this

[examinations](/wiki/Examine "wikilink") may be necessary to ensure that all
relevant characteristics are revealed.*
