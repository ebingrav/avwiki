*Familiarity with every link and plate in his
[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink") enables a fighter to take
advantage of its best defensive capabilities. Once this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is learned, you must
[remove](/wiki/Remove "wikilink") all of your
[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") and re-equip it in order to reap
the benefits of your newfound knowledge. This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is available only to
those whose specialty is fighting, and it is automatic once
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") to its highest level.*

Prerequisite(s): [Armor Lore](/wiki/Armor_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
