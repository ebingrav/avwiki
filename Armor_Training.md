*With armor training, a [Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink")
can use his [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") limited abilities (such as
[resist pain](/wiki/Resist_Pain "wikilink")) with a higher limit; -850 for
[Heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") and -1500 for
[lords](/wiki/Lord "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
