*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") decreases your
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") by forming a magical [barrier of
protection](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink").
It gives a bonus of -20 [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'armor'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is first of four
[armor-class-boosting
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")
that both [clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") and
[priests](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") between [level](Level "wikilink") 1 and
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 50. [Lowmort](Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink")
[paladins](/wiki/Category%3A_Paladins "wikilink") and
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may also
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of many other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it at [hero](Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink"). It is included in both
[Aegis](/wiki/Aegis "wikilink") and [Awen](Awen "wikilink"). Its
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") bonus may be modified by its
[caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")'s choice of [worship](Worship "wikilink").

See also [Armor Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor_Gear "wikilink").
