*An old but very strong armourer, he has made more
[armours](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink") in his lifetime than you have
ever seen.*

**Keywords:** armourer, armorer.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
