*With great skill in archery comes anticipation of where an enemy's
[arrow](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink") will land. This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") represents the ability
to dodge an oncoming [arrow](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink") or
[bolt](/wiki/Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink"). Although not
[archers](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink"), the
[Monks](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") and
[Assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink") can also learn to dodge
arrows, due to their superior physical conditioning and reaction time.*

Prerequisite(s): [dodge](/wiki/Dodge "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
