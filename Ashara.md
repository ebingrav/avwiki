A seamstress from the Kzin home plane offers to trade her wares.

Ashara is dressed in an exquisite silk-like fabric. She offers to trade
you a sarong for some green bananas from Midgaardia. Believe it or not,
green bananas are considered a delicacy on Ashara's home plane.

Plane: [Thorngate](/wiki/Category%3AThorngate "wikilink")  
Area: [Rietta's Wonders](/wiki/Category%3ARietta's_Wonders "wikilink")
