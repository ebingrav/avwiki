Assassins will use the most powerful stealth attack at its disposal
against non-rogues. This includes [sneak
attack](/wiki/Sneak_Attack "wikilink"), [vital shot](Vital_Shot "wikilink"),
[murder](/wiki/Murder "wikilink") for mobs up to level 60, and
[backstab](/wiki/Backstab "wikilink") for mobs between levels 61 and 124.

Assassins will also land [parting shots](/wiki/Assassin_Sight "wikilink")
against fleeing characters.
