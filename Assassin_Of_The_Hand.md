Comments
--------

This mob is hidden in the Rogue's Guild. Only
[rogues](/wiki/Category%3ARogues "wikilink") with [Rogue
Lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink") can unlock the doors to the mob.

Directions
----------

**[Area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"):**
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Midgaard_Map "wikilink")).  
**[Room(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"):** The Master Thief's Secret
Room (portable).  
Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 7n, 2e, 2s, e, \[s\],
\[d\].  
The assassin of the Hand is portalable. However, the [dark and wiry
man](dark_and_wiry_man "wikilink") in the same room checks if you have
[Rogue Lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink"). If you do not, then you will be
[Blackjacked](/wiki/Blackjack "wikilink"). Thus, one must either be a
[rogue](/wiki/Category%3ARogues "wikilink"), or kill exceedingly quickly to
obtain this mob's gear, or target the [dark and wiry
man](dark_and_wiry_man "wikilink") and kill him before he lands his
[blackjack](blackjack "wikilink").
