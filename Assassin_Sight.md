''Opportunism at its most deadly, a
[rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") trained in Assassin's Sight is
adept at sensing and exploiting chances to inflict [vital
shots](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink"). This manifests in three ways. First and
foremost, the [rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") can exploit the
brief opening created when a foe attempts to [flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink")
from them. This "parting shot" requires that the
[rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") and the
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") were fighting each other when it
tried to [flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink"), and also that the
[rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") not be in 'lag' from using [a
skill or spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). Secondly, the
knowledge gained via Assassin's Sight is indispensable in learning more
advanced skills, like [backstab](/wiki/Backstab "wikilink"). Finally, the
[rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") gains the ability to sense a
foe's weakest point when making a [vital strike](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink").
If he doesn't try to aim for a particular region, the rogue can hit this
'weakest point' and thus inflict more pain and suffering with the [vital
shot](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). The increase in
damage is partly based on the [rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink")'s
[intelligence](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink").

Prerequisite(s): [Lethal Aim](/wiki/Lethal_Aim "wikilink"), [Rogue
Lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
