*The caster travels to another
[player](:Category:_Characters "wikilink") using this
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink"). This
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink") is
[clerical](:Category:_Clerics "wikilink") in nature; although many can
[learn](Practice "wikilink") the ways of astral transit, the
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink") always relies on some
[deity](:Category:_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink")'s intercession. The
inventive [clerics](:Category:_Clerics "wikilink") who created the
astral spell intended it as a way to get to
[people](:Category:_Characters "wikilink") in need. As a result, astral
can only be targeted at [other
players](:Category:_Characters "wikilink") and NOT on [creatures
(mobiles)](:Category:_Mobs "wikilink"), even if you're not a
[cleric](:Category:_Clerics "wikilink") and don't intend to help
anyone.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'astral'
\<[character](:Category:_Characters "wikilink")\>.

[Clerics](:Category:_Clerics "wikilink") may find this
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink") rather useful at
[lowmort](:Category:_Lowmort "wikilink")
[tier](:Category:_Tiers "wikilink") but usually [cast](Cast "wikilink")
[Teleport](Teleport "wikilink") instead of it at
[hero](:Category:_Hero "wikilink") [tier](:Category:_Tiers "wikilink")
because [Teleport](Teleport "wikilink") is more versatile.

This is one of the only transport spells available to [Black Circle
Initiates](:Category:_Black_Circle_Initiates "wikilink"). However it has
many limitations such as not being able to astral to a player in an
[infirmary](:Category:Infirmaries "wikilink") flagged room (as well as
[cursed/no-recall rooms](Cursed_Rooms "wikilink") of course).
