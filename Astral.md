*The caster travels to another
[player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") using this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
[clerical](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") in nature; although many can
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") the ways of astral transit, the
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") always relies on some
[deity](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink")'s intercession. The
inventive [clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") who created the
astral spell intended it as a way to get to
[people](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") in need. As a result, astral
can only be targeted at [other
players](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") and NOT on [creatures
(mobiles)](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), even if you're not a
[cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") and don't intend to help
anyone.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'astral'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

[Clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") may find this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") rather useful at
[lowmort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink")
[tier](Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") but usually [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
[Teleport](/wiki/Teleport "wikilink") instead of it at
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") [tier](Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink")
because [Teleport](/wiki/Teleport "wikilink") is more versatile.
