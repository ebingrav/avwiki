*The caster travels to another
[player](:Category:_Characters.md "wikilink") using this
[spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink"). This
[spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink") is
[clerical](:Category:_Clerics.md "wikilink") in nature; although many
can [learn](Practice.md "wikilink") the ways of astral transit, the
[spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink") always relies on some
[deity](:Category:_Gods_And_Goddesses.md "wikilink")'s intercession. The
inventive [clerics](:Category:_Clerics.md "wikilink") who created the
astral spell intended it as a way to get to
[people](:Category:_Characters.md "wikilink") in need. As a result,
astral can only be targeted at [other
players](:Category:_Characters.md "wikilink") and NOT on [creatures
(mobiles)](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink"), even if you're not a
[cleric](:Category:_Clerics.md "wikilink") and don't intend to help
anyone.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](Cast.md "wikilink") 'astral'
\<[character](:Category:_Characters.md "wikilink")\>.

[Clerics](:Category:_Clerics.md "wikilink") may find this
[spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink") rather useful at
[lowmort](:Category:_Lowmort.md "wikilink")
[tier](:Category:_Tiers.md "wikilink") but usually
[cast](Cast.md "wikilink") [Teleport](Teleport.md "wikilink") instead of
it at [hero](:Category:_Hero.md "wikilink")
[tier](:Category:_Tiers.md "wikilink") because
[Teleport](Teleport.md "wikilink") is more versatile.

This is one of the only transport spells available to [Black Circle
Initiates](:Category:_Black_Circle_Initiates.md "wikilink"). However it
has many limitations such as not being able to astral to a player in an
[infirmary](:Category:Infirmaries.md "wikilink") flagged room (as well
as [cursed/no-recall rooms](Cursed_Rooms.md "wikilink") of course).
