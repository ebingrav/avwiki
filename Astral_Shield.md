*Do you hate it when [portals](Portals.md "wikilink") don't agree with
you? Do you wish you had something to help protect you from the
dreaded...energy drain? Well here is your chance to add some protection!
With an astral shield, you too can feel a little safer! Although it
isn't perfect, it definitely gives you the edge you need against even
the most disagreeable [portal](Portals.md "wikilink")!*

Prerequisite(s): [Portal](Portal.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast.md "wikilink") 'astral shield'
\<[character](:Category:_Characters.md "wikilink")\>.

This [spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink") is very useful for
protecting [characters](:Category:_Characters.md "wikilink") from losing
[hit points](Hit_Points.md "wikilink"), [mana
points](Mana_Points.md "wikilink"), and/or [move
points](Move_Points.md "wikilink") when traveling via
[portals](Portals.md "wikilink") or [nexuses](Nexuses.md "wikilink").
