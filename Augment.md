*Augment is a toggle [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") which,
when turned ON, increases the clerical power of [healing
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Heal_Characters "wikilink")
as well as [refresh](/wiki/Refresh "wikilink") spells. You may also set the
power level, however, this added energy comes with a higher price.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: augment on.  
Syntax: augment off.  
Syntax: augment <power:1-?>.  
See also [Quicken](/wiki/Quicken "wikilink") and [Surge](Surge "wikilink"), as
well as [Augmenting Ability](/wiki/Augmenting_Ability "wikilink").
