''By applying your knowledge of the
[psionic](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") art to analyzing a
target's aura, you can determine what
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") [affect](Affects "wikilink")
that person, and for how long. This examination will only reveal
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"); it cannot detect effects from
non-magical [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink").

Prerequisite(s): [Aura Sight](/wiki/Aura_Sight "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'aura focus'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
