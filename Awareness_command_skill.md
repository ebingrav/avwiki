*Little escapes the watchful eye of the hunter. The awareness
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") allows an
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") to see things which are
[hidden](/wiki/Hide_Flag "wikilink"), and aids in keeping a close eye on
remaining [ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: awareness.
