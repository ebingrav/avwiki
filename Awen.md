*This [clerical](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") magic surrounds the
target [character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") with a variety of
[protection
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink"),
including [armor](/wiki/Armor_(spell) "wikilink"), [holy
armor](/wiki/Holy_Armor "wikilink"), [holy aura](/wiki/Holy_Aura "wikilink"),
[bless](/wiki/Bless "wikilink"), [frenzy](Frenzy "wikilink"), [protection
evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"), [protection
good](/wiki/Protection_Good "wikilink"), and
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink"). The Awen
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") allows the
[casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") of a group of spells at once. The amount of
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") it costs is balanced by the speed of
casting a variety of complex spells simultaneously. NOTE: the awen
affect which shows up on your [affects](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") list is
merely a placeholder and does not actually modify anything.*

Prerequisite(s): [Holy Aura](/wiki/Holy_Aura "wikilink"),
[Sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'awen'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") will find this
[cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") "macro"
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") (or its variant
[Aegis](/wiki/Aegis "wikilink")), like both [Foci](Foci "wikilink") and
[Fortitudes](/wiki/Fortitudes "wikilink"), highly valuable for
[buffing](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")
either themselves or others.
