*Given to the [centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") to hide by [the Unicorn
Stallion](/wiki/Unicorn_Stallion "wikilink"), the baby unicorn plays in the
glade like a child of any [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink"). She is
clueless of the war raging around her. She likes apples and
[carrots](/wiki/Single_Carrot "wikilink") but they are a rare treat for her.*
