*The handiwork of this [bag](:Category:_Containers.md "wikilink") seems
too delicate in nature to be made by a [goblin](Goblins.md "wikilink")
or [hobgoblin](Hobgoblins.md "wikilink"), as the wings are woven in and
out of grass blades. So intricate, it is hard to forget how many
[sprites](Sprites.md "wikilink") lost their wings for this one small
[bag](:Category:_Containers.md "wikilink"). The wings shimmer, looking
like the forest leaves with drops of dew clinging to their tips. Even if
this [bag](:Category:_Containers.md "wikilink") was taken outside of
[the forest](:Category:_Ascension_Woods.md "wikilink"), it would be
miracle if it wasn't stolen on the Old East road or on [the
highways](:Category:_Highways/Great_Wall.md "wikilink").*
