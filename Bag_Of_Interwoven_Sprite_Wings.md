*The handiwork of this [bag](:Category:_Containers "wikilink") seems too
delicate in nature to be made by a [goblin](Goblins "wikilink") or
[hobgoblin](Hobgoblins "wikilink"), as the wings are woven in and out of
grass blades. So intricate, it is hard to forget how many
[sprites](Sprites "wikilink") lost their wings for this one small
[bag](:Category:_Containers "wikilink"). The wings shimmer, looking like
the forest leaves with drops of dew clinging to their tips. Even if this
[bag](:Category:_Containers "wikilink") was taken outside of [the
forest](:Category:_Ascension_Woods "wikilink"), it would be miracle if
it wasn't stolen on the Old East road or on [the
highways](:Category:_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink").*
