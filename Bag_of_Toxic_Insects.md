*This bag is filled with live poisonous insects, which periodically try
to bite through the thin cloth. Careful, they're restless!*

`Carries roughly 20 `[`doses`](/wiki/Poison_Values "wikilink")` of `[`biotoxin`](Category%3A_Biotoxin "wikilink")`, a strong `[`poison`](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink")` which which lowers overall health.`  
`This should be used on no less than a level 35 weapon.`
