*With this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") you can
[wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") equal weighted weapon in both hands up to a
certain weight. (Weight is determined by how much skill and how many
balance-like skills you have.)*

Prerequisite(s): [Poise](/wiki/Poise "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
