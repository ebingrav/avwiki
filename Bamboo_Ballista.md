*While physically intimidating, this huge crossbow made entirely of
interwoven bamboo strands is surprisingly light. Not being made of hard
wood makes it unsuited for parrying incoming blows, though.*

`Damage is 15-19 to 44-50.`  
`Modifies hit roll by 18 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** bamboo, ballista  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 50-52.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):** [Light
Crossbow](/wiki/Category%3ALight_Crossbows "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <wielded>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 50 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 5 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** hum.
