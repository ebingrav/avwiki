This "quest" involves murdering away at the sanced Fungal Forest
residents until they grew tired of you and
[Psilocybin](/wiki/Psilocybin "wikilink") tries to bribe you away with a bag
of gems containing gems worth about 1.6 million gold, which includes a
perfect emerald.

As time progresses and you persist in killing them, the myconoids will
grow more and more dangerous, starting to use gas attacks and other
abilities like toss, that were not previously used.

Having completed this quest will net you [Bane Of The Myconoids
Insignia](/wiki/Bane_Of_The_Myconoids_Insignia "wikilink"), and any further
entry into Fungal Forest will draw a lot of attention towards the entire
group, so it is best to let your leader know immediately you are not
welcome in there.

**Warning**: Completing this quest solo is very difficult even for
hogged berserkers or dragons. Major issue is that Fungal Forest is
no-teleport-out area, and once sufficient number of mobs becomes aggie
and hunt you down, it can be difficult to escape alive.
