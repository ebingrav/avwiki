*A thin [ent](/wiki/Ents "wikilink") barely older than the few children
running through the streets, he has started up a shop in
[Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink"). He became interested in the
trade after visiting [his great great
grandfather](/wiki/Edmuntrillion "wikilink") in
[Stonehall](/wiki/Category%3A_Stonehall "wikilink"). Next to him was [a
devilish banker](/wiki/Kir'tal "wikilink") who promised that there was great
wealth in being a banker.*

**Keywords:** banker, ent.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 28.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").
