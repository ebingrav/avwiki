*The banshee appears as the ghost of a hideous, wrinkled woman with an
enormous mouth. From her tears a scream straight from the pits of hell.*

**Keywords:** Banshee, wavering, form.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 26.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"),
[fly](/wiki/Fly "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"), [pass
door](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
