*An account from Wyland the [Druid](/wiki/Category%3A_Druids "wikilink"):
After meditation and fasting, I was ready to reach that inner calm that
allows a [druid](/wiki/Category%3A_Druids "wikilink") to become a living tree.
This stasis is an experience reserved for those within the highest
Circles of magic. I was unsure if I was ready for this step, and in
truth that should have given me a hint, but I was young and ambitious to
be one with the forest. I began the incantation and sensed a wrongness
in my words. Small mistakes in inflection or possibly something I had
yet to learn, when I finished I was not a young Oak bending in the wind.
I was still Wyland, with skin made of rough bark. After several years of
study, I was able to remove the [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
and now it is a common practice among
[druids](/wiki/Category%3A_Druids "wikilink") for
[defense](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink").
(Modifies [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") by -30)*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'barkskin'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rather valuable for
[buffing](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")
oneself or others.

See also [Barkskin Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Barkskin_Gear "wikilink").
