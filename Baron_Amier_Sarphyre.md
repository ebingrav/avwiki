*The Baron is a tall and stately [sprite](/wiki/Sprites "wikilink"), wearing
[rich, dark purple robes](/wiki/Rich_Purple_Robes "wikilink") and a yellowish,
breezy material draped over his wings, making them seem to be made of
golden foil.*

**Keywords:** Baron, Amier, Sarphyre.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 15.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
