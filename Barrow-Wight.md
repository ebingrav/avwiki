*The barrow wight is a ghastly looking creature. Drawing its existence
from the negative material plane, it only half exists in our world. Dark
shadows are its face; shining black claws are its features.*

**Keywords:** wight.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 29.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink"),
[lich](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[invisible](/wiki/Invis "wikilink"), [pass door](Pass_Door "wikilink").
