*You can barter with [shopkeepers](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink")
if you desire. The [shopkeeper](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink") must
desire the [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") and feel it is a good
deal (in favor of the [shopkeeper](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink"))
before actually agreeing to trade [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
for [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). You will have poor luck
trading a shield for a necklace at the jeweller... why would a jeweller
want a shield?! You get the idea. If you can't normally
[sell](/wiki/Sell "wikilink") the item at the
[shop](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink")... it is doubtful you can
barter that [type of item](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"). (An
exception being [treasure items](/wiki/Category%3A_Treasure "wikilink")!) All
trades are final... if you trade a 50,000 gold item for a 10 gold
item... you are stuck with it. Bartering doesn't give you change for the
difference.*

Syntax: barter &lt;[your object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;
&lt;[desired object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.
