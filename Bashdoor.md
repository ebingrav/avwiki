*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") enables a player
to bash open a [door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") to gain entrance. It
may cost a certain amount of [hp](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") to smash the
[door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink"). Although it does not require a
shield, a shield will make it less painful.*

Prerequisite(s): [Bash](/wiki/Bash "wikilink").

Syntax: bashdoor <direction>.
