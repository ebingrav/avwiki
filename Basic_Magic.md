Summary
=======

Lowmort mage lore. Provides access to the hero mage lore, lowmort wizard
and stormlord lores, and basic mage skills, including surge, foci, and
disintegrate.

Prereqs
=======

-   [ initiation](/wiki/Initiation "wikilink")
