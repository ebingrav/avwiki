Comments
--------

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must pass three locked
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") to reach this
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"); to do this, they will need to
either [bash](/wiki/Bashdoor "wikilink") these
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") open or
[unlock](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") them using their respective
[keys](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may obtain their
[keys](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") by doing the following: (1) either
[buy](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") [a mine pass level
one](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One "wikilink") from [the
receptionist](/wiki/Receptionist "wikilink") or complete [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Mine Pass Level One
Quest](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One_Quest "wikilink"), (2) get [a mine pass level
two](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_Two "wikilink") from [a humble
pilgrim](/wiki/Humble_Pilgrim "wikilink"), and (3) get [marlots
key](/wiki/Marlots_Key "wikilink") from [Brauss
Copperfury](/wiki/Brauss_Copperfury "wikilink").

This [ticket](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink") is used in [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Set of Three Loaded Dice
Quest](/wiki/Set_Of_Three_Loaded_Dice_Quest "wikilink").
