The three [tickets](/wiki/Category%3ATickets "wikilink") you need to collect
are:

-   [Necklace Of Forget-Me-Nots](/wiki/Necklace_Of_Forget-Me-Nots "wikilink")

<!-- -->

-   [Badge of Honour](/wiki/Badge_of_Honour "wikilink")

<!-- -->

-   [Damp Handkerchief](/wiki/Damp_Handkerchief "wikilink")

Once you have them all, go to [the ghost of Captain
Faraday](/wiki/Ghost_Of_Captain_Faraday "wikilink"), and give one of the
tickets to her to receive [a baton of
command](/wiki/Baton_Of_Command "wikilink").
