*With this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"), a hunter's knowledge of the animal
kingdom will help him tell if a beast is
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") or not. This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") does not help the
hunter determine the intentions of sentient beings, just animals.*

Prerequisite(s): [Lore](/wiki/Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
