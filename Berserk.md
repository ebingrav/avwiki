*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") puts the Berserk
in [Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink"). When damaged, they
can gain a bonus to [damage roll](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink") at the expense
of their [armor class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"). Several different levels
of rage are possible, depending on how frequently the
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") is hurt. The
[calm](/wiki/Calm "wikilink") and [awe](Awe "wikilink")
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") can slow the effects of berserk,
and the [frenzy](/wiki/Frenzy "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") will exacerbate them. This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is cumulative with the
[racial](/wiki/Racial_Berserk "wikilink") of the same name.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).

See also [Racial Berserk](/wiki/Racial_Berserk "wikilink").
