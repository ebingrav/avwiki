*Bioscan allows the [Psion](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") to
determine the physical and magical capacities of a potential opponent.
You'd be surprised how much you learn about a
[monster](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") by getting into their heads.*

Prerequisite(s): [Aura Sight](/wiki/Aura_Sight "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'bioscan'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
