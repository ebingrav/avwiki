[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will bite
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who are fighting them
and, with each bite, may or may not afflict their victims with,
depending upon their subtypes, either [plague](/wiki/Plague "wikilink") or a
variety of [poisons](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink").
