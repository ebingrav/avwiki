*The blade of the sword is a full meter long, and straight as an arrow,
with lightly fluted sides. Both edges are keened down to perfect lines,
invisibly sharp. Perfection like this could have only been forged by a
god. The black hilt is contrasted only by the faint symbol of the Scales
of Justice. Upon the blade is engraved a poem:*

*The Sword of Justice balances the pans*  
*Of right and wrong, and foul and fair.*  
*Eye for an eye, Doomgiver scans*  
*The fate of all folk everywhere.*
