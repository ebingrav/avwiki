*Named from a type of wood, BlackThorn the weapons maker is very short
but almost as wide as a thicket of his namesake. His dark skin is
scarred from years of working on the forge, giving the impression of
thorns. He carries [a large hammer](/wiki/Forge_Hammer "wikilink") that he
uses to pound iron into various weapons.*

**Keywords:** BlackThorn, weaponsmith.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
