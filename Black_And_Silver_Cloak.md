<b>Mob:</b> [Lizard Man squire](/wiki/Lizard_Man_squire "wikilink") or [Silver

Members carry the level 31-40 version. It is otherwise the same gear
(different bases). The level 20 version is very good gear. Look
elsewhere for level 31-40 neck gear.
