ManyJaws](/wiki/Category%3A_Cavern_Of_ManyJaws "wikilink")' [Necklace of Teeth
Quest](/wiki/Necklace_Of_Teeth_Quest "wikilink"); it unlocks the wall between
Last Bastion of Hope and Lair of ManyJaws.
