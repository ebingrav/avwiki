` Modifies damage roll by 6 continuous.`

It is a one handed weapon which does piercing damage.  
Level: 17  
Damage: 5-6 to 21-22 (average 13-14)  
Weight: 35  
Flags: invis magic noremove  
Mob: [Lieutenant Commander](/wiki/Lieutenant_Commander "wikilink")  
Area: [Town Of Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Town_Of_Solace_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob is portable. If you know walking directions please add them here.

Note the weight of this weapon, and the fact that it is invis. If you
can't deal with that, then you might want to get the
[Shadowfang](/wiki/Dagger_"Shadowfang" "wikilink") instead.
