Comments
--------

This piece of gear loads on the floor of the room. However, the room is
also full of [aggressive mobs](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"), and has no
exits. Either speedwalk down and recall out, or keep
[Invis](/wiki/Invis "wikilink") or [Move Hidden](Move_Hidden "wikilink") up.
