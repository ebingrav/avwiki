*Blast Bolt is a more advanced version of
[Dart](/wiki/Dart_(spell) "wikilink"). It works exactly the same, except that
the caster excites the [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")'s kinetic
energy while in motion, causing it to explode on impact. Those planning
on [casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") Blast Bolt should keep all valuable
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") in
[containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink"), since this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), like
[Dart](/wiki/Dart_(spell) "wikilink"), cannot use
[containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink"). There are no
reimbursements for [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that you lose
with this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")... This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is a prerequisite for
[Detonate](/wiki/Detonate "wikilink") since it is easier to excite the kinetic
energy of moving [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") than
stationary ones.*

Prerequisite(s): [dart](/wiki/Dart_(spell) "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'blast bolt'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
