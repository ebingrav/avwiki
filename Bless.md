*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") improves the [to-hit
roll](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink") and [saving throw versus
spell](/wiki/Saving_Throw "wikilink") of the target
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). The amount of
improvement increases with the [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")'s
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'bless'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in both
[Aegis](/wiki/Aegis "wikilink") and [Awen](Awen "wikilink").

See also [Bless Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Bless_Gear "wikilink").
