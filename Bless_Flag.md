This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") causes all damage to an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") with it to be halved. It can be
added via the [Consecrate](/wiki/Consecrate "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").

See also [Consecrate](/wiki/Consecrate "wikilink").
