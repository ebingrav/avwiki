*By divine intervention of the
[gods](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink"), this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") blinds the target, making it much
harder for them to see, and thus, hit, you.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'blindness'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is practically
never used.

See also [Gear That Applies
'Blindness'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Blindness' "wikilink").
