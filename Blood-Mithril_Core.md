*A heart-shaped core of solid blood mithril is here.*  
**Keywords**: blood mithril core  
**Type**: ticket  
**Flags**: nolocate  
**Plane**: [Noctopia](/wiki/Category%3ANoctopia "wikilink")  
**Area**: [Shiftwatch Orb](/wiki/Category%3AShiftwatch_Orb "wikilink")  
**Mob**: [Iron Claw Sniper](/wiki/Iron_Claw_Sniper "wikilink")  
Originally, to get the mob with this item to appear, you needed to kill
the four [Iron Claw Snipers](/wiki/Iron_Claw_Sniper "wikilink") that surround
[Noctopia](/wiki/Category%3ANoctopia "wikilink") shift.

As of November 2011, the procedure appears to have changed slightly. The
leading theories involve killing all of the [Iron Claw
Sentinels](/wiki/Iron_Claw_Sentinel "wikilink"). While killing the snipers is
no longer sufficient, it may still be necessary.

See [Sword Of Yorimandil,
Restored](/wiki/Sword_Of_Yorimandil,_Restored "wikilink") for more information
