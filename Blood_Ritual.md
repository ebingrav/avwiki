*By attempting the ritual, one may sacrifice their own blood to create a
healing potion. The more blood you have to offer, the more powerful your
potion may become. You need a minimum of 250
[hps](Hit_Points "wikilink") and you \*can\* [cast](Cast "wikilink") it
without [the empty vial](Empty_Clay_Vial "wikilink"). The result is just
a big mess, however, so I suggest getting a
[vial](Empty_Clay_Vial "wikilink") in
[Midgaard](:Category:_Midgaard "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'blood ritual'.
