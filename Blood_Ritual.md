*By attempting the ritual, one may sacrifice their own blood to create a
healing potion. The more blood you have to offer, the more powerful your
potion may become. You need a minimum of 250
[hps](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") and you \*can\* [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") it
without the empty vial. The result is just a big mess, however, so I
suggest getting a vial in [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'blood ritual'.
