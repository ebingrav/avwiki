*The bloodhound [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") makes
[tracking](/wiki/Track "wikilink"), [seeking](Seek "wikilink") or using [blood
scent](/wiki/Blood_Scent "wikilink") or [blood trail](Blood_Trail "wikilink")
much faster and more efficient.*

Prerequisite(s): [Track](/wiki/Track "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
