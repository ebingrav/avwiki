''A [Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink")'s thirst for blood
can make him nigh invincible! This Rite regains
[hp](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") whenever the user makes a melee kill.
Bloodlust will not function if the
[Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") has better than -750
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") at [hero](Category%3A_Hero "wikilink"), or
-1250 AC at [Lord](/wiki/Lord "wikilink").

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
