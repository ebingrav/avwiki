*This blue bracelet is made from shiny crystal, too hard to be
scratched. Yet, it is so soft to the touch, that it can be bended with
the slightest hint of pressure. The veins in the crystal form a regular
pattern, as if this stone was not naturally grown, but made to suit the
liking of a great will.*
