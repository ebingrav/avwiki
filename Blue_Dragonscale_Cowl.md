Comments
--------

An interesting piece of mix gear, especially for wizards and stormlords
who don't get the move hidden skill. It has the same AC, but less mana
than the [Skull With Huge, Curved
Horns](/wiki/Skull_With_Huge,_Curved_Horns "wikilink"), but has the added
bonus of move hidden, allowing a [Chameleon
Cloak](/wiki/Chameleon_Cloak "wikilink") not to be worn and something more
useful worn instead.
