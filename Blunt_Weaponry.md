*This [skill](:Category:_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") imparts a great
proficiency with primitive weapons that can be easily made in the wild,
such as clubs and staffs. [Practicing](Practice "wikilink") it will
increase damage inflicted with any
[pounding](:Category:Melee_Weapons_That_Pound "wikilink"),
[crushing](:Category:Melee_Weapons_That_Crush "wikilink") or
[smacking](:Category:Melee_Weapons_That_Smack "wikilink") weapon.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
