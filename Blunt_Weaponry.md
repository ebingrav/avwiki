*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") imparts a great
proficiency with primitive weapons that can be easily made in the wild,
such as clubs and staffs. [Practicing](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it will
increase damage inflicted with any pounding, crushing or smacking
weapon.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
