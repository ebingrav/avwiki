*This [skill](:Category:_Skills_And_Spells.md "wikilink") imparts a
great proficiency with primitive weapons that can be easily made in the
wild, such as clubs and staffs. [Practicing](Practice.md "wikilink") it
will increase damage inflicted with any
[pounding](:Category:Melee_Weapons_That_Pound.md "wikilink"),
[crushing](:Category:Melee_Weapons_That_Crush.md "wikilink") or
[smacking](:Category:Melee_Weapons_That_Smack.md "wikilink") weapon.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
