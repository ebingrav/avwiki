This is a protective discipline which relies on psionics. BODY BRACE is
a [BIOCENTRIC
spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Biocentric_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") which causes
the caster's body to "brace" for damage by toughening muscle tissue and
body fat. Unless the caster is capable of [BIO
EMPATHY](/wiki/Bio_Empathy "wikilink"), this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") cannot be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
on others.''

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'body brace'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in
[Fortitudes](/wiki/Fortitudes "wikilink").
