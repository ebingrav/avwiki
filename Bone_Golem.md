*The bone [golem](/wiki/Golems "wikilink") is a strange beast. Created by some
insane magician, the bone [golem](/wiki/Golems "wikilink") is a composite of
various bones, from different animals. A large, wicked-looking skull
sits atop its shoulders, and its eyes seem to mock you.*

**Keywords:** bone, golem.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 26.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
