Comments
--------

Bonk can be tough for some newbies, so, if you're weak, then I would
suggest sleeping by [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink") until he
[casts](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") a [spell](Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") or two
on you. Also, be aware that Bonk's followers
[assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink").
