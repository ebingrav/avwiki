*For many years the [giants](Giants.md "wikilink") and the
[ents](Ents.md "wikilink") have battled. The reasons for their enmity
have been lost in time. They still battle today in [the far north of the
land](:Category:_Battleground.md "wikilink"). This book chronicles the
successes and failures of the strategies the [ents](Ents.md "wikilink")
have used in their attempts to defeat the
[giants](Giants.md "wikilink").*

## Comments

[Characters](:Category:_Characters.md "wikilink") must
[unlock](Unlock.md "wikilink") a large oak desk to get this
[ticket](:Category:_Tickets.md "wikilink"); to do this, they will need
to either [pick](Pick.md "wikilink") it or use [a small silver
key](Small_Silver_Key_(Library).md "wikilink") from [the Chief
Librarian](Chief_Librarian.md "wikilink").

This [ticket](:Category:_Tickets.md "wikilink") is used in [Royal
Midgaard Library](:Category:_Royal_Midgaard_Library.md "wikilink")'s
[Manes Quest](Manes_Quest "wikilink").
