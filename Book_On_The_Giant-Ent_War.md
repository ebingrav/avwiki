*For many years the [giants](/wiki/Giants "wikilink") and the
[ents](/wiki/Ents "wikilink") have battled. The reasons for their enmity have
been lost in time. They still battle today in [the far north of the
land](/wiki/Category%3A_Battleground "wikilink"). This book chronicles the
successes and failures of the strategies the [ents](/wiki/Ents "wikilink")
have used in their attempts to defeat the [giants](/wiki/Giants "wikilink").*

**Keyword(s):** book, war.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[ticket](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 2 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 0 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):**
[evil](/wiki/Evil_Flag "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must
[unlock](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") a large oak desk to get this
[ticket](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink"); to do this, they will need to
either [pick](/wiki/Pick "wikilink") it or use [a small silver
key](/wiki/Small_Silver_Key_(Library) "wikilink") from [the Chief
Librarian](/wiki/Chief_Librarian "wikilink").

This [ticket](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink") is used in [Royal Midgaard
Library](/wiki/Category%3A_Royal_Midgaard_Library "wikilink")'s [Manes
Quest](/wiki/Manes_Quest "wikilink").
