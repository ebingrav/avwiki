*The [Immortals](/wiki/Category%3A_Immortals "wikilink") of the Realm have the
power to issue a bounty on the head of an
[inhabitant](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). Issuing a bounty
immediately makes the bountied
[player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") an
[Outlaw](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink") of the Realm. Unlike a regular
[Outlaw](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink"), a bountied
[player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") can be killed by any other
[player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") with no repercussions.
Bounties can be [experience](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink"),
[gold](/wiki/Gold_In_Hand "wikilink") or
[practices](/wiki/Practice_Points "wikilink").*

See also [Outlaw Flag](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink").
