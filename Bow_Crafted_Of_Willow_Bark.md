wwnnwwdsseed In this room is a tomb which hold the unused burial shroud.
Easiest way to get bow is to simply kill the guardian in the room then
pick it. If you aren't a rogue, it may take several trys...but it is way
easier to do this than to go all the way across the area to kill the
statue who has the key. The statue is in the south westish corner of the
area. The aggie one is the one with the key. Grab shroud from tomb  
Pp [Mourning Drow](/wiki/Mourning_Drow "wikilink")  
Give shroud to [Mourning Drow](/wiki/Mourning_Drow "wikilink") and she will
give you Willow Bow  
This is a mean area, I suggest not soloing unless you are really big. If
you move sneak invis, only guards and statue can see you, but most mobs
are aggie and will assist if you are fighting guards.
