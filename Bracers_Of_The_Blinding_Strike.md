Object 'bracers blinding strike' type armor, extra flags magic.  
Weight 3, value 630, level 34.  
Object Quality (100 / 100 hps)  
Armor class is 7.

`Modifies strength by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies damage roll by 3 continuous`

pp elderly sage

Go two south and then up the mountain. Follow the path through the
mountain. When the path T's and you can go three ways (up, east and back
south) go east. Keep going east and take your first north. There will be
two rogues, one of which has the Bracers of the Blinding Strike.
