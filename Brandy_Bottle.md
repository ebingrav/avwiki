*The bottle is a special 'Dragon Blood' brandy bottle. Its neck is
shaped like a small dragon's head. Bottles like these are often worth a
small amount money, even when empty.*

`It's more than full of a golden liquid.`

**Keyword(s):** brandy, bottle.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 39-41.

container](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink").

**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 2 lbs.
