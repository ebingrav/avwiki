*Brauss Copperfury is considered a bit eccentric by his fellow
[dwarves](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") for his strange taste in clothing, but he
is at least tolerated because of his legendary valor. Brauss
single-handedly defended [Marlot](/wiki/Marlot "wikilink")'s storehouse of ale
against a band of [orcs](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink"). Although now retired, he
still keeps his great warhammer, [Kneecap's
Bane](/wiki/Warhammer,_Kneecap's_Bane "wikilink"), at hand in case of
trouble.*

**Keywords:** dwarf, Brauss, Copperfury, wizened.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 27.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.

Comments
--------

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must pass two locked
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") to reach Brauss Copperfury; to do
this, they will need to either [bash](/wiki/Bashdoor "wikilink") these
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") open or
[unlock](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") them using their respective
[keys](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may obtain their
[keys](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") by doing the following: (1) either
[buy](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") [a mine pass level
one](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One "wikilink") from [the
receptionist](/wiki/Receptionist "wikilink") or complete [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Mine Pass Level One
Quest](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One_Quest "wikilink") and (2) get [a mine pass
level two](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_Two "wikilink") from [a humble
pilgrim](/wiki/Humble_Pilgrim "wikilink").

Brauss Copperfury is involved in [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Flask of Marlots Ol' Special
Quest](/wiki/Flask_Of_Marlots_Ol'_Special_Quest "wikilink") and its [Set of
Three Loaded Dice Quest](/wiki/Set_Of_Three_Loaded_Dice_Quest "wikilink").
