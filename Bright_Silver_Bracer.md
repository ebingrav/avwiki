` Modifies hp by 1 continuous.`  
` Modifies moves by 50 continuous.`  
` Modifies strength by 1 continuous.`

Slot: Wrist  
Base: 1  
Level: 4  
Flags: anti-good  
Weight: 7  
Mob: None  
Area: [Crystalmir Lake](/wiki/Category%3A_Crystalmir_Lake "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Crystalmir_Lake_Map "wikilink"))  
This item loads in a stationary container named a [Fire
Pit](/wiki/Fire_Pit "wikilink"). This item is in a room called "Smoldering
Campsite". To get there, head all south through
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3ATown_Of_Solace "wikilink"), then head all west, all
north, and west.
