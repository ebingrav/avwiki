Broken Fangs are from the [Idolatry Of
Lloth](/wiki/Category%3AIdolatry_Of_Lloth "wikilink") and located on the
[Arachnakans](/wiki/Arachnakan "wikilink") found within the web tunnels. They
can contain [Virus](/wiki/Category%3AVirus "wikilink"),
[Biotoxin](/wiki/Category%3ABiotoxin "wikilink"), OR
[Venom](/wiki/Category%3AVenom "wikilink")!
