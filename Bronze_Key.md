Comments
--------

This key opens the bronze door in the eastern tower of [Tower Of The
Ancient Drow](/wiki/Category%3A_Tower_Of_The_Ancient_Drow "wikilink"). The key
is needed to get the [Silver Key](/wiki/Silver_Key "wikilink").
