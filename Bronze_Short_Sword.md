*Made of hammered bronze with a simple wooden hilt, this short sword was
made by a centaur smith to arm those fighting the darklin scourge.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 7 to 20 (average 13).`  
`Modifies `[`damage`
`roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`armor`
`class`](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")` by -5 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`constitution`](/wiki/Constitution "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`
