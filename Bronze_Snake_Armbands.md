Comments
--------

Depending on the difference in ACs of this item and the [Legacy Of King
Vorin](/wiki/Legacy_Of_King_Vorin "wikilink"), you may lose 1-3 points of AC
for the gain of [Sneak](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink") and some additional hit by
using this item, instead of the Legacy.
