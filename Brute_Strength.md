*Brute Strength acts much like [enhanced
damage](/wiki/Enhanced_Damage "wikilink"), except that it only functions when
the user is in direct combat (ie fighting the
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") that's fighting you) and has worse
than -750 [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") at
[Hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") and -1250 at
[Lord](/wiki/Lord "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Enhanced Damage](/wiki/Enhanced_Damage "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
