*Burning hands allows the spellcaster to inflict damage to all
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in a
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"), by shooting jets of flames from
the spellcaster's hands. The amount of damage is determined by the
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") of the spellcaster. As with all area spells,
there is no need to specify a target.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'burning hands'.

[Lowmort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink")
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may find this spell handy when
fighting multiple [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") simultaneously,
though this is not generally a good idea for "splatty"
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink"). It may also be useful for
[tanks](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink") wanting to initiate combat with
[invisible](Invis_Flag "wikilink") [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"),
particularly those [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") that are more
than three [levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink") higher and thus cannot be
[detected](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink").
