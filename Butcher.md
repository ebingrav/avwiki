*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") allows you to
butcher a corpse into an edible steak. You must be
[wielding](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") a [bowie
knife](/wiki/Standard_Issue_Bowie_Knife "wikilink") to use this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). [Bowie
Knives](/wiki/Standard_Issue_Bowie_Knife "wikilink") can be purchased in
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: butcher corpse.

See also [Racial Butcher](/wiki/Racial_Butcher "wikilink").
