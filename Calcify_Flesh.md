*CALCIFY FLESH creates hard, bony plates under the caster's skin.
Although it confers a bit of a lumpy look, it does help to [soak up
damage](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink").
This spell is a [BIOCENTRIC Psionic
Spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Biocentric_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). They can
only be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on others if the Caster first masters
the art of [BIO EMPATHY](/wiki/Bio_Empathy "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'calcify flesh'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
