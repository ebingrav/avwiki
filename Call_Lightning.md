*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") brings down a mighty swath
of power from the [Gods](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink").
Lightning pours down like rain, and decimates every opponent in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). The
[Gods](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") will not answer unless
the [weather](/wiki/Weather "wikilink") is poor, however, and you must be
outdoors. This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") decreases in
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") cost with higher
[levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'call lightning'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is practically never used.
