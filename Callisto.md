*Before you stands a man who has devoted his life to the cloth, bingo,
and making cookies... morning, noon, and night. He
[meeps](/wiki/Meep "wikilink") and offers you an extremely tasty variety of
cookies, which appear to be the staple of his diet.*

**Keywords:** Callisto.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
