*Calm stops all fighting within a [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").
This is an area effect, so the [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
neither requires nor permits a target. In addition to stopping combat,
the calm spell sometimes temporarily overrides the fighting instincts of
[creatures](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") or
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"); these victims will find their
combat abilities hampered for a time. Even with this possible effect, it
is likely that [aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink")
[monsters](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") will still be able to attack
after only a short lull. [Heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") may wish
to note that Calm has the same effects as the [psionic
spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicist_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
[Awe](/wiki/Awe "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Fear](/wiki/Fear "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'calm'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used, though it
may be useful at moments of mortal peril by pausing battle long enough
(hopefully) to allow those nearly-dead to escape to safety.
