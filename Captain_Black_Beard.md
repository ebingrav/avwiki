*The Captain of [this ship](/wiki/Category%3A_S.S._Scurvy "wikilink") has a
long wiry black beard and a greasy handlebar mustache. His jacket is
made of the finest "stolen" materials. Glancing at his [peg
leg](/wiki/Wooden_Peg-Leg "wikilink") and [hook
hand](/wiki/Pirate's_Hook_Hand "wikilink") you can tell he is a very
experienced pirate. He snarls at you and growls, 'What are you doing on
[my ship](/wiki/Category%3A_S.S._Scurvy "wikilink")?*'

**Keywords:** pirate, Captain, Black, Beard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 35.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
