*Derrick is a very big man. He has acquired this station, not by
[brains](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink") or charm, but by brute
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink"). He had fought in three
[orc](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink") wars and is a descendent of a surviving cityguard
of old Thalos. He believes that everything has to be hard won to be
kept, and that laxity is for the weak.*

**Keywords:** Captain, Derrick, city, guard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 27.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[cleric](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [protection
evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
