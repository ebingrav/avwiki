Comments
--------

Captain Drago is not as tough as [Apprentice-Wizard
Arakos](/wiki/Apprentice-Wizard_Arakos "wikilink"), due to the lack of
[Sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink") affect. He is still no pushover. He
casts sorcerer spells. Note that his room is noregen, so it is difficult
to camp him.

Directions
----------

He's in the sunken ship - to get to him walk 2d, w, d, 2s, {w} from
[Mayflower](/wiki/Mayflower "wikilink")'s drop-room. He's behind a locked
pass-proof door, the key to which is on [Apprentice-Wizard
Arakos](/wiki/Apprentice-Wizard_Arakos "wikilink"). The key to his room will
break after a few uses (as few as two).
