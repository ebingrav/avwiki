*The Captain of the Guard is an older but very powerful man. His
obsidian armor seems to glow faintly. The crest of the town of Solace is
displayed with pride on his chestplate. The gold crest of two coins
seems to match the wealth of this town.*

**Keywords:** captain, guard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 19.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[mage](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Category%3A_Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").

Comments
--------

Since this mob is fully equipped with gear and affected by
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink"), it is not recommended to fight this
mob unless you are significantly higher level.
