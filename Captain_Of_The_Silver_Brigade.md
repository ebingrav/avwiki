*This [Lizard Woman](/wiki/Lizardmen "wikilink") must consider it a great
honor to Serve Rauhn, even if it means slogging through muck and mire
for him. This Captain's [armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink") seems to
glow a little brighter than the [armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink") of
her underlings.*

**Keywords:** lizard, man, silver, brigade, captain.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 33.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** none.  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[protection evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
