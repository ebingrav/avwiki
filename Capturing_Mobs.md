[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will not kill
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") in combat but will
instead (and much like in [Arena](/wiki/Category%3A_Avatar_Arena "wikilink")
combat) transport any defeated foes, intact but barely alive, to some
specific [room](/wiki/Jail_Rooms "wikilink").
