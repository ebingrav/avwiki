Modifies AC by -25 continuous  
Bases: 10-12  
Levels: 50-52  
Weight: 1  
Area: [Hunting](/wiki/Hunting "wikilink")  
Mob: [Aruna](/wiki/Aruna "wikilink")  

To obtain a carved bone necklace, portal to [General
Rusla](/wiki/General_Rusla "wikilink"). (gen rusla will also work). From
there, head west to Aruna, who carries the necklace.
