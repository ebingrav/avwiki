*[Sorin](/wiki/Sorin "wikilink")'s mead is the finest known to
[dwarven](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") kind. Out of respect for [his Clan
Chief](Clan_Chief_Voinar "wikilink"), [Sorin](/wiki/Sorin "wikilink") has
refused to supply [Finar](/wiki/Clan_Chief_Finar "wikilink") with the special
mead. [Finar](/wiki/Clan_Chief_Finar "wikilink") hasn't lost his love of it
over the years. To [give](/wiki/Give "wikilink")
[him](/wiki/Clan_Chief_Finar "wikilink") this would be a fine gift!*
