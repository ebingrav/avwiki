Syntax: cast 'cataclysm' \[Area Affect\]

Prerequisite: None

A spellcaster channels energy to form a sphere of destructive energy
around her body. When the amount of energy becomes unstable and chaotic,
it bursts in every direction, like a wave of lightning, to strike the
foes in its path.

This spell is In Class for Lord Mages and Wizards.
