*If an opponent throws a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Throwing_Weapons "wikilink") at you, what better
way to counter than to catch the thing? That's exactly what this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") does. Some
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Throwing_Weapons "wikilink") are particularly easy
to catch, and some can't be caught at all.*

Prerequisite(s): [Dodge](/wiki/Dodge "wikilink"), [Weapon
Lore](/wiki/Weapon_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
