An alphabetical list of all AVATAR MUD areas. Some of these areas have
[maps](:Category:_Maps "wikilink") available for them.

Some areas (namely [Houses](:Category:_Houses "wikilink"),
[Secrets](:Category:_Secrets "wikilink"), and [Ruined
Lairs](:Category:_Ruined_Lairs "wikilink")) consist of multiple portions
that do not connect with each other; each of these parts has its own
category listed below.

-   There are subcategories for areas by level range (see table above),
    as well as for
    [grandfathered](:Category:_Grandfathered_Areas "wikilink"),
    [lawful](:Category:_Lawful_Areas "wikilink"), and
    [special](:Category:_Special_Areas "wikilink") areas.

See also [Mobs](:Category:_Mobs "wikilink"),
[Objects](:Category:_Objects "wikilink"),
[Rooms](:Category:_Rooms "wikilink"), and [Area-Building
Contests](Area-Building_Contests "wikilink").
