*Countless caverns stretch out across the vast underground regions of
the realm. Near their once-proud city, which is now in ruins, the
ancient high [drow](Drow.md "wikilink") created several towers in which
many deadly challenges await. The purpose of the trials is to weed out
the weak from the strong, a concept quite common in
[drow](Drow.md "wikilink") society.*

**Level Range:** 40-46

**Builder:** S. Heart
