*The power hungry [duergar](Duergar.md "wikilink") king
[Domi](King_Domi.md "wikilink") led his troops into what was was known
as the dwarven kingdom of Juargan and quickly vanquished the bits of
resistance he encountered. The once-peaceful domain of the
[dwarves](Dwarves.md "wikilink") was quickly transformed into a powerful
stronghold and is now inhabited by the cruel and bloodthirsty
[duergar](Duergar.md "wikilink") and their crazed
[king](King_Domi.md "wikilink").*

**Level Range:** 15-27

**Builder:** Kariya
