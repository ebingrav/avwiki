**Level Range:** 10-15

**Builder:** Dorsai

## Directions

Walking route from [Aelmon](Aelmon "wikilink"): 9s, 3e, n.

[Portaling](Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested: [wax
master](Wax_Master "wikilink").

## Connections

This connects to [Midgaard](:Category:Midgaard "wikilink") to the south.

## Navigation

The access from [Midgaard](:Category:Midgaard "wikilink") is from the
south. It is a simple loop with a decent into the basement.

## Adventuring

There really is not much here - a few mobs, a
[light](Glowing_Ball_Of_Wax "wikilink"), and a
[cloak](Rat_Fur_Cloak "wikilink").
