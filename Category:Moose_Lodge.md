*The Moose-folk are in trouble! The great prophet
[Nostradomoose](Nostradomoose.md "wikilink") has foretold the end of
their world. Only an outsider to the lodge can stop this prophecy from
coming true. Will you be the one? Adventurers willing to face the
ultimate challenge should seek out Moose Lodge near the [Great
Wall](:Category:_Highways/Great_Wall.md "wikilink"), to the east of
[Midgaard](:Category:_Midgaard.md "wikilink"). This area has a
[quest](Ultimoose_Power_Quest.md "wikilink") which is only permitted to
be completed once per [incarnation](Incarnations.md "wikilink").*

**Level Range:** 20-50

**Builder:** Sancti
