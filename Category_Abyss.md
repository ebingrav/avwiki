*Lore tells of a hole in a cave under a [lizard infested
bog](/wiki/Category%3A_Crescent_Bog "wikilink") so incredibly deep and wide
that it could only have been made by the passing of a huge creature.
Indead, there are now many gigantic insects inhabiting this underground
chasm where only foolish mortals tread.*

**Level Range:** 48-50

**Builder:** Slinky

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 16e, \[e\], 2e, n, e,
2n, 2w, 2n, 2w, 5n, 3d, 2w, d.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested:
[Tapeworm](/wiki/Long_Tapeworm "wikilink").

Connections
-----------

The Abyss connects to the [Crescent
Bog](/wiki/Category%3A_Crescent_Bog "wikilink") above it via an
[underwater](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink") cave. At present, it connects
with no other [areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink").

Navigation
----------

The Abyss centers around a tall vertical shaft that
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must [be
flying](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink") to traverse. This shaft's topmost
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") exits upward into an
[underwater](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink")
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") within the [Crescent
Bog](/wiki/Category%3A_Crescent_Bog "wikilink"); so, if you suddenly find
yourself in a [watery](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink")
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") while navigating this
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"), then you've gone too far up. The
[tapeworm](/wiki/Long_Tapeworm "wikilink") usually stands
[stationary](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink") just below this top entrance,
though sometimes it appears in the bottommost
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this shaft. About midway down
this shaft is a level where it connects with four toyshop
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"), one in each cardinal direction.
On most other levels, one or two [rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink")
adjoin this shaft, each of these containing a single
[stationary](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink") giant insect; from each of these
lairs, one [exit](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Exits "wikilink") returns to this
shaft *on the same level* while another
[exit](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Exits "wikilink") returns to this shaft, also,
but *on the toyshop level* instead (which can potentially be rather
disorienting to those unfamiliar with this
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink")). At the bottom of this shaft is a
[secret door](/wiki/Hidden_Doors "wikilink") leading north into a treasure
room (see [Treasure-Hunting](/wiki/Treasure-Hunting "wikilink")); this
[door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") may be [bashed](Bash "wikilink")
open but [may not be passed magically](/wiki/Passproof_Doors "wikilink").
Various [elven](/wiki/Elves "wikilink") [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")
[wander](/wiki/Wandering_Mobs "wikilink") about this
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink").

Adventuring
-----------

This [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") has little
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") of interest but it can be a great
place for [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 35-40 groups to gain
[experience](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink"), potentially providing more
[XP](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") per kill than [hero
areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero_Areas "wikilink") like [Shashwat
Nisha](/wiki/Category%3A_Shashwat_Nisha "wikilink") with less effort. All
those who come to The Abyss to fight should [be
flying](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink") and all [tanks](Tanks "wikilink") should
be able to see [hidden](/wiki/Hide_Flag "wikilink")
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), via either
[alertness](/wiki/Alertness "wikilink"),
[awareness](/wiki/Awareness_(command/skill) "wikilink"), [detect
hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), or [heighten
senses](/wiki/Heighten_Senses "wikilink"). Although all
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") here are
[hidden](/wiki/Hide_Flag "wikilink"), none here are
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") or
[assistive](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink") (except perhaps for elves when
fighting other elves) or [wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink"), which is a
great advantage for combatants here.

Fighting groups normally avoid attacking both the
evil-[aligned](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink")
[tapeworm](/wiki/Long_Tapeworm "wikilink") and the several
good-[aligned](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink")
[wandering](/wiki/Wandering_Mobs "wikilink") [elven](Elves "wikilink")
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), because (1) they are in
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink") and (2) they [cast offensive spells at
random group members during combat](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink");
however, both explorers and treasure-hunters should note that this
[tapeworm](/wiki/Long_Tapeworm "wikilink") carries both (1) a book introducing
this [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") and (2) a
[key](/wiki/Bloody_Key "wikilink") to its hidden treasure room. Fighting
groups normally kill all eight other [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")
here, which are neutral-[aligned](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") giant insects
and such, which both [eat corpses](/wiki/Corpse-Eating_Mobs "wikilink") and
[pick up objects from the ground](/wiki/Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink"). Each of
these eight [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") carries a piece of candy
that unlocks one of eight bears (two in each toyshop
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink")), each of which contains a piece of
cursed [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink"); these candies may also be
[sacrificed](/wiki/Sacrifice "wikilink") for, normally, about 100 gold each.

Tanks normally [portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") to the
[tapeworm](/wiki/Long_Tapeworm "wikilink") and then move up and down this
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink")'s tall vertical shaft,
[scanning](/wiki/Scan "wikilink") on each level and, whenever they
[scan](/wiki/Scan "wikilink") an insect/whatever in its shaftside lair,
leaving this shaft to kill it and then returning to this shaft via the
same door. Under ideal circumstances, groups may kill all eight of these
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in almost precisely the same amount
of time that it takes for this [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") to
repop, thus being able to fight continuously in this
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") without having to move elsewhere;
other groups may not be so fortunate.
