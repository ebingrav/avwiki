*There are several air pathways above our fair realm that allows anyone
with the [fly spell](/wiki/Fly "wikilink") to soar like an eagle between most
of the major cities. These airways are usually accesable from the dead
center of the realm's bigger towns, such as
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink"),
[Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink"),
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink") and
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink").*

**Level Range:** 10-12

**Builder:** Crom

----------

Adventuring
-----------

Air of Avatar is a fast, easy transport between
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"),
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink"), [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink"), and
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink") by means of the [fly
spell](/wiki/Fly "wikilink"), [griffon wings
bracer](/wiki/Griffon_Wings_Bracer "wikilink"), or any other means of
[flying](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink"). The area hosts several cute flying
mobs.
