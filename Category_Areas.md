An alphabetical list of all AVATAR MUD areas. Some of these areas have
[maps](/wiki/Category%3A_Maps "wikilink") available for them.

Some areas (namely [Houses](/wiki/Category%3A_Houses "wikilink"),
[Secrets](/wiki/Category%3A_Secrets "wikilink"), and [Ruined
Lairs](/wiki/Category%3A_Ruined_Lairs "wikilink")) consist of multiple
portions that do not connect with each other; each of these parts has
its own category listed below.

-   There are subcategories for areas by level range (see table above),
    as well as for
    [grandfathered](/wiki/Category%3A_Grandfathered_Areas "wikilink"),
    [lawful](/wiki/Category%3A_Lawful_Areas "wikilink"), and
    [special](/wiki/Category%3A_Special_Areas "wikilink") areas.

See also [Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"),
[Objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), and
[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").
