*The woods are hidden away on [the eastern
trail](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") that wraps around the
northern edge of [Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink"), near
[Brothers' Grimm](/wiki/Category%3A_Brothers_Grimm "wikilink"). Rumored to be
the domain of bloodthirsty [hobgoblins](/wiki/Hobgoblins "wikilink"), not many
adventurers have gone to actually check it out. Reports from yeomen and
patrolling knights indicate the mass influx of
[goblins](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink") entering the area, although none have
returned, and have possibly been killed by the
[hobgoblins](/wiki/Hobgoblins "wikilink"). Evidence for this, one green knight
says, is the low guttural cries that are heard constantly, sometimes
scattering the doves in front of Grimms' cottage.*

**Level Range:** 10-15

**Builder:** Zahri

Adventuring
-----------

Mostly Goblin-kin running around. A few useful items exist here. Not one
of my favorite places to level lowmorts.

From the author:  
The mob levels increase based on far into the area you get. The mobs
near the entrance are 11-12. The training area (after you go through the
water east/southish, take the west past the living tree) is full of
13-14 level mobs. The tree in the SE corner has the 15-16 mobs.
