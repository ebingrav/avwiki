*In the arena, players may attack and hurt each other in a limited form
of pk. Also, no [death](/wiki/Death "wikilink") takes place - the loser is
'defeated' rather than killed, and so does not have to [retrieve a
corpse](/wiki/Corpse_Retrieval "wikilink") and does not lose the normal
[experience points](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") for dying. Please be
aware that some [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") still have
negative abilities which can be harmful to
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").*

See also [Arena](/wiki/Arena "wikilink") (command).
