*Located far to the northeast of
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"), on the other side of the
great [Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink"), the Battleground
refers to a mountainous region of forest where the
[Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") [Ents](Ents "wikilink") do
battle with invading [giants](/wiki/Giants "wikilink") from the northern
mountains. It is very easy for a wandering traveller to be accidentally
crushed in one of their battles.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** Dev
