*The Black Circle is a secretive cabal of lizard and Dragon-kin
assassins under the service of the Great Dragon Kahbyss. The Circle
teaches a small repertoire of [psionic
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicist_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), including
some unknown outside the circle. The Circle as a whole has a certain
measure of [Reputation](/wiki/Reputation "wikilink"), although individual
Initiates cannot raise this, like an
[Assassin](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink") can. Like all [Prestige
Classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Prestige_Classes "wikilink"), they retain the
original class's [Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"), in this
case, [Dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink"). As a Psionic class, they
possess a Psion's higher degree of success with spells such as
[deception](/wiki/Deception "wikilink"). Joining the Circle is for life.
Initiates cannot become [Assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink") or
[Berserkers](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") like
[Rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") can. Rumor says the Circle
maintains a base in warmer climes, where Masters can often be found.*

**Abbreviation:** Bci.

**[Base Class](/wiki/Category%3A_Core_Classes "wikilink"):**
[Rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink").

**[Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"):**
[Dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink").
