*The Bloodsong is what [Minotaurs](/wiki/Minotaurs "wikilink") call the sound
the wind makes as it blows through the tunnels of this small island.
They claim it is the voice of [Tor](/wiki/Tor "wikilink"), and it makes them
[stronger](/wiki/Strength "wikilink"), [tougher](Armor_Class "wikilink"), and
far more [aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"). Outsiders are
extremely unwelcome. The island was once attached to [Paradise
Point](/wiki/Category%3A_Paradise_Point "wikilink"), but was blasted apart
from the mainland years ago to make it easier to conquer the more tame
[minotaurs](/wiki/Minotaurs "wikilink") there. So far it hasn't helped.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** Dev

Adventuring
-----------

Bloodsong Island is a multi-level area that is near [Paradise
Point](/wiki/Category%3A_Paradise_Point "wikilink"). This area is a favorite
duo place for some [rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink"), but you
should be warned that most of it is
[no-spell](Anti-Magic_Rooms "wikilink"). The [portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink")
point to the area is [Nenba](/wiki/Nenba_The_Archer "wikilink").
