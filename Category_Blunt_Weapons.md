Blunt weapons do
[pounding](/wiki/Category%3AMelee_Weapons_That_Pound "wikilink"),
[crushing](/wiki/Category%3AMelee_Weapons_That_Crush "wikilink") or
[smacking](/wiki/Category%3AMelee_Weapons_That_Smack "wikilink") damage.  
Only [Berserkers](/wiki/Category%3ABerserkers "wikilink") get the [Blunt
Weaponry](/wiki/Blunt_Weaponry "wikilink") skill, so these weapons are more
useful for them.  
The max and average damage of a blunt weapon can be raised similar to
[sharpening](/wiki/Sharpen "wikilink") by utilizing the
[rune](/wiki/Rune "wikilink") spell.
