Brandishes, or "brandies," are [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
that may be both [worn](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") and either
[brandished](/wiki/Brandish "wikilink") or [zapped](Zap "wikilink"), namely
[staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") and
[wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink").

Unlike with [potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink") and
[pills](/wiki/Category%3A_Pills "wikilink"),
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") [cast](Cast "wikilink") using
these [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") can or will
[affect](/wiki/Affects "wikilink")
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") other than their user
(and, for [staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink"), every
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") in their
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink")); and, unlike
[scrolls](/wiki/Category%3A_Scrolls "wikilink"), brandishes may (usually) be
used multiple times before being fully consumed. For these reasons, many
players may find brandishes to be rather handy during certain
circumstances. [Clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink"), for example,
may find them useful either for healing multiple groupmates
simultaneously without using their expensive mass-healing
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") or for continuing to heal their
tank even after their [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") has been depleted.
And [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") may find them useful for
serving as supplemental or substitute [healers](/wiki/Healers "wikilink") in
groups. Two important drawbacks of brandishes are that they may take
time to collect and that they can be very heavy.

See both [Staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") and
[Wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink") below for more information about
individual brandishes.
