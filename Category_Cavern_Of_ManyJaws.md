*These caverns are avoided by all subterranean denizens, including the
dastardly high [drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") who lived precariously close to
this damned place. While no one will speak of this place aloud,
whispered rumors from the mouths of madmen tell of inhuman abominations
and things not of this world.*

**Level Range:** 40-46

**Builder:** Mirlion
