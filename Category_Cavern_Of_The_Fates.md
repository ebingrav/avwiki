*Legend states that in [some forest](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") in
the lands of Avatar the three Fates, [Clotho](/wiki/Clotho "wikilink"),
[Lachesis](/wiki/Lachesis "wikilink"), and [Atropos](/wiki/Atropos "wikilink"), have
made their home. However, they despise adventurers and visitors of all
kinds, and have devised a trap for any that may come their way. It is
rumored that one can only escape by defeating one of the Fates
themselves.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** Hamlet

Navigation
----------

Starting from this area's entrance from
[Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink"), the Gypsy is east while the
main area is south and then down.

Adventuring
-----------

This area is big and fun to learn. This area is scattered with various
mobs. One big difficult with this area is the fleeing mobs it can
seriously slow down a run. Basically there are four groups of mobs here.
The souls, the lost heros are weak easy low exp mobs that can be ignored
if you want. The Guardians of Fate are the wondering [aggie
mobs](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") that are good
[exp](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") and dont [flee](Flee "wikilink"),
the three headed mobs wich are very good
[exp](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") but [flee](Flee "wikilink"), and the
four dragons placed in the corners of the map. In this area you should
generally wonder and kill, but make sure to kill
[aggies](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") first and work your way down to the
others. Some groups may want to try to get
[fatewalkers](/wiki/Fatewalkers "wikilink"), however, be wary not to
[quaff](/wiki/Quaff "wikilink") the wrong [fountain](Fountains "wikilink")
with the risk of being sent to a mystery location. No mobs here are of
exceeding difficulty or provide a real threat though its advised for
large groupies because this area can get swarmy and troublesome for
small groupies.

[Aggie Mobs](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"): Guardians of Fate (Note: these
have various names i.e. guardian/warrior/defender but often will only
respond to guardian when targeted), [Atropos](/wiki/Atropos "wikilink"),
[Lachesis](/wiki/Lachesis "wikilink"), [Clothos](Clothos "wikilink"), and four
dragons.

Non-Aggie Mobs: Gypsy (though not in direct area is decent exp and
required for quest), Souls, Lost Hero, and Three-Headed mobs.
