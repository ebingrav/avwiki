*A class definer is a [spell or
skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") which only that
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") receives. The in-game reason can
be described as a trade secret. The code reason is that every
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") should and does have unique
abilities separate from the other classes. Unlike CD's, other [skills
and spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") typically are
available for all [classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") eventually.*
