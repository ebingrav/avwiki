An alphabetical list of all AVATAR MUD classes.

*Avatar has many different and interesting classes to choose from. If
you prefer the basic hack-n-slash variety,
[warriors](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") or
[rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") may be for you. If you like
magic-users, good choices are [mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink"),
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink"), and
[psions](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink"), while those in between can
opt for the [monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink"), a quick fighter and
part-time [spellcaster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"), as well as a very different
style of play.*

-   There are subcategories for
    [core](/wiki/Category%3A_Core_Classes "wikilink"),
    [prestige](/wiki/Category%3A_Prestige_Classes "wikilink"),
    [remort](/wiki/Category%3A_Remort_Classes "wikilink"), and
    [grandfathered](/wiki/Category%3A_Grandfathered_Classes "wikilink") classes.

See also [Races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") and [Skills &
Spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink").
