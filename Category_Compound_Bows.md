*These intricate bows of [Gnomish](/wiki/Gnomes "wikilink") invention work
mostly like [long bows](/wiki/Category%3A_Long_Bows "wikilink"), but are
considerably more compact and easy to draw. Unfortunately, they are
prone to breakage.*
