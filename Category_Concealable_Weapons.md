Concealable weapons tend to be low weight one handed piercers. They give
a bonus to your stealth check on sneaky rogue skills like
[Backstab](/wiki/Backstab "wikilink"). However, they have lower damage than
normal weapons. Maybe it would be worth it for an ogr rog to try
concealables? As far as I know, concealable weapons have nothing to do
with the rogue skill [Conceal Weapon](/wiki/Conceal_Weapon "wikilink"), which
just keeps other players from seeing your wield when they look at you.
