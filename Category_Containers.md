Comments
--------

It often becomes the case that a traveller picks up a lot of
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") and useful
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), and no one likes a cluttered
[inventory](/wiki/Inventory "wikilink")! The solution? Containers! Below is a
list of containers found in Midgaardia. Also,
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who learn to [skin
corpses](/wiki/Skin_Corpse "wikilink") may make their own [skin
bags](/wiki/Skin_Bags "wikilink").
