"Core" [classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") are creatable
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"). They serve as "base"
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") for AVATAR MUD's [prestige
classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Prestige_Classes "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") in certain core
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") who reach
[lord](/wiki/Lord "wikilink") [tier](Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") may choose
to be [reborn](/wiki/Remort "wikilink") into certain [remort
classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Remort_Classes "wikilink").
