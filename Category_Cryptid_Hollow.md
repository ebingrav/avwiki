*The vast Midgaardian landscape is dotted with a myriad of dark corners
and forgotten places. In those places are known to reside long-hidden
creatures and ancient artifacts that very few see with their own eyes...
and live to tell the tale. One such location is rumored to exist just
beyond [Igecsoz](/wiki/Category%3A_Igecsoz "wikilink"), long disregarded by
the [trogolodyte](/wiki/Troglodytes "wikilink") populous.*

**Level Range:** 20-25

**Builder:** Ibn

This [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") was created for an
[area-building contest](/wiki/Area-Building_Contests "wikilink") in 2005.  
This [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") was added in 2006 Mar.
