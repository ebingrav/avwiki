*The [town of Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink") is built on
the shores of the Great Lake, Crystalmir. Rumors say that there is a
[belt made of pure ice crystals](/wiki/Belt_Of_Crystalmir "wikilink") from the
heart of the lake, frozen in winter. It is said to be guarded by living
[golems](/wiki/Golems "wikilink") of pure crystal somewhere near the lake
shore.*

**Level Range:** 5-15

**Builder:** Strahd

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"): n, 8w, 11s.

Walking route from [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"): 3e, 11s.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested:
[Goldmoon](/wiki/Goldmoon "wikilink").

Connections
-----------

This area is south of the [Town of
Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink").

This is a fairly large and diverse area, which also connects quite a few
areas south of [Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink"). You can
reach the [White Stone
Road](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink"),
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink"),
[Grunka's](/wiki/Category%3A_Grunka's_Kombat_Skool "wikilink"), [Fewmaster's
Folly](/wiki/Category%3A_Fewmaster's_Folly "wikilink"), [Paradise
Point](/wiki/Category%3A_Paradise_Point "wikilink"),
[Rivers](/wiki/Category%3A_Rivers_Of_Avatar "wikilink"), and [Descent to
Hell](/wiki/Category%3A_Descent_To_Hell "wikilink") by travelling through this
area.

Adventuring
-----------

The general difficulty of the most common
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), in increasing order, is:

-   Hobgoblin Flunky
-   Rats
-   Farmers
-   Fish
-   Barraccudas

This of course doesn't include "special" mobs like
[Goldmoon](/wiki/Goldmoon "wikilink"), Riverwind, or the Farmer.

As a rule, the [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") [will
assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink") here, so be careful fighting in large
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). There are also
[hidden](/wiki/Hide_Flag "wikilink") [wandering
mobs](/wiki/Wandering_Mobs "wikilink") akin to the field rats in
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink"), so even though a
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") may appear safe, be wary of other
[hidden](/wiki/Hide_Flag "wikilink") [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")
[assisting](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink").

You may come across Fewmaster Toede in your travels through the area. He
is a smallish [aggy](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") who has
[sanc](/wiki/Black_Aura "wikilink") and a wield. He's not terribly difficult,
but he could catch a lower [hp](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink")
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") off guard.

Directly south and east of the Solace entrance you will find
[Goldmoon](/wiki/Goldmoon "wikilink"). She will act as a
[healer](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink") and cast spells on you if you
are below level 10, but her room is \_not\_
[nofight](/wiki/Safe_Rooms "wikilink"), so you are not safe from Toede here.
Up and north will take you to the White Stone Road ([Highways/Great
Wall](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink")) and the trail around
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink").

The area south of [Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink") has
various wandering [hobgoblins](/wiki/Hobgoblins "wikilink"), farmers, and rats
eating grain, none of which are terribly difficult. 2swn from the
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink") entrance will land you
in [Grunka's](/wiki/Category%3A_Grunka's_Kombat_Skool "wikilink").

The lake itself is located 3w from the
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink") entrance. Going d will
take you underwater into a looping maze filled with alternating rooms of
fish and barraccuda. Both [will assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink"), and
the barraccuda [will aggy](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"), so be \_very\_
careful if you choose to go down here unspelled.

wnw from the [Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink") entrance
will take you to the [Rivers](/wiki/Category%3A_Rivers_Of_Avatar "wikilink")
area that holds the mini-quest for the [belt of
Crystalmir](/wiki/Belt_Of_Crystalmir "wikilink"). Continuing to walk west will
bring you back to the other side of the shore.

All west, all north, and west will bring you to the smoldering campsite.
From here you can go west to [Fewmaster's
Folly](/wiki/Category%3A_Fewmaster's_Folly "wikilink"), south to [Paradise
Point](/wiki/Category%3A_Paradise_Point "wikilink"), or you can pull the
[bracer](/wiki/Bright_Silver_Bracer "wikilink") out of the fire for a +mv +hp
+str anti-good wrist item. Don't wander too far south into [Paradise
Point](/wiki/Category%3A_Paradise_Point "wikilink") if you're tiny though :)

West, all north, and all west will take you on a path to [Descent to
Hell](/wiki/Category%3A_Descent_To_Hell "wikilink"). The farm dogs [will
aggy](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"), and have the needed key to get inside
the house, but are not terribly difficult. The pooh bear inside is of
moderate difficulty. South through the book in the child's room will
bring you into [descent](/wiki/Category%3A_Descent_To_Hell "wikilink"): the
path is one-way, so don't walk through unless you actually intend to do
some killing there.

Finally, if you wander in the southwestern corner of the map, keep an
eye out for the Farmer and the room full of robed figures - the former
has a wield and is a bit tougher, the latter are bigger mobs that (you
guessed it) [will assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink") if you start
attacking.
