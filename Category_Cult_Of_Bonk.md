*Beneath the tree of Knowledge is the pygmie pixie cult dedicated to the
worship of [Bonk](/wiki/Bonk "wikilink"). Rumor has it that
[Bonk](/wiki/Bonk "wikilink")'s minions are poised ready to stage a hostile
takeover of the entire [meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink"). Many
visitors enjoy visiting the pixies and laughing at how scary they think
they are.*

**Level Range:** 2-4

**Builder:** Crom
