*Deep beneath the surface of Midgaardia, there is a small mining town of
[deep gnomes](/wiki/Deep_Gnomes "wikilink") known as Deephome. They were
recently found by their surface cousins as the
[gnomes](/wiki/Gnomes "wikilink") from above carved their own portion of
passages through the [Underway
Tunnels](/wiki/Category%3A_Underway_Tunnels "wikilink"). They now use the
[Underway](/wiki/Category%3A_Underway_Tunnels "wikilink") as a trading route
between the two villages.*

**Level Range:** 13-20

**Builder:** Copycat
