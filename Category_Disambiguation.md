Disambiguation pages are used when more than one page shares a single
name.

For example, in Avatar MUD, Panacea is the name of both a potion and a
spell; so, here in Avatar Wiki, three pages are used for Panacea:
<font color=blue>Panacea (potion)</font> for this potion,
<font color=blue>Panacea (spell)</font> for this spell, and
<font color=blue>Panacea</font> to help Wiki visitors to find whichever
of these two pages they may be looking for. This third page is called a
"disambiguation" page.

Disambiguation pages are categorized below. Links to any of these
disambiguation pages should (usually) be changed so that they point to
whichever page was actually intended.
