Some AVATAR MUD [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") (non-magical)
[exits](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Exits "wikilink") have doors.

Players must either open these doors or magically [pass through
them](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink") to use these
[exits](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Exits "wikilink").

Some doors are of at least one special type. These types are listed
here.
