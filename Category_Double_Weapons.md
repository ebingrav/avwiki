Double headed weapons are two handed.  
With a double weapon you can [Double Hit](/wiki/Double_Hit "wikilink"),
[Double Parry](/wiki/Double_Parry "wikilink"), and
[Whirlwind](/wiki/Whirlwind "wikilink").  
A double weapon is the only type of weapon with which you can perform a
two-handed [Riposte](/wiki/Riposte "wikilink").  
I haven't found a sharpenable double weapon yet, but they could exist.
