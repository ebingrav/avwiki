*There is a story of a refuge for those who have lost their Way. A place
among the wilderness where a pool of water sits, mirror smooth. Still as
death. The animals don't go near it, and the
[rangers](/wiki/Category%3A_Rangers "wikilink") who patrol the bog have a name
for it: The Pool of Despair.*

**Level Range:** 40-50

**Builder:** Nadreck
