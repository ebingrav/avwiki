*The crypt is the resting place of this realm's deceased
[Dragons](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink"), and is guarded by ferocious wyrm
servants. Seeking [treasure](/wiki/Category%3A_Treasure "wikilink"), a well
known party of Midgaardian adventurers set out in search of the crypt
recently, led by [Svlad the Very Burly](/wiki/Svlad_The_Very_Burly "wikilink")
and [Millament](/wiki/Millament "wikilink"), High Sister of the Order of UL.
It has been a long time since that party disappeared east of
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink").*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** GTMP
