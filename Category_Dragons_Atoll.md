*The great sea [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") Myxss claims this atoll as
her lair. She has mingled in sorcery, creating a race of super
[lizardmen](/wiki/Lizardmen "wikilink") to guard her atoll. She raids far and
wide, destroying ships of all nations and allegiances, earning her the
animosity of both [Lord Audis](/wiki/Lord_Audis "wikilink") and of the various
pirate clans. Her wealth is said to be unmatched, as is the viciousness
of her guards.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** S. Heart
