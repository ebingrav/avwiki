*Underneath the mines of the [Dwarven
city](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink") is a series of labyrinthian
tunnels that were once part of the [Dwarves](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink")'
extensive mining system. What occupies these catacombs now is anyone's
guess.*

**Level Range:** 15-26

**Builder:** Raff
