*Due east of the [Meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink") is a
[tunnel](/wiki/Category%3A_Underway_Tunnels "wikilink") leading north
underground to [Mt Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink"). Near the
opening of this path is a small cave where the
[Dwarves](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") leave their young in the care of trained
[Dwarven](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") nannies.*

**Level Range:** 3-5

**Builder:** Sandman

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"): s, 7e, 2s.

Adventuring
-----------

While this area is not necessarily
[experience](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") heavy, it does contain a few
pieces of [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") which are very helpful for
starting [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). The [pink ice
ring](/wiki/Pink_Ice_Ring "wikilink") can be found on various dolls and gives
+1 str and +6 hp and the [linen robe](/wiki/Linen_Robe "wikilink"), +1 wisdom
and +5 mana, and [amber potion](/wiki/Amber_Potion "wikilink"), armor and cure
light, can both be found on the [nanny](/wiki/Nanny "wikilink"). The [Tiamat
mask](/wiki/Tiamat_Mask "wikilink"), +1 dr, is also around this area.

The bears and soldiers will [assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink"), and the
[nanny](/wiki/Nanny "wikilink") can be a difficult fight for small players.
