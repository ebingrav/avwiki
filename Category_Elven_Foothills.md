*There is a settlement of gray [elves](/wiki/Elves "wikilink") and
[half-elves](/wiki/Half-Elves "wikilink") to the north, at the foot of the
northern mountain range, near [Morgan
Vale](/wiki/Category%3A_Morgan_Vale "wikilink") and the [court of Baron
Sarphyre](/wiki/Category%3A_Sarphyre's_Court "wikilink"). Having lived there
for many centuries peacefully, the [elves](/wiki/Elves "wikilink") now are
threatened by the presence of a band of harpies in the volcano called
[Lloth's Peak](/wiki/Category%3A_Lloth's_Peak "wikilink"). Rumor has it that
the harpies fly out at night and kidnap the young. The foothills are
located north of the intersection between the White Stone road and the
Dwarven Trade route.*

**Level Range:** 10-20
