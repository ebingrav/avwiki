An alphabetical list of all AVATAR MUD etchers.

*You can [etch](/wiki/Etch "wikilink") items at certain places like the
[Jeweller](/wiki/Jeweller "wikilink") in
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") for a price. This gives the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") one additional keyword. It is a
one etch deal, however, and cannot be un-etched.*

Syntax: [etch](/wiki/Etch "wikilink")
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt; <keyword>.
