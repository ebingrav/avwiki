*Some people take longer to fully express their potential than others.
In addition to [training](/wiki/Train "wikilink")
[attributes](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistics "wikilink") and
[practicing](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") new [spells and
skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), it is possible for
some [races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") to evolve into more advanced
creatures during their leveling progress. Such evolution is accomplished
with the [rebuild](/wiki/Rebuild "wikilink")
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") and is voluntary. After a
successful [rebuild](/wiki/Rebuild "wikilink") you may find you're better than
you were before: better - stronger - faster! In particular, the
[firedrake](/wiki/Firedrakes "wikilink") race is known to evolve from a
hatchling of moderate destructive potential into a fearsome beast.
Likewise, the cute little fuzz balls called
"[verbits](/wiki/Verbits "wikilink")" are capable of becoming huge, fierce,
deadly [balls of fuzz](/wiki/Varsils "wikilink"). On account of this
evolution, it is advised not to keep [verbits](/wiki/Verbits "wikilink") as
[pets](/wiki/Category%3A_Pets "wikilink").*

See also [Rebuild](/wiki/Rebuild "wikilink").
