The planar shift into Arcadia drops into the middle of a beautiful
eldritch forest where dreams grow on trees and daydreams flow down the
river.

Unfortunately, according to Astra, the Fae are xenophobes of the worst
kind and will view the Midgaardian Lords' intrusion as we might a plague
of locusts. The Fae have set Balefire to the woods, trying to erase the
"Midgaardian Stain" from their fair plane.

This area is great for running exp. A group of 15 will have no problems
here; a group as small as 6 may be able to survive.

The following mobs cast [Migraine](/wiki/Migraine "wikilink"):

-   [Chimerical Wyvern](/wiki/Chimerical_Wyvern "wikilink")
-   [Chimerical Naga](/wiki/Chimerical_Naga "wikilink")
-   [Chimerical Mammoth](/wiki/Chimerical_Mammoth "wikilink")
-   [Chimerical Demon](/wiki/Chimerical_Demon "wikilink")
-   [Chimerical Manticore](/wiki/Chimerical_Manticore "wikilink")
