*There is a small path through the woods between the [Oak
Circle](/wiki/Category%3A_Oak_Circle "wikilink") and
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink") to the west. For reasons
known only to himself, [Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink")'s
[Fewmaster Toede](/wiki/Fewmaster_Toede "wikilink") has built a crude wall
smack in the middle of this path, and declared that none may be allowed
to pass.*

**Level Range:** 8-9

**Builder:** Crom

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"): n, w, \[w\], 2w, s, d, w, u,
n, 3w, 2s.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested: hobgoblin flunky
(note: there are many in various areas).
