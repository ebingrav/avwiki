Adventuring
-----------

One room south of the entrance is an un-cursed room in
[Apocalypse](/wiki/Apocalypse "wikilink"), so recall can be set there. Be
careful as [War](/wiki/War "wikilink") is just to the west.

The area is of course swarmy, being an
[EHA](/wiki/Category%3AElite_Hero_Areas "wikilink"), so be prepared.

Initial notes:  
The second room into the area is [nospell](nospell "wikilink") (n from
first room). At least one of the other rooms off of it is also nospell
(n e from first room). I've only managed to get to those three rooms so
far, please add more info here as it's discovered ([and a map would be
handy](/wiki/Forge_Map "wikilink")).
