*Long ago during a great war, a [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") crashed to
the earth. It crashed so hard that it had buried itself within the
earth. Legends have it that this [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink")
crash-landed near the [now ruined drow city of
Reveria](/wiki/Category%3A_Ruins_Of_Reveria "wikilink"). Recent reports of
earthquakes and hot steam rising up from cracks within the earth have
brought up the story of this legend once again. Some believe the
[dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") that entombed itself eons ago still lives
and breathes beneath the city, while others say it's just a shift in the
tectonic plates and it is nothing to worry about. Whichever it is, it
deserves to be investigated further.*
