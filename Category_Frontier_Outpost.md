*Back in the time before the [Great
Wall](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") existed, the
[Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") posed a significant threat
to the defenseless lands of [Midgaard
Country](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard_Country "wikilink"), the
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") between the
[Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") and the city of
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"). An outpost was constructed
on the eastern banks of the
[river](/wiki/Category%3A_Rivers_Of_Avatar "wikilink") that flows down the
center of the landscape to keep a watchful eye on anything that may
emerge from the [Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") and threaten
the people. However, eventually the [Great
Wall](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") was built and the
outpost was no longer needed, and only kept minimally complemented. That
is, until a band of savage [orcs](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink") had taken up
residence in the abandoned vineyards to the east and began raiding the
farms across the [river](/wiki/Category%3A_Rivers_Of_Avatar "wikilink"). Now,
the outpost is painfully understaffed and overwhelmed by the constant
brutality of the [orcish](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink") raiders.*

**Level Range:** 18-22

**Builder:** Copycat
