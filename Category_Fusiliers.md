*The first Fusiliers were [gnomish](/wiki/Gnomes "wikilink"),
[dwarvish](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") and [halfling](Halflings "wikilink")
heroes during the Great Wars when the Giants of Straul devastated the
world. Armed with little more than
[slings](/wiki/Category%3A_Slings "wikilink"), throwing axes and boomerangs,
they fought enemies five times their size and prevailed. This tradition
continues in the Fusilier [Prestige
Class](/wiki/Category%3A_Prestige_Classes "wikilink"). They are adept at
abusing their small size to confound larger opponents, are highly
skilled with s[slings](/wiki/Category%3A_Slings "wikilink") and [throwing
weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Throwing_Weapons "wikilink"). Like all [Prestige
Classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Prestige_Classes "wikilink"), they retain the
original class's [Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"), in this
case, [Dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink"). Fusiliers cannot become
[Assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink"), like
[Archers](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") can. Given that Fusiliers hunt
[giants](/wiki/Giants "wikilink"), the few who remain might be found in the
vicinity of their prey.*

**Abbreviation:** Fus.
