*Some say that we are all merely pawns in a grand chess game played by
the [Immortals](/wiki/Category%3A_Immortals "wikilink"). Some say that even
the greatest of our Realm's [Heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") and
[Lords and Ladies](/wiki/Lord "wikilink") are merely playing pieces of the
[Great Gods](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") upon the
Chessboard of Destiny. Others say this chessboard floats on a cloud over
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"), but few believe such a
crazy tale.*

Adventuring
-----------

Area works just like a chess board. Eight by sixteen squares. When
running here pretty much wander and kill. One problem with this area is
a lack of mobs (32). Also avoid bringing large groupies to this area
with 3000hp+ if you can. Good for low hero or sub200. Excellent
experience if you can kill fast and not become unlucky. This area does
get swarmy. It is important to learn which mobs are aggie and which are
not when running here.

-   Aggie Mobs: Queens (note: avoid fighting these, they are high level
    and can seriously hurt the tank/groupies), knights, and Shizaga
    pawns (note: Shizaga pawns will clone groupies; this is why you
    should avoid large groupies).

<!-- -->

-   Non-Aggie Mobs: Kings, rooks (note: rooks are also large mobs but a
    decent group can take them), bishops, and Bhyss pawns.
