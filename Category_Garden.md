A great area for groups size 5 to 15. Home of the [Stone
Wings](/wiki/Stone_Wings "wikilink"), [Great Shield
Tyranny](/wiki/Great_Shield_Tyranny "wikilink"), and [Black
Armband](/wiki/Black_Armband "wikilink"). The [Digger
Demons](/wiki/Digger_Demon "wikilink") that roam parts of the garden are also
very nice exp.
