Gear consists of all [types](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink") of
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") *except for*
[fountains](/wiki/Fountains "wikilink"), [furniture](Furniture "wikilink"),
[markings](/wiki/Markings "wikilink"), [nexuses](Nexuses "wikilink"), and
[portals](/wiki/Portals "wikilink").

-   There are subcategories for level
    [1-10](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_1-10_Gear "wikilink"),
    [11-20](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_11-20_Gear "wikilink"),
    [21-30](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_21-30_Gear "wikilink"),
    [31-40](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_31-40_Gear "wikilink"),
    [41-50](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_41-50_Gear "wikilink"), and
    [hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero_Gear "wikilink") gear.

<!-- -->

-   There are subcategories for
    [archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archer_Gear "wikilink"),
    [hit](/wiki/Category%3A_Hit_Gear "wikilink"),
    [mana](/wiki/Category%3A_Mana_Gear "wikilink"), and
    [tank](/wiki/Category%3A_Tank_Gear "wikilink") gear, as well as
    "[casties](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellcasting_Gear "wikilink")".

<!-- -->

-   There are subcategories for
    [statistic](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistic_Gear "wikilink")/[level](Category%3A_Level_Gear "wikilink")
    ([con](/wiki/Category%3A_Con_Gear "wikilink"),
    [dex](/wiki/Category%3A_Dex_Gear "wikilink"),
    [int](/wiki/Category%3A_Int_Gear "wikilink"),
    [str](/wiki/Category%3A_Str_Gear "wikilink"), and
    [wis](/wiki/Category%3A_Wis_Gear "wikilink")),
    [hit-point](/wiki/Category%3A_Hit-Point_Gear "wikilink"),
    [move-point](/wiki/Category%3A_Move-Point_Gear "wikilink"), and
    [saving-throw](/wiki/Category%3A_Saving-Throw_Gear "wikilink") gear.
