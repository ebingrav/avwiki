[Centaur Hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink") has 21
[takable](/wiki/Get "wikilink") [objects](Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"),
several of which may be quite desirable. Its three [ticket-quest
rewards](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quest_Rewards "wikilink") (namely [an Orb of
Sunshine](/wiki/Orb_Of_Sunshine "wikilink"), [a darklin carapace
ring](/wiki/Darklin_Carapace_Ring "wikilink"), and [Konk's Gigantic Clobberin'
Stick](/wiki/Konk's_Gigantic_Clobberin'_Stick "wikilink")) are particularly
nice, though few [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") will be
[strong](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") enough to [wield](Wield "wikilink")
[Konk](/wiki/Konk "wikilink")'s [big
stick](/wiki/Konk's_Gigantic_Clobberin'_Stick "wikilink"). Four of its
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") [wield](Wield "wikilink") decent
[missile weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink") (either [a
sprite fusilier's sling](/wiki/Sprite_Fusilier's_Sling "wikilink") or [a
simple hunter's longbow](/wiki/Simple_Hunter's_Longbow "wikilink")); small
bits of [ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink") for these
[missile weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink") may be found
in [this area](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink"), as well. Its
three [ents](/wiki/Ents "wikilink") each [wear](Wear "wikilink")
[bracelets](/wiki/Entish_Woven_Bracelet "wikilink") that serve as decent [hit
gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Hit_Gear "wikilink"), its thirteen
[centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") each [wear](Wear "wikilink")
[hipposandals](/wiki/Fine_Set_Of_Hipposandals "wikilink") that make nice [dex
gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Dex_Gear "wikilink"), and one of these
[centaurs](/wiki/Centaur "wikilink") (namely [Silvanus
Wayfinder](/wiki/Centaur_Ranger "wikilink")) also [wears](Wear "wikilink") a
[wreath of laurels](/wiki/Wreath_Of_Laurels "wikilink") that makes nice [wis
gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Wis_Gear "wikilink"). Plus, two of its three
[lizardmen](/wiki/Lizardmen "wikilink") [wear](Wear "wikilink")
[cloaks](/wiki/Simple_Dyed_Silvan_Cloak "wikilink") that are useful for
[moving hidden](/wiki/Move_Hidden "wikilink"). And, finally, any
[psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") with 23(26)
[intelligence](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink") may enjoy
[darting](/wiki/Dart_(spell) "wikilink") its [centaur
psionicist](/wiki/Centaur_Psionicist "wikilink")'s [granite
rock](/wiki/Granite_Rock "wikilink"). For more data concerning each individual
piece of [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink"), see its respective page
listed below.
