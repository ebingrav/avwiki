Recommended group size: 10 - 14

Shift to [Kzinti Homeworld](/wiki/Category%3AKzinti_Homeworld "wikilink").
After you kill the [Denizen Of The
Wastes](/wiki/Denizen_Of_The_Wastes "wikilink") be sure to loot the [Astral
Disruption Rod](/wiki/Astral_Disruption_Rod "wikilink") from his corpse. Then
follow the path, as there's only one way to go.

When you come to a fork in the path head east. This will take you to
[Inferno Peak](/wiki/Category%3AInferno_Peak "wikilink"), which is a swarmier
and gassier area than the one you just left. Make your way down into the
volcano, then go all east, and all north. When you see a down exit take
it.

This will put you in the lava tunnels. This part of the area is less
swarmy. The mobs are bigger and yield better exp. There are a series of
private rooms along this part of the path, so be sure to tell your
groupies which way to go (even though it's a hallway). Try your hardest
not to leave anyone behind during this part.

You'll know you're close to the gear room when you start fighting [Fire
Elementals](/wiki/Fire_Elemental "wikilink") and [Lava
Trolls](/wiki/Lava_Troll "wikilink"). Make sure to stop and make a tank chain
when you see the prince and the [Projection Of
Imix](/wiki/Projection_Of_Imix "wikilink") on scan below you. When you're
ready, enter that room and target the prince. Once he is dead, cast calm
(if necessary) to prevent your groupies from killing the projection.

Give the [Astral Disruption Rod](/wiki/Astral_Disruption_Rod "wikilink") to
the [Projection Of Imix](/wiki/Projection_Of_Imix "wikilink") and he will
create a portal for you. Have the first tank in the chain enter lava,
then have the rest of the group follow and clear the moderately
difficult gear room. Once you've cleared the room and looted the
[Sceptre Of Blazing Fury](/wiki/Sceptre_Of_Blazing_Fury "wikilink") portal to
the [Denizen Of The Wastes](/wiki/Denizen_Of_The_Wastes "wikilink") and
homeshift from there.
