From [Kzinti Homeworld](/wiki/Category%3AKzinti_Homeworld "wikilink") shift
you can only go in one direction for a while. At the first fork in the
path, head north. This will take you to the [Savage
Jungle](/wiki/Category%3ASavage_Jungle "wikilink"). In the jungle be wary of
invis [Kzinti Hunters](/wiki/Kzinti_Hunter "wikilink") who stab. Also, psions
need to be wary of [Sussuruses](/wiki/Sussurus "wikilink") who stomp, on scan
they show up as a mass of vegetation or something like that. From here
head north, searching for a fountain. Drink from it to enter the [Kzinti
Spire Of Knowledge](/wiki/Category%3AKzinti_Spire_Of_Knowledge "wikilink").

Track priest (or roam seeking exits going up). Once you find a room
containing the [Flock Of Ravens](/wiki/Flock_Of_Ravens "wikilink"), [Horde Of
Rodents](/wiki/Horde_Of_Rodents "wikilink"), and [Swarm Of
Insects](/wiki/Swarm_Of_Insects "wikilink"), send a tank south to areaspell
the assassins. The [Peaceful Kzinti](/wiki/Peaceful_Kzinti "wikilink") is in
the room south of that; do not kill him.

Continue tracking priestess, and be forewarned that the [Chaotic
Cloud](/wiki/Chaotic_Cloud "wikilink") can detonate! Note that the [Chaotic
Cloud](/wiki/Chaotic_Cloud "wikilink") now has the [Pair Of Simple
Gloves](/wiki/Pair_Of_Simple_Gloves "wikilink"). Kill the high priestess, and
if you do not have the first key, bring the ticket ([Glowing Kzinti Holy
Symbol](/wiki/Glowing_Kzinti_Holy_Symbol "wikilink")) you get from the
priestess to the [Peaceful Kzinti](/wiki/Peaceful_Kzinti "wikilink") to get
the [Silver Kzinti Insignia](/wiki/Silver_Kzinti_Insignia "wikilink"), then
return to the high priestess' room. Unlock/go up, and track
evolutionary.

When you find a room with two mobs above you, that will be the
Evolutionary; enter/clear it with a short tank chain, then get the group
to enter gateway quickly to avoid repop (no-flee room).

From here the full safe room is e2swn. Note that while this room is full
safe, it is not lloydable, so you can't use create shrine or planar
anchor

If you don't have the second key ([Golden Kzinti
Insignia](/wiki/Golden_Kzinti_Insignia "wikilink")) you'll need to head up. In
this part of the area are a bunch of kzinti warriors who all look the
same on scan. One of them has the key, so wander and kill until you find
it. Once you get the key return to the saferoom, regen fully and
respell.

Go up as before, then up again, and kill the guardians. From there go s,
u to reach [Alchyzer](/wiki/Alchyzer "wikilink"); he loads the [Robes Of
Wizardry](/wiki/Robes_Of_Wizardry "wikilink") (level gear) and the [Book Of
Shard Storm](/wiki/Book_Of_Shard_Storm "wikilink"). From here you have to
choose which piece of gear you want to get. [Kortez](/wiki/Kortez "wikilink")
with the [Boots Of The Conqueror](/wiki/Boots_Of_The_Conqueror "wikilink")
lies east and is the easiest of the three gear rooms. Next up in order
of difficulty is [Raskartan](/wiki/Raskartan "wikilink"), who holds the
[Crimson Bloodsash](/wiki/Crimson_Bloodsash "wikilink") and [Kzinti
Commander's Claw](/wiki/Kzinti_Commander's_Claw "wikilink"). He lies west and
keeps company with guards who rescue him while he throws and disarms.
[Taragual](/wiki/Taragual "wikilink") with the [Kzintium Blade
Bracer](/wiki/Kzintium_Blade_Bracer "wikilink"), [Necklace Of Severed Fae
Ears](/wiki/Necklace_Of_Severed_Fae_Ears "wikilink"), and [Aura Of
Domination](/wiki/Aura_Of_Domination "wikilink") lies north and keeps company
with an invisible assassin. One of the mobs with
[Taragual](/wiki/Taragual "wikilink") loads the [Amulet Of The Cat's
Eye](/wiki/Amulet_Of_The_Cat's_Eye "wikilink").

Note that once you enter any of these three gear rooms you can't leave,
so if you plan on camping you have to stay put after you clear it.

[Taragual](/wiki/Taragual "wikilink") and [Raskartan](/wiki/Raskartan "wikilink")
are very difficult, but the single most taxing part of this run is its
length. Don't try to lead this run unless your group has time for at
least 2 spellups.
