[Outland Proper](/wiki/Category%3AOutland_Proper "wikilink") is usually just
run for small group exp, but it has another side to it: the infamous
"commander" run.

Suggested Group Size: 15

After shift, take group 3s2w2d to the [Githyanki
Spy](/wiki/Githyanki_Spy "wikilink"). **From here (including the spy!) until
the gear room mobs detonate!**

Go 2d2s from there to find the room in which you can regen, and from
that room it's another 1s to the [Githyanki
Leader](/wiki/Githyanki_Leader "wikilink"), who holds the [Silver
Chestplate](/wiki/Silver_Chestplate "wikilink") and the [Silver
Sword](/wiki/Silver_Sword "wikilink"). The commander's gear room does NOT
detonate. But watch out: this is still a very risky room. It's no-exit,
so if/when you clear it you need to portal to a mob in [Outland
Proper](/wiki/Category%3AOutland_Proper "wikilink") (such as [Githyanki
Brute](/wiki/Githyanki_Brute "wikilink")) to get out. The gear room has two
slaves; one eats corpses and the other stomps gear. So if you die before
they do, you can kiss all your hard-earned gear good-bye. Because of
this huge risk, you should wait outside of the commander's room until
the [Githyanki Spy](/wiki/Githyanki_Spy "wikilink") repops. The last thing you
want is for this gear room to repop on you!

Also in this room are a couple [Silver Helms](/wiki/Silver_Helm "wikilink").

Note that the [Githyanki Leader](/wiki/Githyanki_Leader "wikilink") does not
repop normally, so you can't really camp this run.
