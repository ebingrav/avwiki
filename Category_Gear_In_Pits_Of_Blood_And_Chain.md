The main mob of interest is the [Broken Oni](/wiki/Broken_Oni "wikilink"). He
holds the [Blood-Mithril Shackle](/wiki/Blood-Mithril_Shackle "wikilink"), the
[Pick Of The Mastador](/wiki/Pick_Of_The_Mastador "wikilink"), and
[Rediscovering Ether, Book 2 In A 3 Part
Series](/wiki/Rediscovering_Ether,_Book_2_In_A_3_Part_Series "wikilink"). The
Oni can also be skinned for the [Eye Of The
Oni](/wiki/Eye_Of_The_Oni "wikilink").

Directions
----------

The first section of this run is purely no regen to acquire the broken
key (needed to reach the Oni, if you have this key already, you can skip
this part). You will encounter small mobs with the long description 'a
small fiend'. These mobs can be immolated or astral prisoned by sors,
but they must be quick as the mobs will aggie and die very quickly.

Once you enter the area through the sudden drop, the path will be 4n. To
the west of here is a locked door, it can be picked or you will need the
bloody key, which is found on a random bloodwalker in the area
(bloodwalkers are invis, so look for eyes). You may need the bloody key
for the next door, so it's not necessarily recommended to bother picking
it. The next door is at 3w n. The door is north, you will either need
the key or to bashdoor this one. Then, the broken key can be found
neenee after entering the final section of the bleeding tunnels. It is a
ground spawn so get key. Once you've acquired this key, you're looking
for one of the many up exits. The most recommended one is to backtrack
to the first door room and go east north. This door is chosen as it will
allow you to guarentee avoiding the corpse collectors. Bashdoor up and
you're in the prison area.

The second section is the prison area. There are some drifters here but
nothing too dangerous. You will be able to regen now if desired. From
the up exit given, it's south west then pick south. Then go all east,
and north til you see an eastern exit. This is the door to the Oni.
There are 5 mobs in this small section, 1 in the northern room, 2 in the
southern room and two which wander back and forth. None of these mobs
are that big. Clear them out and go south to kill the oni. For camping
purposes, either regen 1n of the oni, or go n w s s as it's a fairly
safe room. Note, if you leave the Oni area, the key has limited uses so
be aware if it breaks.
