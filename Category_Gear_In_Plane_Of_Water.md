Suggested group size: 10 or so if you're trying to get a
[tattoo](/wiki/Watermark_Tattoo "wikilink").

Note that this category is meant to describe the gear runs in "Water
Proper", whose full name is (unfortunately) [Plane Of
Water](/wiki/Category%3APlane_Of_Water "wikilink").

To get the [Watermark Tattoo](/wiki/Watermark_Tattoo "wikilink") go 4n e from
[Water](/wiki/Category%3AWater "wikilink") shift. There you will find a
maelstrom. Enter it. Both times I did so no mobs awaited us on the other
side. After you enter the maelstrom 1e should be a sole [Surly Water
Elemental](/wiki/Surly_Water_Elemental "wikilink") who isn't too tough and
gives good exp. Another east from him is the [Water Elemental
Ruler](/wiki/Water_Elemental_Ruler "wikilink") along with 4 [Huge Water
Elementals](/wiki/Huge_Water_Elemental "wikilink"). Make sure you're tanks and
casters are ready before taking him on. After you clear the room you'll
need to portal out. "wyrm" is a good portal point because you're
guaranteed to get out of the Ruler's court. The [Water Elemental
Ruler](/wiki/Water_Elemental_Ruler "wikilink") also carries a
[Watersphere](/wiki/Watersphere "wikilink").

Somewhere else in [Water Proper](/wiki/Category%3APlane_Of_Water "wikilink")
[Frostbite](/wiki/Frostbite "wikilink"), [Blizzard](Blizzard "wikilink"), and
the [Ice Efreet](/wiki/Ice_Efreet "wikilink") hang out together. They're high
level invis mobs, so you'll know you found them when you see a clump of
"red eyes watching" on scan. Currently I don't know how to walk to them.
I know you can't teleport to them, and I doubt you can track them,
considering you can't see them. Anyway, the mobs in that room have the
[Icesphere](/wiki/Icesphere "wikilink"), the [Ice Hound's
Tooth](/wiki/Ice_Hound's_Tooth "wikilink"), and the [Ice
Collar](/wiki/Ice_Collar "wikilink"). Note that they are very wimpy when
compared to the [Watermark Tattoo](/wiki/Watermark_Tattoo "wikilink") room.

Wandering around [Water Proper](/wiki/Category%3APlane_Of_Water "wikilink")
are some [Deadly Water Elementals](/wiki/Deadly_Water_Elementals "wikilink").
At least one of them (but definitely not all) carries a [Water
Ring](/wiki/Water_Ring "wikilink"), which is a new [2nd tier
ac](/wiki/Category%3ALord_Second_Tier_Tank_Gear "wikilink")/[save
vs](/wiki/Category%3ALord_Save_Vs_Spell_Gear "wikilink")/[+hp](Category%3ALord_HP_Gear "wikilink")
ring.

As far as I know the Watershapes are on random elementals throughout the
area. If anyone understand the mob-&gt;slot correlation for watershapes,
please feel free to share that information with the rest of us.
