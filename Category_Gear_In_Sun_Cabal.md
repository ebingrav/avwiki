**You will need the [Platinum Scale](/wiki/Platinum_Scale "wikilink") from
[Memory Lane](/wiki/Category%3AMemory_Lane "wikilink").**

Recommended group size: 30

Have everyone spellup on [Thorngate](/wiki/Category%3AThorngate "wikilink"),
then shift to [Midgaardia](/wiki/Category%3AMidgaardia "wikilink"). You will
be in shadow form only until you enter the vortex. Portal tys san and go
2sunus5u5e2nu to reach [Ramahdon](/wiki/Ramahdon "wikilink"). The dragon (and
mobs along the way) will aggie, but give [Ramahdon](/wiki/Ramahdon "wikilink")
the [Platinum Scale](/wiki/Platinum_Scale "wikilink") before he dies to open
the vortex. This portal only lasts a few ticks, so have the first tank,
then the rest of the group enter quickly.

There are three rooms to clear, none of which repop before reboot; once
a room is clear, you can regen/respell there as needed. Drinking will
transport you to subsequent rooms. The [Incomplete Shield Of
Lords](/wiki/Incomplete_Shield_Of_Lords "wikilink") loads on [Bahamut's
Avatar](/wiki/Bahamut's_Avatar "wikilink") in the last room, and all 6 mobs
load sigils. The shield and all the sigils must be given to
[Gilliad](/wiki/Gilliad "wikilink") on
[Thorngate](/wiki/Category%3AThorngate "wikilink") to form the shield. This is
done by giving him the shield, don't give him one of the sigils first,
because then you'll have to beg an imm to get it back for you.
