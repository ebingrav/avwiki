Getting to [Under The Stars](/wiki/Category%3AUnder_The_Stars "wikilink")
-------------------------------------------------------------------

If the [Planar Anchor](/wiki/Planar_Anchor "wikilink") isn't already set, you
have to experience the joy that is
[Noctopia](/wiki/Category%3ANoctopia "wikilink") shift. Get a ton of brutes,
have them all shift together, and hope that everyone doesn't splat.
If/when you clear shift and finish CRing your groupies, go s,d,w. From
here you need to bashdoor down to enter [Under The
Stars](/wiki/Category%3AUnder_The_Stars "wikilink"). Note that once you enter
[Under The Stars](/wiki/Category%3AUnder_The_Stars "wikilink") there won't be
any regennable rooms for a while. Also note that the first room of
[Under The Stars](/wiki/Category%3AUnder_The_Stars "wikilink") is where you
can summon the [Planar Anchor](/wiki/Planar_Anchor "wikilink").

[Yorimandil The Blind](/wiki/Yorimandil_The_Blind "wikilink")
-------------------------------------------------------

To get [Yorimandil's Blindfold](/wiki/Yorimandil's_Blindfold "wikilink")
portal to pet. This will put you in the "Iron Walkways" portion of the
area, which is [Homeshiftable](/wiki/Homeshift "wikilink") and regenable.
[Yorimandil The Blind](/wiki/Yorimandil_The_Blind "wikilink") can be found in
a gazebo in the northeast portion of the walkways. Note that
[Yorimandil](/wiki/Yorimandil_The_Blind "wikilink") can't always be found in
this area; it seems like he likes to split his time between here and
[Obsidian Arena; Floor](/wiki/Category%3AObsidian_Arena;_Floor "wikilink"). If
[Yorimandil](/wiki/Yorimandil_The_Blind "wikilink") is home you should be able
to where/track him.

[Ether Link Spellbook](/wiki/Rediscovering_Ether,_Book_1_In_A_3_Part_Series "wikilink")
---------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Another piece of gear that can be found [Under The
Stars](/wiki/Category%3AUnder_The_Stars "wikilink") is [Rediscovering Ether,
Book 1 In A 3 Part
Series](/wiki/Rediscovering_Ether,_Book_1_In_A_3_Part_Series "wikilink"). To
get this item you need to wander the bleeding tunnels portion of this
area, killing multiple [Bloodmaw
Tentacles](/wiki/Bloodmaw_Tentacle "wikilink"). I'm not sure how many you have
to kill (5?), but eventually the [Bloodmaw](/wiki/Bloodmaw "wikilink") will
spawn. Once you kill the [Bloodmaw](/wiki/Bloodmaw "wikilink") the spellbook
will load on the ground. Note that the tentacles and the bloodmaw are
all trackable.

[Ethereal Blade](/wiki/Ethereal_Blade "wikilink")
-------------------------------------------

Patrolling the bleeding tunnels are the [Ethereal
Stalkers](/wiki/Ethereal_Stalker "wikilink"). These mobs hit hard,
bash/disarm, and track you. Killing an [Ethereal
Stalker](/wiki/Ethereal_Stalker "wikilink") will net you an [Ethereal
Blade](/wiki/Ethereal_Blade "wikilink").
