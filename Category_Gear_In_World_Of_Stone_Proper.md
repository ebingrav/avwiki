Suggested Group Size: 10+, depending on breakdown

------------------------------------------------------------------------

To get the [Elemental Earth Ring](/wiki/Elemental_Earth_Ring "wikilink") shift
to the [World Of Stone](/wiki/Category%3AWorld_Of_Stone "wikilink") and portal
to prince. If portal fizzles, you may need to wait til he moves out of
the Earth Lord's room or track him (but avoid the [Roc Wyrm
Mother](/wiki/Roc_Wyrm_Mother "wikilink")'s room 4n from shift if group is
small). Kill prince and regen, if needed. You should be able to tell
which room is the gear room on scan.

**The Lord is no longer the only mob to detonate on this run.** (That's
why it is run more rarely today.) Also, some of the mobs in this area
have [Lavabombs](/wiki/Lavabombs "wikilink"), which can deal considerable
damage to a single target in one hit.

The [Shaleskin Arm Guard](/wiki/Shaleskin_Arm_Guard "wikilink"), while
technically in [World Of Stone
Proper](/wiki/Category%3AWorld_Of_Stone_Proper "wikilink"), is almost always
obtained at the beginning of a [dstone](/wiki/Living_Daemonstone "wikilink")
run, as the [Roc Wyrm Mother](/wiki/Roc_Wyrm_Mother "wikilink") lies at the
entrance to the [Mountain Of
Madness](/wiki/Category%3AMountain_Of_Madness "wikilink").
