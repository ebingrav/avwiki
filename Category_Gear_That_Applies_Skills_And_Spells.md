Some [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
[affect](/wiki/Object_Affects "wikilink") any
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who
[wear](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") them with certain [skills and/or
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") until they are
[removed](/wiki/Remove "wikilink"). These
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") are categorized below.

[Wearing](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") so
as to be [affected](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") by certain
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") can have advantages over
possible methods such as [casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") or [quaffing](Quaff "wikilink")
[potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink"), as (1) nothing needs to be
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") and no [mana](Mana_Points "wikilink")
needs to be used, (2) nothing needs to be consumed and (perhaps)
replaced, and (3) such [affects](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") have no fixed
duration and may be easily added or removed at will simply by
[wearing](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") or [removing](/wiki/Remove "wikilink") whichever
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") produces them. Doing this can
also have certain disadvantages, however, as (1) these
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") may prevent other desired
[gear](Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") from being [worn](/wiki/Wear "wikilink")
and (2), unlike [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"),
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") take up some
[inventory](/wiki/Inventory "wikilink") space and may become lost or damaged
over time.

See also [Spellcasting Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellcasting_Gear "wikilink")
and [Skills & Spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink").
