*Gemstones are how you can carry a large quantity of currency without
dealing with all of the hassle of [weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink").
Gemstones can be used for [bartering](/wiki/Barter "wikilink") in any
[store](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink") of AVATAR. These gemstones
were created by the banking guild using love, care, and a bit of magic
to guarantee equal [value](/wiki/Object_Worth "wikilink") for equal types of
gemstone.*

*Gemstones, since they are created and regulated by the
[banks](/wiki/Category%3A_Banks "wikilink"), are possible to
[withdraw](/wiki/Withdraw_(command) "wikilink") or
[deposit](/wiki/Deposit "wikilink") within the
[banks](/wiki/Category%3A_Banks "wikilink") themselves.*

These [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") are somtimes found
together with [Money](/wiki/Category%3A_Money "wikilink"). Some are also found
in random [lockboxes](/wiki/Small_Wooden_Lockbox "wikilink").

For information on where to find nice troves of
