*Created as a small military base for invasion, this is the first
foothold upon the lands of this world by the Githzerai.*

**Level Range:** 42-51

**Builder:** Crom

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 16e, \[e\], 2e, n, 2e,
3s, 3e, 3n, 2w, 3n, w, n, e, n, e, 5n, w.

Walking route from [Ulrich](/wiki/Ulrich "wikilink"): \[n\], w, 6n, e, 2n, w.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested: none.

Connections
-----------

This [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") is in
[Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink"), not far from the [Hamlet of
Kreigstadt](/wiki/Category%3A_Hamlet_Of_Kreigstadt "wikilink").

Adventuring
-----------

A [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink")
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"). Contains [loose fitting black silk
pants](/wiki/Loose_Fitting_Black_Silk_Pants "wikilink") (hit), [a black
bracer](/wiki/Black_Bracer "wikilink") (mana), [a belt of
sorcery](/wiki/Belt_Of_Sorcery "wikilink") (mana), [a dull silver
bow](/wiki/Dull_Silver_Bow "wikilink"), and [a
whetstone](/wiki/Whetstone "wikilink") (but only if [a
warrior](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") kills the
[weaponsmith](/wiki/Githzerai_Weaponsmith "wikilink")).

Be [invis](/wiki/Invis_Flag "wikilink"), as all creatures are
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") but most don't [see
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink") (exception being the sanced wandering
[leader mob](/wiki/Gith_Leader,_Wiv'githmuri "wikilink")).
