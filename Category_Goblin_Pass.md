*A path that may cut some distance off the journey between the
[Meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink") and
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") exists in the corner of the
[meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink") of [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"). Some
fear it, some laugh at it... you must decide.*

**Level Range:** 4-5

**Builder:** Crom

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"): 4s, 5e.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested: goblin boulder.

Adventuring
-----------

This is primarily used as a connector area between the [White Stone
Road](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") and
[Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink") in the southeast part of the
[meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink"). There are a few pieces of
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") in this area disguised as branches
and leaves but none of it is particularly useful. All the
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in the path are
[goblins](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink") even though they might not look it. Not
really anything of interest here, and not suitable for anything other
than a quick few hundred [xp](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") for real
small [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). There are much
better places to fight in the surrounding
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink").
