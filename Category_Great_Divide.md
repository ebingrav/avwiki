*Long ago, before [humans](/wiki/Humans "wikilink") reckoned time as they now
do, there was a terrible war between the [trolls](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink") and
[dwarves](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink"). From the [dwarves](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink")
emerged a powerful leader, [Kra](/wiki/Kra "wikilink"), gifted with the
healing touch. [Kra](/wiki/Kra "wikilink") was also a ruthless military leader
who used whatever means necessary to vanquish her enemies. The
[Dwarf](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink")-[Troll](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink") war lasted for
years with both sides taking heavy casualties but neither willing to
give in. In the year 478 of the Fae calendar, a terrible battle took
place at the foot of [Mt. Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink"). It is
said that the fighting was so intense that a river of blood carved deep
canyons in the desert lands. [Kra](/wiki/Kra "wikilink"), along with most of
the [dwarves](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") and [trolls](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink") were
killed during the battle. Both sides lacked the resources to continue
the war, which marked the end of the
[Dwarf](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink")-[Troll](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink") war. After the
battle, many of the [dwarves](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") settled north of [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink") and built a temple to their fallen
leader. Unfortunately, the story does not end there. The followers of
[Kra](/wiki/Kra "wikilink") were divided with some worshiping her for her
healing powers while others for her military might. As time passed, the
two factions began to argue more and small battles broke out. During the
second war of the [Giants](/wiki/Giants "wikilink") and
[Ents](/wiki/Ents "wikilink") the sky thundered and the ground rocked and much
was destroyed. The devastation split the temple into two parts and cast
them east and west. Only the altar, the heart of [Kra](/wiki/Kra "wikilink")'s
power, remained untouched as violent upheavals cracked the earth. As the
earth slowly stilled and the destruction ceased, the
[dwarves](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") regrouped. The followers of
[Kra](/wiki/Kra "wikilink")'s dark path took up residence in the western half
of the temple while the Light followers of [Kra](/wiki/Kra "wikilink") resided
in the eastern half. Since then they have waged constant war, each
trying to destroy the other in their quest for [Kra](/wiki/Kra "wikilink")'s
power.*
