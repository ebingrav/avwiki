This [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") comes and goes. It provides
temporary access to player-built [areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink")
submitted for [area-building
contests](/wiki/Area-Building_Contests "wikilink"). Once all of these contest
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") have been judged and their
individual fates have been determined and brought about, this hall
disappears until it is needed again for another
[contest](/wiki/Area-Building_Contests "wikilink").
