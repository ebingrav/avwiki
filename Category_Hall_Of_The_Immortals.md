*The gods have give [Snikt](/wiki/Snikt "wikilink") the role of guardian for
their universe. He has in turn recruited others through time to serve as
caretakers for Midgaardia and the otherworldly planes. While they serve
the realm, they are [immortal](/wiki/Category%3A_Immortals "wikilink"). These
[immortals](/wiki/Category%3A_Immortals "wikilink") work hard to keep the
realm running smoothly. Here,
[immortals](/wiki/Category%3A_Immortals "wikilink") past and present are
commemorated for all time.*

**Level Range:** all

**Builder:** Cerdwyn
