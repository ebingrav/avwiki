*Between the great city of [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink")
and the southern coast lies a desolate oak forest. Some say it is
haunted; others maintain that an evil necromancer inhabits [a dangerous
tower](/wiki/Category%3A_Necromancer's_Tower "wikilink") deep within the wood.
A few brave souls claim to have survived an encounter with a fearsome
[dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") whose lair is hidden in the forest.*

**Level Range:** 35-51

**Builder:** AsaMaro
