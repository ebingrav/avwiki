An alphabetical list of all AVATAR MUD healers.

AVATAR MUD's three most famous healers are [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"),
[Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"), and [Aelmon](Aelmon "wikilink").

*There are several [mobiles](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") who cast
helpful spells on players [between levels 1 and
10](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_Levels_1-10 "wikilink"). They are there as a
boon to help new players while they're still learning. A few examples of
these healers are [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"), the peaceful spirit in the Tree
of Knowledge, [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"), [Aelmon](Aelmon "wikilink"),
[Goldmoon](/wiki/Goldmoon "wikilink") and [Zin](Zin "wikilink").*
