*After [Tannah Machadae](/wiki/Tannah_Machadae "wikilink")'s army sacked
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3AOfcol "wikilink"), and she took her residence in the
[newly created town](/wiki/Category%3ACathracharraig "wikilink") and
[tower](/wiki/Category%3ADaingean_Machadae "wikilink"), she allowed her former
lieutenant [Lezgnar](/wiki/Lezgnar "wikilink") to start a life of his own at
her old tower. She reshaped it, until all was fine and dandy, and now
Lezgnar, together with his wife [Shaybl](/wiki/Shaybl "wikilink"), rules this
region of the land in the name of Mistress Mach.*
