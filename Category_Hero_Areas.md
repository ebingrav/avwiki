Popular areas for lowhero groups to gain experience include [Dream
Steppes](/wiki/Category%3A_Dream_Steppes "wikilink"), [Frozen
Wastes](/wiki/Category%3A_Frozen_Wastes "wikilink"), [Greed's
Gallows](/wiki/Category%3A_Greed's_Gallows "wikilink"), [Hamlet of
Kreigstadt](/wiki/Category%3A_Hamlet_Of_Kreigstadt "wikilink"),
[Igecsoz](/wiki/Category%3A_Igecsoz "wikilink")' Trog Market and Fortress Keep
portions, and [Toruga Cay](/wiki/Category%3A_Tortuga_Cay "wikilink") (for
spellcasters).

Popular areas for highhero groups to gain experience include [The Great
Divide](/wiki/Category%3A_Great_Divide "wikilink").
