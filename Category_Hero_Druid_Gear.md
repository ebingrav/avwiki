Recommended Gear
----------------

These items have the hitroll, for archer damage, and mana, for druid
spellcasting.  
Some druids may prefer to run in [pure archer
gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero_Archer_Gear "wikilink"), or [pure mana
gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero_Mana_Gear "wikilink") with the addition of a bow
and arrows.
