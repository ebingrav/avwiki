Between [hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[sublevels](/wiki/Sublevel "wikilink") 1 and 100, many
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") will be able to learn a
variety of [skills & spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
from other [classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink").

Between [hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[sublevels](/wiki/Sublevel "wikilink") 101 and 999, most
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") will be able to learn a
few additional [skills &
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") from their own
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink").

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may see which [skills &
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") they may
[learn](Practice "wikilink") during [hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") by typing [hlist](Hlist "wikilink")
while playing. This same information (and more) may be found within
those categories listed below.
