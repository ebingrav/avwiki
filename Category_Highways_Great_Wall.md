*The Highways include the White Stone Road, which runs around
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink") and north of the
[Meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink"); the Dwarven Trade Route, which
runs from north of [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") around to
[Mt Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink"); and the Highway that runs
from [Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink") to
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") to
[Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink"). The Great Wall is a mammoth
construct east of [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"), which is
meant to keep the various nasties of
[Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") out of the more civilized
parts of the Realm.*

**Level Range:** 5-12

**Builder:** Crom

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"): 1n, 8w.

Walking route from [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"): 3e.

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 10n *or* 10e *or* 11s
*or* 11w.

Connections
-----------

This area is the main connection area for AVATAR and as such has links
with a lot of lower level [areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"), as well
as some higher ones too. These connections include: [the
Meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink"),
[Stonehall](/wiki/Category%3A_Stonehall "wikilink"),
[Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink"),
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"),
[Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink"), [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink"),
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink") and [the
Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink").

Many, many other [areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") are connected to
the Highway network.

Navigation
----------

The various areas of the highway system have different names and as such
are a convenient way to divide up the area.

-   **The White Stone Road** is probably the part of the Highway that
    any new character will come across as it connect to [the
    Meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink"),
    [Stonehall](/wiki/Category%3A_Stonehall "wikilink") and
    [Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink"). To the north it
    leads to the Crossroads which connects with the Dwarven Trade Route
    and the Mountain Path. To the south it turns into the Path
    Around Solace.
-   **The Old East Road** and **Woodbridge Road** together run from
    [Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink") in the west to
    [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") in the east.
-   **The Dwarven Trade Route** runs from [Mt.
    Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink") and [Odevar
    Hills](/wiki/Category%3A_Odevar_Hills "wikilink") east to the
    Farmer's Way.
-   **Farmer's Way** is a straight north-south road which connects
    [Midaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") and
    [Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink").
-   **The Mountain Path** runs from the Crossroads north and then east
    to [Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink"). This section runs through
    [mountainous territory](/wiki/Mountain_Terrain "wikilink") and can be
    quite intensive on the movement points. This means that the Dwarven
    Trade Route and Farmer's Way is preferred route for simply getting
    from A to B.
-   **The Mountain Trail** is a short straight road which runs east from
    [Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink") to [the Barrier
    Wastes](/wiki/Category%3A_Barrier_Wastes "wikilink"). Be careful not to
    wander into the [River](/wiki/Category%3A_Rivers_Of_Avatar "wikilink") as
    there is no easy way back out again and it can be dangerous without
    a [boat](/wiki/Category%3A_Boats "wikilink") or other method of dealing
    with the water.
-   **Ocean Trade Route** runs north-south from
    [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") to
    [Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink").
-   **Defender's Way** leaves
    [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") and leads east to the
    Great Wall and [the Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink").
-   **The Great Wall** starts in the north at the [Tortured
    Terrain](/wiki/Category%3A_Tortured_Terrain "wikilink") and winds via the
    gate to [the Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") south to
    eventually end at [Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink"). The north
    tower by the the gate to [the
    Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") also has a connection to
    the [Air of Avatar](/wiki/Category%3A_Air_Of_Avatar "wikilink").

Adventuring
-----------

Highways, like the White Stone Road and the Farmer's Way will allow you
to travel between cities easily. Peddlers, Knights, Bandits and Monks
often wander these roads.
