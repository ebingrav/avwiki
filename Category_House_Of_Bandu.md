All the killable mobs in this area are reboot only. Since their lord
mobs, you should count on them being aggie.

From center go all w, u, e, and kill the Githzerai Mage. Get his key,
it's used to unlock the door to the east.

Kill [Ulgrat](/wiki/Ulgrat "wikilink"). He's a bit bigger. He has another key.

The next two mobs are det so many groups choose to stop here.

East from [Ulgrat](/wiki/Ulgrat "wikilink")'s room are two detting [Githzerai
Guards](/wiki/Githzerai_Guard "wikilink"). Huge surprise, in this room you get
another key.

North from the gith guards is [Smeed](/wiki/Smeed "wikilink"). He's in a
nofight room, but his description talks about killing him...wonky.
There's a chest in his room with another key in it.

Use that key on the door north of [Ulgrat](/wiki/Ulgrat "wikilink") and you'll
be in a nospell room. East of that are 5 [Golden
Guardians](/wiki/Golden_Guardian "wikilink") that are very gassy. So far I've
found the best approach to killing this room is bringing a group of 2
casters, a tank, and a healer. If you don't bring a healer chances are
the gas will kill your casters before you clear the room.

Each of the five [Golden Guardians](/wiki/Golden_Guardian "wikilink") holds
two fizora gemstones. So if you clear the room your group gets to split
1m coins. Not a bad deal in my book. The guardians room alone is worth
about ~140exp each for a group of 4.
