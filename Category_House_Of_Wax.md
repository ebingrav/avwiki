*This area is part of the [Houses](/wiki/Category%3A_Houses "wikilink") area.*

**Level Range:** 10-15

**Builder:** Dorsai

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 9s, 3e, n.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested: [wax
master](/wiki/Wax_Master "wikilink").

Connections
-----------

This connects to [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3AMidgaard "wikilink") to the
south.

Navigation
----------

The access from [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3AMidgaard "wikilink") is from the
south. It is a simple loop with a decent into the basement.

Adventuring
-----------

There really is not much here - a few mobs, a
[light](/wiki/Glowing_Ball_Of_Wax "wikilink"), and a
[cloak](/wiki/Rat_Fur_Cloak "wikilink").
