*The Hunting is a forested area somewhere east of the [Great
Wall](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") where the
[Troglodytes](/wiki/Troglodytes "wikilink") spend their time gathering the
meat of any creature found within. It's a confusing maze of twisting
forest paths that lead wayward [Heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
toward a brutal confrontation with the leader of this group of
[Trogs](/wiki/Troglodytes "wikilink"), [Aruna](/wiki/Aruna "wikilink"). Rumor has it
that [Aruna](/wiki/Aruna "wikilink") wears a magical [bone
necklace](/wiki/Carved_Bone_Necklace "wikilink") that makes her almost
invincible.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** Arundel
