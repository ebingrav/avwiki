An alphabetical list of all AVATAR MUD identifiers.

*If you can find a shopkeeper who is willing, you can pay a price to
have them [identify](/wiki/Identify_(spell) "wikilink")
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") for you. What is the cost?
Roughly 10 times whatever the [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") of the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") is how much it'll cost you. What
does it do? It gives you information about the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), of course.
[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"), what it is, and more can be seen
with this spell and shop ability. If you add "appraise" after the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") name, you will learn how much
[gold](/wiki/Gold_In_Hand "wikilink") it will cost to
[identify](/wiki/Identify_(spell) "wikilink").*

Syntax: [identify](/wiki/Identify_(command) "wikilink")
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.  
Syntax: [identify](/wiki/Identify_(command) "wikilink")
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt; appraise.
