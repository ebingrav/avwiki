An alphabetical list of all AVATAR MUD infirmaries.

*When [sleeping](/wiki/Sleep_(command) "wikilink") in a hospital you will gain
[hit points](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") and [mana](Mana_Points "wikilink")
at twice the normal rate, provided you have the [cash in
hand](/wiki/Gold_In_Hand "wikilink") to pay for it. Current cost is
([level](/wiki/Level "wikilink")^2) gold pieces per extra [hit
point](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") and [mana](Mana_Points "wikilink") gained.
Hospital [rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") are identifiable by the (
Infirmary ) [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") in their titles.
Only [Clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") and
[Rangers](/wiki/Category%3A_Rangers "wikilink") can see this
[flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink").*
