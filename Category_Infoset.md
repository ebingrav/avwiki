*Infoset will allow you to customize the information you get when you
turn on your [INFO channel](/wiki/Info_Channel "wikilink"). If you have your
[INFO channel](/wiki/Info_Channel "wikilink") on, you'll be able to see
whatever is turned on when you type '[infoset](/wiki/Infoset "wikilink")'.*

See also [Infoset](/wiki/Infoset "wikilink") (command).
