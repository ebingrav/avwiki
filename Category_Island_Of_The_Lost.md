*The Island of the Lost is rumored to be in the ocean island chain.
Tales of mighty [warriors](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") and strange
beasts have surfaced from time to time. It is also said to have many
[fountains](/wiki/Fountains "wikilink"), both beautiful and strange.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** Meldur
