*Somewhere in the blue
[ocean](/wiki/Category%3A_Sea_Of_Strayed_Hopes "wikilink") lies The Island of
Utami. A small island, covered by a large tropical forest. Its many
inhabitants range from rare insects to tigers, from monkeys to [ancient
varans](/wiki/Ancient_Varan "wikilink"). It is rich with natural treasures of
many kinds. Built upon the island lies the fabled [Temple of
Utami](/wiki/Category%3A_Temple_Of_Utami "wikilink"), home of Gods.*

**Level Range:** 25-40

**Builder:** Kariya
