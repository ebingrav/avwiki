*West of [Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink"), South of the Mountains,
and North of the Dwarven Trade Route, there lies the Kingdom of the
Willows. It is home to the Great Hall of Spiriel, ruling seat of the
Kings of [Elves](/wiki/Elves "wikilink") since Ancient Days, when the Fae
themselves walked the Mortal World. The [High
Elves](/wiki/High_Elves "wikilink") now are dwindling, and only a few still
dwell under the Willows, singing sadly of the bygone times as their land
is consumed by the lesser kiths of the World. The territory of the
Kingdom of the Willows extends from [Lloth's
Peak](/wiki/Category%3A_Lloth's_Peak "wikilink") to Devil's Peak and reaches
south to the borders of [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") in
the east, and south to [Crystalmir
Lake](/wiki/Category%3A_Crystalmir_Lake "wikilink") in the west. However, the
decline of the [High Elves](/wiki/High_Elves "wikilink") has limited the
Kingdom's control severely, mostly to the large royal gardens southwest
of [Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink"), due north of the
[halfling](/wiki/Halflings "wikilink") village of [Heather
Flats](/wiki/Category%3A_Heather_Flats "wikilink").*

Adventuring
-----------

The Kingdom of the Willows lies hidden deep within the woods west of
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink"). [Elves](/wiki/Elves "wikilink") are the
inhabitants of this green city, fighting back the
[Orcs](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink"), lost in thoughts about the old and glorious
times of their race.
