*Within the [Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") forest is a
pleasant grove that is rather a paradox amidst the danger of
[Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink"). It is rumored to be the
home of a wily bunch of nature-loving humanoids called korred. However,
it seems that a malevolent [wizard](/wiki/Category%3A_Wizards "wikilink") has
set his sights on something that the korred are protecting, and they are
quite the territorial bunch. He has recruited a band of evil
[trolls](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink") to aid him in capturing this item, but the
korred haven't given way yet.*

**Level Range:** 25-35

**Builder:** Copycat
