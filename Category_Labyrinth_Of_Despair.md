*A vast labyrinth is rumoured to exist near [an ancient Drow
ruin](/wiki/Category%3A_Ruins_Of_Reveria "wikilink") deep underground. It was
created by one of the first of the [Lord](/wiki/Lord "wikilink")
[magi](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") at the moment of his passing from
the realms, to guard an ancient and powerful
[treasure](/wiki/Category%3A_Treasure "wikilink"). Be warned, however, the
labyrinth is no longer the only guardian of the most coveted items deep
within ... rumours circulate the taverns and inns of Midgaardia of a
beholder colony who have made their home here.*

**Level Range:** 45-49

**Builder:** Chuft
