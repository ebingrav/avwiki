[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") may [speak](/wiki/Speak "wikilink")
[Common](/wiki/Common_Language "wikilink").

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of many
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") may [speak](/wiki/Speak "wikilink") one
additional language.

Only those who [practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") [Rogue
Lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink") may [speak](/wiki/Speak "wikilink") [Thief
Cant](/wiki/Thief_Cant "wikilink").

See also [Speak](/wiki/Speak "wikilink").
