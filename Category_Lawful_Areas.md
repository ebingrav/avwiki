*Many years ago, the king of [Midgaard
City](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") contracted with a powerful
security cartel to provide indefinite protective services for the area.
Over time, this hired help has evolved from a band of thugs into a
sizable group of [city guards](/wiki/Townguard_Mobs "wikilink") and elite
enforcers. A misanthropic [jailer](/wiki/Jailer_Mobs "wikilink") wields
executive power over detainees in cramped, dirty
[facilities](/wiki/Jail_Rooms "wikilink") that are a relic of the original
Midgaardian settlement. The city's
[executioner](/wiki/Executioner_Mobs "wikilink") is a morbid fellow with
sadistic tendencies. It's all a bit savage, but the system ensures that
the rules are mostly followed; and the
[guards](/wiki/Townguard_Mobs "wikilink") extend protection to all law-abiding
[citizens](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink").*

*Recently, a distant cousin of the
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink")
[executioner](/wiki/Executioner_Mobs "wikilink") settled into
[Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink"). Shortly thereafter, for
unspecified reasons, the mayor made several rather drastic changes to
the city's legal code. The [Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink")
[executioner](/wiki/Executioner_Mobs "wikilink") hired an appropriately cruel
[person](/wiki/Jailer_Mobs "wikilink") to manage the two-cell
[prison](/wiki/Jail_Rooms "wikilink"), and [citizens](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink")
of [Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink") are now subject to the same
draconian "protections" provided to
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink")
[citizens](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink").*

*Wronging a [citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink") under the protection of a
lawful city will cause you to branded as an
[OUTLAW](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink") almost immediately, and punishment is
sure to follow if you are caught.*

*Membership in a law-bound state is not a matter of physical appearance
but a state of mind; properly trained
[psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") have found a way to
[discern](/wiki/Bioscan "wikilink") whether a person holds such allegiance.
Those trained in the [ways](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink") of
[rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") can also discern whether a
person's mannerisms are those of a conscientious
[citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink").*
