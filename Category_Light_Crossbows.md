*Light crossbows are extremely easy to use, and suffer no penalty when
used in direct combat. However, they cannot perform the
[longshot](/wiki/Longshot "wikilink") or [scattershot](Scattershot "wikilink")
skills. One of the advantages to both crossbow types is that
[bolts](/wiki/Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink") are sturdier than
[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink") and have a higher survival
rate.*

Light crossbows suffer no penalty in direct combat, and are probably the
best choice for soloing in my humble opinion. Another choice for soloing
are [Heavy Crossbows](/wiki/Category%3A_Heavy_Crossbows "wikilink").

See also [Bolts](/wiki/Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink").
