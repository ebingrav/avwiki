*Long bows are the only ones that can perform both
[longshot](/wiki/Longshot "wikilink") and
[scattershot](/wiki/Scattershot "wikilink"), but suffer a large penalty to
usage in direct combat.*

Longbows are terrible if you're in direct combat! The best idea is to
have an alias to switch to a [ Light
Crossbows](/wiki/Category%3A_Light_Crossbows "wikilink") when you get aggied.

See also [Arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink").
