Best Lord long bow is the [Long Bow "Deep
Shadow"](/wiki/Long_Bow_"Deep_Shadow" "wikilink"). 2nd tier bow is the [Aurora
Bow](/wiki/Aurora_Bow "wikilink").

For crossbows, best is [Finn's Crossbow](/wiki/Finn's_Crossbow "wikilink"). I
think the [Lightning Crossbow](/wiki/Lightning_Crossbow "wikilink") is 2nd
tier.

Fusiliers use the [Dwarven Sling](/wiki/Dwarven_Sling "wikilink").
