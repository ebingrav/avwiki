The 1st tier one-handed weapon is the 30/30 [Silver
Sword](/wiki/Silver_Sword "wikilink"). If you can transmute one of these you
can offhand it as well.

The 1st tier piercer is the 25 dr [Ancient Dragon
Claw](/wiki/Ancient_Dragon_Claw "wikilink"). The 2nd tier piercer is the [Ruby
Dagger](/wiki/Ruby_Dagger "wikilink"), 3rd tier is the [Netherworld
Dagger](/wiki/Netherworld_Dagger "wikilink"), and 4th tier is the [Diamond
Dagger](/wiki/Diamond_Dagger "wikilink").

For two handed weapons, warriors typically use the [Iron Barbed
Flail](/wiki/Iron_Barbed_Flail "wikilink"), which is a [chain
weapon](http://avatar.melanarchy.info/index.php?title=Category:Chain_Weapons)
that can be sharpened.

The 1st tier bzk weapon is the [Staff Of
Karnath](/wiki/Staff_Of_Karnath "wikilink"). 2nd tier is the [Staff Of
Prophecy](/wiki/Staff_Of_Prophecy "wikilink"). 3rd tier is the [Staff Of The
Lower Planes](/wiki/Staff_Of_The_Lower_Planes "wikilink").
