Popular areas for soloers at levels 1-5 to gain experience include [Cult
of Bonk](/wiki/Category%3A_Cult_Of_Bonk "wikilink"),
[Wayside](/wiki/Category%3A_Wayside "wikilink"), and [Goblin
Pass](/wiki/Category%3A_Goblin_Pass "wikilink").

Popular areas for soloers at levels 6-10 to gain experience include
[Goblin Caverns](/wiki/Category%3A_Goblin_Caverns "wikilink"), [Witch's
Forest](/wiki/Category%3A_Witch's_Forest "wikilink") and [Grunka's Kombat
Skool](/wiki/Category%3A_Grunka's_Kombat_Skool "wikilink").
