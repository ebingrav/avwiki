Comments
--------

Note that most "newbie gear" is just
low-[level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") [level
gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Level_Gear "wikilink"). As
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
[train](/wiki/Train "wikilink") their [wis](Wisdom "wikilink") and
[con](/wiki/Constitution "wikilink") and [int](Intelligence "wikilink") and,
thus, develop less need for [level
gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Level_Gear "wikilink"), they may begin to focus on
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") that will raise their [armor
class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"), their [hit roll](Hit_Roll "wikilink")
and/or [damage roll](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink"), or their [mana
points](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink"). See subcategories below for these
specialized types of [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink").
