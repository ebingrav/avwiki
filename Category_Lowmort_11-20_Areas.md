Popular areas for soloers at levels 11-15 to gain experience include
[Heather Flats](/wiki/Category%3A_Heather_Flats "wikilink"), and especially
the [Temple of AVATAR](/wiki/Category%3A_Temple_Of_AVATAR "wikilink").

Popular areas for soloers at levels 16-20 to gain experience include
[Midgaard Country](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard_Country "wikilink"),
[Undrehand](/wiki/Category%3A_Undrehand "wikilink"), [Senex
Operis](/wiki/Category%3A_Senex_Operis "wikilink"), and
[Igecsoz](/wiki/Category%3A_Igecsoz "wikilink").
