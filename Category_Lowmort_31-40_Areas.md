Popular areas for groups at levels 31-35 to gain experience include the
Volcano portion of [Trials of
Zin](/wiki/Category%3A_Trials_Of_Zin "wikilink").

Popular areas for groups at levels 36-40 to gain experience include [The
Abyss](/wiki/Category%3A_Abyss "wikilink"),
[Antharia](/wiki/Category%3A_Antharia "wikilink"), [House of
Beasts](/wiki/Category%3A_House_Of_Beasts "wikilink"), and [Valley of the
Sun](/wiki/Category%3A_Valley_Of_The_Sun "wikilink").
