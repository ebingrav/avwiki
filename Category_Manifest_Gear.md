Certain [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") may be
[embedded](/wiki/Embed "wikilink") with a specific "recipe" of perfect
[gemstones](/wiki/Category%3A_Gemstones "wikilink") and then magically
transformed (using a "[manifest](/wiki/Manifest "wikilink")"
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")) into other
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). Such "manifest gear" is
categorized below.

See also [Manifest](/wiki/Manifest "wikilink") and [Lord Manifest
Gear](/wiki/Category%3ALord_Manifest_Gear "wikilink").
