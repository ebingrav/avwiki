Adventuring
-----------

This [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") is home to a variety of
friendly woodland creatures who you can mercilessly slaughter for newbie
[exp](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink"). Aside from the [golden
feather](/wiki/Golden_Feather "wikilink"), there isn't really any
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") to speak of, so you might be better
off exploring some of the other [areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink")
surrounding the meadow.

None of the [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") are
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"), although a fair number may
[assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink"), so be careful fighting in heavily
populated [rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). All of the
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") are of good
[alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"), so you will quickly become evil while
fighting in the area; for this reason, some newly-created
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") prefer to fight in
either the [The Cult of Bonk](/wiki/Category%3A_Cult_Of_Bonk "wikilink") or
[Wayside](/wiki/Category%3A_Wayside "wikilink") instead.
