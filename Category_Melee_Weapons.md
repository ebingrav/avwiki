AVATAR MUD has a variety of melee weapon types, including hilted,
hafted, double, chained, and concealable. Note that this is different
than damage type, such as piercing, crushing, or smacking.

See also [Melee Weapon Values](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink") and
[Poisoned Weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisoned_Weapons "wikilink").
