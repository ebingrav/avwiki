Weapons that [rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") and
[assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink") use to stab stuff. Note
that weapons with damage type "stab" don't work, it has to be damage
type "pierce"
