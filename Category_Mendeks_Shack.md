*This area is part of the [Houses](/wiki/Category%3A_Houses "wikilink") area.*

**Level Range:** 10-20

**Builder:** Mendek

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 8n, 5w, s.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested: none.

Connections
-----------

-   [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink")

Navigation
----------

It is pretty simple. A small area containing sixteen rooms.

Adventuring
-----------

The key to the front door is under the rug. The rug is a container. The
most notable item here is the [Enchanted
Lockpick](/wiki/Enchanted_Lockpick "wikilink") which is the best/easiest chest
and door lockpick you can get. Just have autoloot on as most of the mobs
are [scavengers](/wiki/Category%3AScavenging_Mobs "wikilink").
