*Set on a gently rolling plain to the north-east of
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"), several farms were
established at the behest of the King, in order to better feed the
masses living within [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"). The
King granted land to four petitioners: two [humans](/wiki/Humans "wikilink"),
simple minded but hard working; a [centaur](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink")
shepard, for his obvious talents; and an old [gnome](/wiki/Gnomes "wikilink"),
as a favor to of the [Gnomish](/wiki/Gnomes "wikilink") government. These
farms continue to nourish the kingdom today. This region also contains
[an old military post](/wiki/Category%3A_Frontier_Outpost "wikilink"), [Lake
Triumph](/wiki/Category%3A_Lake_Triumph "wikilink") and [an ancient
Vineyard](/wiki/Category%3A_LeMans_Vineyard "wikilink"). It is also rumored
that a gang of [thieves](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") operates out of
[a hidden base](/wiki/Category%3A_Outsiders "wikilink") in the area.*

**Level Range:** 15-20
