*Mindbenders are those [Psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink")
who shun
[telekinesis](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") as
an impure application of the Science, and focus instead on
[telepathic](/wiki/Category%3A_Telepathic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") magic.
This focus on the purity of mind-on-mind domination makes Mindbender
spells much harder to resist, and their attack spells more damaging.
Like all [Prestige Classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Prestige_Classes "wikilink"),
they retain the original class's [Prime
Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"), in this case,
[Intelligence](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink"). As a Psionic class, they
possess a Psion's higher degree of success with spells such as
[deception](/wiki/Deception "wikilink"). Mindbenders are prone to the type of
arrogance that can lead a caster down darker paths. They can become
[Sorcerors](/wiki/Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink") earlier than
[Psions](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") can. Mindbender masters are
known to seek solitude and quiet.*

**Abbreviation:** Mnd.

**[Base Class](/wiki/Category%3A_Core_Classes "wikilink"):**
[Psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink").

**[Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"):**
[Intelligence](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink").
