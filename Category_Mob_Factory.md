*The Factory sits at the end of a short path south of
[Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink")'s [Meadow](Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink") and is
responsible for the manufacture of all kinds of monsters and creepy
crawlies. Lately though, it has been rumored to experience technical
difficulties.*

**Level Range:** 3-6

**Builder:** Revised

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"): 3w, 7s, 2e.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested:
[Floyd](/wiki/Fix-It_Floyd "wikilink").

Connections
-----------

Look for a down exit in [Floyd](/wiki/Fix-It_Floyd "wikilink")'s bathroom,
this will take you to the [Murky
Lair](/wiki/Category%3A_Murky_Lair "wikilink"), a sewer of sorts where there
are slimes who carry [slimy rings](/wiki/Slimy_Ring "wikilink").

Navigation
----------

Adventuring
-----------

Another favourite of [newbie
areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_1-10_Areas "wikilink"), the Mob Factory is a
good place for [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") a bit
more brutish (or real low level grouped chars) to run, as the
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") here seem to do a bit more damage
than in other [areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"). It has a high
concentration of [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") that give good
[exp](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink").

All [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") will
[assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink") (except for
[Floyd](/wiki/Fix-It_Floyd "wikilink")), and most
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") (with the exception of the severed
heads) will [flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") easily. Everything is of about equal
difficulty, and the only [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") not worth
fighting is [Floyd](/wiki/Fix-It_Floyd "wikilink") himself because of his
sanctuary.
