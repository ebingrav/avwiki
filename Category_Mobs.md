*A mob, short for mobile, is any computer-run character (the things you
kill). This is similar to the distinction between [player
character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") (PC, what your player-run
character is) and non-player character (NPC, a computer-run mob). Mobs
can be squirrels, [humans](/wiki/Humans "wikilink"), or
[dragons](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink"). Some will attack you, some can
[train](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink") you, others will [sell
things](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink") to you.*

-   There are subcategories for mob
    [auras](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Auras "wikilink"),
    [flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Flags "wikilink"), and
    [types](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"); these
    [types](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") include, most notably,
    [etchers](/wiki/Category%3A_Etchers "wikilink"),
    [healers](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink"),
    [identifiers](/wiki/Category%3A_Identifiers "wikilink"),
    [shopkeepers](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink"),
    [smiths](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink"), and
    [trainers](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink"), as well as
    [pets](/wiki/Category%3A_Pets "wikilink").
