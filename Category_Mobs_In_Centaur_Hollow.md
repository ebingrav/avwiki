[Centaur Hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink"): population 25.
Usually less, except during special [centaur](/wiki/Centaur "wikilink")
gatherings, but lately swollen due to
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink")'s darklin infestation, both
with [centaur](/wiki/Centaur "wikilink") refugees and with those who have come
from both near and far to help them in retaking their native
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink") from these evil darklin.
Each [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") here has his/her own story to
share and most are happy to do so; and, as they speak, they may even
unknowingly share a clue or two that can be used to solve [Centaur
Hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink")'s present big mystery.
Between getting to know one other, these
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") may also be seen performing domestic
chores within a [centaurish](/wiki/Centaur "wikilink") cavern home, struggling
to hone their combat skills, or enjoying an impromptu archery contest in
a grotto, among other activities. Be careful, though, for [one of
them](/wiki/Voracious_Doppelganger "wikilink") is not what he appears to be!
More detail regarding each of [Centaur
Hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink")'s
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") may be found on its respective page
listed below.
