These [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") are a bit different than
other [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") because, when
[gotten](/wiki/Get "wikilink") from the ground or from a
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")'s corpse or whatnot, they disappear
and become part of one's [Gold in Hand](/wiki/Gold_In_Hand "wikilink"). They
are somtimes found together with
[Gemstones](/wiki/Category%3A_Gemstones "wikilink").

For information on where to find nice troves of money and/or
[gemstones](/wiki/Category%3A_Gemstones "wikilink") around Midgaardia, see
[Treasure-Hunting](/wiki/Treasure-Hunting "wikilink").

See also [Money Values](/wiki/Money_Values "wikilink") and
[Gemstones](/wiki/Category%3A_Gemstones "wikilink").
