*The Moose-folk are in trouble! The great prophet
[Nostradomoose](/wiki/Nostradomoose "wikilink") has foretold the end of their
world. Only an outsider to the lodge can stop this prophecy from coming
true. Will you be the one? Adventurers willing to face the ultimate
challenge should seek out Moose Lodge near the [Great
Wall](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink"), to the east of
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"). This area has a
[quest](/wiki/Ultimoose_Power_Quest "wikilink") which is only permitted to be
completed once per [incarnation](/wiki/Incarnations "wikilink").*

**Level Range:** 20-50

**Builder:** Sancti
