*You have heard whispers in the [meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink")
of a series of sewer tunnels that run from beneath the [Dwarven Day
Care](/wiki/Category%3A_Dwarven_Day_Care "wikilink") to underneath the [Mob
Factory](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Factory "wikilink").*

**Level Range:** 4-6

**Builder:** Frey

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"): s, 7e, 3s, e, \[d\].

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested: [water
spider](/wiki/Water_Spider "wikilink").

Connections
-----------

This sewer of sorts can be accessed from two places in the [Mob
Factory](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Factory "wikilink") and one in the [Dwarven Day
Care](/wiki/Category%3A_Dwarven_Day_Care "wikilink").

Navigation
----------

Adventuring
-----------

There are slimes who carry [slimy rings](/wiki/Slimy_Ring "wikilink"), which
give the same bonuses as the [pink ice rings](/wiki/Pink_Ice_Ring "wikilink").
Take care crossing through the passage, as most
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") will
[assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink"), and there is a nasty
[aggy](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") alligator which is
[hidden](/wiki/Hide_Flag "wikilink") in the middle of the path (Room desc:
Alligator's lair; you may also see one pair of "red glowing eyes" on
[scan](/wiki/Scan "wikilink")). You can manoeuvre safely around him, however,
if you are careful.
