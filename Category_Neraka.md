*The demon world of Neraka is the realm of the
[Demon](/wiki/Demons "wikilink") house of [Belag](/wiki/Belag "wikilink"). Sent into
exile here after his defeat by the hands of [Kariya](/wiki/Kariya "wikilink")
and Dyah Yuyun Utami, [Belag](/wiki/Belag "wikilink") is held captive in the
Pinnacle of Angker. Many of his minions still dwell in this accursed
place, hoping to escape from that dreaded realm and spread chaos through
the multiverse once again.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** Kariya
