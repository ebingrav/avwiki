*North of Ofcol? There's nothing north of
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink"). Not anymore, anyway. Used to be
that's where the Borleys lived, a long time ago when
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink") was just a little farming village.
They had a few tenant farmers, [a grand
Mansion](/wiki/Category%3A_Borley_Manor "wikilink") and a reputation as the
best musicians in the realm. They're dead now, but I hear [their
place](/wiki/Category%3A_Borley_Manor "wikilink") is haunted. There's [an old
keep](/wiki/Category%3A_Shadow_Keep "wikilink") up there too, even older than
the Borleys'. It was rumored to be haunted long before they came.
Probably still is. Rumor has it a group of [mountain
Ogres](/wiki/Mountain_Ogre "wikilink") have taken over the old abandoned farms
up there.*
