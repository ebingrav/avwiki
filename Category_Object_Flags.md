Each [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") has a set of "flags" that
affect it; some may have none, some may have many. Object pages on this
Wiki list each [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")'s *default*
flags—those that they have upon creation. An individual
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")'s default flags may be changed
to some degree over time via certain actions, such as by
[donating](/wiki/Donate "wikilink") it or by [casting](Cast "wikilink")
certain [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") (like '[enchant
armor](/wiki/Enchant_Armor "wikilink")' or '[rune](Rune "wikilink")' or
'[ward](/wiki/Ward "wikilink")') on it. Some flags even affect how certain
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") may affect those
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that have them in return.
Pages for each object flag are all listed below.
