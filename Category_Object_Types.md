All [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") found in Avatar MUD are of
one and only one of these types shown below. Object types that
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may take are categorized
here; other types that are not (normally) takable have pages here
instead of categories.

Note that, on this Wiki, [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that
[identify](/wiki/Identify "wikilink") as type "arrow" are filed under
[Ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink"),
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that
[identify](/wiki/Identify "wikilink") as type "bow" are filed under [Missile
Weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink"), and
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that
[identify](/wiki/Identify "wikilink") as type "weapon" are filed under [Melee
Weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").

See also [Object Values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").
