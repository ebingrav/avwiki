Each and every object in AVATAR MUD has a defined [level
range](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"),
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"), [set of "slots" in which it
may be worn](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"),
[quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"),
[weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"), [set of "flags" that affect
it](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"), [set of "affects" that it
applies to those who are wearing it](/wiki/Object_Affects "wikilink"), and
[worth](/wiki/Object_Worth "wikilink"). In addition to these universal
qualities, objects of each [type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
share one additional [set of
values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink") specific to their
respective [type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"). Each of these
characteristics has its own subcategory or page listed below.

[Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") is a very broad subcategory below
spanning all objects that
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may obtain and use.

See also [Areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"),
[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), and
[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").
