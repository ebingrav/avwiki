*Ofcol is the Great Northern City, nestled against the rocky foothills
of the northern mountains. It is home to the citadel of the Dragon
Lords, the [Grand Marshall Diana](/wiki/Marshall_Diana "wikilink"), and [a
sewer system](/wiki/Category%3A_Sewer "wikilink") rumored to adjoin the lair
of a [red dragon](/wiki/Red_Dragon "wikilink"). It is a short walk north from
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink").*

**Level Range:** 19-37

**Builder:** Revised
