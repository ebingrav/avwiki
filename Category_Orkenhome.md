*Near the [remains of the kingdom of the High
Drow](/wiki/Category%3A_Ruins_Of_Reveria "wikilink"), there exists an
underground city of [orcs](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink") called Orkenhome. Though no
one is quite sure why, the [orcs](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink") of this region aren't
like others of their kind. They don't make war, they earn their keep
through labor, and, worst of all, they act downright civilized.*

**Level Range:** 23-29

**Builder:** Teneaos
