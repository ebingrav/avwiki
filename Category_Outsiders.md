*The Outsiders are a notorious gang of
[thieves](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") and ruffians that plague the
Kingdom of [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"). Their
headquarters is suspected to be underground somewhere in northeast
[Midgaard Country](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard_Country "wikilink").*

**Level Range:** 14-17

**Builder:** Dev

3e, s, 2e, \[n\], u, n, \[d\].

Connections
-----------

One entrance is located in a barn found in [Midgaard
Country](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard_Country "wikilink"). The other is under
the [rogue guildmaster](/wiki/Guildmaster_(rogue) "wikilink"). If you do not
know [the ways of the thief](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink") then do not enter.
