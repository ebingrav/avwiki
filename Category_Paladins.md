*The temples soon realized that the world was much harsher than they
could handle. They openned their doors to the righteous anger of youth
prevalent in the knighthood of the realm and molded it into their
religious image. Paladins now roam the land, seeking to right wrongs
done against their Gods, using holy magic and the strength of steel to
defend their honor. Their [primary
attribute](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink") is
[STRength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink"). Paladins are a class only available
through re-mortality by the [Warrior](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink")
and [Cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") Classes of AVATAR.*

**Abbreviation:** Pal.

**[Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"):**
[Strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink").
