*Many travelers have returned to [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink") the
[Healer](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink"), telling him wondrous stories
about a fabled courtyard south of the
[meadow](Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink"). [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink") usually
only smiles and nods, closing his eyes as a peaceful look crosses his
features. Sometimes [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink") speaks of the occupants,
mentioning the eager young [priests](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") who
came to him in ages long since past, petitioning to have a place set up
for the newcomers from
[mudschool](/wiki/Category%3A_Avatar_Mudschool "wikilink") who might wish to
learn, to understand; those who seek guidance in their adventures in
this realm.*

**Level Range:** 3-7

**Builder:** Zahri

Adventuring
-----------

Although there isn't really any great
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") here, this is a great
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") for a few other reasons. First off
it's good [experience](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") at [early
levels](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_Levels_1-10 "wikilink") although you might
want to avoid the [stationary mobs](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink") mobs in
each [god](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink")'s temple because
they are a little harder. In addition, if you [look](/wiki/Look "wikilink") at
each [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") before fighting it you will
notice that they give a little insight into how each
[god](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") will benefit and
detriment his/her [worshippers](/wiki/Worship "wikilink"). The [stationary
mobs](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink") are exceptionally insightful.
