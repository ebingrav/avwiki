An alphabetical list of all AVATAR MUD pets.

*You can buy pets in any [pet shop](/wiki/Category%3A_Pet_Shops "wikilink").
You may only own up to two pets at a time (three at
[lord](/wiki/Lord "wikilink") [level](Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink")). This
policy prevents wholesale abuse of pets. To [buy](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") a
particular pet, you must be its [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") or higher.*

*When you [buy](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") a pet, it automatically
[follows](/wiki/Follow "wikilink") and [groups](Group "wikilink") with you. To
direct your pet's actions, use the [ORDER](/wiki/Order "wikilink")
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"). Note that a pet will \*not\*
[follow](/wiki/Follow "wikilink") you through a [portal](Portals "wikilink")
or [nexus](Nexuses "wikilink") unless [ordered](/wiki/Order "wikilink").*

*You are responsible for the actions of your pets. Conversely, if you
attack someone else's pet, you will be penalized as if you attacked the
player. It is a good idea to refrain from attacking creatures labeled
'[(Charmed)](/wiki/Charmed_Flag "wikilink")'. To release a pet from service,
use the [FREE](/wiki/Free "wikilink")
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink").*

*You may name your pet as you buy it by typing "[buy](/wiki/Buy "wikilink")
<pet> <petname>". No one will see a visible name change, but you can
then [order](/wiki/Order "wikilink") your pet by the name you choose. Do NOT
name your pets after common [healer](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink")
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), such as [Nom](Nom "wikilink") or
[Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"), as it causes
[portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink")/[teleportation](Teleport "wikilink")
misdirections.*

Syntax: [buy](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") <pet> <petname>.

See also [Pet Shops](/wiki/Category%3A_Pet_Shops "wikilink").
