Pills are eaten to gain the effect of the spell or spells within them.
They are considered slightly inferior to
[potions](/wiki/Category%3APotions "wikilink") due the lag that eating them
can cause.

See also [Pill Values](/wiki/Pill_Values "wikilink") and
[Potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink").
