*Potions have one charge each, and affect only the user. Potions can
have up to three [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), which are
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") in order. They are often contained in vials, but
not always. To use a potion, just [QUAFF](/wiki/Quaff "wikilink") it.*

See also [Potion Values](/wiki/Potion_Values "wikilink") and
[Pills](/wiki/Category%3A_Pills "wikilink").
