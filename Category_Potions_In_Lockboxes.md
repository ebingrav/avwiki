Each [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") spawns with a (small) random
chance that it will carry [a lockbox](/wiki/Small_Wooden_Lockbox "wikilink")
and each [lockbox](/wiki/Small_Wooden_Lockbox "wikilink") spawns with a random
chance that it will contain a healing
[potion](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink")—a colorful "vial of healing"
that [casts](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") one or more of the following five spells:
[cure light](/wiki/Cure_Light "wikilink"), [cure
serious](/wiki/Cure_Serious "wikilink"), [cure
critical](/wiki/Cure_Critical "wikilink"), [heal](Heal_(spell) "wikilink"),
and [divinity](/wiki/Divinity "wikilink"). These
[potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink") are usually
[level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") 25 and rarely
[level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") 51. Each of these
[potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink") has an individual page listed
below.
