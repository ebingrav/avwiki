*Prestige [Classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") are schools of
learning open only to a select few. Each class has a set of requirements
required for transition, which is done at mortal level 50. You retain
all of the [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") from your
[low-mort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink") levels, although in some
cases, [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") contrary to
the new class's teachings may be removed. After level 50, you level as
your new class in all ways, including gains, new [skills and
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), and so on. You retain
the same [Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink") as your original
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), and the same
[stats](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistics "wikilink") and attributes you had
before. The [Prestige](/wiki/Prestige "wikilink")
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") is used to select a Prestige
Class and is accessible starting at level 30. You can only be inducted
into a Prestige Class by another member. This can be a [teacher
mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink"), or a player who has practiced
[tutor other](/wiki/Tutor_Other "wikilink"). Choosing a Prestige Class is a
one way trip. You can't go back to your prior
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), so be certain before you use
the command.*
