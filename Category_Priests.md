*Long ago, in the city of Keresh, there existed an order of
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") known as Paeans, endowed with
incredible healing powers. Recently, the order has resurfaced,
restructured around [Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink"), and simply calling
themselves 'Priests.' Priests learn some very powerful healing and
curative spells, but are dedicated to their paths of peace and honesty,
and so never learn attack spells, or most
[warrior](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") or
[rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") skills. They are less
item-oriented than [Clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink"), and so do
not learn many item-oriented abilities, or learn them later. Their
[Prime statistic](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink") is
[Wisdom](/wiki/Wisdom "wikilink"). Priest trainers will not teach the Holy
Disciplines to anyone who is not [worshipping](/wiki/Worship "wikilink")
[Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink"), and Priests must maintain a high
[alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") for these spells to work their best.*

**Abbreviation:** Prs.

**[Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"):**
[Wisdom](/wiki/Wisdom "wikilink").
