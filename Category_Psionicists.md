*The PSIONICIST class is a 'spellcasting' class similar to
[Mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") and
[Clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink"), but with several important
differences. Where a [Mage](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink")'s power comes
from harnessing external energies present in all matter, and a
[Cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink")'s comes from his or her deity,
the Psionicist derives all of his or her power from within. Psions are
masters of [BIOCENTRIC
SPELLS](/wiki/Category%3A_Biocentric_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") that alter
the body on a cellular level, [Telepathic
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Telepathic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") that can
cripple the weak-minded, and [TELEKINETIC
SPELLS](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") that range
from simple [levitation](/wiki/Levitation "wikilink") to complex attack
spells. Although slightly less powerful than
[MAGEs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") in terms of spell damage, they
make up for it with their versatility and bevy of [unique
abilities](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicist_Class_Definers "wikilink"). The [Prime
Stat](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink") of a Psion is
[INTelligence](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink").*

**Abbreviation:** Psi.

**[Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"):**
[Intelligence](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink").
