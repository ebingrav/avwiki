[Centaur Hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink") has three minor
[ticket quests](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink") that
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may complete, each of
which yields a rather nice piece of [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink")
for them to [wear](/wiki/Wear "wikilink"). Those who explore [this
area](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink") thoroughly and remain
moderately observant while doing so should have little trouble finding
and completing each of them. Those who, for whatever reason, would
rather save themselves this trouble may find detailed information about
each of these [quests](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink") on its
respective page listed below.
