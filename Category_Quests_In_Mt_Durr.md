There are at least four [ticket
quests](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink") in [this
area](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink"), not counting some of the multi
area quests from neighbouring [areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink")
which might bring you here.

-   Can you help a delivery boy with his errands?
-   Can you deliver [Borgin](/wiki/Borgin "wikilink")'s book to his friend
    Marlot?
-   Can you make peace between the feuding clan leaders?
-   Can you put the spirit of the ancient dwarven king at ease?
