Walking Woods Plague Quest
--------------------------

*You look at the corpse of a dwarf here...*  
*His blood is still very wet, mingled with the dewy grass. Unless the
curse of the Walking Woods is lifted, it will invariably rise again.*

This quest is for level 20 players, if you are going in solo, or players
in the high teens if grouped. The area is overrun with undead, and
[Captain Balgaar](/wiki/Captain_Balgaar "wikilink") is dying. The "boss" mob
in the area has the first ticket you need. The prize is the [Stonehall
Hammer](/wiki/Stonehall_Hammer_(Quest) "wikilink").

Nota bene:

-   [Captain Balgaar](/wiki/Captain_Balgaar "wikilink") can be found in the
    upper level of the area.
-   The boss is sanced.
-   The boss animates corpses.
-   The [guardian ents](/wiki/Guardian_Ent "wikilink") in the area are aggie
    and hit hard. Use a [Ring of the Rat](/wiki/Ring_Of_The_Rat "wikilink") if
    you can't invis yourself. *Seriously!*
-   One ticket is exchanged in this area, but the prize is actually
    awarded in another.

To get to [ Walking Woods](/wiki/Category%3A_Walking_Woods "wikilink"):

**From [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"):** 4s, 6e, s, 2e, s, 2e, n.  
**From [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"):** Go 3e, s, 3e, 3s, 5e, 2n.  
**From [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"):** Go 17w, 1n.  
**[Portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") to:** [Terrified
Goblin](/wiki/Terrified_Goblin "wikilink").
