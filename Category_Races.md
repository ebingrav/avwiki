An alphabetical list of all AVATAR MUD races.

-   There are subcategories for
    [creatable](/wiki/Category%3A_Creatable_Races "wikilink"),
    [evolutionary](/wiki/Category%3A_Evolutionary_Races "wikilink"),
    [remort](/wiki/Category%3A_Remort_Races "wikilink"), and
    [ascension](/wiki/Category%3A_Ascension_Races "wikilink") races.

<!-- -->

-   There are also subcategories for
    [languages](/wiki/Category%3A_Languages "wikilink") and
    [racials](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Every race is a certain [size](:Racial_Size "wikilink") and is
    subject to a set of racial
    [archery](:Racial_Archery_Modifier "wikilink"),
    [damage](:Racial_Damage_Modifier "wikilink"),
    [stealth](:Racial_Stealth_Modifier "wikilink"), [spell
    cost](:Racial_Spell_Cost_Modifier "wikilink"), and [magic
    resistance](:Racial_Magic_Resistance "wikilink") modifiers.

See also [Classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink").
