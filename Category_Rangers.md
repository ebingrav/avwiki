*Rangers are a [grand-fathered
class](/wiki/Category%3A_Grandfathered_Classes "wikilink") that no longer fits
into the overall scheme of AVATAR. Since their replacement class
([Archers](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink")) was so disimilar to them in
gameplay, some Rangers opted to keep their old class, knowing full-well
that no future development for the class would ever occur.*

**Abbreviation:** Arc.

**[Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"):**
[Constitution](/wiki/Constitution "wikilink").
