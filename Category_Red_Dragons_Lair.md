*Somewhere beneath [Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") lies the
lair of [Nigurathus](/wiki/Nigurathus "wikilink"), the ancient Red Dragon.
Rumor says that the lair is more of a breeding ground, and that
adventurers who go in looking for one dragon are likely to find many
more than they bargained for, and maybe more than they can handle. Some
say that [Jake the Thorn-Slayer](/wiki/Jake_The_Thorn-Slayer "wikilink"),
bartender of the Dragon's Bane in
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"), offers a reward to those
who slay [Nigurathus](/wiki/Nigurathus "wikilink").*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** Thandor
