Adventuring
-----------

Due to some recent revisions made to this area, some information in this
section my no longer be valid.

This [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") is often only entered for the
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") in it, particularly the
[mushroom-shaped amulet](/wiki/Mushroom-Shaped_Amulet "wikilink"), the
[red-eyed jewel](/wiki/Red-Eyed_Jewel "wikilink"), and the [copper
bracelet](/wiki/Copper_Bracelet "wikilink").

Both the dark dwarves ([duergars](/wiki/Duergar "wikilink")) and the driders
will [aggy](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"), so stack
[commands](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") when entering
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). The 3 duergars will wander the
[water](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink") area, and the 2 driders will wander
the area between the [water](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink") and the
[dracolich](/wiki/Dracolich "wikilink"). The duergars have wields, but they
don't do a lot of damage if you take them on one at a time.

s2e2s from the entrance is the area where the myconoids are. They are
easy to kill and will [flee](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink"), but they will drop
your [align](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"). At the end of the land is the
[myconoid shaman](/wiki/Myconoid_Shaman "wikilink"), who has the very useful
[mushroom-shaped amulet](/wiki/Mushroom-Shaped_Amulet "wikilink"). He's sanced
but not incredibly difficult.

s3e2n from the entrance will lead you towards the other end of land,
which is where the [dracolich](/wiki/Dracolich "wikilink") resides. Like the
[myconoid shaman](/wiki/Myconoid_Shaman "wikilink"), he's sanced. Unlike the
myconoid shaman, he's got a wield and hits HARD - be careful killing him
unspelled. If you manage to kill him, you can loot his [glowing suit of
banded mail](/wiki/Glowing_Suit_Of_Banded_Mail "wikilink") (+str on body
gear), his wield, and use the key to unlock the chest to get a [copper
bracelet](/wiki/Copper_Bracelet "wikilink") (wrist hit gear), the [red-eyed
jewel](/wiki/Red-Eyed_Jewel "wikilink") (held +2int gear), and various other
not-so-useful gems, which can be sold at the
[jeweller](/wiki/Jeweller "wikilink") for a bit of cash.
