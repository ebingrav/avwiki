Every AVATAR MUD [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") may have up to six
non-magical exits. Each of these exits both leads in one of six
[directions](/wiki/Exit_Directions "wikilink") and may either be a simple
connection or else have a [door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink").
Information about these subjects is categorized here.

In addition, some [rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") may have at
least one magical exit. Each of these exits is provided by one of
certain magical [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), namely
[portals](/wiki/Portals "wikilink"), [nexuses](Nexuses "wikilink"), and some
[fountains](/wiki/Fountains "wikilink"). Information about these
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") are categorized elsewhere.

See also [Room Types](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") and
[Terrains](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink").
