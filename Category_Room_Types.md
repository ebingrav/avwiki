Every AVATAR MUD room may be of one or more types, most of which are
coded as its "room flags" by area builders.

Room types of particular interest include
[banks](/wiki/Category%3A_Banks "wikilink"), [donation
rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Donation_Rooms "wikilink"),
[infirmaries](/wiki/Category%3A_Infirmaries "wikilink"), and [pet
shops](/wiki/Category%3A_Pet_Shops "wikilink").

See also [Room Exits](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Exits "wikilink") and
[Terrains](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink").
