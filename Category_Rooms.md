Each AVATAR MUD [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") contains a
collection of individual rooms.

Each room may be of at least one special
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink"), has one specific
[terrain](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink"), and may have up to six
non-magical [exits](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Exits "wikilink") to other rooms.

Note that individual rooms do not have their own pages on this Wiki,
except for those of a few special
[types](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") (namely
[banks](/wiki/Category%3A_Banks "wikilink"), [donation
rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Donation_Rooms "wikilink"),
[infirmaries](/wiki/Category%3A_Infirmaries "wikilink"), and [pet
shops](/wiki/Category%3A_Pet_Shops "wikilink")). See these categories for
lists of such rooms.

See also [Areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"),
[Maps](/wiki/Category%3A_Maps "wikilink"),
[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), and
[Objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink").
