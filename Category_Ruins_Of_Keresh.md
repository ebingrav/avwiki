*Recent explorations done by
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink")'s surveyors have uncovered
an ancient ruin nestled in the forest to the southwest of
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"). Though several surveyors
were killed in an intact portion of the ruin, the [royal
surveyor](/wiki/Midgaard_Royal_Surveyor "wikilink") team continues to map and
thoroughly explore around the ruins.*

**Level Range:** 12-18

**Builder:** Nadreck

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 16s, w.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested: [Midgaard Royal
Surveyor](/wiki/Midgaard_Royal_Surveyor "wikilink").

Adventuring
-----------

There is a [ticket quest](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink") in this
area. Kill the Stone Serpent of Keresh and take its tail to the Royal
Surveyor. He will give you a scale to take to Paige. Paige will give you
a letter to take to the Lord of the Hunt (located in the Park - 5s,w of
Aelmon). Your reward is a [moonstone](/wiki/Moonstone "wikilink") (6000 gold
pieces). This paragraph's information should be placed in [Category%3A
Quests In Ruins Of
Keresh](/wiki/Category%3A_Quests_In_Ruins_Of_Keresh "wikilink").
