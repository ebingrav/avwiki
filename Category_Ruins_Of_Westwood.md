*Deep beneath the lush, green and infested forests of
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink") lie dark, ruined and aging
structures. Rumour has it that some of its inhabitants still have yet to
migrate to the [camp](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Camp "wikilink") the rest of
the [Centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") have fled to. Even still, its
remaining denizens have managed to carve out a survivable existance,
still battling the darklin hordes.*

**Level Range:** 10-15

**Builder:** Zahri
