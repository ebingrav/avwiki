*The town of [Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink") is one of only two
towns to be built on the coast. The Pirate
[Captain](/wiki/Captain_Black_Beard "wikilink") of the S.S. Scurvy says he is
docked south of [Mervue](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink") in order to
trade goods, and is not there to watch for heavily laden ships leaving
the port. You believe him, don't you?*
