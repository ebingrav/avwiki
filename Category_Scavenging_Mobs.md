[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will pick up any and all
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that they see lying on the
ground around them.

See also [Janitor Mobs](/wiki/Janitor_Mobs "wikilink").
