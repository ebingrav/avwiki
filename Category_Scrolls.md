*A scroll has one charge, but can affect several
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). A scroll (like a
[potion](/wiki/Category%3APotions "wikilink")) can have three
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") which are cast in sequence. To
use a scroll, [RECITE](/wiki/Recite "wikilink") it. If the
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") can be cast on another
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or on an
[object](Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), [RECITE](/wiki/Recite "wikilink")
<scroll> <target>, like "[recite](/wiki/Recite "wikilink")
[identify](/wiki/Identify_(spell) "wikilink") sword". You must be adept with
the "[read magic](/wiki/Read_Magic "wikilink")"
[skill](Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") to [recite](/wiki/Recite "wikilink")
scrolls.*

See also [Scroll Values](/wiki/Scroll_Values "wikilink").
