*The shrine near the [ruins of
Reveria](/wiki/Category%3A_Ruins_Of_Reveria "wikilink") was built in the time
of the High [Drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink"), and had originally been a place of
worship, but soon became a place where outcast [drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink"),
known as veldri, fled from their kin and mysteriously vanished.
Recently, reports of creatures of stone moving in the shrine have been
cited. [King Domi](/wiki/King_Domi "wikilink") has ordered a thorough
investigation of this area, seeking to rid the place of magic.*

**Level Range:** 35-41

**Builder:** Masamun
