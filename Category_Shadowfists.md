*Sneaky and manipulative, Shadowfists corrupt the teachings of
[Gorn](/wiki/Gorn "wikilink") to justify their sinister world view; The
massacre of all those who stand against them through any means
neccesary. They combine unarmed fighting with the dishonorable tactics
usually favored by [Rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") and
[Assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink"). Like all [Prestige
Classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Prestige_Classes "wikilink"), they retain the
original class's [Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"), in this
case, [Dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink"). Shadowfists have strayed too
far from the light to ever become
[Priests](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink"), like
[Monks](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") can, but they can become
[Assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink"), albeit later than
[Rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink"). Shadowfists masters can
sometimes be found training in inhospitable environs.*

**Abbreviation:** Shf.

**[Base Class](/wiki/Category%3A_Core_Classes "wikilink"):**
[Monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink").

**[Prime Requisite](/wiki/Prime_Requisite "wikilink"):**
[Dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink").
