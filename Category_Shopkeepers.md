An alphabetical list of all AVATAR MUD shopkeepers.

*Stores and shops can be found in most of the major cities. You will
find a large assortment of [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") for
sale, such as [armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink"),
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink"),
[potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink") and
[scrolls](/wiki/Category%3A_Scrolls "wikilink"),
[treasures](/wiki/Category%3A_Treasure "wikilink"),
[food](/wiki/Category%3A_Foods "wikilink") and
[drink](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink"),
[containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink"), and even
[pets](/wiki/Category%3A_Pets "wikilink").*
