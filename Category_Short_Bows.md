''Short bows do less damage than their [larger
cousins](/wiki/Category%3A_Long_Bows "wikilink"). They suffer a moderate
penalty in direct combat, as they are somewhat cumbersome. Short bows
can perform [scattershot](/wiki/Scattershot "wikilink"), but not
[longshot](/wiki/Longshot "wikilink").

See also [Arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink").
