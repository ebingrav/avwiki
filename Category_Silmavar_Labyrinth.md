*The Silmavar is a colossal underground series of caves, caverns,
tunnels and ruins. Its origin and purpose are completely unknown, even
to the myriad creatures that make their home there. Legends speak of a
conclave of crazed [humans](/wiki/Humans "wikilink"), lead by their king,
[Saeren](/wiki/Saeren "wikilink"), and of hordes of
[undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink") in the thrall of the great Lich,
[Numenor](/wiki/Numenor "wikilink"), and of [goblins](Goblins "wikilink") and
balrogs and lakes of fire and magma. Some even speak in hushed whispers
of a small group of ancient [dragons](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") guarding some
of the labyrinth's oldest ruins. Silmavar is a place of many wonders,
and very few have seen all of them.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** Draeger
