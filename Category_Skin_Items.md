"Skin items" are special objects obtained from certain
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), not by taking their equipment or
inventory but rather by [skinning](/wiki/Skin "wikilink") their corpses. To do
this, [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must
[practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") [Skin Corpse](/wiki/Skin_Corpse "wikilink"),
[wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") a [Bowie
knife](/wiki/Standard_Issue_Bowie_Knife "wikilink"), use the
[skin](/wiki/Skin "wikilink") [command](Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"), and
have an empty corpse of the appropriate
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") either in their inventory or on the
ground in their [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").

Determining which [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")' corpses will
yield special "skin items" beforehand is impossible. Most
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") corpses, like all
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") corpses, when
[skinned](/wiki/Skin "wikilink"), will yield either generic [skin
bags](/wiki/Skin_Bags "wikilink") (usually) or other "junk" items like coats
or belts (rarely), all named after whatever they were made from, or
perhaps nothing but a "bloody mess," depending on luck. Only a few
select [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") will yield special items,
instead, like those listed below.

Note that, at present, only [archers](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink")
and [fusiliers](/wiki/Category%3A_Fusiliers "wikilink") may
[practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") [Skin Corpse](/wiki/Skin_Corpse "wikilink") to
produce these items.

See also [Skin-Item Mobs](/wiki/Skin-Item_Mobs "wikilink") and [Skin
Corpse](/wiki/Skin_Corpse "wikilink").
