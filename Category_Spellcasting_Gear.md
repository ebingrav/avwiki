[Object types](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink") that
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") [spells](Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") are
[staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink"),
[wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink"),
[pills](/wiki/Category%3A_Pills "wikilink"),
[potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink"), and
[scrolls](/wiki/Category%3A_Scrolls "wikilink").

-   There are subcategories for
    [brandishes](/wiki/Category%3A_Brandishes "wikilink"), as well as for
    [pills](/wiki/Category%3A_Pills "wikilink"),
    [potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink"),
    [scrolls](/wiki/Category%3A_Scrolls "wikilink"),
    [staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink"), and
    [wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink").
