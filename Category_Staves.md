*A staff can have more than one charge, and tends to affect many
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"), depending on the
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). To use a staff,
[HOLD](/wiki/Hold "wikilink") it, then [BRANDISH](Brandish "wikilink"). Battle
staves usually have area spells that affect all
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"), healing staves heal all
[players](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"), and so on. You must be adept with
the "[arcane knowledge](/wiki/Arcane_Knowledge "wikilink")"
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") to use staves.*

See also [Staff Values](/wiki/Staff_Values "wikilink") and
[Wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink").
