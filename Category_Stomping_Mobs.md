[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will stomp on any
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that they may see laying on the
ground around them during combat.
