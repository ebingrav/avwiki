**Level Range:** 5-15

**Builder:** Crom

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"): n, 9w.

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 17w, s, 5w, 3n, 3w, n,
w.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested:
[Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink").

Connections
-----------

Stonehall connects with the [White Stone
Road](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") (part of Midgaardia's
[highway system](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink")) via a
narrow path to its east; opposite this road is a narrow trail that leads
into [the Meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink").

A wooded trail leads northward from this same narrow path into the
boundaries of the neighboring [Oak
Circle](/wiki/Category%3A_Oak_Circle "wikilink"). A [hidden
door](/wiki/Hidden_Doors "wikilink") in Stonehall's northwest corner opens
westward into the [Oak Circle](/wiki/Category%3A_Oak_Circle "wikilink"), as
well.

At present, Stonehall's second floor connects temporarily to a Hall of
Judging, where various player-designed areas submitted for 2005's
lowmort [area-building contest](/wiki/Area-Building_Contests "wikilink") are
accessible; eventually, these contest areas will either disappear
forever or settle into a permanent spot somewhere else in Midgaardia.

Navigation
----------

Stonehall's main section is a 3-by-3 square centered around a marble
[fountain](Fountains "wikilink") where [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink") stands.
Various other useful [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") stand around
its periphery. Steps in its northwest corner lead upward to its second
floor, which is ring-shaped, free of
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), and lined with benches. See
[Stonehall Map](/wiki/Stonehall_Map "wikilink") for more detail.

Adventuring
-----------

As Stonehall has very few [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), and as
none of them may be killed, players will need to venture elsewhere to
gain [experience](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink"). The
[highways](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") just east of
Stonehall provide convenient access to many nearby
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") while vast
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink") and its environs lie not far
westward beyond the [Oak Circle](/wiki/Category%3A_Oak_Circle "wikilink"). See
[Lowmort 11-20 Areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_11-20_Areas "wikilink") for
more information on this subject.

Although Stonehall does not offer
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") any opportunities to
gain [experience](/wiki/Experience "wikilink"), it does offer them many useful
services via its various [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") and
[rooms](Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"), who stands
at Stonehall's heart, is both a [healer](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink")
and a default [recall](/wiki/Recall "wikilink") point for all
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") between
[levels](Level "wikilink") 5 and 14. Southwest of [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink")
lies a [bank](/wiki/Category%3A_Banks "wikilink") and southeast of him stands
a [trainer](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink") named
[Carl](Carl "wikilink"). Also, south of [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink") is a nice
[ent](/wiki/Ents "wikilink") named [Edmuntrillion](Edmuntrillion "wikilink")
who can [identify](/wiki/Identify_(command) "wikilink")
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") for a price. Stonehall has three
[shopkeepers](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink"), as well: northeast of
[Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"), [Tilly](Tilly "wikilink") sells maps of local
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"); north of [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"),
[a half-elven girl](/wiki/Fletcher's_Daughter "wikilink") sells
[arrows](/wiki/Standard_Arrows "wikilink") and
[bolts](/wiki/Standard_Bolts "wikilink"), which
[archers](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") may find especially handy;
and, west of [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"),
[Silverbeard](/wiki/Silverbeard "wikilink") sells
[food](/wiki/Category%3A_Foods "wikilink") and
[drink](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink") (however, since
Avatar's [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") no longer
experience hunger and thirst, his wares have little practical value
these days). [Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") wanting to
[buy](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") other types of
[goods](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") without traveling to
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") might find them for sale in
nearby [Solace](/wiki/Category%3A_Town_Of_Solace "wikilink").
