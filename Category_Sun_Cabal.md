The last of the great dragons have formed a mysterious alliance with
powerful lizard and Tuataur magicians. The goal of their cooperation is
an unknown evil.  
Plane: [Nowhere](/wiki/Category%3ANowhere "wikilink"), but you enter from
[Midgaardia](/wiki/Category%3AMidgaardia "wikilink")  
To get here you need to give the [Platinum
Scale](/wiki/Platinum_Scale "wikilink") to [Ramahdon](Ramahdon "wikilink")  
This is the run where you get the [Shield Of
Lords](/wiki/Shield_Of_Lords "wikilink")
