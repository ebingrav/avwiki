*Telekinesis is the basic [Psionic](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink")
principle of moving [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") and
creating forces with one's mind. These spells are used for a variety of
purposes. It is not possible for even the most powerful
[psion](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") to directly damage a foe
with telekinesis. A squirming, fighting opponent is simply too hard to
get a secure mental grip on, so crushing or mangling with telekinesis is
implausible. Psionic Telekinetic attack spells thus use
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") as weapons, and include
[DART](/wiki/Dart_(spell) "wikilink"), [BLAST BOLT](Blast_Bolt "wikilink"),
[TORCH](/wiki/Torch_(spell) "wikilink"), [DETONATE](Detonate "wikilink"),
[WEAPON RUSH](/wiki/Weapon_Rush "wikilink"), and [DANCING
WEAPON](/wiki/Dancing_Weapon "wikilink"). Other Telekinetic spells create
forces for defense or other purposes. They include [ENERGY
SHIELD](/wiki/Energy_Shield "wikilink"), [MENTAL
BARRIER](/wiki/Mental_Barrier "wikilink"), and
[LEVITATION](/wiki/Levitation "wikilink"). Since applying force to other
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") and
[people](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") is as easy as applying it to
oneself, all telekinetic spells can be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on
others.*
