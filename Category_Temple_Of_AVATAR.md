*In this Realm, only [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") Keep
itself comes close to the size and grandeur of the Temple of the Gods of
Avatar. Its enormous dome houses statues to all the
[Immortals](/wiki/Category%3A_Immortals "wikilink") of our World, as well as
the [Cleric's](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") Guild of
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") and a library containing the
oldest texts ever created.*

**Level Range:** 15-35

**Builder:** Dev
