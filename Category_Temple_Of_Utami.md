*Looking out over the [island of
Utami](/wiki/Category%3A_Island_Of_Utami "wikilink") is the fabled Temple,
home of the empress and emperor of the once huge Empire of Utami. These
days the few remaining inhabitants live a secluded life and they go
about their business without seemingly interfering with the outside
world too much. It is said that many treasures are to be found on the
island and in the Temple itself several artifacts of power are hidden.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** Kariya

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 39s, e, 5s, 4e, 5s, 3w,
5s, e, 4s (note: crosses [water terrain](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink")).

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested:
[Merline](/wiki/Merline "wikilink").

Adventuring
-----------

A small [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") with very few
[aggies](/wiki/Aggressive_Mops "wikilink"), good
[exp](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink"), and some
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") to boot. It also seems to repop very
quickly. Great for lowhero runs.

Only a few [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") here, so dont expect to
go all of spells here while on a run.

Mobs of interest:

-   [Statue Of The Dark Evil
    Ruler](/wiki/Statue_Of_The_Dark_Evil_Ruler "wikilink") has the [dark
    flaming blade](/wiki/Dark_Flaming_Blade "wikilink"), which makes a
    nice offhand.
-   [Statue Of The Red Archer](/wiki/Statue_Of_The_Red_Archer "wikilink") has
    the [red bone bow](/wiki/Red_Bone_Bow "wikilink"), the easiest to get
    [hero-level bow](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero_Missile_Weapons "wikilink").
-   [Mavis Wariso](/wiki/Mavis_Wariso "wikilink") has the [ring of
    Purity](/wiki/Ring_Of_Purity "wikilink").
