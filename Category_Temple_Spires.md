*Atop the domed roof of the [Temple of
Avatar](/wiki/Category%3A_Temple_Of_AVATAR "wikilink") stand several very old,
very unstable towers of stone. Occasionally, stones from these towers
will come loose, slide down the curve of the dome and brain a passing
citizen. Some say [Gargoyles](/wiki/Gargoyles "wikilink") throw these rocks.*

**Level Range:** 25-30

**Builder:** Dev

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 5w, 6n, u, 3n, u, w, u.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested: [ugly
gargoyle](/wiki/Ugly_Gargoyle "wikilink").

Adventuring
-----------

A nice rarely-run [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"), suitable to be
soloed from about 20 on with a few spells.

A few things to be mindful of. Getting here involves walking north and
up from [Temple of Avatar](/wiki/Category%3A_Temple_Of_AVATAR "wikilink"),
going PAST the statue of Snikt, which will likely
[aggie](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") you, but at 20+ shouldnt be terribly
difficult.

When you reach the spires, be careful of a few
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). One is the Anthropomorphic Dragon
Gargoyle, which hits alot harder than the others. If you choose to enter
any of the towers, make sure you dont wander into [The
Game](/wiki/Category%3A_Game "wikilink"), unless you REALLY wanna die.

You can find the hidden [hunchback](/wiki/Hideous_Hunchback "wikilink") here,
and he has [a bent and mangled ring](/wiki/Bent_And_Mangled_Ring "wikilink"),
which is excellent hitgear if you are evil or get it harmzed or
[faded](/wiki/Fading "wikilink").
