Every AVATAR MUD [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") has one and only
one terrain type, coded as its "[sector](/wiki/Sectors "wikilink") flag" by
area builders. These terrain types affect
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")' [movement
costs](/wiki/Movement_Costs "wikilink"), among other things.

See also [Room Exits](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Exits "wikilink") and [Room
Types](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink").
