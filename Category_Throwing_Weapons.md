These [weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") provide a little
something extra for [rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink"),
[fusilers](/wiki/Category%3A_Fusiliers "wikilink"),
[warriors](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink"), and [black circle
initiates](/wiki/Category%3A_Black_Circle_Initiates "wikilink"). Some of these
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") have many attributes so close
examination is important.

See also [Throwing Weapon Values](/wiki/Throwing_Weapon_Values "wikilink").
