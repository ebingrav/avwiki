These [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") are obtained not simply
by killing [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") or looting
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") or such but by completing [ticket
quests](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink"). Because of this, most of
these [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") cannot be found using
[Locate Object](/wiki/Locate_Object "wikilink") except (perhaps) for those
carried by other [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").
Players may, with some time and effort, figure out how to complete these
[quests](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink") on their own if they know
where to start from. Those who, for whatever reason, would rather learn
from others' experience than from their own may find instructions for
completing many of these [quests](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink")
under [Ticket Quests](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink").

See also [Ticket Quests](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink").
