*[Practicing](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") [spells and
skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is a little different.
To [learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") [a spell or a
skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), you need to find your
trainer or guildmaster, and be in the room.*

-   *For [skills and spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
    between [levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 1 and 5, seek the [Spirit of the
    Tree](/wiki/Spirit_Of_The_Tree_Of_Knowledge "wikilink") who lives on a
    south-pointing branch of the Tree of Knowledge.*

<!-- -->

-   *For [skills and spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
    between [levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 6 and 15, seek the trainer called
    [Carl](/wiki/Carl "wikilink") in the rest place called
    [Stonehall](/wiki/Category%3A_Stonehall "wikilink").*

<!-- -->

-   *For all other [skills and
    spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), you'll need to
    locate your guildmaster in the city of
    [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") (or, for
    [monks](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") - the [Temple of
    Gorn](/wiki/Category%3A_Temple_Of_Gorn "wikilink") north of
    [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink")).*

Syntax: [practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink")
&lt;[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")&gt;.

-   There are subcategories below both for
    [general](/wiki/Category%3A_General_Trainers "wikilink") and for
    [class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink")-specific trainers.

See also [Practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") and [Skills &
Spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink").
