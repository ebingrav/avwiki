*Some [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") carry or guard treasure
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), which are high
[value](Object_Worth "wikilink") [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
that can be sold to jewelers.*

In addition to the [Jeweller](/wiki/Jeweller "wikilink") in Midgaard the
[Grocer](/wiki/Grocer "wikilink") (enw from the Jeweller) will also buy
treasure items. Having multiple places to sell items is good as if the
shopkeeper already has the item they will pay about 60% less.

See also [Treasure Values](/wiki/Treasure_Values "wikilink").
