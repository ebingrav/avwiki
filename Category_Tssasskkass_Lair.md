*Within the bowels of Devil's Peak lies the lair of the
[demon](/wiki/Demons "wikilink") Tssasskkas. A mighty necromancer, the
[demon](/wiki/Demons "wikilink") is said to raise the bodies of those who fall
within her realm so that they may serve her forever.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** S. Heart

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 19n, 3e, 5n, u, e, u, w,
n, u, n, u, 6n, w, u, n, u, e, u, n, w, s, u, n, u, e.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested:
[spawner](/wiki/Spawner "wikilink").

Adventuring
-----------

This area is good for experience, but it's also very dangerous. Among
its hazards are:

-   [corpse-eaters](/wiki/Corpse-Eating_Mobs "wikilink").
-   a blind, [private](/wiki/Private_Rooms "wikilink")
    [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").
-   [levers](/wiki/Markings "wikilink") that only certain classes can see,
    which open [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") that swing shut
    after someone enters.
-   and, of course, swarms of large scary
    [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink").

Be careful when exploring this place!
