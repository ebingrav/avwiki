*The [priests](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") and
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") of
[Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink"), the
[goddess](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") of healing, wanted
a place where they could [train](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink") their
followers in solitude. So they sought a place away from the cities and
towns and there they built their hospice. Nestled in the northern
mountains, they provide a place of healing for those who cannot find it
elsewhere. They also provide [training](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink")
to those who have dedicated themselves to the healing arts, no matter
their [god](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink").
[Priests](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink"),
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink"), chirugeons, and
[paladins](/wiki/Category%3A_Paladins "wikilink") all make this glade their
home.*

**Level Range:** 17-22

**Builder:** Cerdwyn
