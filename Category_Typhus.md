*Near the top of the Devil's Peak, a battle of wills between the
[elven](/wiki/Elves "wikilink") healer [Tyslin San](/wiki/Tysiln_San "wikilink") and
her enemies has begun to threaten the mortal realm. The powerful [demon
lord Typhus](/wiki/Demon_Lord_Typhus "wikilink") has formed a vast
[portal](/wiki/Portals "wikilink") and is pulling his forces through to lay
waste to the fair lands of Midgaardia. [Tyslin](/wiki/Tysiln_San "wikilink")
has trapped the [demons](/wiki/Demons "wikilink") inside a mystical
[fountain](/wiki/Fountains "wikilink"), but it is unknown how long her
enchantment can hold back the tide.*

**Level Range:** 51-51

**Builder:** Striper

Adventuring
-----------

The starting area is a rectangular area containing Spined Devils (note:
these use wields), Dretches, and Quasits (note: these are
[invisible](/wiki/Invis_Flag "wikilink") and will
[assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink") mobs). The Dretches are the most
experience, and Spined Devils are a tad bit annoying as they are
[flying](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink") [fleers](Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink"). Once
you have cleared out this section, head in a southeastern direction till
you reach a passageway that leads to the big
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). You will find a Stone Guardian at
the end of the passageway that can smush small groups/groupies so be
careful. After you defeat this one, you will find another after you go
down and through the northwestern passageway. Once you have conquered
this, open the west door and you will find a square room with two Demon
Guardians and the [Demon Lord Typhus](/wiki/Demon_Lord_Typhus "wikilink")
himself. The Guardians are good exp for minimal work. If your up to the
challenge Typhus is a big mob with some nifty stuff (see gear) and alot
of exp for small groups. He lies in the southwest corner of this plot.
This area is suitable for experienced duos/trios with a bit of
[hp](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink"), and small groups finishing or starting a
run.

-   Aggie Mobs: Stone Guardians, Demon Guardians, and Typhus.
