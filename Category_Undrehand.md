*The Man known as [Undrehand](/wiki/Undrehand "wikilink") is an
ex-[thief](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") who has recently claimed to be
of Noble Blood, and seized all the land between [Woodbridge
Road](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") and the [Dwarven Trade
Route](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") as his own, including
the [halfling](/wiki/Halflings "wikilink") farming village of [Heather
Flats](/wiki/Category%3A_Heather_Flats "wikilink"). According to most maps,
this land is really part of the protectorate of the [High
Elven](/wiki/High_Elves "wikilink") [Kingdom of the
Willows](/wiki/Category%3A_Kingdom_Of_The_Willows "wikilink").*

**Level Range:** 15-18

**Builder:** Dev
