*Somewhere off [White Stone
Road](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") lies a small valley.
It is rumored to be a short cut to the [goblin
caverns](/wiki/Category%3A_Goblin_Caverns "wikilink"), but this hasn't been
confirmed. The adventurers that went there--and made it back alive--say
that a large pack of wild dogs prey here, along with
[goblin](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink") hunting parties. Many brave men, women and
its have found their demise here. Will you be one of them?*

**Level Range:** 5-10

**Builder:** Kariya

This [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") was added in 2006 Mar.
