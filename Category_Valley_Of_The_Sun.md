*There is a path leading northwest from the [Elven](/wiki/Elves "wikilink")
settlements in the foothills of [Lloth's
peak](/wiki/Category%3A_Lloth's_Peak "wikilink"). It is rumored to lead all
the way through the pine forest to the north of the settlements, and
through the mountains known as Tiamat's Teeth. On the other side, there
is reputed to be an oasis of warmth and sun amidst the frigid
mountains.*

**Level Range:** 45-47

**Builder:** Dev

Directions
----------

Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 14n, 8w, 3n, 6w, 4n, w,
u, n, w, d.

[Portaling](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point(s) suggested: [Black Circle
Master](/wiki/Black_Circle_Master "wikilink").
