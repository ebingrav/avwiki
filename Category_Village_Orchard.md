*To the north of the kobold town of
[Wayside](/wiki/Category%3A_Wayside "wikilink") is a large, rich apple orchard
owned by a man named [Owen](/wiki/Owen_The_Orchard-Owner "wikilink"). Rumor
says that the reason there are so many [kobolds](/wiki/Kobolds "wikilink")
selling apples in [Wayside](/wiki/Category%3A_Wayside "wikilink") is because
they pilfer them from [Owen](/wiki/Owen_The_Orchard-Owner "wikilink")'s
Orchard.*

**Level Range:** 3-5

**Builder:** Xeon

Adventuring
-----------

Another relatively simplistic lowbie area, it can be reached by going
straight north from [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink") (opening the hidden gate in
[Wayside](/wiki/Category%3A_Wayside "wikilink")) and west. The
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in this area seem to be a bit easier
than the [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in
[Wayside](/wiki/Category%3A_Wayside "wikilink"), but it is hard to tell at low
[levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink"). They don't seem to hurt
[alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"), either.

The gardener has the [wooden pipe](/wiki/Wooden_Pipe "wikilink"), which is low
level [wisdom gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Wis_Gear "wikilink") for the head slot.
Otherwise, there is not much of note in this
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"). If you go far enough north, you
will end up on the [White Stone
Road](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") that runs north of
[Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink").
