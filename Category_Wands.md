*Wands can have multiple charges, but usually only 1
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") is affected. To use a wand,
[HOLD](/wiki/Hold "wikilink") it, then [ZAP](Zap "wikilink") your target. You
must be adept with the "[arcane knowledge](/wiki/Arcane_Knowledge "wikilink")"
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") to use wands.*

See also [Wand Values](/wiki/Wand_Values "wikilink") and
[Staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink").
