*One of the homes of the creatures overtaking the [ruins of the city of
High Drow Elves](/wiki/Category%3A_Ruins_Of_Reveria "wikilink"), this small
cave is on the northern edge of the ruins. For years, it has been ruled
by Zaswyi, who lived in relative peace with his [drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink")
neighbors. Recently he was overthrown in a coup by
[Octyin](/wiki/Octyin "wikilink"), whose sole goal was to plunder the
[drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") city for
[treasure](/wiki/Category%3A_Treasure "wikilink"). Rumor has it the lesser
[drows](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") were planning to raid the cave in retaliation
for the creatures invading the ruins and taking the temple
[treasures](/wiki/Category%3A_Treasure "wikilink"). A band of
[drows](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") were sent to defeat the creatures, but thus far
the battle is a stalemate, with each side taking heavy casualties.*

**Level Range:** 33-39
