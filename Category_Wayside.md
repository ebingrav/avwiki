*The [Kobold](/wiki/Kobolds "wikilink") town of Wayside is a small row of
ramshackle dwellings just over the hill to the north of [The
Meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink"), and serves as home to the many
failures who can't quite seem to successfully graduate
[MUDschool](/wiki/Category%3A_Avatar_Mudschool "wikilink"). Wayside caters to
the needs of the average adventurer, and the
[kobolds](/wiki/Kobolds "wikilink") will be more than happy to sell you
everything from stolen apples to leaky waterskins. If you are in town
and after a little quality merchandise, the General Store run by
[Liam](/wiki/Liam "wikilink") the [gnome](Gnomes "wikilink") is supposedly the
best deal in town. The free apple cider in Wayside's Fat Badger Inn is
rumored to pack quite a wallop.*

**Level Range:** 2-5

**Builder:** Dev

Adventuring
-----------

One of the few evil-[aligned](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") [newbie
areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_1-10_Areas "wikilink"). A fun little
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"), not as bland or homogenous as the
[meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink"). Definitely worth exploring if
you've never checked it out before.

There are two [aggy](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") characters in the area:
the carnivorous squirrel in the tree of death ("Uh oh! This squirrel has
big pointy teeth" on [scan](/wiki/Scan "wikilink")) and Ugly-Beard in the
hidden room north of the Mudschool Dropout Tent. If you are a smallish
character, it's probably best to avoid both these or stack kill
[commands](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") in them. Uglybeard has a
vial of briny seawater which is a low-[level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink")
[cure light](/wiki/Cure_Light "wikilink") and [protection
evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink")
[potion](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink").

As far as other [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") goes, Burt the
Village Idiot has the [Village Idiot's paper
crown](/wiki/Village_Idiot's_Paper_Crown "wikilink"), which is wisdom head
gear. None of the other [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") in this
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") is really of any mention.

Most of the [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in the area will
[assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink"), so be wary of big rooms of
[kobolds](/wiki/Kobolds "wikilink") if you are low on
[hp](Hit_Points "wikilink"). [Liam](/wiki/Liam "wikilink") (fat little
[gnome](Gnomes "wikilink") on [scan](/wiki/Scan "wikilink")) is
[nofight](/wiki/Safe_Rooms "wikilink"), but the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") isn't
[cursed](/wiki/Cursed_Rooms "wikilink") so you can always
[flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") and [recall](Recall "wikilink") if in trouble.
