*The sprites of [Oak Circle](/wiki/Category%3A_Oak_Circle "wikilink") are safe
from the surrounding dangers of the [Westwood
Forest](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink") due to the volunteers at the
Westwood Outlook. Drop in, share a tale, and maybe you can help keep the
[Oak Circle](/wiki/Category%3A_Oak_Circle "wikilink") safe from the darklin
menace.*

**Level Range:** 15-18

**Builder:** Quidt

This [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") was created for an
[area-building contest](/wiki/Area-Building_Contests "wikilink") in 2005.  
This [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") was added in 2006 Mar.
