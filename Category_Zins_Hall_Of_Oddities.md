Adventuring
-----------

One of the most commonly run mid-lowmort
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"). Groups come here after around
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 20 with spells, otherwise 25 or so. Decent
[xp](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") for 4-5 member groups, better with
less. [Archers](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") will have a hard time
hitting here, period. As in will do no damage, so dont expect to duo
with an arc here, even with [held shot](/wiki/Held_Shot "wikilink").

All [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") will
[assist](Assistive_Mobs "wikilink") after [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink")
30/31 or so.

A few things to be mindful of:

-   If you cant seem to locate a mob, try kill ab.
-   When it says read the sign, do so, unless you want to wander into
    the solo quest for [killing gloves](/wiki/Killing_Gloves "wikilink")
    ([hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")+ recommended).

Ideally, solo here at 29+ for [Dragons](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink"). With good
[ac](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") and
[hr](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")/[dr](Damage_Roll "wikilink") you wont get hit
too often, or too hard (my [drg](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink")
[war](Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") is [lv](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 31,
-141 and 45/75, solos here for 80+xp/kill usually, running off crims,
sanc pills, and healies).
