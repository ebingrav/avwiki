Quests
------

You give a note to a friend to a priestess of Tul-Sith. Why thank you. I
have not heard from my friend for a long while. Here, take this as a
token of my gratitude in acting as a messenger.

A priestess of Tul-Sith gives you a potion bearing all the colors of the
rainbow!

Object 'potion rainbow' type potion, extra flags bless. Weight 1, value
0, level 1. Object Quality (1 / 1 hps) Level 23 spells of: 'cure
serious'.
