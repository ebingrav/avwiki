This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") prevents an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") from taking damage from
"caustic" sources. It can be added via the [Ward](/wiki/Ward "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").

See also [Ward](/wiki/Ward "wikilink").
