*Armed with a finely crafted arbalest, the Celestial is prepared to
defend itself from any and all attacks. It would perhaps be best to not
attack the physical manifestation of the universe; it looks like its got
an itchy trigger finger.*

**Keywords:** celestial, universe.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 100.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** marksman,
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invisible](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [detect
hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink").
