*Most people think that a poison has to be removed from the body before
it can be
[healed](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Heal_Characters "wikilink").
What if the victim could alter his/her body chemistry to adapt to and
destroy the poison? That's exactly what this Psionic
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") lets the caster do. CELL
ADJUSTMENT is a [BIOCENTRIC Psionic
Spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Biocentric_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). It can only
be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on others if the caster first masters the art
of [BIO EMPATHY](/wiki/Bio_Empathy "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'cell adjustment'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
