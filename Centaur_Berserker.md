*A [centaur](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink")
[berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") looks up at you and says,
"Oh, dear [Werredan](/wiki/Werredan "wikilink"), not another! Sometimes, I
think that [Silvanus](/wiki/Centaur_Ranger "wikilink") must have asked every
willing and able-bodied forest-dweller from [the
Qanza](/wiki/Category%3A_Qanza_Prairie "wikilink") to
[Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink") to come and fight with us.
Oh, well, we'll probably need every single one of them, though, if we're
ever to reclaim [Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink") from those
cursed darklin. They seem to be everywhere in
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink") now and they just keep
expanding their presence day by day. Every
[centaur](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") who sets hoof outside of these walls must
now live in constant fear, it seems. All but our boldest must stay off
of [Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink")'s trails for fear of
their lives. Some wandering bands have taken shelter in [crumbling
underground ruins](/wiki/Category%3A_Ruins_Of_Westwood "wikilink") or
[abandoned villages](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Camp "wikilink") under constant
threat of attack, some [centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") have abandoned
their woodland dwelling-spots and fled to seek refuge with those few of
us who live full-time here in [this
hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink")... Well, I don't need to
tell you how tough it's been for all of us
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink")
[centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink"). Please forgive my feeling a bit angered
for a moment there about having to patch together some more
mattresses--really, I'm delighted that you answered
[Silvanus](/wiki/Centaur_Ranger "wikilink")' plea for help."*
