*A [centaur](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") [monk](Category%3A_Monks "wikilink")
notices your gaze and looks up from her activities. "I'm sorry, but all
is not ready to eat yet," she says to you. "But, if you have a moment,
would you kindly take those potatoes out to [Konk](/wiki/Konk "wikilink")?
Ever since his [elven](/wiki/High_Elves "wikilink") master Kemenvarno
disappeared, [Konk](/wiki/Konk "wikilink") has spent nearly all of his time
standing outside of our gate, waiting patiently for Kemenvarno to
return. Although his heart is even bigger than his stomach, poor
[Konk](/wiki/Konk "wikilink") must be famished by now. I sure hope that our
two missing fighters will turn up soon. I'm rather enjoying having such
an unusual number of guests staying here, including so many
non-[centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink"), despite all of the extra work that
it brings us, but I wish that it were under better circumstances. Well,
[Gorn](/wiki/Gorn "wikilink") willing, here's to happier days ahead!" And, at
that, she returns to preparing a hearty feast big enough to feed an
army.*

**Keywords:** centaur, monk.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
