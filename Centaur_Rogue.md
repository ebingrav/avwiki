*A [centaur](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") [rogue](Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink")
trots right up to you and says, "Well met, friend, and welcome to [our
secret hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink")! Since
[Leah](/wiki/Leah "wikilink") summoned [Silvermane](Silvermane "wikilink") and
those other [rangers](/wiki/Category%3A_Rangers "wikilink") to
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink") to help fight our darklin
foes, they have been doing wonderfully, but still not well enough to
turn the tide against them. It's about time that we
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink")
[centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") organized ourselves and fought to
reclaim our home. Once, I used to roam about
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink")'s paths without fear; but,
now, I've had to take refuge here in [our ancestral
hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink") like so many others of
our herd. I'm not very well versed in combat--in fact, few of us
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink")
[centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") are yet--but we've all been learning to
fight quickly here. I'm more of an actor, myself. Why, I once played the
notable role of Chironos Skytrotter in our amphitheater just over there
to the east. Oh, how I wish that you could gathered here with the rest
of our herd to see it!" And, having said that, he commences to recite a
few dramatic lines in [Faerie](/wiki/Faerie_Language "wikilink") for your
enjoyment before wandering away.*

**Keywords:** centaur, rogue.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** none.  
**Affects:** [alertness](/wiki/Alertness "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
