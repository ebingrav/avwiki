*A [centaur](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink")
[warrior](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") grins at you and says,
"Salutations, friend! Here amidst all of this peace and beauty, it can
be easy to forget what terror and destruction lie beyond these
protective walls. But it was so different not very long ago. My uncle,
[Achilles Firmflanks](/wiki/Older_Centaur_With_Dim_Eyes "wikilink"), retired
to [these ancient woods](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink") not long
before the darklin first showed themselves here, and he bade me come
here to help him in fighting them. He's now off somewhere helping to
defend some other [centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") and to protect some of
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink")'s endangered unicorns, while
I've been helping to get this militia here ready for battle. Most of
these [centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") know combat only from books, so
it's been a difficult project for me, but they've been very willing
pupils under present circumstances. Those
[rangers](/wiki/Category%3A_Rangers "wikilink") that [Leah](Leah "wikilink")
summoned have been doing phenomenally, but it hasn't been enough, and
it's become clear to this herd and to others in this region that they
need to join in this fight. Anyway, I'd be delighted to teach you
anything that I can; after all, [practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") is
essential to develop [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), and
[skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") are key to our victory."*
