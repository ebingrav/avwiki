*A magical being whose upper half consists of a human torso, and the
lower half being that of a horse. These mystical beings are superior to
[humans](/wiki/Humans "wikilink") in most ways. They are
[stronger](/wiki/Strength "wikilink"), can [travel
farther](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink"), are higher in
[intellect](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink") and [wisdom](Wisdom "wikilink"),
are [quicker](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink"), [hardier](Constitution "wikilink"),
and make better spellcasters. They excel at most anything, and are quite
powerful creatures, but do not advance in power quickly at all. Centaurs
are afforded limited protection in melee combat by their [thick
hide](/wiki/Racial_Armorhide "wikilink"), and can attack with their
[hooves](/wiki/Racial_Hoof "wikilink") as well.*

[Racial(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink"):

[Hoof](/wiki/Racial_Hoof "wikilink").
