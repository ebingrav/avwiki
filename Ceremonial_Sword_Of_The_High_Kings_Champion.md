------------------------------------------------------------------------

To get this piece of gear, you need to turn in 4 tickets to
[Killaris](/wiki/Killaris "wikilink"), who wanders around
[Thorngate](/wiki/Category%3AThorngate "wikilink"). The actual ticket that you
give him is the [Pile Of Green
Webbing](/wiki/Pile_Of_Green_Webbing "wikilink"), but you also need to have
the [Lab Memo From Jungle](/wiki/Lab_Memo_From_Jungle "wikilink"), the [Lab
Memo From Spires](/wiki/Lab_Memo_From_Spires "wikilink"), and the [Vial Of
Unfinished Portal Serum](/wiki/Vial_Of_Unfinished_Portal_Serum "wikilink") in
your inventory when you do so.

The [webbing](/wiki/Pile_Of_Green_Webbing "wikilink") loads on the [Kzinti
Emissary](/wiki/Kzinti_Emissary "wikilink"), a mob in [Astral
Invasion](/wiki/Category%3AAstral_Invasion "wikilink") (6th leg I think). See
[Gear In Astral Invasion](/wiki/Category%3AGear_In_Astral_Invasion "wikilink")
for more information about that run.

The other tickets that you need are all in the [Kzinti
Homeworld](/wiki/Category%3AKzinti_Homeworld "wikilink"). The [jungle
memo](/wiki/Lab_Memo_From_Jungle "wikilink") is on a [Kzinti
Priest](/wiki/Kzinti_Priest "wikilink") just north of the fountain that you
quaff from to enter the [Kzinti Spire Of
Knowledge](/wiki/Category%3AKzinti_Spire_Of_Knowledge "wikilink"). The
[serum](/wiki/Vial_Of_Unfinished_Portal_Serum "wikilink") and the [spires
memo](/wiki/Lab_Memo_From_Spires "wikilink") are on the [Kzinti
Scientist](/wiki/Kzinti_Scientist "wikilink") in the same room with a [Kzinti
Savage](/wiki/Kzinti_Savage "wikilink"). They are located in the northwest
corner of the first floor of the [Kzinti Spire Of
Knowledge](/wiki/Category%3AKzinti_Spire_Of_Knowledge "wikilink"). The [Kzinti
Savage](/wiki/Kzinti_Savage "wikilink") shows up as a "test subject" on scan.
See [Gear In Kzinti Spire Of
War](/wiki/Category%3AGear_In_Kzinti_Spire_Of_War "wikilink") for more details
on the spires run.

Currently the item has keyword quest, do not insure if you want to
transfer it.
