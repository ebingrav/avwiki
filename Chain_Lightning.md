*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") calls upon lightning to
inflict damage upon all enemies in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). Be aware that this may cause
[monsters](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") to attack that were not fighting
you before! Chain lightning decreases in [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink")
cost as you advance in [levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Lightning Bolt](/wiki/Lightning_Bolt "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'chain lightning'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used.
