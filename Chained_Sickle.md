`Modifies hit roll by 9 continuous.`  
`Modifies damage roll by 9 continuous.`

Object 'chained sickle' type weapon, extra flags glow magic anti-evil.  
Weight:10  
Highest Natural Max Damage: 50  
It is a chain weapon which does slashing damage.  
Mob: [Veiled Guardian](/wiki/Veiled_Guardian "wikilink")  
Area: [Veil of Ecstasy](/wiki/Category%3A_Veil_Of_Ecstasy "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Veil_Of_Ecstasy_Map "wikilink"))  
The short blade is weighted so it can be spun around on the end of the
long chain, making it an elegant long distance weapon, far more deadly
than a clumsy spear or pole-axe.  
There are four different [Veiled Guardians](/wiki/Veiled_Guardian "wikilink")
in [veil](/wiki/Category%3AVeil_Of_Ecstasy "wikilink"). They are all found
relatively early in the area.  
Chain weapons and double-headed weapons are the only weapons that you
can perform the skill double hit with. Because this weapon does slashing
damage, it is also sharpenable.
