Here's the steps to get the [Chainmail Morning
Breeze](/wiki/Chainmail_Morning_Breeze "wikilink"). I'm not sure of the
difficulty levels of the mobs, because I did this as a hero, and I also
use portal a lot. If someone wants to find the walking dirs, please do,
otherwise, here we go! (if someone wants to mess with format, please do)

**`1:` `Get` `black` `wasp` `sting` `from` `a` `pitch` `black` `wasp,`
`which` `is` `a` `portable` `mob.`**

**`2:` `Portal` `to` `Lhadvar` `Mother.`**  
`   then Give sting to Mother.`  
`   then Get small clay flask.`

**`3:` `Portal` `to` `Wounded` `Hunter.`**  
`   then Give flask to hunter.`  
`   then Get elabroate grass weaving.`

**`4:` `Portal` `to` `Osreva.`**  
`   then Give weaving to Osreva.`  
`   then Get (new) elabroate grass weaving.`

**`5:` `Portal` `to` `Khoridvar` `Clan` `Chief.`**  
`   then Give weaving to Clan Chief.`  
`   then Get small cone of black stone.`

**`6:` `Portal` `to` `Wounded` `Huntress.` `(note:` `ess` `on` `the`
`end` `of` `huntress!)`**  
`   then Give cone to Proud Huntress. (not the wounded one)`  
`   then Get (new) small cone of black stone. (this cone is a key fyi)`  
`   then Enter rift (in same room). (note: invis mob in the room that can detect hidden.  cannot detect invis.)`  
`   then Unlock Fourth. Open Fourth. Get Black Stone Spiral Fourth.`

**`7:` `Portal` `to` `Lhadvar` `Shaman` `(might` `need` `to` `move` `a`
`few` `north` `from` `the` `spiral` `room.` `The` `rift` `acts` `as` `a`
`portal` `element` `in` `the` `room).`**  
`   then 2n`  
`   then Kill T'dvar assassin (Td works), which is invis.`  
`   then Get Shimmering Metal Sphere from corpse.`  
`   then Here's where my walking directions get confused, so:`

**`7` `1/2:` `Portal` `to` `Osreva.`**  
`   then n w n d n e u s 4e 2s d e.`  
`   then unlock e, open e, e.`  
`   then get Gleaming Drop.`

**`8:` `Portal` `to` `Osreva.`**  
`   then Give Spiral to Osreva.`  
`   then Get (new) Black Stone Spiral.`  
`   then Unlock w, open w, w.`

**`9:` `Give` `drop` `to` `sarcophagus.`**  
`   then Open sarcophagus.`  
`   then Get foundation sarco.`  
`   then e.`  
`   then Give foundation osreva.`

WOW! There's the chainmail! ....
