*Taking a cue from the chameleon, the
[psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") have developed an easy
way to change the appearance and coloration of body, armor, and
equipment to blend in with the surroundings. This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is only effective if
its practitioner remains absolutely still.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [chameleon](/wiki/Chameleon "wikilink").

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is superfluous to
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") affected by
[Hide](/wiki/Hide "wikilink"). See [Hide Flag](Hide_Flag "wikilink") for more
information about its effects.
