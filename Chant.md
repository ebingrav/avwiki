*Chant allows [priests](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") to
[preach](/wiki/Preach "wikilink") any improvement spell (example:
[armor](/wiki/Armor_(spell) "wikilink"), [giant
strength](/wiki/Giant_Strength "wikilink")).*

Prerequisite(s): [Sermon](/wiki/Sermon "wikilink").

Syntax: [preach](/wiki/Preach "wikilink")
&lt;[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")&gt;.
