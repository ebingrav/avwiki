Comments
--------

This mob's wield is cursed, has an inventory flag, and is poisoned with
[Necrotia](/wiki/Necrotia "wikilink"). Immaculate, detonate, & shatterstrike
are your friend when facing these guys.

Also note that he casts [Shard Storm](/wiki/Shard_Storm "wikilink"). This is
particularly rough on small casters when you enter the det portion of
the [Unravelling](/wiki/Category%3AUnravelling "wikilink").
