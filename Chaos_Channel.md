*As everyone knows, a magic user's [combat
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Harm_Mobs "wikilink") channel
internal energy into deadly elemental attacks. Experienced combat mages
are capable of harnessing the energy of the chaos of battle and using it
as a fuel source for these attacks. This can effectively halve the
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") cost of these spells, although the
technique is very exacting. The caster must be involved in combat, but
not under direct attack. The spell must be a
[Hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") or lower [level](Level "wikilink")
attack spell using internal energy. The technique cannot assist in
casting spells based on mental, kinetic, Demonic or Godly power, or in
casting spells altered by other magical skills, such as
[surge](/wiki/Surge "wikilink") or [quicken](/wiki/Quicken "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Quicken](/wiki/Quicken "wikilink"),
[Surge](/wiki/Surge "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is invaluable for
[mage](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") [hitters](Hitters "wikilink"),
allowing them to roughly halve their [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink")
usage during runs.
