*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") allows a magic user to
store one of several types of charges in a shield
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). When an
[attacker](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") strikes the shield, the charge
will go off, harming them. There is a chance, dependent on the innate
magical skill of the user, that the charge will backfire on them. Number
and type of charges can be seen by [identifying](/wiki/Identify "wikilink") a
charged [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). Damage to the shield can
be reduced by [Consecrate](/wiki/Consecrate "wikilink"), or eliminated by
[Major Ward](/wiki/Major_Ward "wikilink") or the appropriate elemental
[ward](/wiki/Ward "wikilink"). Below are the types of charges. The stronger
ones are harder to cast. A shield cannot take a charge unless it is
higher [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") than listed.*

`CHARGE TYPE      `[`LEVEL`](/wiki/Level "wikilink")`   PREREQUISITE     INFO`  
`Venom            0       `[`Poison`](/wiki/Poison_(spell) "wikilink")`           No damage to shield/foe, +poison`  
`Flash            0       `[`Blindness`](/wiki/Blindness "wikilink")`        No damage to shield/foe, +blindness`  
`Jolt             10      `[`Chain`
`Lightning`](/wiki/Chain_Lightning "wikilink")`  Small damage to shield and foe`  
`Frostbite        30      `[`Icestrike`](/wiki/Icestrike "wikilink")`        Moderate damage to shield and foe`  
`Flametongue      51      `[`Fireball`](/wiki/Fireball "wikilink")`         Large damage to shield and foe`

Prerequisite(s): [Enchant Armor](/wiki/Enchant_Armor "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'charge shield' <shield> <charge type>.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is highly useful to
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") [mage](Category%3A_Mages "wikilink")
soloers and [tanks](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink") in maximizing their overall combat
damage. Even mage [hitters](/wiki/Hitters "wikilink") or
[healers](/wiki/Healers "wikilink") who get hit only occasionally may enjoy a
charged shield's added damage on those occasions.
