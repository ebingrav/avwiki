This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") typically means that an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") has been
[donated](/wiki/Donate "wikilink"), although some
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") (such as the [Antharian Signet
Ring](/wiki/Antharian_Signet_Ring "wikilink")) load with this
[flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may not
[sacrifice](/wiki/Sacrifice "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") with this
[flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") and must
[practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") [Fence](Fence "wikilink") in order to
[sell](/wiki/Sell "wikilink") them.

See also [Donate](/wiki/Donate "wikilink").
