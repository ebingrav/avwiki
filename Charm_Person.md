*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), if successful, causes the
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") to [follow](Follow "wikilink") you
and to take orders from you. Use [ORDER](/wiki/Order "wikilink") to order your
[charmed](/wiki/Charmed_Flag "wikilink") followers. You are responsible for
the actions of your followers. Conversely, other people who attack your
followers will be penalized as if they attacked you. Please do not spam
any [channels](/wiki/Category%3A_Channels "wikilink") with your
[charmed](/wiki/Charmed_Flag "wikilink") followers. Be careful where you leave
your followers; leading high-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink")
[creatures](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") to
low-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") [areas](Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") or
moving a common [portal](/wiki/Portals "wikilink") point is highly frowned
upon. If you [quit](/wiki/Quit "wikilink"), your
[charmed](/wiki/Charmed_Flag "wikilink") followers will disappear.*

Prerequisite(s): [Sleep](/wiki/Sleep_(spell) "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'charm person'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") has limited practical value,
though it can be highly entertaining for some.
[Charmed](/wiki/Charmed_Flag "wikilink") [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")
will display a [(Charmed) "flag"](/wiki/Charmed_Flag "wikilink").
