Kill [Clockwork Homunculus](/wiki/Clockwork_Homunculus "wikilink") and get the
[Heavy Silver Key](/wiki/Heavy_Silver_Key "wikilink"). Directions: pp clock
homun.

Get the [Runed Tower Key](/wiki/Runed_Tower_Key "wikilink"). Directions: pp
[Vymm](/wiki/Vymm "wikilink"), 2s requires the [Heavy Silver
Key](/wiki/Heavy_Silver_Key "wikilink")

Kill the [Empty Servitor](/wiki/Empty_Servitor "wikilink") to spawn the
[Silent Slice Of Void](/wiki/Silent_Slice_Of_Void "wikilink"). Then, kill the
[Silent Slice Of Void](/wiki/Silent_Slice_Of_Void "wikilink") and get the
[Cold Iron Master Key](/wiki/Cold_Iron_Master_Key "wikilink"). Directions: pp
[Vymm](/wiki/Vymm "wikilink"), su3eu, requires the [Runed Tower
Key](/wiki/Runed_Tower_Key "wikilink").

Go up from the [Empty Servitor](/wiki/Empty_Servitor "wikilink")/[Silent Slice
of Void](/wiki/Silent_Slice_of_Void "wikilink") room. Directions: pp
[Vymm](/wiki/Vymm "wikilink"), su3e2u, requires [Cold Iron Master
Key](/wiki/Cold_Iron_Master_Key "wikilink").
