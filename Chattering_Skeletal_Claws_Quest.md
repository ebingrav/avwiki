This is a quest that starts in [Sun God
Altar](/wiki/Category%3ASun_God_Altar "wikilink") but *may* include other
areas. Speak to [Ruddloaf Mortlock](/wiki/Ruddloaf_Mortlock "wikilink") and
see if he has anything for you to do.

Once completed it may award the [Chattering Skeletal
Claws](/wiki/Chattering_Skeletal_Claws "wikilink").
