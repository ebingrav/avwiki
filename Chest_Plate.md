*A finely crafted chest plate, this
[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink") was designed to deflect glancing
blows, requiring a skilled piercing blow to get through its fine
defenses. Unfortunately, this benefit is counteracted due to the weight
of the materials necessary to make such a piece of
[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink").*

[`Armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 4-6.`  
`Modifies `[`armor`
`class`](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")` by -15 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`dexterity`](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink")` by -2 continuous.`
