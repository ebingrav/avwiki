[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will cause any
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") that they see either
within [forbidden rooms](/wiki/Forbidden_Rooms "wikilink") or engaging in
unlawful activity to be designated as [outlaws](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink").
They will also summon [townguards](/wiki/Townguard_Mobs "wikilink") to any
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") in which they see an
[outlawed](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink")
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").
