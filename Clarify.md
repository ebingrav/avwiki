*The clarifying Light of [Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink") clears
[blindness](/wiki/Blindness "wikilink") and some mental impediments. It may
need to be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") multiple times to be fully effective.
The clarifying affect can also raise [alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink")
slightly.*

Prerequisites: [Absolve](/wiki/Absolve "wikilink"), [Via
Elimo](/wiki/Via_Elimo "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'clarify'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
