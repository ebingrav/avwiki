This is one of the quests done in [Rim](/wiki/Category%3ARim "wikilink").

Goal is to gather various lore in form of writing that corresponds with
the four sea deities:

-   Muo Wishene - the whale
-   Crattenos - the shark
-   Lumoloth - the kraken
-   Mnephrikos - lord of the dead

Tickets are difficult to find, each requires either a small subquest of
its own or is hidden in relatively inaccessible location. Once gathered,
give them to the questgiver on the other side of the fog.

Follow the plotline to Gorn's Anchor, and grab the award: the [Aura Of
The Storyteller](/wiki/Aura_Of_The_Storyteller "wikilink") and the [Compendium
Of Island Mythology](/wiki/Compendium_Of_Island_Mythology "wikilink").
