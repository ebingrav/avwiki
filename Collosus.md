*Pure rock, pure strength, Collosus rules by force and intimidation.
None can challenge his power yet he is not the absolute ruler of the
valley for he has yet to defeat [the power liche
Azric](/wiki/Power_Liche_Azric "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** collosus.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 95.  
**Scan Description:** A towering stone golem stalks the valley, looking
for you!  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** [very
agressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink"),
[protection (good/evil?)](/wiki/Protection_Good "wikilink").
