*This [ability](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") focuses your energies on
defense, tapping your [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") to allow you to
more adroitly avoid the blows of your enemies.*

Prerequisite(s): [Meditation](/wiki/Meditation "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'concentrate'.
