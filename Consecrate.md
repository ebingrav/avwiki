*A [cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") can
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") to consecrate
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), blessing them with the [ability
to resist
damage](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Enhance_Gear "wikilink"). By
praying successfully, this may occur and [Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink")
shall grant their request. NOTE: Consecrate will raise [item
level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") by 2 and if you fail, it may curse the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") instead.*

Prerequisite(s): [Bless](/wiki/Bless "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'consecrate'
&lt;[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.

See also [Bless Flag](/wiki/Bless_Flag "wikilink").
