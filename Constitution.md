*This attribute controls how quickly a
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") can heal. Higher
constitution means quicker
[hp](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink")/[moves](Move_Points "wikilink") recovery,
AND a higher [HP](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") gain when leveling.*

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may wear
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Con_Gear "wikilink") to increase their constitutions.
