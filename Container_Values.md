[Container](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have four
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **Weight Capacity:** This value determines how much weight this
    [container](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") may hold at most.

<!-- -->

-   **Accessibility:** This value determines how easily this
    [container](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") may be accessed; it
    may be openable/closeable, pickproof, closed, locked, or some
    combination of these.

<!-- -->

-   **[Key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink"):** This value determines which
    [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink"), if any, may be used to
    [unlock](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") this
    [container](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **Trap Type:** This value determines which type of trap this
    [container](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") has, which may be
    either none, blade, detonator, electric, poison darts, poison gas,
    poison needle, spores, or venom.
    [Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who
    [practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") [Disarm
    Traps](/wiki/Disarm_Traps "wikilink") may [inspect](Inspect "wikilink")
    [containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") to determine if they
    are trapped and then use [trap-disarming
    kits](/wiki/Category%3A_Trap-Disarming_Kits "wikilink") to
    [dismantle](/wiki/Dismantle "wikilink") any traps that they may find.

See also [Containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink").
