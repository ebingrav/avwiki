At the moment we are establishing some basic layout for organizing
information. If you have something to add, but aren't sure where it
belongs, just do your best. If we feel that the information is relevant
somewhere else, we'll add a link.

We have some simple guidelines in place for naming new pages. Omit any
articles ("the" "a" "an") at the beginning of a name. Also, capitalize
each word in a multi-word title.

Placing new pages in appropriate categories is very helpful. Category
naming follows the same rules as page naming, but use plural nouns when
appropriate. [Midgaard Mobs](/wiki/Midgaard_Mobs "wikilink"),
[Tiers](/wiki/Tiers "wikilink") and [Hero Tank
Gear](/wiki/Hero_Tank_Gear "wikilink") are some example categories. More
specific categories are better places to put new pages. Note that when
you're naming a Category, you have to precede its name with "Category:".

Other than that we don't have any big secrets to making nice Wiki pages.
To edit a page, click on the edit link at the top of the page. From
there the Wiki software does a good job of explaining the editing
features. You can also use simple HTML to format a page if you'd like.
If you can't figure something out, try looking
[here](http://meta.wikimedia.org/wiki/Help:Editing)
