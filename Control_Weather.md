*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") makes the
[weather](/wiki/Weather "wikilink") either better or worse. It is often useful
in combination with such [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") as
[call lightning](/wiki/Call_Lightning "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Call Lightning](/wiki/Call_Lightning "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'control weather' better.  
Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'control weather' worse.  
This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is practically
never used, though it is a prerequisite for [Acid
Rain](/wiki/Acid_Rain "wikilink"), which is very useful to
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink").
