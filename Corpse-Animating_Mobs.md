[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will animate corpses around
them. When animated, these corpses' contents become their inventories;
they will then attempt to wear any [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink")
that they may be carrying.
