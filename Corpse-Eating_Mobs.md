[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will eat certain corpses
(either any player corpses or any [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")
corpses but never both) that lay on the ground around them, leaving
these corpses' contents, if any, laying on the ground.

See also [Corpse-Sacrificing Mobs](/wiki/Corpse-Sacrificing_Mobs "wikilink").
