[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will sacrifice any
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") corpses that lay on the ground around
them, leaving these corpses' contents, if any, laying on the ground.

See also [Corpse-Eating Mobs](/wiki/Corpse-Eating_Mobs "wikilink").
