## Comments

points](Move_Points.md "wikilink"), may find this candy very handy and
may want to keep a bagful of it stocked.

[races](:Category:_Races.md "wikilink") who have not yet
[practiced](Practice.md "wikilink") [refresh](Refresh.md "wikilink") may
also want to keep some of this candy on hand.
