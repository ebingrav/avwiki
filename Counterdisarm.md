*While blocking with [crane style](Crane_Style "wikilink"), a quick
[Monk](:Category:_Monks "wikilink") can attempt to
[disarm](Disarm "wikilink") the enemy. This counter is a little draining
on the [Monk](:Category:_Monks "wikilink")'s defenses.*

Prerequisites: [Counter](Counter "wikilink"),
[Disarm](Disarm "wikilink").

Syntax: [ctr](Ctr "wikilink") disarm.
