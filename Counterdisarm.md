*While blocking with [crane style](/wiki/Crane_Style "wikilink"), a quick
[Monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") can attempt to
[disarm](/wiki/Disarm "wikilink") the enemy. This counter is a little draining
on the [Monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink")'s defenses.*

Prerequisites: [Counter](/wiki/Counter "wikilink"),
[Disarm](/wiki/Disarm "wikilink").

Syntax: [ctr](/wiki/Ctr "wikilink") disarm.
