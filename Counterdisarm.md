*While blocking with [crane style](Crane_Style.md "wikilink"), a quick
[Monk](:Category:_Monks.md "wikilink") can attempt to
[disarm](Disarm.md "wikilink") the enemy. This counter is a little
draining on the [Monk](:Category:_Monks.md "wikilink")'s defenses.*

Prerequisites: [Counter](Counter.md "wikilink"),
[Disarm](Disarm.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [ctr](Ctr.md "wikilink") disarm.
