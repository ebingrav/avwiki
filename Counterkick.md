*While the hand blocks, the foot can retaliate. Like standard counters,
counterkicks follow successful [crane style](/wiki/Crane_Style "wikilink")
blocks.*

Prerequisite(s): [Counter](/wiki/Counter "wikilink"), [Kick](Kick "wikilink").

Syntax: [ctr](/wiki/Ctr "wikilink") kick.
