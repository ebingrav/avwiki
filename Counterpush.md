*The result of a successful counterpush is quite similar to the shield
[bash](/wiki/Bash "wikilink") [skill](Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"); the foe
is knocked down, off balance and less able to defend. This counter is
somewhat draining on the [Monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink")'s
defenses.*

Prerequisite(s): [Bash](/wiki/Bash "wikilink"), [Counter](Counter "wikilink").

Syntax: [ctr](/wiki/Ctr "wikilink") push.
