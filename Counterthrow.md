While blocking with [crane style](/wiki/Crane_Style "wikilink"), a
[Monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") with this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") can use his foe's momentum to
hurl them groundward. This counter is somewhat draining on the
[Monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink")'s defenses.

Prerequisite(s): [Counter](/wiki/Counter "wikilink"), [Toss](Toss "wikilink").

Syntax: [ctr](/wiki/Ctr "wikilink") throw.
