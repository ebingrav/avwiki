*While using countertrip, a [Monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink")
redirects the enemy's momentum, and trips them as they become
unbalanced. This counter is a little draining on the
[Monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink")'s defenses.*

Prerequisite(s): [Counter](/wiki/Counter "wikilink"), [Trip](Trip "wikilink").

Syntax: [ctr](/wiki/Ctr "wikilink") trip.
