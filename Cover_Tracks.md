*Those adept at following tracks learn a trick or two when called upon
to obscure their own. The use of this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") can throw off pursuers who are
following the user after a battle. The odds of success are based upon
the victims' [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") and
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"). Beasts and
[Psions](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") are particularly hard to
lose. It only works on enemies who are hunting a player, not currently
fighting, and in the same [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") (but not
the same [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink")) as their target.*

Prerequisite(s): [Woodcraft](/wiki/Woodcraft "wikilink").

Syntax: cover tracks.
