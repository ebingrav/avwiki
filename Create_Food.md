*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") creates a Magic Meatloaf or
a cookie, which you or anyone else can [eat](/wiki/Eat "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'create food'.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") has no practical
value at present, as [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") no
longer suffer from hunger and thirst, though it may provide some degree
of amusement.

See also [Create Food Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Create_Food_Gear "wikilink").
