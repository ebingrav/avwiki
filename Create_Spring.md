*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") brings forth a magical
spring from the ground, which has the same properties as a
[fountain](/wiki/Fountains "wikilink"). However, as thirst has been banished
from the realm, this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") assists only
the creatures of Avatar.*

Prerequisite(s): [Create Water](/wiki/Create_Water "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'create spring'.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") has no practical
value at present, as [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") no
longer suffer from hunger and thirst, though it may provide some degree
of amusement.
