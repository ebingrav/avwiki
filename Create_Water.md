*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") replenishes a [drink
container](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink") with water. However,
as thirst has been banished from the realm, this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") assists only the creatures of
Avatar.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'create water'
&lt;[drink-container](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink")&gt;.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") has no practical
value at present, as [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") no
longer suffer from hunger and thirst.
