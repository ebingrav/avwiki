Comments
--------

At first glance, an odd weapon, not fully mana gear, and again not
entirely hitgear, this weapon can nevertheless be
[runed](/wiki/Rune "wikilink") with AC runes to get a possibly good
[bladedancer](/wiki/Category%3ABladedancers "wikilink") tank wield.
