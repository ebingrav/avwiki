` Modifies hit roll by 3 continuous.`  
` Modifies damage roll by 3 continuous.`  
` Modifies armor class by -20 continuous.`  
` Modifies moves by 100 continuous.`

Level: 41  
Slot: Head  
Base: 10  
Flags: magic  
Area: [Gem of Destiny](/wiki/Category%3A_Gem_Of_Destiny "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Gem_Of_Destiny_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: [Snakeman](/wiki/Snakeman "wikilink")  
Note: This gear is reboot only. If you have access to the spell [Locate
Object](/wiki/Locate_Object "wikilink"), try locating it before you head out
to obtain it.

Once you get inside the area, which is connected to the [Ruins Of
Reveria](/wiki/Category%3ARuins_Of_Reveria "wikilink"), find the Keymaster.
Kill him and take his key shaped sword, and give it to the Gatekeeper.
This will open up a portal. Enter the portal and head north until
there's only two directions you can go: north, and east (I think). Head
east. The mob with the crown will be at the end of this path.
