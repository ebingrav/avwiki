*This [spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink") cures damage on the target
[character](:Category:_Characters "wikilink"). The
[level](Level "wikilink") of this [spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink")
on your [slist](Slist "wikilink") gives a hint at its power of healing.*

Prerequisite(s): [Cure Serious](Cure_Serious "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'cure critical'
\<[character](:Category:_Characters "wikilink")\>.

This [hit-point](Hit_Points "wikilink")-healing
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink") is third (and third most
efficient) of five that both [clerics](:Category:_Clerics "wikilink")
and [priests](:Category:_Priests "wikilink") may (and should)
[learn](Practice "wikilink") between [level](Level "wikilink") 1 and
[level](Level "wikilink") 50. Many other
[classes](:Category:_Classes "wikilink") may
[learn](Practice "wikilink") it at [hero](:Category:_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](:Category:_Tiers "wikilink") and will find it useful as well.
When [cast](Cast "wikilink") by
[clerics](:Category:_Clerics "wikilink"), it normally heals 75 [hit
points](Hit_Points "wikilink") but this amount may be altered somewhat
by their respective choices of [worship](Worship "wikilink"), if any.

See also [Cure Critical Gear](:Category:_Cure_Critical_Gear "wikilink").
