*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") cures the target of one
[affect](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") of a disease, such as virus or plague.
Since both diseases have multiple [affects](/wiki/Affects "wikilink"), it may
take a couple of [casts](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") to cure entirely.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'cure disease'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rather valuable for all
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").

See also [Cure Disease Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Cure_Disease_Gear "wikilink").
