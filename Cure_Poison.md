*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") cures one
[affect](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") of a
[poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") per [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"). It
will take multiple [casts](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") to cure
[poisons](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") with multiple
[affects](/wiki/Affects "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Detect Poison](/wiki/Detect_Poison "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'cure poison'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rather valuable for all
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").

See also [Cure Poison Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Cure_Poison_Gear "wikilink").
