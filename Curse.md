*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") reduces the
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")'s [to-hit
roll](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink") and [save versus
spell](/wiki/Saving_Throw "wikilink"). It also renders the character unclean
in the eyes of the [gods](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") and
unable to [RECALL](/wiki/Recall "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'curse'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is practically
never used.

See also [Gear That Applies
'Curse'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Curse' "wikilink") and [Curse
Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Curse_Gear "wikilink").
