[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") prevent players from entering
them using [teleport](/wiki/Teleport "wikilink"), [portal](Portal "wikilink"),
and [nexus](/wiki/Nexus "wikilink") [spells](Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"),
as well as from exiting them using either [recall](/wiki/Recall "wikilink") or
[sanctum](/wiki/Sanctum "wikilink")
[commands](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink").
