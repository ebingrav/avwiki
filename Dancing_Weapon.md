*This ability must be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") after combat has already
begun. When [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"), your main
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") flies out of your hand and
attacks your current target. Generally, it does as much damage as a
round of combat in which you did not miss. The weapon MIGHT return to
your hand when the spell is complete. Or it MIGHT fall to the ground,
where it will be subject to [scavengers](/wiki/Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink"),
[stompers](/wiki/Stomping_Mobs "wikilink"), and the infamous imp... There are
no reimbursements for [weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") that you
lose with this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")...*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'dancing weapon'.
