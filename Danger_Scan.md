*Along with an increased awareness of one's surroundings sometimes comes
a vague intuition of impending doom. This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") puts those subconscious warnings
to work and gives the caster early warning of nearby danger.
[Casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") Danger Scan is a lot like
[scanning](/wiki/Scan "wikilink") normally, except that the caster will be
able to detect the [aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") tendencies
of certain creatures in the surrounding
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). He will not be able to tell WHICH
creatures are dangerous; all he will glean is a general sense of peril
coming from any directions which harbor
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") creatures.*

Prerequisite(s): [Heighten Senses](/wiki/Heighten_Senses "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'danger scan'.
