*[Psions](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") can sense
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") tendencies a mile away. By the
time they reach high [levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink"), they become able to
constantly maintain a state of alertness similar to the affects of the
[danger scan](/wiki/Danger_Scan "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). Any time a
[psion](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") [scans](/wiki/Scan "wikilink")
after [practicing](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), it will be as if he had
[cast](Cast "wikilink") [Danger Scan](/wiki/Danger_Scan "wikilink"). This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is
[class-defining](/wiki/Category%3A_Class_Definers "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Danger Scan](/wiki/Danger_Scan "wikilink").

Syntax: [scan](/wiki/Scan "wikilink").
