*This dark gray metal [key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") bears the sigil
of [Werredan](Werredan "wikilink") carved deeply into the metal. It is
warm in your hands. It is impossible to not know the
[key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") belongs to the temple of
[Werredan](Werredan "wikilink").*

**Keyword(s):** key, gray, dark.  
**[Level(s)](Object_Level "wikilink"):** 29-31.  
**[Type](:Category:_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](:Category:_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") unlocks the door east of
Werredan's Temple Secretary's Office, which leads to Werredan's Treasury
and Vault.
