`Modifies damage roll by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies armor class by -8 continuous.`  
`Modifies hp by 30 continuous.`

Slot: Arms  
Level: 45  
Base: 11  
Weight: 2  
Area: [Githzerai Tower](/wiki/Category%3A_Githzerai_Tower "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Githzerai_Tower_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: [Githzerai Swordsman](/wiki/Githzerai_Swordsman "wikilink")  
  
Because this item has good ac and hp bonuses, it might work well for low
hp casters looking to solo...  
*Make sure you are invis, or mobs will aggie you.*  
Port [Ulrich](/wiki/Ulrich "wikilink") and go, \[n\], 2n, w, 4n, e, 2n, w, 2u,
then search around for Swordsmen.  
Item is invisible.
