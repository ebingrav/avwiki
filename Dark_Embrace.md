*With a simple incantation, you can summon an otherworldly embrace of
darkness which will protect you from the harshness of the sun. This is
most helpful to those who [cannot bear the
sunlight](/wiki/Racial_Nosun "wikilink"), like underground
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'dark embrace'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
