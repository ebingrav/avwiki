This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") prevents
[lord](/wiki/Lord "wikilink") [clerics](Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") from
[casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") a certain
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") on
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that have it. It can be added
via the [Violation](/wiki/Violation "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").

See also [Violation](/wiki/Violation "wikilink").
