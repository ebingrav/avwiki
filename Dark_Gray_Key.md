*This dark gray metal [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") bears the
sigil of [Werredan](Werredan.md "wikilink") carved deeply into the
metal. It is warm in your hands. It is impossible to not know the
[key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") belongs to the temple of
[Werredan](Werredan.md "wikilink").*

**Keyword(s):** key, gray, dark.  
**[Level(s)](Object_Level.md "wikilink"):** 29-31.  
**[Type](:Category:_Object_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](Object_Slots.md "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](Object_Quality.md "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](Object_Weight.md "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](:Category:_Object_Flags.md "wikilink"):** none.  

## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") unlocks the door east of
Werredan's Temple Secretary's Office, which leads to Werredan's Treasury
and Vault.
