*This dark gray metal [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") bears the sigil
of [Werredan](/wiki/Werredan "wikilink") carved deeply into the metal. It is
warm in your hands. It is impossible to not know the
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") belongs to the temple of
[Werredan](/wiki/Werredan "wikilink").*

**Keyword(s):** key, gray, dark.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 29-31.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks the door east of
Werredan's Temple Secretary's Office, which leads to Werredan's Treasury
and Vault.
