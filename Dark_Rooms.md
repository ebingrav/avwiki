[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") prevent players within them
from seeing anything without [wearing](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") a
[light](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink").

See also [Permdark Rooms](/wiki/Permdark_Rooms "wikilink").
