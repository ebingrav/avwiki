<b>Bases:</b> 10-12  
<b>Levels:</b> 51-52  
<b>Weight:</b> ?  
<b>Area:</b> [The Great Divide](/wiki/Category%3A_Great_Divide "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Great_Divide_Map "wikilink"))  
<b>Mob:</b> [Grantus](/wiki/Grantus "wikilink") or
[Blackhand](/wiki/Blackhand "wikilink") or [Arcanthra](/wiki/Arcanthra "wikilink")  
Modifies armor class by -10 continuous.

To get this piece, one must venture into [The Great
Divide](/wiki/Category%3A_Great_Divide "wikilink"). There are 3 mobs that
carry this item and they can all be found in the north-westish part of
the area. [Arcanthra the Black](/wiki/Arcanthra "wikilink") and [Grantus,
dwarven military strategist](/wiki/Grantus "wikilink") are in the same room,
and of all 3 mobs [Arcanthra](/wiki/Arcanthra "wikilink") is the toughest
(also sancted). [Blackhand, master-at-arms](/wiki/Blackhand "wikilink") is
close by these guys also.  
*Note: Be careful because there are lots of aggie dwarves along the
way.*
