*The [evil creature](/wiki/Voracious_Doppelganger "wikilink") responsible for
[Centaur Hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink")'s disappearing
fighters happens to be carrying a [valuable family
heirloom](/wiki/Centaurish_Family_Heirloom "wikilink") from its latest victim.
A [member of this victim's family](/wiki/Centaur_Paladin "wikilink") would be
very grateful to receive it...*
