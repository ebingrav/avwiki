A very basic sling is here, a leather patch with a cord on either side.
The sling comes with a convenient and apparently unlimited supply of
rocks.  
Object 'david's sling leather rocks ' type bow, extra flags magic.  
Weight: 3  
Level Range: 50 - 52  
Object Quality: 50 hps  
Damage is 18 to 45 (average 31).  
Modifies hit roll by 20 continuous.

Area: [Veil of Ecstasy](/wiki/Category%3A_Veil_Of_Ecstasy "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Veil_Of_Ecstasy_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: [The Giants' Nightmare](/wiki/The_Giants'_Nightmare "wikilink")  
Notes: To get this sling, you have to enter the window where
[Eschard](/wiki/Eschard "wikilink") is. Wander around there and look for the
little guy waving his sling around.
