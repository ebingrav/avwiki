*A DEATH FIELD is a life-sapping region of chaotic psionic energy.
Essentially, it applies the same destructive kinetic energies in the
[Detonate](/wiki/Detonate "wikilink") [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
to living tissue. Although this energy is not godly in origin, usage of
this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") may attract the attention of
the god of death, [Bhyss](/wiki/Bhyss "wikilink"), who has been known to have
a little fun with those who attract his attention.*

Prerequisite(s): [Ultrablast](/wiki/Ultrablast "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'death field'.
