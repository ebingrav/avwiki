*A bargain with death, if you can comprehend, takes place with the
magical death shroud you create. While this shroud is upon you, if you
die, your loss of [experience](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") will be
kept minimal. Consider it death insurance to ease your spirit through
the whole ugly process of being killed. (This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
[race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") dependent and will not reduce your
[experience](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") loss to 0.)*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'death shroud'.
