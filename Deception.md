*Think you have a pretty good [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")?
What if it turned into a rattlesnake during a fight? With the Deception
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), you can make your victim think a
piece of [equipment](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") has turned into
something dangerous, so they try to [remove](/wiki/Remove "wikilink") or
[drop](/wiki/Drop "wikilink") it. You can't control what
[equipment](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") is affected, it's random.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'deception'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
