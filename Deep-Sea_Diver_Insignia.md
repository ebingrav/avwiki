This insignia means you have completed the [Deep-Sea Diver
Quest](/wiki/Deep-Sea_Diver_Quest "wikilink") in [Exploration
Outpost](/wiki/Category%3AExploration_Outpost "wikilink") on
[Eragora](/wiki/Category%3AEragora "wikilink").

This insignia grants either +3% melee power, or +3% spell power, and
lasts throughout Hero.
