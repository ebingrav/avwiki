Also known as svirfneblin, a breed of gnome that has grown to shun the
surface world and has adapted to a subterranean lifestyle. They are
short in stature, yet more muscular than humans, have slightly better
reflexes than humans, and, like their surface- dwelling cousins, tend to
be a little wiser.

Deep gnomes tend to be a little hardier than some races, and make
slightly above average spellcasters as well. Deep gnomes also tend to be
resistant to magic effects.

Deep gnomes live underground, and have developed the ability to see in
complete darkness. They can all sneak up on their opponents as well as
hide from them rather well. Deep Gnomes are subterranean dwellers, and
cannot function very well in direct sunlight. When sunlight hits their
skin they lose the ability to heal both physical health and magical
energy, and experience excruciating pain as well.
