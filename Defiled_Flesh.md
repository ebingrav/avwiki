*By offering their very flesh to their unseen Demonic masters,
[Sorcerors](/wiki/Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink") receive an increase in
maximum health. All
[healing](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Heal_Characters "wikilink")
they receive is halved.*

Prerequisite(s): [Demonic Intent](/wiki/Demonic_Intent "wikilink"),
[Torment](/wiki/Torment "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'defiled flesh'.
