*The small pieces of metal that make up this delicately woven necklace
are extremely thin, yet neither scratch, indentation nor any other sort
of damage is visible. At the front the tiny squares are arranged into
something like a reptilian shape. It is impossible to make any
assumptions about the age of the ornament, but it is definitely not of a
common style.*
