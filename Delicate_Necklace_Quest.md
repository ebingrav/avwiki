[Portal](/wiki/Teleport "wikilink") to [a huge troll](Huge_Troll "wikilink")
and go e,n.

-   Get a [a torn scroll](/wiki/Torn_Scroll "wikilink") from the pile
    of corpses.

<!-- -->

-   Portal to [Osreva](/wiki/Osreva,_King_Of_The_Odevar "wikilink") and
    [give](/wiki/Give "wikilink") a torn scroll to Osreva and recieve a [a
    sealed letter](/wiki/Sealed_Letter "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Portal to [Voinar](/wiki/Clan_Chief_Voinar "wikilink"). Give a sealed
    letter to Voinar. He will give you a [a folded
    note](/wiki/Folded_Note "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Portal to Osreva and give him the folded note. Receive a [a small
    piece of metal](/wiki/Small_Piece_Of_Metal "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Portal to Voinar and give a small piece of metal to Voinar and
    receive [a seal from solid
    durrite](/wiki/Seal_From_Solid_Durrite "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Portal back to Osreva. Give a him the seal and receive [a delicate
    necklace](/wiki/Delicate_Necklace "wikilink").
