*The demon scepter is a evil-looking item. [Demons](/wiki/Demons "wikilink")
carved of bronze adore the sides of the
[staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink"), leering at you. The
[staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") is made of an ancient oak, and
looks quite potent.*

`Has 8-9 charges at level 40 '`[`fire`
`breath`](/wiki/Fire_Breath "wikilink")`'.`  
`Faintly lowers resistance to water continuous`  
`Insignificantly increases resistance to fire continuous`

**Keyword(s):** Demon, Scepter.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 50-52.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <held>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 60 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 5 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):**
[evil](/wiki/Evil_Flag "wikilink"), [magic](Magic_Flag "wikilink").
