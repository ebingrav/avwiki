*Little golden stars twinkle evilly in the deep blue surface of this
gem.*  

Comments
--------

You need to see the demonic crafter in the area north of Bo'Vul. You
need the [Blood-Mithril Key](/wiki/Blood-Mithril_Key "wikilink") from Bo'Vul
to get inside. The crafter's near the end of the area. Giving the
crafter the [demonic gem](/wiki/Demonic_Gemstones "wikilink") and [perfect
gem](/wiki/Perfect_Gemstones "wikilink") allows him to modify things.

LOOK MENU to see what he has to offer.

-   [Silver Chestplate](/wiki/Silver_Chestplate "wikilink")
    -   Robust - Hp bonus to player
    -   Stealth - Sneak on the item

<!-- -->

-   [Black Armband](/wiki/Black_Armband "wikilink")
    -   Robust - Hp bonus to player
    -   Stealth - Sneak on the item
    -   Fortify - Extra ac bonus on item

<!-- -->

-   [Assassin's Armband](/wiki/Assassin's_Armband "wikilink")
    -   Weaponize - Damage boost and sneak but loss of archery mods
    -   Specialize - Hitroll boost and archer modifications

<!-- -->

-   [Watermark Tattoo](/wiki/Watermark_Tattoo "wikilink") - This will be bound
    to the player. These give up to 24 hours of the effect but end
    at midnight. There is a 5 day exhaust period (shows up as a "water
    stain" insignia).
    -   Defensive - Changes the affinity so player can receive
        werrebocler 1 day in 5
    -   Offensive - Changes the affinity so player can receive damroll
        bonus 1 day in 5

<!-- -->

-   [Silver Sword](/wiki/Silver_Sword "wikilink") - One from commander only
    -   Brutish - Reforges the sword into a double hitting staff
    -   Divine - Reforges the sword into a small scepter
