Black hearted, and red skinned, these evil creatures are big, and mean.
Although they are not as spry as the smaller imps and demonseeds, they
are nothing to be discounted. The growth of demonseeds into demons is a
relatively unknown practice, yet it is not impossible to witness the
grotesque birth of this kind of monstrosity. Claws, teeth, and a bad
temper...not pretty.
