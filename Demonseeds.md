Pure Demons can't exist on Midgaardia without the support of some major
Magic. To bypass this, Demonkind occasionally impregnate human mothers,
and the result is a Demonseed. These children stop aging at about 10
years old, and begin to develop a demonic appearance. They are
incredibly fast, surprisingly powerful magically and physically, and
irredeemably evil.

Demonseeds have RACIAL-EVIL, RACIAL-NOSUN and RACIAL-SNEAK

Demonseeds are only available via the remort command.
