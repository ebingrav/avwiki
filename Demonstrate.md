*Once you've [learned](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") this teaching skill, you
will be able to teach [skills and
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") like a
[guildmaster](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink") up to a certain
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"). Like a
[guildmaster](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink"), you only know what your
guild offers and the more teaching skills you
[know](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"), the higher the level of [skills and
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") you'll be able to
teach.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used.
