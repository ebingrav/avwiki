*A single tearing force will rip through your
[opponent](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), rending limbs from them and in
general causing havoc. It also removes water from them, making them
thirsty. All basic attack and area spells are more powerful for in-class
users who do not have to fear a counterattack thanks to [a group-mate
tanking the target](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Shocking Grasp](/wiki/Shocking_Grasp "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'desiccate'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This single-target damage [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
ninth of ten that [mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") between [level](Level "wikilink") 1 and
[level](Level "wikilink") 50. [Mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may
find it useful until they [learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink")
[Disintegrate](/wiki/Disintegrate "wikilink") or when they are low on
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of many other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it at [hero](Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") but will find it fairly useless to
them.
