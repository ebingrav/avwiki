*With a vision gifted from your
[God](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink"), you shall gain the
ability to distinguish good from evil. An aura or nimbus will surround
any you look upon, portraying their [alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Detect Evil](/wiki/Detect_Evil "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'detect alignment'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is practically never used.

See also [Gear That Applies 'Detect
Alignment'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Detect_Alignment' "wikilink").
