*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") enables the caster to
detect evil [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"), which will
reveal a characteristic red aura.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'detect evil'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is not especially useful
compared to most. It is included in [Holy Sight](/wiki/Holy_Sight "wikilink").

See also [Gear That Applies 'Detect
Evil'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Detect_Evil' "wikilink") and
[Detect Evil Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Detect_Evil_Gear "wikilink").
