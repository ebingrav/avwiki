*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") enables the caster to
detect [invisible](/wiki/Invis_Flag "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") and
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").
[Creatures](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") and
[players](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") more than 3
[levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink") above yourself will NOT be detected,
however.*

Prerequisite(s): [Detect Hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"),
[Invis](/wiki/Invis "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'detect invis'.

See also [Racial Seeinvis](/wiki/Racial_Seeinvis "wikilink").

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is fairly useful to
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") wishing to
[attack](Kill "wikilink") [invisible](/wiki/Invis_Flag "wikilink")
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") or to heal
[invisible](/wiki/Invis_Flag "wikilink")
[players](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or whatnot. It is included
in [Holy Sight](/wiki/Holy_Sight "wikilink"). It is superfluous to
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") affected by [Heighten
Senses](/wiki/Heighten_Senses "wikilink").

See also [Gear That Applies 'Detect
Invis'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Detect_Invis' "wikilink") and
[Detect Invis Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Detect_Invis_Gear "wikilink").
