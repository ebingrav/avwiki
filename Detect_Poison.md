*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") detects the presence of
[poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") in food or drink.*

Prerequisite(s): [Poison](/wiki/Poison_(spell) "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'detect poison'
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used, especially
as [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") no longer need to eat
or drink.
