*Reduced to its essential form, the detonate
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is simply an activation of the
psionic energies within an inanimate
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). If these
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") are close enough to the
intended victim, they can cause great damage. For this reason, most
[psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") choose the victim
itself as the target, since there's bound to be SOMETHING dead on it,
sooner or later. Put another way, this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is not at all effective on living
flesh; it requires a target who has some
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") equipped.*

Prerequisite(s): [Blast Bolt](/wiki/Blast_Bolt "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'detonate'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
