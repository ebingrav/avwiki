*Dexterity is especially important for
[rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink"); nimble fingers help when
[stealing](/wiki/Steal "wikilink"). But for all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), Dex improves the
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")'s [ability to hold many
items](/wiki/Carrying_Capacity "wikilink"), and improves the overall AC, or
[armor class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink").*
