*The beast looks at you from blood shot eyes, yawning and scratching its
throat with noticeable pleasure. It's a [troll](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink")! And
one that is [wearing](/wiki/Wear "wikilink")
[armor](/wiki/Glowing_Suit_Of_Banded_Mail "wikilink") and [a huge
halberd](/wiki/Mighty_Halberd "wikilink") too! Waiting for the attack you
raise your [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink"), but nothing
happens. You realize that this creature must have come here to die, and
would rather be left alone. For a moment the idea flashes up you could
deprive the old [troll](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink") from [its
armor](/wiki/Glowing_Suit_Of_Banded_Mail "wikilink") - but
[considering](/wiki/Consider "wikilink") the monstrous thing further you
realize that it could probably still slay you offhandedly.*

[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").
