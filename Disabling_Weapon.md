*Being struck by a disabling weapon can cause a foe to lose confidence
in their combat abilities. This proves to be a self-fulfilling prophecy,
as the loss in confidence reduces their effectiveness in melee combat.*

Prerequisite(s): [Distracting Weapon](Distracting_Weapon "wikilink")
