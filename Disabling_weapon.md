Syntax: cast 'disabling weapon' (self only)

Being struck by a disabling weapon can cause a foe to lose confidence in
their combat abilities. This proves to be a self-fulfilling prophecy, as
the loss in confidence reduces their effectiveness in melee combat.

See also: KINETIC ENHANCERS, STUNNING WEAPON, DISTRACTING WEAPON,
RESTRICTING WEAPON, FELLING WEAPON, CONSCIOUS WEAPON, INTELLIGENT WEAPON
