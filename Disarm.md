*DISARM is an auxiliary fighting
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") to relieve your opponent of
his [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink"). Needless to say, using
one's hands to steal an opponent's sword is not smart, so you must be
[wielding](Wield "wikilink") a [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")
to use this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"). Attempting to disarm
an opponent who wields no [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") is
pointless as well. Certain [weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") are
particularly useful in disarming opponents.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: disarm &lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
