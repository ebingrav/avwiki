*Any [openable object](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") may contain a
trap (excluding corpses). Traps range from mild poisons to lethal
explosions. Some go off when the chest is [opened](/wiki/Open "wikilink"),
some when the lock is [picked](/wiki/Pick_Lock "wikilink"). The [Rogue
Lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink") [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") will
allow you to [INSPECT](/wiki/Inspect "wikilink")
[containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink"), looking for traps. The
Rogue skill DISARM TRAPS allows you to [DISMANTLE](/wiki/Dismantle "wikilink")
traps on chests without setting them off, unless, of course, you slip,
or attempt to remove the wrong kind of trap. Trap disarming
[tools](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink") are available at many rogue
guilds. As with all [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") involving
[ROGUE TOOLs](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink"), the rate of success
is heavily dependant on the quality of the
[tool](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Rogue Lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: [inspect](/wiki/Inspect "wikilink")
&lt;[container](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink")&gt;.  
Syntax: [dismantle](/wiki/Dismantle "wikilink") <trap-type>
&lt;[container](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink")&gt;.
