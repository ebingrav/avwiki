*[Berserkers](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") with this Rite take
less of a hit to [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") when they
[berserk](/wiki/Berserk "wikilink"). This applies to both
[racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") and
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") abilities.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
