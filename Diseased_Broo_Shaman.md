Comments
--------

There are actually two of these [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), one
with [a flute](/wiki/Broo_Shaman's_Flute "wikilink") and one with [a
brooch](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Brooch "wikilink") that is used in [Broo
Village](/wiki/Category%3A_Broo_Village "wikilink")'s [Orosca's Enchanted
Wrist Guard Quest](/wiki/Orosca's_Enchanted_Wrist_Guard_Quest "wikilink"). For
those on [this quest](/wiki/Orosca's_Enchanted_Wrist_Guard_Quest "wikilink"),
it would be courteous to kill *both* shamans when possible; otherwise,
when [this area](/wiki/Category%3A_Broo_Village "wikilink") repops, it will
contain *two* shamans holding [flutes](/wiki/Broo_Shaman's_Flute "wikilink")
but *zero* carrying [brooches](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Brooch "wikilink").
