*This is the single most powerful [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
a [Mage](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") can [learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink");
it unleashes a raw destructive force against one
[opponent](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") comes at a great cost
of [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink"). All basic attack and area spells are
more powerful for in-class users who do not have to fear a counterattack
thanks to [a group-mate tanking the target](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Desiccate](/wiki/Desiccate "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'disintegrate'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This single-target damage [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
tenth of ten that [mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") between [level](Level "wikilink") 1 and
[level](Level "wikilink") 50. [Mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") can
expect to use it continually throughout
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") [tier](Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink")
to target single [opponents](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), rarely (if
ever) using other single-target offensive
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), except for
[wizards](/wiki/Category%3A_Wizards "wikilink"), will never be able to
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it.
