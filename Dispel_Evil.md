*The forces of Good normally tend to healing and health, but when this
spell invokes them, all the wrath of the Good is expelled onto an evil
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). You must be of good
[alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") for this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") to inflict damage.*

Prerequisite(s): [Detect Evil](/wiki/Detect_Evil "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'dispel evil'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is practically never used.

See also [Dispel Evil Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Dispel_Evil_Gear "wikilink").
