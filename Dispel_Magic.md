*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") removes magical affects and
spells from a target. Removing a [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
is more difficult than placing it, however-it may take many tries of
dispel magic to remove any [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Detect Magic](/wiki/Detect_Magic "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'dispel magic'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used.
