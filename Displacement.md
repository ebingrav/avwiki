*This is a protective discipline which relies on psionics. DISPLACEMENT
makes the caster appear to be displaced from his or her actual position,
thus making him or her more difficult to hit. The caster's mind
continuously adjusts the image for the duration of this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), so it is impossible to displace
another with this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'displacement'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in
[Fortitudes](/wiki/Fortitudes "wikilink").
