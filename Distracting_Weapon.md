*With this spell, a psionicist can cause their opponent to become
distracted. As they lose focus on the battle their attacks become less
likely to land.*

Prerequisite(s): [Fandango](Fandango "wikilink"), [Stunning
Weapon](Stunning_Weapon "wikilink")

Syntax: [cast](cast "wikilink") 'distracting weapon' (self only)
