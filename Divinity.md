*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") cures damage on the target
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). It is more effective
than the [heal spell](/wiki/Heal_(spell) "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Heal](/wiki/Heal_(spell) "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'divinity'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [hit-point](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink")-healing
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is fourth (and second most
efficient) of five that both [clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink")
and [priests](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") may (and should)
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") between [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 1 and
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 50. Only
[paladins](/wiki/Category%3A_Paladins "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it at [hero](Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") and will find it useful as well.
When [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") by
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink"), it normally heals 250 [hit
points](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") but this amount may be altered somewhat
by their respective choices of [worship](/wiki/Worship "wikilink"), if any.
