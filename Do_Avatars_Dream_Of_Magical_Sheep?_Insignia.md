This insignia is a reward for doing all the chores for [Fix-It
Floyd](/wiki/Fix-It_Floyd "wikilink") in [Fix-It Floyd
Quest](/wiki/Fix-It_Floyd_Quest "wikilink"), available at the [Mob
Factory](/wiki/Category%3AMob_Factory "wikilink").

It awards either an ability or stat boost that lasts until Hero level
101.
