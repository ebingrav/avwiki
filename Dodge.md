*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") lets you dodge incoming
attacks, taking no damage from them. Use of the
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is automatic once you have
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
