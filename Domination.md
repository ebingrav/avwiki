*By [casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), a talented
[mentalist](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") may twist the psionic
energies to DOMINATE the mind of an intended victim, causing the victim
to do exactly as the caster says. Failure to completely control the
target's mind is certain to attract its attention and likely to arouse
its wrath. As with [CHARM](/wiki/Charm_Person "wikilink"), the actions of
dominated [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") affect the
status of the dominator; don't whine if you get an [OUTLAW
flag](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink"). Be careful where you leave your
followers; leading high-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink")
[creatures](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") to
low-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") [areas](Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") or
moving a common [portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point is highly frowned
upon. [Quitting](/wiki/Quit "wikilink") will make your followers disappear.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'domination'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
