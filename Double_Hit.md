*An adept of this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") can attempt to
hit twice on every attack, if wielding a [double-headed
weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Double_Weapons "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Double Wield](/wiki/Double_Wield "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
