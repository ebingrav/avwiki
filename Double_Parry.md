*This [ability](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") grants a second
[parry](/wiki/Parry "wikilink") attempt to anybody who is double-wielding a
[hafted](/wiki/Category%3A_Hafted_Weapons "wikilink") or
[double-headed](/wiki/Category%3A_Double_Weapons "wikilink")
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") in both hands.*

Prerequisite(s): [Double Wield](/wiki/Double_Wield "wikilink"),
[Parry](/wiki/Parry "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
