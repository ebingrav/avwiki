*Although anybody can hold a [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") in
two hands, it takes extra training to get the most oomph out of your
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink"). This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") improves damage done while double
wielding. As anyone who tries will soon learn, some weapons simply
cannot be double-wielded. If you find you must have something that you
can double-wield, perhaps it's time to search for a new weapon!*

Prerequisite(s): none (automatic).

Syntax: [wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink")
&lt;[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")&gt; (for weapons that can
be wielded two-handed only)  
Syntax: [wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink")
&lt;[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")&gt; double (for weapons
that can be wielded either one-handed or two-handed)
