Comments
--------

Be careful [teleporting](/wiki/Teleport "wikilink") to this
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), as there are other portable
dracoliches in Midgaardia that are *much* bigger and nastier and may
even kill you instantly; [myconoid shaman](/wiki/Myconoid_Shaman "wikilink")
is a much safer portal-point to use. This dracolich is relatively hard
for [its area](/wiki/Category%3A_River_Tunnels "wikilink"), so please take
caution before attacking it!
