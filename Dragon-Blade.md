*An elegant weapon, the hilt is made from dragon bone, carved into the
shape of spread wings to create the guard. The narrow blade was forged
from the razor sharp tooth of a black dragon.*

` Modifies melee attacks with fire damage continuous.`  
` Modifies `[`damage`
`roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 11 continuous.`  
` Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 11 continuous.`  
` Damage is 16 to 45-50.`
