*This person looks almost like a [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") itself.
His golden scales gleam with power. He smiles at you with a vicious
grin. You're happy that he doesn't look upset at your presence.*

**Keywords:** dragonlord, lord.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 29.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[multi-berather](/wiki/Breathing_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [protection
evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
