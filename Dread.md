*When [cast](Cast "wikilink") in battle, Dread summons the victim's
worst fear, and makes it believe it cannot hope to win. Thus, most
[monsters](:Category:_Mobs "wikilink") will turn tail and
[flee](Flee "wikilink") if they are able, unless it is painfully obvious
that the [Psion](:Category:_Psionicists "wikilink") is outmatched. Dread
cannot be [cast](Cast "wikilink") outside of combat.*

Prerequisite(s): [Fear](Fear "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'dread'.
