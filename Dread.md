*When [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") in battle, Dread summons the victim's
worst fear, and makes it believe it cannot hope to win. Thus, most
[monsters](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") will turn tail and
[flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") if they are able, unless it is painfully obvious
that the [Psion](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") is outmatched.
Dread cannot be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") outside of combat.*

Prerequisite(s): [Fear](/wiki/Fear "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'dread'.
