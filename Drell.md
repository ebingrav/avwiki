*Drell Blackflint looks every bit the part of a Mountain Guard, with
shoulders almost as broad as he is tall, and sinewy forearms bearing the
scars of many battles. Dutifully he stands his post at Durr's Left Eye,
ever watchful for any external threat to the serenity of [the Dwarven
home town](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** dwarf, Drell, guard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 23.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
