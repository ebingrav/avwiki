*The drider witch plots magical means to thwart the warding efforts of
the [High Sylph](High_Sylph "wikilink")'s [Nyad](Nyad "wikilink"). She
would pay dearly for [a talisman of some
sort](Lock_Of_Long_White_Nyad_Hair "wikilink") from the
[Nyad](Nyad "wikilink"). With it she could easily undo all the good the
[Nyad](Nyad "wikilink") has wrought.*

**Keywords:** drider, witch.  
**[Level](Level "wikilink"):** 27.  
**[Alignment](Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[mage](Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").
