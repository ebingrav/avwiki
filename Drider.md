*Half drow, half giant spider, the driders are hideous monstrosities
unequaled.*

**Keywords:** drider.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 24.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Category%3A_Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink"),
[wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink").
