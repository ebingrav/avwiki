*The drider witch plots magical means to thwart the warding efforts of
the [High Sylph](/wiki/High_Sylph "wikilink")'s [Nyad](/wiki/Nyad "wikilink"). She
would pay dearly for a talisman of some sort from the
[Nyad](/wiki/Nyad "wikilink"). With it she could easily undo all the good the
[Nyad](/wiki/Nyad "wikilink") has wrought.*

**Keywords:** drider, witch.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 27.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
