[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will "drift" from one
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") to another (and not necessarily an
adjacent one) within a given [sector](/wiki/Sectors "wikilink") without using
any [exits](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Exits "wikilink").

See also [Sentinel Mobs](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink") and [Wandering
Mobs](/wiki/Wandering_Mobs "wikilink").
