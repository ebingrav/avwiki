[Drink-container](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have four
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **Capacity, Maximum:** This value determines how much liquid this
    [drink container](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink") may hold
    at most.

<!-- -->

-   **Capacity, Current:** This value determines how much liquid this
    [drink container](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink") holds
    at present.

<!-- -->

-   **Liquid Type:** This value determines whether this [drink
    container](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink")'s liquid is ale,
    beer, blood, champagne, cherry cola, coffee, darkale, firebreather,
    grape juice, lemonade, local speciaty, milk, orange juice, Pepsi,
    salt water, slime mold juice, tea, water, whiskey, or wine.

<!-- -->

-   **Toxicity:** This value determines whether or not this [drink
    container](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink") will
    [poison](/wiki/Poison_(spell) "wikilink") those who
    [drink](/wiki/Drink "wikilink") from it.

See also [Drink Containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink").
