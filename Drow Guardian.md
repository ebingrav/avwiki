*This [warrior](:Category:_Warriors "wikilink") is of unusual stature,
especially in light of his [race](:Category:_Races "wikilink"). He is
also an expert swordsman, capable of rending the very life from an enemy
in one well-placed stroke. An impassive expression, one to endure all
catastrophes, adds to this [warrior](:Category:_Warriors "wikilink")'s
almost tangible aura of silent ferocity.*

**Keywords:** drow, guardian, massive.  
**[Level](Level "wikilink"):** 43.  
**[Alignment](Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"), [very
aggressive](Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").

## Comments

A drow guardian is involved in [The Frozen
Court](:Category:_Frozen_Court "wikilink")'s [Sword of Bloodied Snow
Quest](Sword_Of_Bloodied_Snow_Quest "wikilink").

[The key to E'Kim's royal
cellar](Key_To_E'Kim's_Royal_Cellar "wikilink") unlocks the door to [The
Frozen Court](:Category:_Frozen_Court "wikilink")'s lower level. [The
Guardian's chamber key](Guardian's_Chamber_Key "wikilink") unlocks the
door to The Guardian's Quarters where A drow guardian is.
