*Dressed in a black cloak and gloves, this assassin's eyes are piercing
and hypnotizing. Having just killed the ogre guard, he lets the corpse
fall to his feet with a small sigh of pleasure, for only a true assassin
considers killing an art, not a sin.*

**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.

[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").
