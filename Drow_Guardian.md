*This [warrior](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") is of unusual stature,
especially in light of his [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink"). He is
also an expert swordsman, capable of rending the very life from an enemy
in one well-placed stroke. An impassive expression, one to endure all
catastrophes, adds to this [warrior](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink")'s
almost tangible aura of silent ferocity.*

**Keywords:** drow, guardian, massive.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 43.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"), [very
aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").

Comments
--------

A drow guardian is involved in [The Frozen
Court](/wiki/Category%3A_Frozen_Court "wikilink")'s [Sword of Bloodied Snow
Quest](/wiki/Sword_Of_Bloodied_Snow_Quest "wikilink").

[The key to E'Kim's royal
cellar](/wiki/Key_To_E'Kim's_Royal_Cellar "wikilink") unlocks the door to [The
Frozen Court](/wiki/Category%3A_Frozen_Court "wikilink")'s lower level. [The
Guardian's chamber key](/wiki/Guardian's_Chamber_Key "wikilink") unlocks the
door to The Guardian's Quarters where A drow guardian is.
