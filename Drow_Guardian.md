*This [warrior](:Category:_Warriors.md "wikilink") is of unusual
stature, especially in light of his
[race](:Category:_Races.md "wikilink"). He is also an expert swordsman,
capable of rending the very life from an enemy in one well-placed
stroke. An impassive expression, one to endure all catastrophes, adds to
this [warrior](:Category:_Warriors.md "wikilink")'s almost tangible aura
of silent ferocity.*

**Keywords:** drow, guardian, massive.  
**[Level](Level.md "wikilink"):** 43.  
**[Alignment](Alignment.md "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs.md "wikilink"), [very
aggressive](Aggressive_Mobs.md "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](Detect_Hidden.md "wikilink"), [detect
invis](Detect_Invis.md "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](Sanctuary.md "wikilink").

## Comments

A drow guardian is involved in [The Frozen
Court](:Category:_Frozen_Court.md "wikilink")'s [Sword of Bloodied Snow
Quest](Sword_Of_Bloodied_Snow_Quest.md "wikilink").

[The key to E'Kim's royal
cellar](Key_To_E'Kim's_Royal_Cellar.md "wikilink") unlocks the door to
[The Frozen Court](:Category:_Frozen_Court.md "wikilink")'s lower level.
[The Guardian's chamber key](Guardian's_Chamber_Key.md "wikilink")
unlocks the door to The Guardian's Quarters where A drow guardian is.
