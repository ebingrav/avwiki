*[I'Sal](/wiki/I'Sal's_Spirit "wikilink"), his only daughter, the only chance
for the survival of the [drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink")
[race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink"), has fallen in love with a
[human](Humans "wikilink"). The [drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") will vanish
forever; words alone, minute fragments of knowledge, will survive to
tell of the greatness of his [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink"). Now,
the king must silently age as the remnants of his nation remain,
untouched by time. If ever they should awaken, the
[drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") will once more fade into antiquity.*
