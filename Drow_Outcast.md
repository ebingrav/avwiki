*Outcast from the others due to a heinous crime, this drow has nowhere
to go except to these ruins. Branded with a mark that identifies him as
veldri, or outcast, he glares at the walls of the temple through a
peculiar looking glass.*

**Keywords:** drow, outcast.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 37.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** none.  
**Affects:** [invis](/wiki/Invis "wikilink"), [pass
door](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink").
