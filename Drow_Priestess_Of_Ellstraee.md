*Charged with ministering to the spiritual needs of the
[drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") guards here, she carries [a
harp](/wiki/Harp_Of_Ellstraee "wikilink") that will never see use again.
Mournful, yet willing to sacrifice all to bring the beauty of her
Goddess to the hearts of others, she remains in [the Frozen
Court](/wiki/Category%3A_Frozen_Court "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** drow, female, priestess, Ellstraee.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 43.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
