## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") is used in [The Frozen
Court](:Category:_Frozen_Court.md "wikilink")'s [Sword of Bloodied Snow
Quest](Sword_Of_Bloodied_Snow_Quest.md "wikilink"). It unlocks four
doors in [The Frozen Court](:Category:_Frozen_Court.md "wikilink")'s
eastern (prison) corridor.
