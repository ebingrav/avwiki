Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is used in [The Frozen
Court](/wiki/Category%3A_Frozen_Court "wikilink")'s [Sword of Bloodied Snow
Quest](/wiki/Sword_Of_Bloodied_Snow_Quest "wikilink"). It unlocks four doors
in [The Frozen Court](/wiki/Category%3A_Frozen_Court "wikilink")'s eastern
(prison) corridor.
