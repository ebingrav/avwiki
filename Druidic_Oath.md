*[Druids](/wiki/Category%3A_Druids "wikilink") undertake an Oath to live by
the [bow](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink"). As a consequence of
this, their [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") become cheaper if
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") while [wielding](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") a
[bow](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink"), but twice as expensive if
they [wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") any other type of
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") or wear a shield. The Oath also
enables a [Druid](/wiki/Category%3A_Druid "wikilink") to assemble natural
fletch kits using the [forage](/wiki/Forage "wikilink")
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") in a wilderness setting, and
unlocks the secrets of [mystic
arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Mystic_Warheads "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
