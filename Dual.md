*DUAL enables a person to use two
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") more effectively. As the
"offhand" is naturally weaker, the secondary
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") must be lighter in weight than
the primary. While the offhand [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")
will only do one extra attack per melee round, it is still one more than
you'd normally have without the [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink").
Some say that [warriors](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink"),
[rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") and
[assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink") with sufficient
[poise](/wiki/Poise "wikilink") and [balance](Balance "wikilink") can go
beyond what is possible with dual alone and instead can master using two
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") of similar
[weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink") at the same time.*

Prerequisite(s): [Parry](/wiki/Parry "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
