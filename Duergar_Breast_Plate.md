Comments
--------

Two different mobs load with this gear. The [Duergar
Commander](/wiki/Duergar_Commander "wikilink") loads with a lower level one.

Also, since [Dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink") provides a -10
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") bonus, the modifier on this armor is not
that useful unless you can raise your [Dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink")
by other means.
