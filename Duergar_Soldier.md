*One of the outcast grey dwarves who has been enlisted into the war. A
glint of madness can be seen in his eyes and he grasps his weapon with
fervor, ready to smite any enemy.*

**Keywords:** duergar, soldier.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 15.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").

Comments
--------

This mob exists in two areas. If you want a [Duergar
Helm](/wiki/Duergar_Helm "wikilink"), go to [Duergar
Stronghold](/wiki/Category%3ADuergar_Stronghold "wikilink"), they do not load
in [Drow City](/wiki/Category%3ADrow_City "wikilink").
