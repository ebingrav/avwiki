This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") is added to
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink") that are
"[dulled](/wiki/Dulling "wikilink")" while being
[sharpened](/wiki/Sharpen "wikilink"); some
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") may even load with it.
[Weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink") with this
[flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") may never be
[sharpened](/wiki/Sharpen "wikilink") as much as they may have been without
it.

See also [Dulling](/wiki/Dulling "wikilink").
