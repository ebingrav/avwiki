[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") are where [rogue guild
guards](/wiki/Guild_Guard_Mobs "wikilink") will dump
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") after
[blackjacking](/wiki/Blackjack "wikilink") and [robbing](Steal "wikilink")
them and where [graverobbing mobs](/wiki/Graverobbing_Mobs "wikilink") will
dump [character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") corpses.

See also [Rogue Guild Terrain](/wiki/Rogue_Guild_Terrain "wikilink").
