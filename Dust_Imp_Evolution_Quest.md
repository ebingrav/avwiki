In the [ancient bronze wyrm](/wiki/Category%3AAncient_Bronze_Wyrm "wikilink")
area, find the the Bronze Wyrm. You can't enter the room with Vincent
without killing the wyrm because the lever needed to open the door won't
appear until the Wyrm is dead. Use an alt or ask a friend, it's pretty
big and it tails/breaths (obviously). Go north after you pull the lever.
You'll find Vincent and all you have to do is listen to him talk, when
he has finished talking give him the scroll you got from the MNI quest,
listen to him again and then you can evolve. Going NW from Vincent takes
you to a portal to exit the cursed/aggie zone that you're presumably
unspelled in.
