*This thief controls all the thieves who work Solace in daylight hours.
It is unusual for a halfling to rise so high in the thieves guild, but
the dutymaster was an exceptional thief. Although slowed by age and
fading eyesight, the dutymaster remains an important player in the
guild, keeping the working thieves in order and teaching them the tricks
of the trade.*

**Keywords:** old, halfling, thief, dutymaster.  
**[Level](Level.md "wikilink"):** 8.  
**[Alignment](Alignment.md "wikilink"):** apathetic.

[trainer](:Category:Trainers.md "wikilink"),
