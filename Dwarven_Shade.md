*The shade died a painful, horrible death in these caverns long ago, and
cannot rest.*

**Keywords:** dwarf, miner, shade.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 38.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink"), [lich
spellcaster](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink"), [pass
door](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

A Dwarven shade wanders within the *Ice cave* rooms of the area and
won't wander outside of them.
