*Earthbind only works against [flying](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink")
[creatures](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), taking away their ability to
stay aloft, and damages them somewhat when they fall.*

Prerequisite(s): [Fly](/wiki/Fly "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'earthbind'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is hardly ever used.
