*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") inflicts damage on every
[enemy character](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). Beware that other
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") who are not yet fighting may
attack you as a result!*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'earthquake'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is the most powerful
area-effect [damage
spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Harm_Mobs "wikilink") that
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") at
[lowmort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink"). Because
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") normally focus on
[casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") defensive spells rather than offensive
spells, it is hardly ever used. It is, however, useful for cleric tanks
to initiate battle with multiple mobs.

See also [Earthquake Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Earthquake_Gear "wikilink").
