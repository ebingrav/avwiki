*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") enables the
caster to pass through most closed
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'ectoplasmic form'.

[Psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") may find this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") rather useful at
[lowmort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") but usually [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
[Pass Door](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink") instead of it at
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink"). It is superfluous to
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") affected by [Pass
Door](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink"). See [Translucent
Flag](/wiki/Translucent_Flag "wikilink") for more information about its
effects.
