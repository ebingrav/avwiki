*Edgar is a scientist who is testing a new form of farming for the
gnomish peoples, involving multiple types of animals in the same farm,
allowing for more diversity and self-reliance.*

**Keywords:** Edgar, Farmer, gnome.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** none.  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [detect
hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [protection
evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"), .
