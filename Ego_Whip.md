*One well-versed in the discipline of psionics is at home while toying
with the mind's inner workings. Such a person can learn to EGO WHIP a
victim; this attack destroys confidence by bringing up repressed
childhood memories, embarrassing moments, and failures. Victims suffer a
significant reduction in combat capabilities.*

Prerequisite: [Fear](/wiki/Fear "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'ego whip'
