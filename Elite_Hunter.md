*This is one of the half-dozen or so elite hunters of
[Icewolf](/wiki/Category%3A_Icewolf "wikilink") who have distinguished
themselves above all others. They work in shifts, two guarding the House
of Elders at all times while the others rest or lead hunting parties.*

**Keywords:** hunter.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 47.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
