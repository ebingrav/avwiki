*Embalm preserves a decaying corpse, preventing it from decaying as
quickly. If the corpse belongs to a
[player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"), a kind passerby can use
this to give the player more time to [retrieve
equipment](/wiki/Corpse_Retrieval "wikilink"). However, this is a
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), not a
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") - you'll need
[bandages](/wiki/Some_Bandages "wikilink") to be successful.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: embalm <corpse>.
