*Some [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") have the ability
to embed particular [gemstones](/wiki/Category%3A_Gemstones "wikilink") into
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). If the right combination of
[gemstones](/wiki/Category%3A_Gemstones "wikilink") is embedded into the right
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), then a player can attempt to
[manifest](/wiki/Manifest "wikilink") that
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") into something more powerful.
Please note that [etched](/wiki/Etched_Flag "wikilink")
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") cannot have
[gems](/wiki/Category%3A_Gemstones "wikilink") added.*

Prerequisite(s): [Gem Lore](/wiki/Gem_Lore "wikilink"), [Weapon
Lore](/wiki/Weapon_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: embed <perfect-[[Category%3A Gemstones |gemstone]]>
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.
