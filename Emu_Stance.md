*Emu kicks with all her power. Your [kicks](/wiki/Kick "wikilink") strike for
extra damage. Emu Stance ends early if you attempt a
[toss](/wiki/Toss "wikilink") or [trip](Trip "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Serpent Technique](/wiki/Serpent_Technique "wikilink"),
[Whirlwind Kick](/wiki/Whirlwind_Kick "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](/wiki/Stance "wikilink") emu.

See also [Tiger Stance](/wiki/Tiger_Stance "wikilink").
