*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") improves the
[bonuses](/wiki/Object_Affects "wikilink") the target
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") gives to
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"). This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") causes the
[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink")'s alignment to become the
[alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") of the caster, although it does not
remove an existing alignment. [Enchanting](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink") also
causes the [armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink") to be increased in
[level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"). There are several results that can
happen when this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"). The [armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink") can
be successfully [enchanted](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink") normally, or
[brilliantly](/wiki/Brilling "wikilink"), which means the enchantment bonus is
greater. The [armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink") can also
[fade](/wiki/Fading "wikilink"), which removes all existing
[enchants](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink") as well as [item
flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"). Worst of all, the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") has a chance to explode and be
lost forever. ([Containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") which
explode also destroy all contents...)*

Prerequisiste(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'enchant armor'
&lt;[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink")&gt;.

Spellcasters may find this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") very useful, as all
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") benefit from
[wearing](Wear "wikilink") [enchanted](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink")
[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink"). See also
[Enchanting](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink").

See also [Enchant Armor
Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Enchant_Armor_Gear "wikilink").
