*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") magically
[enchants](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink") a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink"), increasing its
[hitroll](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink") and [damroll](Damage_Roll "wikilink")
[bonuses](/wiki/Object_Affects "wikilink"). This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") causes the
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink")'s alignment to become the
[alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") of the [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"),
although it does not remove an existing alignment.
[Enchanting](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink") also causes the
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink") to be increased in
[level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"). There are several results that can
happen when this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"). The
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink") can be successfully
[enchanted](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink") normally, or
[brilliantly](/wiki/Brilling "wikilink"), which means the enchantment bonus is
greater. The [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink") can also
[fade](/wiki/Fading "wikilink"), which removes all existing
[enchants](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink") as well as [item
flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"). Worst of all, the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") has a chance to explode and be
lost forever.*

Prerequisiste(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'enchant weapon'
&lt;[meleeweapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink")&gt;.

Spellcasters may find this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") very useful, as all
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") benefit from
[wielding](Wield "wikilink") [enchanted](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink")
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink"). See also
[Enchanting](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink").
