*Comprised of the stolen jewels from various
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"), this crown is the ultimate insult
to [the drows](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") and the rest of [the
ogre](/wiki/Octyin "wikilink")'s victims. Its beauty surpasses the realm of
understanding; staring at it for a long time can make one go mad.*

[`Armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 9-10.`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 4 continuous.`
