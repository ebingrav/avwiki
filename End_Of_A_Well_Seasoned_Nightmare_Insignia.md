This insignia is gained by completing [End Of A Well Seasoned Nightmare
Quest](/wiki/End_Of_A_Well_Seasoned_Nightmare_Quest "wikilink") in [Sun God
Altar](/wiki/Category%3ASun_God_Altar "wikilink") on
[Eragora](/wiki/Category%3AEragora "wikilink").

It grants [Sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink") that lasts. Long. Not
forever but long enough. Really long.
