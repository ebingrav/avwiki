This is a quest that starts in [Sun God
Altar](/wiki/Category%3ASun_God_Altar "wikilink") but involves other Eragoran
areas. Speak to [Seneca Rotberry](/wiki/Seneca_Rotberry "wikilink") and see
what weighs heavy on this healer's conscience.

Once completed it awards the [End Of A Well Seasoned Nightmare
Insignia](/wiki/End_Of_A_Well_Seasoned_Nightmare_Insignia "wikilink").
