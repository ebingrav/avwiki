*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") saps [hit
points](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") from the
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). It also has the potential of
negatively affecting all [stats](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistics "wikilink")
including [experience](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink"). Be wary of casual
use on [friends](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") and beware
[foes](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") who wield this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Chill Touch](/wiki/Chill_Touch "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'energy drain'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used.

See also [Energy Drain Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Energy_Drain_Gear "wikilink").
