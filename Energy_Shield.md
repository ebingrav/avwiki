*One of the most dreaded kinds of attacks is the energy weapon. Spells
like gas breath, lightning, etc can not only hurt you, but destroy your
[equipment](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink"). Energy Shield helps prevent
that by improving your "[save vs](/wiki/Saving_Throw "wikilink")" rating. With
this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), you can absorb a breath
attack with much less chance of losing
[equipment](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink"). Since this is a [TELEKINETIC
spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), it can
easily be cast on others.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'energy shield'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
