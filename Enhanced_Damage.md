*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), once learned, will give
the [wielder](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") of a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") the knowledge to get the most
out of a sword or spear. When you can achieve this enhanced damage, your
average swing of a club becomes something more powerful.*

Prerequisites: none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
