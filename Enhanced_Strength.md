*Giving great concentration to the microscopic arrangement of one's
muscle cells, one may increase their efficiency and density. The
resulting gain in [strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") will gradually wear
off as the less ordered configuration returns. ENHANCED STRENGTH is a
[BIOCENTRIC Psionic
Spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Biocentric_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). It can only
be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on others if the caster first masters the art
of [BIO EMPATHY](/wiki/Bio_Empathy "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'enhanced strength'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

[Psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") may find this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") rather useful at
[lowmort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") but usually [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
[Giant Strength](/wiki/Giant_Strength "wikilink") instead of it at
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink"). It increases its target
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")'s
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") by 4, though not more than 3 above its
target's maximum [trainable](/wiki/Train "wikilink")
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink"). It is superfluous for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") affected by [Giant
Strength](/wiki/Giant_Strength "wikilink").
