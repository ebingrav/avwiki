*By [learning](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), you shall regen your
physical energy quicker. It only works if you are
[sleeping](/wiki/Sleep_(command) "wikilink"), however, and only works up to a
point. To regen quicker, you will have to [learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink")
further [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink").*

Prerequisites: none.

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is rather
beneficial to [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") as it increases their
[move-point](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink") (?) regeneration rates.
