*Taking on a simple human-like shape formed of gravel and granite this
elemental thunders its fists upon the floor and bellows its rage to the
sky. Whoever forced it to inhabit that servitor-husk had better run!*

**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 60.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**Affects:** [ aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

As the elemental dissipates, it leaves behind a trace of its [
essence](/wiki/Earth_Essence "wikilink").

Arcanists](/wiki/Category%3A_Citadel_Of_Arcanists "wikilink")' [Smooth Stone
Tablet Quest](/wiki/Smooth_Stone_Tablet_Quest "wikilink").
