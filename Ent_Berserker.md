*This [ent](/wiki/Ents "wikilink")
[berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink"), if not the oldest
[ent](/wiki/Ents "wikilink") that you have ever seen, may well be the
oldest-looking one, with her battle-scarred torso, knotty warped limbs,
and scraggly mold-covered branches. She appears to be looking for
someone and not in much of a mood to linger and chat with anyone for
long, but she does pause just long enough to greet you and to introduce
herself to you briefly--briefly for an [ent](/wiki/Ents "wikilink"), at least.
She explains that [Silvanus](/wiki/Centaur_Ranger "wikilink") had asked
[Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink")'s Entmoot to provide some
[ents](/wiki/Ents "wikilink") to fight alongside him, and that she is one of
three [ents](/wiki/Ents "wikilink") sent here by them in response. Apparently,
she is not native to [Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink") like
her peers but traveled here long ago from beyond
[Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink"), which she explains is very
far eastward from this region. She mentions having heard rumors that
some of her relatives there are now battling against invading
[giants](/wiki/Giants "wikilink").*
