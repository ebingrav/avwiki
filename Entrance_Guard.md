*This hulking brute of a [warrior](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink")
stands here protecting the entrance to [the Hall of
Warriors](/wiki/Category%3A_Hall_Of_Warriors "wikilink") against unwelcome
guests. His icy glare chills you, and the great
[bardiche](/wiki/Bardiche "wikilink") he wields is very functional.*

**Keywords:** Entrance, Guard, hulking, warrior.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 30.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
