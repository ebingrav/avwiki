This insignia is gained by completing [Animal Handler
Quest](/wiki/Animal_Handler_Quest "wikilink") in
[Rapture](/wiki/Category%3ARapture "wikilink") on
[Eragora](/wiki/Category%3AEragora "wikilink").

Insignia grants +200 HP for a set duration of time. Entire group gets it
once the quest is completed.
