`Modifies moves by 150 continuous.`  
`Modifies damage roll by 2 continuous.`  
`Modifies hit roll by 2 continuous.`

Slot: Light  
Level: 27  
Base: n/a  
Weight: 3  
Area: [Morgan Vale](/wiki/Category%3A_Morgan_Vale "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Morgan_Vale_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: [Banshee](/wiki/Banshee "wikilink")  
  
Port [High Sylph](/wiki/High_Sylph "wikilink"), go s, d, s, 2w, 4n, e, 4n, 3w,
3n, 3w, s, get [lichen](/wiki/Black_Lichen "wikilink") and go back to [High
Sylph](/wiki/High_Sylph "wikilink").  
Give lichen [Sylph](/wiki/High_Sylph "wikilink") to get Dryad Key, then go s,
d, s, 2w, 3n, 2w, u, {w}, 2w to get to [Banshee](/wiki/Banshee "wikilink").
Kill her for the Sceptre.  
Note that {w} means that door is locked, use the Dryad Key to unlock it.

*<Font color=red>Be careful when entering the cave! If you die and the
door closes you will have to wait for the lichen to repop to get a new
key to retrieve your corpse.</font>*
