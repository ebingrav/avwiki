Directions
----------

**[Area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"):** [Erisian
Temple](/wiki/Category%3A_Erisian_Temple "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Erisian_Temple_Map "wikilink")).  
Erisian High Priestess [wanders](/wiki/Wandering_Mobs "wikilink"), but she is
usually found in the back half of the temple. The [Purple
Key](/wiki/Purple_Key "wikilink") or [Pass Door](Pass_Door "wikilink") is
needed to get past a door.

Erisian High Priestess is portable, but the lack of unique keywords
makes it unreliable.
