*You can etch [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") at certain places
like the [Jeweller](/wiki/Jeweller "wikilink") in
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") for a price. This gives the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") one additional keyword. It is a
one etch deal, however, and cannot be un-etched. You can only etch words
with 4-12 characters. It is also not possible to etch
[scrolls](/wiki/Category%3A_Scrolls "wikilink"), corpses, and certain other
types of [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). We're offering a
potentially powerful tool to you. Treat it wisely. WARNING\#1: Color
keywords work... but not in a good way. We suggest not wasting your
money on color unless you want to type your keywords in color.
WARNING\#2: The [Immortal Staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Immortals "wikilink") can
see what you add as a keyword. Abuse of current language and in-theme
[policies](/wiki/Category%3A_Policies "wikilink") will be enforced and can
result in repercussions if ignored. (If there's too much abuse, the
command will no longer be offered to everyone.)*

Syntax: etch &lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt; <keyword>.

See also [Etchers](/wiki/Category%3A_Etchers "wikilink").
