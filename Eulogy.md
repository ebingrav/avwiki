*This spell is simple but should be considered carefully before casting.
This is a way to transfer your corpse from an [ELITE HERO
AREA](/wiki/Category%3AElite_Hero_Areas "wikilink") to your location as long
as you are in the Sanctum. To get this CR, the price is steep.*

*If the spell worked:*

*You will lose 1 point of your low stat. Not only that, but you will be
drained of hit points, mana and movement after casting. This is an
emergency spell used in dire times.*

*Prime Stat vs. Low Stat (Check your prime stat's opposite.)*  
*Str ...........Int*  
*Int ...........Str*  
*Wis ...........Con*  
*Dex ...........Int*  
*Con ...........Wis*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'eulogy'.
