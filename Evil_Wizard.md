*Looking like any other evil [wizard](/wiki/Category%3A_Wizards "wikilink"),
this man wears black robes and has a devious sneer. There is nothing
unique about the man, and he appears to be your garden variety evil
[wizard](/wiki/Category%3A_Wizards "wikilink") type. He isn't very pleased at
your intrusion, nor at the thought that you might hamper his plans to
retrieve a certain object from the korred. Good thing he has that big
[troll](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink") to back him up.*

**Keywords:** wizard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 35.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[mage](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"), [very
aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
