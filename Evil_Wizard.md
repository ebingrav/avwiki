*Looking like any other evil [wizard](:Category:_Wizards.md "wikilink"),
this man wears black robes and has a devious sneer. There is nothing
unique about the man, and he appears to be your garden variety evil
[wizard](:Category:_Wizards.md "wikilink") type. He isn't very pleased
at your intrusion, nor at the thought that you might hamper his plans to
retrieve a certain object from the korred. Good thing he has that big
[troll](Trolls.md "wikilink") to back him up.*
