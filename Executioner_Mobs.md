[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") stand at [executioner's
blocks](/wiki/Executioner's_Block_Rooms "wikilink") and will execute
[outlawed](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink")
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who have been sentenced
to this fate.
