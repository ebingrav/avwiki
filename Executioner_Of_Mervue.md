*A large, beefy [troll](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink"), this
[executioner](/wiki/Executioner_Mobs "wikilink") was hired to keep justice in
[the city](/wiki/Category%3A_Mervue "wikilink"), and also to keep the
dissenters against [the Lord](/wiki/Lord_Audis "wikilink") at bay. A steely
glint in his eyes keeps most prisoners frozen.*
