*Some [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") have an affect called
exhaustion. You will not be able to [recast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") that
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") until the exhaustion is gone.
[PSYPHON](/wiki/Psyphon "wikilink") and [TIME SKIP](Time_Skip "wikilink") are
examples of exhausting spells.*
