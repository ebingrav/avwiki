*Your character advances in power by gaining experience.
[Humans](/wiki/Humans "wikilink") need 1000 experience points for each
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") of experience. Other
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") need some percentage of 1000 exp
for each [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"), from 800 to 75000 exp points.*

*You gain experience by:*

` `[`killing`](/wiki/Kill "wikilink")` a mob`  
` being part of a group that kills a mob`  
` healing other players (under certain circumstances, as a `[`Priest`](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink")`)`

*You lose experience by:*

` `[`fleeing`](/wiki/Flee "wikilink")` from combat`  
` `[`recalling`](/wiki/Recall "wikilink")` out of combat`  
` being the target of certain spells`  
` dying`

*The experience you get from a kill depends on several things: how many
players are in your group; your [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") versus the
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") of the monster; how many of this monster have
been killed lately; and some random variation.*
