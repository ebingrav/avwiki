## Comments

How to use: Wear diamond, and you will have sanc for a few tics.

The nitty gritty: the item has it's own timer (maybe 5 tics?) that
counts down only when equipped on a tic. So... if you make a effort of
taking it off outside of battle and during regen, you will be able to
make a diamond last MUCH longer than normal.

See also [Suggested Buffing
Gear](Suggested_Spellcasting_Gear_#Suggested_Buffing_Gear.md "wikilink").
