Comments
--------

The negative [Dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink") modifier cancels the
[Armor Class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") modifier it grants, so you will
need to raise your [Dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink") in a different
manner to take advantage of the [Armor Class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")
modifier.
