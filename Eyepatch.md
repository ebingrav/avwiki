## Comments

The negative [Dexterity](Dexterity "wikilink") modifier cancels the
[Armor Class](Armor_Class "wikilink") modifier it grants, so you will
need to raise your [Dexterity](Dexterity "wikilink") in a different
manner to take advantage of the [Armor Class](Armor_Class "wikilink")
modifier.
