*Here stands the leader and mastermind of the veldri. The one who came
up with the fusion of the veldri into [golems](Golems.md "wikilink"),
Eylidre has been planning her assault on the
[duergar](Duergar.md "wikilink") for months. Though she holds contempt
for her fellow [drow](Drow.md "wikilink") for casting her out, her
hatred towards the [duergar](Duergar.md "wikilink") is even greater, as
she prepares for the assault against [King
Domi](King_Domi.md "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Eylidre, leader, veldri, drow.  
**[Level](Level.md "wikilink"):** 42.  
**[Alignment](Alignment.md "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[mage](Spellcasting_Mobs.md "wikilink"),
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs.md "wikilink"), [very
aggressive](Aggressive_Mobs.md "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](Detect_Hidden.md "wikilink"), [detect
invis](Detect_Invis.md "wikilink"), [hide](Hide.md "wikilink"),
[invis](Invis.md "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary.md "wikilink").

## Comments

One must [push](Push.md "wikilink")/[pull](Pull.md "wikilink") a crest
(visible only to those [detecting magic](Detect_Magic.md "wikilink")) in
order to open the trapdoor to [this
area](:Category:_Secrets_Of_The_Shrine.md "wikilink")'s lower level.

One must use [an onyx key](Onyx_Key.md "wikilink") from [Statue of
Cerberus](Statue_Of_Cerberus.md "wikilink") to
[unlock](Unlock.md "wikilink") the
[door](:Category:_Doors.md "wikilink") that leads to Eylidre's Bedroom.
