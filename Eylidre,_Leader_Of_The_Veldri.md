*Here stands the leader and mastermind of the veldri. The one who came
up with the fusion of the veldri into [golems](/wiki/Golems "wikilink"),
Eylidre has been planning her assault on the
[duergar](/wiki/Duergar "wikilink") for months. Though she holds contempt for
her fellow [drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") for casting her out, her hatred
towards the [duergar](/wiki/Duergar "wikilink") is even greater, as she
prepares for the assault against [King Domi](/wiki/King_Domi "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Eylidre, leader, veldri, drow.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 42.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[mage](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"), [very
aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [hide](Hide "wikilink"),
[invis](/wiki/Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").

Comments
--------

One must [push](/wiki/Push "wikilink")/[pull](Pull "wikilink") a crest
(visible only to those [detecting magic](/wiki/Detect_Magic "wikilink")) in
order to open the trapdoor to [this
area](/wiki/Category%3A_Secrets_Of_The_Shrine "wikilink")'s lower level.

One must use [an onyx key](/wiki/Onyx_Key "wikilink") from [Statue of
Cerberus](/wiki/Statue_Of_Cerberus "wikilink") to [unlock](Unlock "wikilink")
the [door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") that leads to Eylidre's
Bedroom.
