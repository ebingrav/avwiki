*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") reveals all manner of
[invisible](/wiki/Invis_Flag "wikilink"), [hidden](Hide_Flag "wikilink"), and
[sneaking](/wiki/Sneak_Flag "wikilink")
[creatures](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in the same
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") as you.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'faeire fog'.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used.
