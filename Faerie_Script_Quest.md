-   Retrieve [a black etched tablet](/wiki/Black_Etched_Tablet "wikilink")
    from [Lich Queen](/wiki/Lich_Queen "wikilink") in Astral.

<!-- -->

-   Give [a black etched tablet](/wiki/Black_Etched_Tablet "wikilink") to
    [Ashara](/wiki/Ashara "wikilink") on Thorngate, she will give you [a
    silken scarf](/wiki/Silken_Scarf "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Give [a silken scarf](/wiki/Silken_Scarf "wikilink") to
    [Ceilican](/wiki/Ceilican "wikilink") on Midgaard, he will give you [a
    strand of golden hair](/wiki/Strand_Of_Golden_Hair "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Give [a strand of golden hair](/wiki/Strand_Of_Golden_Hair "wikilink") to
    [Laz](/wiki/Laz "wikilink") on Thorngate, he will give you [a Faerie
    script](/wiki/Faerie_Script "wikilink").
