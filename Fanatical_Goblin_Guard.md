*This goblin seems almost as crazy as some of the earlier goblins you
met wandering the tunnels. However he seems more aware of his
surroundings, and is determined not to let you go any further. There is
a metal bottle from round his neck.*

**Keywords:** fanatical, goblin, guard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 8.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonically.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
