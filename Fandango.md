*When cast, your main weapon and your offhand fly from your hands to
wreak havoc upon your current target.*

*The weapons MIGHT return to your hand when the spell is complete. Or it
MIGHT fall to the ground, where it will be subject to scavengers,
stompers, and the infamous imp...*

*There are no reimbursements for weapons that you lose with this
spell...*

Prerequisite(s): [Dancing Weapon](/wiki/Dancing_Weapon "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") fandango.

Comments
--------

It costs very little mana, but has a slighly higher lag than say
brimstone or maelstrom. However, since psis worship Shizaga, which
reduces lag, it evens out.

See also: [Kinetic Weapon Control](/wiki/Kinetic_Weapon_Control "wikilink")
