This insignia is given by the [Far Hunter](/wiki/Far_Hunter "wikilink") in the
[Rim](/wiki/Category%3ARim "wikilink") after you prove your hunting prowess by
murdering the four sea demigods that dwell there.

It is a cosmetic insignia that slightly alters the results of the
[Religious Artifacts Hunting
Quest](/wiki/Religious_Artifacts_Hunting_Quest "wikilink") reward.

See the [Far Hunter Tattoo Quest](/wiki/Far_Hunter_Tattoo_Quest "wikilink").
