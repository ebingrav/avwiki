-   After arriving to the [Rim](/wiki/Category%3ARim "wikilink"), go wild.
-   Eventually you will have attracted the attention of one or more sea
    deities that dwell there.
-   Kill them all.
-   [Far Hunter](/wiki/Far_Hunter "wikilink") will want to tattoo your
    achievements onto your skin so find an appropriate utensil.
-   Gain the [Far Hunter Tattoo
    Insignia](/wiki/Far_Hunter_Tattoo_Insignia "wikilink").
