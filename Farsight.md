*With a bit of [druidic](/wiki/Category%3A_Druids "wikilink") magic upon the
eyes, one can temporarily gain the ability to focus farther while
scanning for trouble. Farsight, as this incantation is known, allows one
to [scan](/wiki/Scan "wikilink") in a single direction up to two
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") away.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'farsight'.
