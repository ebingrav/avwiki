*[Archers](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") are experienced wanderers and
travelers. By using this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), they
will consume less [movement points](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink") when they
walk. Note that as farstride is a walking
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), it is not compatible with the
[movement cost](/wiki/Movement_Costs "wikilink") reduction granted by
[flying](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Scout](/wiki/Scout "wikilink").

Syntax: farstride.
