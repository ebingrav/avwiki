*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") allows a
[person](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") to regenerate their
[hitpoints](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") at a much faster rate when
[sleeping](/wiki/Sleep_(command) "wikilink"). Once
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"), this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is automatic.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is highly
beneficial to [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") as it increases their
[hit-point](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") regeneration rates.
