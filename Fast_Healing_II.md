*This gives an additional boost of recovery while
[sleeping](/wiki/Sleep_(command) "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Fast Healing](/wiki/Fast_Healing "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), just like its
predecessor [Fast Healing](/wiki/Fast_Healing "wikilink"), is highly
beneficial to [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") as it increases their
[hit-point](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") regeneration rates.
