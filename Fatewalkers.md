Modifies armor class by -20 continuous.  
Modifies moves by -200 continuous.  
Modifies damage roll by 4 continuous.  
Bases: 10-12  
Levels: 51-52  
Weight: 2  
Area: [Cavern Of The Fates](/wiki/Cavern_Of_The_Fates "wikilink")  
Mob: [Atropos](/wiki/Atropos "wikilink"), [Clotho](/wiki/Clotho "wikilink"),
[Lachesis](/wiki/Lachesis "wikilink"). ([Ticket
Quest](/wiki/Ticket_Quest "wikilink"))  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

pp [Ulrich](/wiki/Ulrich "wikilink"), go n, w, 6n, 3s, 2s, 3e, 3s. This will
take to you a room in [Cavern Of The
Fates](/wiki/Cavern_Of_The_Fates "wikilink") titled "A Strange Rock", which is
west of the gypsy's wagon. This will be your base of operations as you
attempt to get the Fatewalkers. You need to go deeper into the cavern
and kill the 3 fates.

to get to [Atropos](/wiki/Atropos "wikilink"), who holds the [Golden Scissors
Of Fate](/wiki/Golden_Scissors_Of_Fate "wikilink"), go s, d, n, n, w, q. (q is
short for quaff, which is another word for drink). Kill her, take her
stuff, and quaff again to return to the "Strange Rock" room.

Next up do [Clotho](/wiki/Clotho "wikilink"), who holds the [Spindle Of Golden
Thread](/wiki/Spindle_Of_Golden_Thread "wikilink"). Directions to her are s,
d, 4n, 3w, 3n, 2w, s, q. Same deal, once its dead q to return to the
beginning.

Last fate is [Lachesis](/wiki/Lachesis "wikilink"), who holds the [Rod Of
Fate](/wiki/Rod_Of_Fate "wikilink"). Directions: s, d, 4n, 3w, 3n, w, 2n, 2e,
s, e, q

Once you have all 3 tickets, go to the gypsy and give one of them to
her. She'll reward you with the Fatewalkers.
