*A basic creation of the [Psionic](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink")
art, the fear spell allows the [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") to instill a
feeling of fright into the [enemy](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). Once
filled with fear, an [enemy](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") has trouble
aiming at its targets. Also, the [victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")
will be so frightened of danger as to be unable to use the
[rescue](Rescue "wikilink") [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). Not
all of the effects of the fear spell are detrimental to the
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). A frightened
[creature](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") is generally more wary and on
its guard, so its [saving throw](/wiki/Saving_Throw "wikilink") is improved.
The effects of this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") become more
dramatic as the [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") advances in
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") and can create a more forceful impression of
fear. Because this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
relies partly on manipulating an unaware
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")'s perception of reality, it can
only be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") when initiating combat.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'fear'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

[Psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") may find this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") useful for starting fights.
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") hardly ever use it (or do
they?).
