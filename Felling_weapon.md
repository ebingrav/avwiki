Syntax: cast 'felling weapon' (self only)

A felling weapon convinces your enemy that its blows are landing with
much greater force than they actually are. This can cause the opponent
to lose their footing and tumble onto the ground.

See also: KINETIC ENHANCERS, STUNNING WEAPON, DISTRACTING WEAPON,
DISABLING WEAPON, RESTRICTING WEAPON, CONSCIOUS WEAPON, INTELLIGENT
WEAPON
