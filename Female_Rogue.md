*The humanoid rogue flips back her hair, glaring at you from afar. In
her right hand she clutches a dagger, and in the left is a blackjack
poised and ready to use. She vanishes back into the shadows for a
second, her light green eyes the only component of her still visible.*

**Keywords:** female, rogue, shadows.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 32.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Category%3A_Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink"),
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[thief](/wiki/Thieving_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[protection good](/wiki/Protection_Good "wikilink"),
[hidden](/wiki/Hide "wikilink"), [sneak](Sneak "wikilink").
