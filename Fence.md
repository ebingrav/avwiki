*[Haggle](/wiki/Haggle "wikilink") and Fence are similar 'shopping' skills,
and let you use your natural ability to finagle a better deal from
almost anyone. Fence can help you to [sell](/wiki/Sell "wikilink") otherwise
worthless [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). Both skills are
automatic, and of course must be [practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") before
being used.*

Prerequisite(s): [Haggle](/wiki/Haggle "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
