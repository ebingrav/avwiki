*A [paladin](/wiki/Category%3A_Paladins "wikilink")'s faith is such that their
God may drive them into a religious fervor, enhancing their ability in
combat. This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is not compatible
with any [frenzy](/wiki/Frenzy "wikilink") or [berserking](Berserk "wikilink")
abilities and it will cease if the
[paladin](/wiki/Category%3A_Paladins "wikilink") becomes
[frenzied](/wiki/Frenzy "wikilink") or [berserked](Berserk "wikilink") whilst
[affected](/wiki/Affects "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Bless](/wiki/Bless "wikilink"), [Enhanced
Damage](/wiki/Enhanced_Damage "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'fervor'.

See also [Frenzy](/wiki/Frenzy "wikilink"), [Racial
Frenzy](/wiki/Racial_Frenzy "wikilink").
