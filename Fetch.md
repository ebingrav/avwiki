*Fetch is a [telekinetic
spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") that forms
a magical lasso which can carry small, inanimate
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") to the caster, sometimes from
several [rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") away. However, you can
only fetch [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that are within the
same [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"), and under a certain
[weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"). There are also certain magical
properties of [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") and
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that may help, hinder or
outright prevent a fetch attempt. Be as specific as possible with the
argument of this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). Use full words
instead of abbreviations, and use more than one keyword for the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") if possible. This will help to
avoid mix-ups.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'fetch'
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.
