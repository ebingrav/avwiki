*Go down swinging with the Final Fury Rite! When reduced to 10%
[hps](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink"), the
[Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") gets a bonus to damage.
Final Fury will not function if the
[Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") has better than -750
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") at [hero](Category%3A_Hero "wikilink"), or
-1250 [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") at [Lord](Lord "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
