*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") inflicts damage on the
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). Damage spell power is determined
by [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") unless otherwise specified. All basic
attack and area spells are more powerful for in-class users who do not
have to fear a counterattack thanks to [a group-mate tanking the
target](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'fireball'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This single-target damage [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
fourth of ten that [mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") between [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 1 and
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 50. [Mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may
find it useful until they [learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") [Lightning
Bolt](/wiki/Lightning_Bolt "wikilink") or when they are low on
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of many other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it at [hero](Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") but will find it fairly useless to
them.

See also [Fireball Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Fireball_Gear "wikilink").
