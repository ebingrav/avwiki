The ancient history is mostly lost, but the modern story of the
firedrakes began when a stone was moved and an insidious hissing was
heard. The poor discoverer realized her mistake too late! Steam poured
out from the earth, boiling her flesh within her armor as draconian
beings escaped into the air. Firedrakes were upon the land.

The firedrakes who sought to colonize Midgaardia Above quickly searched
for spawning grounds. The growing hatchlings quickly spread, inspiring
fear and hatred from the existing races. War was soon waged to eradicate
the firedrakes.

By the end of the war, all but one of the species were slain, an ancient
matriarch who wished only to see her species survive. With that hope,
she entrusted her unhatched eggs to the Gods and firedrakes still remain
among us.

This is an evolutionary race. They can go through growth spurts at the
following levels once they decide to evolve:

Lev.25-&gt;Hero(1)-&gt;Hero(250)-&gt;Hero(500)-&gt;Lord(1)-&gt;Lord(250)-&gt;Lord(500)-&gt;Lord(750)
