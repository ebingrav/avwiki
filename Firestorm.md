*FIRESTORM washes the entire [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") with
flames, engulfing all of your [enemies](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in
fire. All basic attack and area spells are more powerful for in-class
users who do not have to fear a counterattack thanks to [a group-mate
tanking the target](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Magma Blast](/wiki/Magma_Blast "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'firestorm'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is hardly ever used.
