*This is one of the unique fishing spears used by the fishermen of
[Icewolf](:Category:_Icewolf "wikilink"). It is a three-pronged spear.
The two side prongs are longer than the center one and have points that
turn in. Once the center point spears the fish the side prongs hold it
in place as it is pulled from the water.*

[`Damage`](Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 15 to 41-42 (average 28).`  
`Modifies `[`damage`` ``roll`](Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 3 continuous.`
