*When this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"), a quick prayer for divine intervention is
followed by a searing magical bolt of flame that mercilessly strikes a
single target. All basic attack and area spells are more powerful for
in-class users who do not have to fear a counterattack thanks to [a
group-mate tanking the target](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'flamestrike'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is practically never used.

See also [Flamestrike Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Flamestrike_Gear "wikilink").
