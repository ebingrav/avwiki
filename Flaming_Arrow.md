This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") allows the
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") to create [flaming
arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Flaming_Warheads "wikilink"). You will need
progressively higher [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") [fletching
kits](/wiki/Fletching_Tools_And_Materials "wikilink") to make the higher
[level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") [arrow](Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink")
items.

Prerequisite(s): [Fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink").

Syntax: [fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink")
&lt;[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink")/[bolts](Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink")/[stones](Category%3A_Stones "wikilink")&gt;
flaming.
