*The minion of hell is manlike, and flaming. It has horns and a horribly
disfigured face. Its skin is beet red, and his fingers and toes end in
terrible talons. It looks at you, and with a fiery laughter, it says:
'So, you are foolish enough to enter the circle. You may win this
battle, but you will not stop our plan!*

**Keywords:** flaming, beast, hell.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 35.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[fire-breather](/wiki/Breathing_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
