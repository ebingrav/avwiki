*With knowledge comes power and with power come nice ways to make things
go your way. One is the perfect little flash which when timed well can
[blind](/wiki/Blindness "wikilink") everyone in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). This leaves you free to do some
serious blood letting and soul wrenching without prying eyes.*

Prerequisite(s): [Blindness](/wiki/Blindness "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'flash'.

This spell has a low (5%?) chance to [blind](/wiki/Blindness "wikilink") mobs
that aren't already attacking the caster.

If this spell is cast on mobs that are already attacking on a caster, it
is guaranteed to blind them. One way to ensure that this happens is to
[Quicken](/wiki/Quicken "wikilink") 9 and cast [Burning
Hands](/wiki/Burning_Hands "wikilink") before casting Flash. If there's only
one mob that you'd like to Flash, [Quicken](/wiki/Quicken "wikilink") 9
[Fear](/wiki/Fear "wikilink") is a good way to start the fight.

Note that [Wizards](/wiki/Category%3AWizards "wikilink") learn this spell
through a [Spellbook](/wiki/Category%3ASpellbooks "wikilink"), and that is it
out-of-class, and therefore has double the lag of s
[Sorcerer's](/wiki/Category%3ASorcerer "wikilink") Flash.
