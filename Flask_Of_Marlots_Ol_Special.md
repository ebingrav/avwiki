*[Marlot](/wiki/Marlot "wikilink")'s own home brewed ale is infamous
throughout [Mt Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink") even though few
have ever actually drunk some. It is rumoured to be a panacea for all
ills, a drink so strong, it will inebriate the strongest and yet leave
no hangover.*
