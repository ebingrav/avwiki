their shoulder, this [skill](:Category:_Skills "wikilink") is used by
[archers](:Category:_Archers "wikilink") to fire a last
[arrow](:Category:_Arrows "wikilink") when [fleeing](Flee "wikilink")
from combat.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
