*Sometimes little more than a desperate [shot](/wiki/Shoot "wikilink") over
their shoulder, this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is used by
[archers](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") to fire a last
[arrow](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink") when [fleeing](Flee "wikilink")
from combat.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
