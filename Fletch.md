*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") gives an
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") the know-how to make his own
[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink"). All he needs is some time, and
a [fletching tool kit](/wiki/Fletching_Tools_And_Materials "wikilink"). Making
[ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink") is time consuming, but
much cheaper than [buying](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") it, and less dangerous than
getting it from enemies. Either normal
[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink") or crossbow
[bolts](/wiki/Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink") can be made, although the
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") must learn other
[skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") to make more complex
[types](/wiki/Category%3A_Warhead_Types "wikilink") of
[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink"). If making [poison
arrows](/wiki/Poison_Arrows "wikilink"), you must have a [poison
item](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") in your inventory, and specify it
in the [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") line. [Fletching
kits](/wiki/Fletching_Tools_And_Materials "wikilink") will wear out. Their
longevity is based on their [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"). Also note
you will need higher [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink")
[kits](/wiki/Fletching_Tools_And_Materials "wikilink") to make the higher
level [arrow types](/wiki/Category%3A_Warhead_Types "wikilink"). It is
possible through [practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") to improve at making
particular [arrow types](/wiki/Category%3A_Warhead_Types "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Archery Lore](/wiki/Archery_Lore "wikilink").
