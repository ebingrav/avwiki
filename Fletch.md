*This [skill](:Category:_Skills "wikilink") gives an
[archer](:Category:_Archers "wikilink") the know-how to make his own
[arrows](:Category:_Arrows "wikilink"). All he needs is some time, and a
[fletching tool kit](Fletching_Tools_And_Materials "wikilink"). Making
[ammunition](:Category:_Ammunition "wikilink") is time consuming, but
much cheaper than [buying](Buy "wikilink") it, and less dangerous than
getting it from enemies. Either normal
[arrows](:Category:_Arrows "wikilink") or crossbow
[bolts](:Category:_Bolts "wikilink") can be made, although the
[archer](:Category:_Archers "wikilink") must learn other
[skills](:Category:_Skills "wikilink") to make more complex
[types](:Category:_Warhead_Types "wikilink") of
[arrows](:Category:_Arrows "wikilink"). If making [poison
arrows](Poison_Arrows "wikilink"), you must have a [poison
item](:Category:_Poisons "wikilink") in your inventory, and specify it
in the [command](:Category:_Commands "wikilink") line. [Fletching
kits](Fletching_Tools_And_Materials "wikilink") will wear out. Their
longevity is based on their [level](Object_Level "wikilink"). Also note
you will need higher [level](Object_Level "wikilink")
[kits](Fletching_Tools_And_Materials "wikilink") to make the higher
level [arrow types](:Category:_Warhead_Types "wikilink"). It is possible
through [practice](Practice "wikilink") to improve at making particular
[arrow types](:Category:_Warhead_Types "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Archery Lore](Archery_Lore "wikilink").
