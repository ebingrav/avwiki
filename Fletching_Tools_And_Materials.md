Comments
--------

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must
[hold](/wiki/Hold "wikilink") fletching kits in order to
[fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink") [arrows](Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink"),
[bolts](/wiki/Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink"),
[darts](/wiki/Category%3A_Darts "wikilink"), and [sling
stones](/wiki/Category%3A_Stones "wikilink").

Fletching kits can be purchased from
[Kreigstadt](/wiki/Category%3A_Hamlet_Of_Kreigstadt "wikilink")'s [village
bowmaster](/wiki/Village_Bowmaster "wikilink"); he only sells
[level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink")-51 kits (which are required to
[fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink") [splinter
arrows](/wiki/Splinter_Arrows "wikilink")). If you are low on money, you can
kill [village watchmen](/wiki/Village_Watchman "wikilink") (who wander about
the area around this [bowmaster](/wiki/Village_Bowmaster "wikilink")) but many
of their kits are below [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") 51. [Drider
hunters](/wiki/Drider_Hunter "wikilink") also have fletching kits, some even
above [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") 51, but [their
area](/wiki/Category%3A_Idolatry_Of_Lloth "wikilink") can be a very dangerous
(due to [aggie](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink")
[snipers](/wiki/Sniping_Mobs "wikilink")). You can also buy [splinter
arrows](/wiki/Splinter_Arrows "wikilink") from
[Kreigstadt](/wiki/Category%3A_Hamlet_Of_Kreigstadt "wikilink")'s
[bowmaster](/wiki/Village_Bowmaster "wikilink") rather than
[fletching](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink") them; he charges about 30k gold for 500
[splinter arrows](/wiki/Splinter_Arrows "wikilink") (less when
[haggling](/wiki/Haggle "wikilink")).
