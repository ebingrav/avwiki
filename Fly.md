*This can be used on oneself or another.
[Flying](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink") gives you several advantages, such as
reduced [movement costs](/wiki/Movement_Costs "wikilink"), and allow you to go
through [airborne](/wiki/Air_Terrain "wikilink") or
[watery](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink")
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'fly'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This is a very useful [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
for all [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") (who can) to
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"). It is superfluous for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") affected by
[Levitation](/wiki/Levitation "wikilink"). It is included in
[Foci](Foci "wikilink"). See [Flying Flag](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink") for
further information.

See also [Gear That Applies
'Fly'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Fly' "wikilink") and [Fly
Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Fly_Gear "wikilink").
