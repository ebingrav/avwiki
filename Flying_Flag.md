Flying [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") may hover above
[water](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink"), go up in certain places, access
certain rooms, [use less moves](/wiki/Movement_Costs "wikilink"), and more.

There are many ways to fly.
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of some
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") possess [an innate ability to
fly](/wiki/Racial_Fly "wikilink") for certain lengths of time.
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of some
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") to [levitate](Levitation "wikilink"), to
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") [Fly](Fly "wikilink") on both themselves and
others, or both. Even [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
without such [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") may
consume [certain objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellcasting_Gear "wikilink") like
[potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink") to make themselves fly
temporarily. And some [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") (like [griffon
wings bracers](/wiki/Griffon_Wings_Bracer "wikilink")) may cause either
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") that [wear](Wear "wikilink") it to
fly until they [remove](/wiki/Remove "wikilink") it. Some
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") may also have a
"[flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Flags "wikilink")" that causes them to fly
permanently unless [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") a [spell](Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") like
[Earthbind](/wiki/Earthbind "wikilink") on them.
