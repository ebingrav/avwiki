*This focusing of magical energies allows the caster to simultaneously
cast [giant strength](/wiki/Giant_Strength "wikilink"), [stone
skin](/wiki/Stone_Skin "wikilink"), [shield](Shield "wikilink"),
[fly](/wiki/Fly "wikilink"), and [pass door](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink"). The Foci
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") allows the
[casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") of a group of spells at once. The amount of
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") it costs is balanced by the speed of
casting a variety of complex spells simultaneously. NOTE: the foci
affect which show up on your [affects](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") list is
merely a placeholder and does not actually modify anything.*

Prerequisite(s): [Giant Strength](/wiki/Giant_Strength "wikilink"), [Pass
Door](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'foci'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") will find this
[mage](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") "macro"
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), like both
[Fortiudes](/wiki/Fortitudes "wikilink") and either [Aegis](Aegis "wikilink")
or [Awen](/wiki/Awen "wikilink"), highly valuable for
[buffing](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")
either themselves or others.
