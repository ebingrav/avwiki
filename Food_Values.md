[Food](:Category:_Foods "wikilink")-[type](:Category:_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](:Category:_Objects "wikilink") have two
[values](:Category:_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **Nutritiousness:** This value determines for how many
    [tics](Tics "wikilink") this [food](:Category:_Foods "wikilink")
    will keep hunger satiated for those who [eat](Eat "wikilink") it.
    Because Avatar MUD's [characters](:Category:_Characters "wikilink")
    no longer experience hunger, this value is currently irrelevant.

<!-- -->

-   **Toxicity:** This value determines whether or not this
    [food](:Category:_Foods "wikilink") will
    [poison](Poison_(spell) "wikilink") those who [eat](Eat "wikilink")
    it.

See also [Foods](:Category:_Foods "wikilink").
