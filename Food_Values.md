[Food](/wiki/Category%3A_Foods "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have two
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **Nutritiousness:** This value determines for how many
    [tics](/wiki/Tics "wikilink") this [food](/wiki/Category%3A_Foods "wikilink")
    will keep hunger satiated for those who [eat](/wiki/Eat "wikilink") it.
    Because Avatar MUD's [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
    no longer experience hunger, this value is currently irrelevant.

<!-- -->

-   **Toxicity:** This value determines whether or not this
    [food](/wiki/Category%3A_Foods "wikilink") will
    [poison](/wiki/Poison_(spell) "wikilink") those who
    [eat](/wiki/Eat "wikilink") it.

See also [Foods](/wiki/Category%3A_Foods "wikilink").
