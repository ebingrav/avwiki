[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") within
[lawful](/wiki/Category%3A_Lawful_Areas "wikilink")
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") will cause any players within
them, upon being seen by either a [townguard](/wiki/Townguard_Mobs "wikilink")
or a [citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink"), to become
[outlawed](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink").
