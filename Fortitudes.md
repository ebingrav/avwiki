*By concentrating his willpower, a
[psionicist](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") acquires the effects of
[adrenaline pump](/wiki/Adrenaline_Pump "wikilink"),
[anticipate](/wiki/Anticipate "wikilink"),
[biofeedback](/wiki/Biofeedback "wikilink"), [calcify
flesh](/wiki/Calcify_Flesh "wikilink"), [mental
barrier](/wiki/Mental_Barrier "wikilink"), [body
brace](/wiki/Body_Brace "wikilink"), [displacement](/wiki/Displacement "wikilink"),
and [energy shield](/wiki/Energy_Shield "wikilink"). The fortitude
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") allows the
[casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") of a group of spells at once. The amount of
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") it costs is balanced by the speed of
casting a variety of complex spells simultaneously. NOTE: the fortitude
affect which shows up on your [affects](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") list is
merely a placeholder and does not actually modify anything.*

Prerequisite(s): [Displacement](/wiki/Displacement "wikilink"), [Energy
Shield](/wiki/Energy_Shield "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'fortitudes'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") will find this
[psionicist](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") "macro"
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), like both
[Foci](/wiki/Foci "wikilink") and either [Aegis](Aegis "wikilink") or
[Awen](/wiki/Awen "wikilink"), highly valuable for
[buffing](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")
either themselves or others.
