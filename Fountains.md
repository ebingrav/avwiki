[Objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink") are practically always
permanent fixtures of [areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") that
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may not [pick
up](/wiki/Get "wikilink"). Fountains were more useful in days of yore when
Avatar's [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") could suffer
from hunger and thirst. Certain fountains remain useful, however, as
fountains can do more than just provide a steady source of one of many
[types](/wiki/Drink_Container_Values "wikilink") of liquid refreshment. A
given fountain may or may not also do one of the following whenever a
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
[drinks](/wiki/Drink "wikilink") from it: (1) [cast](Cast "wikilink") a
predictable [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") on this
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") (or possibly one of two
different [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") dependent upon this
drinker's [alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink")), (2)
[poison](/wiki/Poison_(spell) "wikilink") this
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"), (3) instantly transport
this [character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") to a certain other
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"), and (4) disappear (until its
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") repops) while a certain
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") appears in its place. Each fountain's
individual qualities are determined by its
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")-specific
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").
