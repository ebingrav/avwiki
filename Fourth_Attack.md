*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") lets you attack more than
once during a combat round. Use of this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is automatic once you have
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it. The degree of expertise this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") requires is attainable by only a
few highly specialized fighters.*

Prerequisite(s): [Third Attack](/wiki/Third_Attack "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
