*Although any Mindbender could write you a thesis on the subtle
differences between Fracture and Mindwipe, the important things
(according to the scattered accounts of survivors) are that Fracture
doesn't stun but is surgeable.*

Prerequisites: [Mindwipe](/wiki/Mindwipe "wikilink")

Syntax: c 'fracture' enemy
