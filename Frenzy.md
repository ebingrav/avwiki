*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") puts the target into a
frenzy, raising its [hit](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink") and
[damroll](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink") and raising (worsening) the target's
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'frenzy'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

See also [Racial Frenzy](/wiki/Racial_Frenzy "wikilink").

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in
[Awen](/wiki/Awen "wikilink") but *not* in [Aegis](Aegis "wikilink"). Some
[tanks](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink") prefer to run with Frenzy and some
[tanks](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink") prefer to run without it. Like
[Sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink"), Frenzy has a relatively short
duration compared with other [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").

See also [Frenzy Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Frenzy_Gear "wikilink").
