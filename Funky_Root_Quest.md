Manduck Root Hunt Reward: 250 coins

If you visit [Liam](/wiki/Liam "wikilink")'s store, you can look at a paper
(LOOK PAPER). This paper is a notice from [Liam](/wiki/Liam "wikilink"), and
requests that you find a Manduck Root for [Liam](/wiki/Liam "wikilink").

You can find the root in Wayside. Search for Grubb, the kobold mining
chief, and the [root](/wiki/Funky_Root "wikilink") is in the pile of dirt near
him. Give the root to Liam. The reward is a handful of coins, worth 250
gold.
