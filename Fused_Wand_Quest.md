*[A ranger](/wiki/Ranger "wikilink") has found [a broken
part](/wiki/Broken_Wand "wikilink") of [a wand](/wiki/Fused_Wand "wikilink")—[a
wand](/wiki/Fused_Wand "wikilink") that once belonged to [a friend of
his](/wiki/Moss_Shrouded_Man "wikilink")—in northwestern [Shadow
Forest](/wiki/Category%3A_Shadow_Forest "wikilink"). Perhaps he could do
something with it if he were to receive [its other
part](/wiki/Broken_Wand "wikilink")...*

From [white haired druid](/wiki/White_Haired_Druid "wikilink"):

-   Go n, 3w, 4n, e, n, w, s, w, [push](/wiki/Push "wikilink") a cover of
    moss, w, s.

<!-- -->

-   [Kill](/wiki/Kill "wikilink") [a moss shrouded
    man](/wiki/Moss_Shrouded_Man "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   [Get](Get "wikilink") [a broken wand](/wiki/Broken_Wand "wikilink") from
    his corpse.

<!-- -->

-   Go n, [push](/wiki/Push "wikilink") a cover of moss, 2e, n, e, s, w, 4s,
    e, s.

-   [Give](Give "wikilink") [a broken wand](/wiki/Broken_Wand "wikilink") to
