2014
----

-   **2014 Jan:** Portions of
    [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3AMidgaard "wikilink") revised. All
    [Houses](/wiki/Category%3AHouses "wikilink") grandfathered (6 subareas).

2013
----

-   **2013 Nov:** Area [Arx Nivea](/wiki/Category%3AArx_Nivea "wikilink") was
    given an overhaul.
-   **2013 Oct:** Area [River Etuan](/wiki/Category%3ARiver_Etuan "wikilink")
    was added.
-   **2013 Sep:** Lord quest involving
    [Allegaagse](/wiki/Allegaagse "wikilink") in Thorngate was added.
-   **2013 Aug:** Area [Forge](/wiki/Category%3AForge "wikilink") (eha)
    was added. Area [Shadowlands](/wiki/Shadowlands "wikilink") (Lord)
    was added.
-   **2013 Jul:** Lord area [Tarterus
    Proper](/wiki/Category%3ATarterus_Proper "wikilink") made easier for very
    small groups.
-   **2013 Jun:** Lord skill [volley](volley "wikilink") added. Hero
    area [Rapture](/wiki/Category%3ARapture "wikilink") was added. Changes to
    [the Outland plane](/wiki/Category%3AOutland "wikilink") (proper and
    Commander subarea) and changes to [Githzerai
    Keep](/wiki/Category%3AGithzerai_Keep "wikilink")
-   **2013 May:** [Companions](/wiki/Companions "wikilink") for low morts
    added (help companions)
-   **2013 Apr:** Cleric pantheon spells
    [Discordia](/wiki/Discordia "wikilink"), [Divine
    Adjutant](/wiki/Divine_Adjutant "wikilink"), [Sylvan
    Benediction](/wiki/Sylvan_Benediction "wikilink") and [Divine
    Grace](/wiki/Divine_Grace "wikilink") added. Lowmort area [Arcana
    Excelsis](/wiki/Category%3AArcana_Excelsis "wikilink") added.
-   **2013 Mar:** Cleric pantheon spell [Artificer
    Blessing](/wiki/Artificer_Blessing "wikilink") added. [Chromatic
    Dragons](/wiki/Chromatic_Dragons "wikilink") unveiled.
-   **2013 Feb:** Hero area [Rim](/wiki/Category%3ARim "wikilink") was added.

2012
----

-   **2012 Dec:** Low mortal areas
    [Zodiac](/wiki/Category%3AZodiac "wikilink") and [World
    Tree](/wiki/Category%3AWorld_Tree "wikilink") implemented. The area
    [Descent To Hell](/wiki/Category%3ADescent_To_Hell "wikilink")
    was grandfathered. [Bonkers!
    Quest](/wiki/Bonkers!_Quest "wikilink") added. [Elemental
    Giants](/wiki/Giants#Elemental_Giants "wikilink") unveiled.
-   **2012 Nov:** Hero area [Arx Nivea](/wiki/Category%3AArx_Nivea "wikilink")
    added with it's notable [Demonic Intent
    Quest](/wiki/Dark_Acolyte_Quest "wikilink"), [Great Summoning
    Quest](/wiki/Great_Summoning_Quest "wikilink") implemented.
-   **2012 Oct:** [Racial Resistance](/wiki/Racial_Resistance "wikilink") and
    damage types implemented. [Solace
    Patrol](/wiki/Solace_Patrol_Quest "wikilink") and [Mob
    Factory](/wiki/Category%3AMob_Factory "wikilink") revised, [Order's Last
    Mission](/wiki/Order's_Last_Mission_Quest "wikilink") implemented.
-   **2012 Sept:** Hero area [Glyntaff
    Pass](/wiki/Category%3AGlyntaff_Pass "wikilink") was added.
-   **2012 May:** [Miraars](/wiki/Miraars "wikilink") were changed to a
    creatable race, with increased TNL. [Morgue](/wiki/Morgue "wikilink")
    was implemented. Clerics received first in a series of new spells -
    [Unholy Rampage](/wiki/Unholy_Rampage "wikilink"). Area [Sun God
    Altar](/wiki/Category%3ASun_God_Altar "wikilink") received a facelift.
-   **2012 Apr:** Area [Bo'Vul's
    Vault](/wiki/Category%3ABo'Vul's_Vault "wikilink") was added, with
    its sub-areas.
-   **2012 Jan:** [Rebirth](/wiki/Rebirth "wikilink") became available.

2011
----

-   **2011 Aug:** Area [Exploration
    Outpost](/wiki/Category%3AExploration_Outpost "wikilink") was added to
    [Eragora](/wiki/Category%3AEragora "wikilink").
-   **2011 Sep:** Area [Sem Vida](/wiki/Category%3ASem_Vida "wikilink") (eha)
    was added.
-   **2011 May:** A new continent
    ([Eragora](/wiki/Category%3AEragora "wikilink")) was added with many new
    areas as a result of the area building contest. Eleven new areas
    added.<sup>1</sup>
-   **2011 Mar:** Area [The Fungal
    Forest](/wiki/Category%3AFungal_Forest "wikilink") (hero) was added. After
    a series of events the areas [Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3AOfcol "wikilink")
    and [Obsidian Tower](/wiki/Category%3AObsidian_Tower "wikilink") have been
    replaced with [Cathracharraig](/wiki/Category%3ACathracharraig "wikilink")
    and [Helikon
    Tower](/wiki/Category%3AHelikon_Tower "wikilink") respectively.
    Additionally the areas [Ofcol Refugee
    Camp](/wiki/Category%3AOfcol_Refugee_Camp "wikilink"), [Hospice Refugee
    Camp](/wiki/Category%3AHospice_Refugee_Camp "wikilink"), and [Daingean
    Machadae](/wiki/Category%3ADaingean_Machadae "wikilink") were added. The
    area [Bard's House](/wiki/Category%3ABard's_House "wikilink") was
    also removed.
-   **2011 Feb:** Area [The
    Deepways](/wiki/Category%3ADeepways "wikilink") (hero) was added.
-   **2011 Jan:** Area [Fortress of
    Kra](/wiki/Category%3AFortress_Of_Kra "wikilink") (hero) was added and
    [Caverns Of Despair](/wiki/Category%3ACaverns_Of_Despair "wikilink")
    was removed. Area [Pariah's
    Paradise](/wiki/Category%3APariah's_Paradise "wikilink") (hero) was added.

2010
----

-   **2010 Nov:** Areas [Drow City](/wiki/Category%3ADrow_City "wikilink") and
    [Air Of Avatar](/wiki/Category%3AAir_Of_Avatar "wikilink") were removed
    and [The Airways](/wiki/Category%3AAirways "wikilink") (14-19), [Graw
    Akkuurattaan](/wiki/Category%3AGraw_Akkuurattaan "wikilink") (18-23), and
    [Tiureess](/wiki/Category%3ATiureess "wikilink") (23-28) were added.
-   **2010 Jan:** [Patriarchs'
    Gulch](/wiki/Category%3APatriarchs'_Gulch "wikilink") (lord) and [Abbaye
    Du Livre](/wiki/Category%3AAbbaye_Du_Livre "wikilink") (hero) areas
    were added.

2008
----

-   **2008 Dec:** [Firedrakes](/wiki/Firedrakes "wikilink") have been
    added back.
-   **2008 Nov:** [Circus](/wiki/Category%3ACircus "wikilink") removed and
    [Carnival](/wiki/Category%3ACarnival "wikilink") is added.
-   **2008 Oct:** [Lesser Imps](/wiki/Lesser_Imps "wikilink") added.
    [Verbits](/wiki/Verbits "wikilink") are removed (no word on
    [Firedrakes](/wiki/Firedrakes "wikilink")). The quest/area [Diplomatic
    Corps](/wiki/Category%3ADiplomatic_Corps "wikilink") is added.
-   **2008 Aug:** New lowmort (31-40) area [Defender's
    Graveyard](/wiki/Category%3ADefender's_Graveyard "wikilink") is added.
    [Graveyard](/wiki/Category%3AGraveyard "wikilink") is removed.
-   **2008 May:** New (elite) hero area [Aculeata
    Jatha-La](/wiki/Category%3AAculeata_Jatha-La "wikilink") is added.
-   **2008 Apr:** New lowmort (41-50)/hero area [Citadel Of
    Arcanists](/wiki/Category%3ACitadel_Of_Arcanists "wikilink") is added. New
    race [Miraars](/wiki/Miraars "wikilink") added.

2007
----

-   **2007 Dec:** New hero manifest item [Mega's Spark In A
    Bottle](/wiki/Mega's_Spark_In_A_Bottle "wikilink") is added.
-   **2007 Aug:** New hero area [Alpha
    Thule](/wiki/Category%3AAlpha_Thule "wikilink") is added.
-   **2007 May:** New (elite) hero area [Abishai's
    Pass](/wiki/Category%3AAbishai's_Pass "wikilink") is added.
-   **2007 Apr:** The race [harpies](/wiki/Harpies "wikilink") is added
-   **2007 Feb:** The prestige class
    [Bladedancer](/wiki/Category%3ABladedancers "wikilink") is added
-   **2007 Jan:** Outland.org gets a spiffy new makeover!

2006
----

-   **2006 Oct:** Added [Stormlords](/wiki/Category%3AStormlords "wikilink")
    prestige class.
-   **2006 Jan:** Added [leadership
    skills](/wiki/Category%3ALeadership_Skills "wikilink").
-   **All 2006:** Added and fixed numerous spells, skills and
    [Legend](/wiki/Category%3ALegend "wikilink") features.

2005
----

-   **2005 Dec:** Ominous "big bug fix." No further explanation.
-   **2005 Jun:** Added [Bodyguards](/wiki/Category%3ABodyguards "wikilink")
    prestige class.
-   **2005 Jun:** The prestige class
    [Fusiliers](/wiki/Category%3AFusiliers "wikilink") is added.
-   **2005 May:** [Wizards](/wiki/Category%3AWizards "wikilink") becomes a
    prestige class for [Mage](/wiki/Category%3AMages "wikilink")
