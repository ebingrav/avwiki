*Used by the fearsome "Fish Speakers", the Myrmillo, the gaulan shield
was made of oak then wrapped in studded leather, where the symbol of the
Fish Speakers was branded on. It was excellent in deflecting melee
attacks, but didn't fair as well against piercing attacks.*

[`Armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 4-6.`  
`Modifies `[`save` `vs`
`fire`](/wiki/Saving_Throw "wikilink")` by -3 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`save` `vs`
`lightning`](/wiki/Saving_Throw "wikilink")` by -3 continuous.`
