*These interesting gauntlets empower their wearer with the ability to
use weapons that they normally would not possess. A merchant discovered
them in his travels, and found that they were quite useful in allowing
him to aid his guards in defending his goods.*

[`Armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 1.`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`  
`It improves archery accuracy.`  
`It improves chances of archery critical hits.`
