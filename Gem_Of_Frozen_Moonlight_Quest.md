This quest starts in [Odevar Hills](/wiki/Category%3A_Odevar_Hills "wikilink")
at the [Huge Troll](/wiki/Huge_Troll "wikilink"). You can teleport or walk.
Once there check out her description.

From [Huge Troll](/wiki/Huge_Troll "wikilink")

-   Walk e d s, get the [Stained Blanket](/wiki/Stained_Blanket "wikilink")
    from the woolen blanket on the ground. WARNING. There are several
    aggie mobs in this room.

<!-- -->

-   Find the Merchant near
    [Osreva](/wiki/Osreva,_King_Of_The_Odevar "wikilink"). From Osreva he is s
    d e s. Give the blanket to him and receive Huge Armor.

<!-- -->

-   Return to the [Huge Troll](/wiki/Huge_Troll "wikilink") and give it
    to her. She will then give you a [Marked
    Bone](/wiki/Marked_Bone "wikilink") and instructions to go to Mt Durr and
    find the [Troll Spy](/wiki/Troll_Spy "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Give the [Marked Bone](/wiki/Marked_Bone "wikilink") to the spy in return
    for a Square of soft skin.

<!-- -->

-   Return to the [Huge Troll](/wiki/Huge_Troll "wikilink") and give her the
    skin and receive the [Gem Of Frozen
    Moonlight](/wiki/Gem_Of_Frozen_Moonlight "wikilink").
