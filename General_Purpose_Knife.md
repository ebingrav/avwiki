*This knife has a nice bone handle. The blade is 9 inches long. One edge
is serrated. A blood groove runs down the middle of the blade running
from the handle almost to the tip. Both handle and blade are stained
from skinning and preparing crocodiles.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 9-10 to 25-26 (average 17).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 2 continuous.`
