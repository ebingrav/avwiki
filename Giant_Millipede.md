*The millipede is quite a bit larger than you. I don't know if you'd
want to mess with it. It waves its antennae at you as if to smell you.
It licks its mandibles and looks at you with big black shiny eyes. It
looks like you will have to fight it. There is a long key with a
millipede simple design on it.*

**Keywords:** millipede.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 50.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.

[cannibal](/wiki/Corpse-Eating_Mobs "wikilink"),
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Category%3A_Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink").
