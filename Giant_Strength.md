*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") increases the
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") of the target
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") up to 2x his normal
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'giant strength'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This is a very useful [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
for all [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") (who can) to
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"). It increases its target
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")'s
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") by 4, though not more than 3 above its
target's maximum [trainable](/wiki/Train "wikilink")
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink"). It is superfluous for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") affected by [Enhanced
Strength](/wiki/Enhanced_Strength "wikilink"). It is included in
[Foci](/wiki/Foci "wikilink").

See also [Giant Strength
Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Giant_Strength_Gear "wikilink").
