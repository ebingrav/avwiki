Comments
--------

Enter the window next to [Eschard](/wiki/Eschard "wikilink"). Then go north,
west, south.

The mob is non-aggie and the room is safe to sleep in; no other mobs
will wander in.

The stones are [explosive sling
stones](/wiki/Explosive_Sling_Stones "wikilink"). There are 7 of them on the
mob.
