*A young [human](/wiki/Humans "wikilink") of a quiet disposition, this
[shopkeeper](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink") sells his wares to the
passersby. Having a gift with glass has made him most popular in these
parts, and his smile is warm and gentle, if not a touch reserved.*

**Keywords:** Giles, man.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** ??.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[shopkeeper](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
