*Ginormous Dingersen is a "world famous" explorer and wizard, who has
come to Eragora to research the history of this place, and is equally
interested in finding answers and treasure of great magic. Short in
stature, this deep gnome has an aura of power that can't be ignored.*

**Keywords:** wizard, explorer, ginormous  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 125.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[protection evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

Quest](/wiki/Deep-Sea_Diver_Quest "wikilink").
