*A fairly standard short sword, the Gladius is double-edged, with a
point suitable for stabbing. Though effective offensively, it has very
little in the ways of defense, lacking a hilt or basket to protect one's
hands.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 7-10 to 22-26 (average 14-18).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** Gladius, sword.
