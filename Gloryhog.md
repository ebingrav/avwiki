*1 GLORYHOG  
*Masters of this Rite will automatically leap in to finish off an  
*enemy they are directly fighting if that enemy is left nearly  
*dead by any group-mates. It functions just like Deathblow,  
*except that it can be trigger off anybody's attack.  
  
*Deathblow is a prerequisites for Gloryhog.  
  
*Gloryhog will not function if the Berserker has better than -750  
*AC at hero, or -1250 AC at Lord.  
  
*This is a [Rite](Rites.md "wikilink"), not a
[skill](:Category:_Skills.md "wikilink").* See HELP RITES for more info.
