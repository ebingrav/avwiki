Comments
--------

One must kill [a huge black demon](/wiki/Huge_Black_Demon "wikilink") just
outside this room to get its two [keys](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") in
order to both enter this room and open this chest. Note that this chest
is trapped with a blade trap, which may be
[dismantled](/wiki/Dismantle "wikilink") by characters who know how to [disarm
traps](/wiki/Disarm_Traps "wikilink").
