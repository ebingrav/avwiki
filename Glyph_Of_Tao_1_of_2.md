Comments
--------

There are two of this item, one with modifies hit roll -10 and a useful
one which modifies damage roll +10. Nice item for low heroes before they
can make a decent [Shard Of Black Doom
Marble](/wiki/Shard_Of_Black_Doom_Marble "wikilink") or [Orb Of Amorphous
Essence](/wiki/Orb_Of_Amorphous_Essence "wikilink").

Directions
----------

**[Area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"):** [Lair Of
Shyu'Yao](/wiki/Category%3A_Lair_Of_Shyu'Yao "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Lair_Of_Shyu'Yao_Map "wikilink")).  
**[Mob(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"):** [the Chi of Zhang
Kai](/wiki/Chi_Of_Zhang_Kai "wikilink").  
[Teleport](/wiki/Teleport "wikilink") [Stone
Gargoyle](/wiki/Stone_Gargoyle "wikilink") and go 2s, 2w, u, e to get to the
Lair of Shyu'Yao area.

From here go e,s,u to enter a maze. To get through the maze go n, u, e,
n, e and get key from ground. The knight in this room carries the
version with -10 hit roll.

Go w, d, w - takes you back to the room before maze. Unlock d and go 2d,
s unlock e and go east and kill the lich. Loot corpse for keys (claw and
red key) and a poison item.

Go w, n, 2u, 2e and unlock and open e and e. Unlock heart and get key
heart unlock down.

Go down and kill The Chi of Zhang Kai for Glyph.

There are probably some aggie mobs along the route,
[sneak](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink")/[move hidden](Move_Hidden "wikilink") seems to
make them leave you alone except for lich which you have to kill
anyways. Also if there are ghosts in the room with the Chi they can
assist.
