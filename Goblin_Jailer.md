*Before you stands a small [goblin](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink"), sauntering
along carrying a chain of [keys](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink"). Seemingly
a jailer, he seems to be so lost in thought that he is totally unaware
of his surroundings. That's not hard to do if you're a
[goblin](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink"), but not something one does very often.*

**Keywords:** goblin, jailer.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 6.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Thieving_Mobs "wikilink"),
[wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
