The goblin magician needs your help in obtaining a tome.

**Start Location:** [Ascension
Woods](/wiki/Category%3AAscension_Woods "wikilink")  
**Starting Room:** In Air, look at the [Goblin
Magician](/wiki/Goblin_Magician "wikilink")'s description.  
**Reward:** There may be more to this quest than what's given in the
spoilers (below). The reward is a single gold coin.  
<spoiler> Go to the [Cleric](/wiki/Cleric "wikilink") in [Royal Midgaard
Library](/wiki/Category%3A_Royal_Midgaard_Library "wikilink"), and kill him to
get the [Leather-Bound Book](/wiki/Leather-Bound_Book "wikilink"). Give the
book to the goblin. </spoiler>
