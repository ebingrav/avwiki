*A short sword, the work of goblins, rests here, emanating an aura of
evil.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 5 to 11 (average 8).`  
`Modifies `[`damage`
`roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 2 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** goblin, sword.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 6.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <wielded>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 50 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 3 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):**
[evil](/wiki/Evil_Flag "wikilink"), [anti-good](Anti-Good_Flag "wikilink"),
[anti-neutral](/wiki/Anti-Neutral_Flag "wikilink").  
It is a one handed weapon which does piercing damage.
