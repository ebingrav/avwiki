This is a sub-human race of the world. Small, like halflings, hairless
and dark of coloration. They are weak, unintelligent, and spineless.
With a high regard for self preservation, a goblin will throw his best
friend in the path of an enemy. It is just as well that the only thing
they're good at is running.

Goblins have RACIAL-WIMPY.
