`Modifies damage roll by +6`

Slot: Wield  
Level: 21 or 37 depending on which type you get  
Max: Varies accordingly  
Weight: 20  
Area: [Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Ofcol_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: [Dragonknight](/wiki/Dragonknight "wikilink"),
[Dragonlord](/wiki/Dragonlord "wikilink"), [Ancient Gold
Dragon](/wiki/Ancient_Gold_Dragon "wikilink")  
  
There's actually two different items that share the same name and damage
roll modifier. They differ by level. The lower level version is carried
by the [Dragonknights](/wiki/Dragonknight "wikilink") and
[Dragonlords](/wiki/Dragonlord "wikilink"). The higher level version is
carried by the [Ancient Gold Dragon](/wiki/Ancient_Gold_Dragon "wikilink").
All these mobs are found in the temple of
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink"). From
[Diana](/wiki/Marshall_Diana "wikilink"): 3n 4e 2u e
