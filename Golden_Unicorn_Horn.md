` Modifies hit roll by 1 continuous.`  
` Modifies damage roll by 1 continuous.`

Level: 4  
Weight: 2  
Max: 12  
Mob: [Golden Unicorn](/wiki/Golden_Unicorn "wikilink")  
Area: [Petting Zoo](/wiki/Category%3A_Petting_Zoo "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Petting_Zoo_Map "wikilink"))  

To get to the zoo start from Sol. Go **3e s w n**, you are now at the
entrance to the Petting Zoo. Go **e n w n** after that and you will find
the unicorn, kill it for the horn.
