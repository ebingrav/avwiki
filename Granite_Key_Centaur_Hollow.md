Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is used in [Centaur
Hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink")'s [Orb of Sunshine
Quest](/wiki/Orb_Of_Sunshine_Quest "wikilink"). It unlocks a granite chest in
The Southwestern Part of a Verdant Cavern.
