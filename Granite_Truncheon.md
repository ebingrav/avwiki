Comments
--------

This is the best blunt powerswinger that isn't in an EHA ([Avalanche,
Maul Of The Mountain](/wiki/Avalanche,_Maul_Of_The_Mountain "wikilink")).

The [Stout Branch](/wiki/Stout_Branch "wikilink") is another good option (I
like it even better, actually, because the weight makes it better for
smashing).
