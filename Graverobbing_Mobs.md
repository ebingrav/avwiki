[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will send all corpses of any
players that they kill to a specific "[dump
room](/wiki/Dump_Rooms "wikilink")" somewhere.
