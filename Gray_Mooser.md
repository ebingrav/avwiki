*This moose man is smaller than most and dressed entirely in gray. He is
confident, like a man with an escape route already planned.*

**Keywords:** gray, mooser.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 33.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Thieving_Mobs "wikilink"),
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[wimpy](Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink"), [thief](/wiki/Thieving_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink").

Comments
--------

The Gray Mooser is not [sentinel](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"). However, he
is usually locked up in his own room. If his door is opened, then he
might wander about the area.
