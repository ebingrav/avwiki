*A veteran of many combat campaigns, Grunka knows more about warfare
than any [goblin](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink") alive. His lectures draw large
crowds of [goblins](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink") and others to hear his stories
of high adventure. You notice that his scars are all on his backside
indicating his mastery of the [goblin](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink") high art,
[fleeing](/wiki/Flee "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Grunka, scarred, goblin.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 11.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
