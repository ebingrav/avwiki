*A veteran of many combat campaigns, Grunka knows more about warfare
than any [goblin](Goblins.md "wikilink") alive. His lectures draw large
crowds of [goblins](Goblins.md "wikilink") and others to hear his
stories of high adventure. You notice that his scars are all on his
backside indicating his mastery of the [goblin](Goblins.md "wikilink")
high art, [fleeing](Flee.md "wikilink").*
