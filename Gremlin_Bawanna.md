*The Bawanna is the emotional, spiritual, and day-to-day leader of the
gremlins. The strongest, smartest male rises to this position by
physical dominance and personal ambition. Leader of a
[race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") as pitiful and filthy as the
gremlins is truly an ignoble accomplishment.*

**Keywords:** gremlin, Bawanna.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 10.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
