''The fighting techniques covered in this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") teach defense and evasion while
prone or downed. A [monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") with this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") will not take the usual extra
damage that comes with being knocked down.

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
