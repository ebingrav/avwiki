*Growling and shouting something about glittering stones and a chest the
[orc](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink") is about to push a shivering and dripping wet
[goblin](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink") into the pool. He turns around to look for
some other victim to go diving for him, but there are no other
[goblins](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink") to be seen.*

**Keywords:** orc, angry.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 8.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
