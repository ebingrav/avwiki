Give [the Great Grunka](/wiki/Great_Grunka "wikilink") 10 coins to begin your
lesson. The prize is the [Badge Of
Completion](/wiki/Badge_Of_Completion "wikilink"), which is constitution and
dexterity gear for on body. Must be completed between levels 7 and 19.
