Lore](http://avatar.melanarchy.info/index.php?title=Armor_Lore), [Weapon

This skill represents a skilled fighter's ability to turn glancing blows
and near misses into palpable hits. Once practiced, this skill will
enable the user to hit more effectively against enemies with extremely
high AC
