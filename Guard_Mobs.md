[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will join any fight in which
one or more combatants (whether [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") or
players) have [alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") below 300, attacking
whichever combatant is [aligned](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") lowest. Some
guard mobs will also attack any outlawed
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") that they see and some
will not.
