This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") is used in [The Frozen
Court](:Category:_Frozen_Court.md "wikilink")'s [Sword of Bloodied Snow
Quest](Sword_Of_Bloodied_Snow_Quest.md "wikilink"). It unlocks a door
between Chamber of Art and The Guardian's Quarters.
