*This simple sword of blackened steel has a black ring around the
pommel. In the center is the sigil of [Werredan](/wiki/Werredan "wikilink").*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 9-10 to 23-26 (average 16-18).`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 2 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** sword, guardsman.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 19-21, 24-26.

[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").
