*This simple tabard is made of fine linen in the darkest gray and lined
with red silk. Above the heart is the sigil of
[Werredan](/wiki/Werredan "wikilink") surrounded by black embroidery that
looks almost like a sword bent into a circle.*

[`Armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 5.`  
`Modifies `[`armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")` by -5 continuous.`
