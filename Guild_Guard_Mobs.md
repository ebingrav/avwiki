[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") operate only in
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") with [rogue guild
terrain](/wiki/Rogue_Guild_Terrain "wikilink"). Whenever these
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") encounter
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who are less than 50%
adept at [rogue lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink"), they will blackjack these
[characters](:Cataegory:_Characters "wikilink"),
[steal](/wiki/Steal "wikilink") every piece of gold that they may be carrying,
and then leave them lying unconscious in some specific "[dump
room](/wiki/Dump_Rooms "wikilink")."
