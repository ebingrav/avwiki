[Urka Hammertoes](/wiki/Urka_Hammertoes "wikilink") offers you a membership in
the [Graw Akkuurattaan](/wiki/Category%3AGraw_Akkuurattaan "wikilink") branch
of the Guild Of Explorers And Cavern Crawlers, if you can provide him
with a [Flask Of Marlots Ol'
Special](/wiki/Flask_Of_Marlots_Ol'_Special "wikilink").

1.  Get a [Flask Of Marlots Ol'
    Special](/wiki/Flask_Of_Marlots_Ol'_Special "wikilink"), and give it
    to Urka.
2.  Pay the yearly (in-game) membership fee of 10000 gold, or one
    [Aquamarine Gemstone](/wiki/Aquamarine_Gemstone "wikilink").

You will receive the [Guild Of Explorers And Cavern Crawlers
Insignia](/wiki/Guild_Of_Explorers_And_Cavern_Crawlers_Insignia "wikilink"),
and will be able to trade with him and other Guild members.
