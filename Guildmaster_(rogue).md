[thief](:Category:_Rogues "wikilink"). He is dressed in poor clothing,

## Comments

[Rogues](:Category:_Rogues "wikilink") may also find this guildmaster by
[practicing](Practice "wikilink") [rogue lore](Rogue_Lore "wikilink")
and then following thief marks that they will detect around
[Midgaard](:Category:_Midgaard "wikilink").

Note if you go in without rogue lore he will blackjack you. It is
possible to be killed in this manner.
