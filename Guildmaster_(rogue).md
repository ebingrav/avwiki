*This is one of the demonic followers of the Daemon
[Snikt](Snikt.md "wikilink"). You realize that whenever your guildmaster
moves, you fail to notice it: the way of the true
[thief](:Category:_Rogues.md "wikilink"). He is dressed in poor
clothing, having the appearance of a beggar.*

[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs.md "wikilink"),
[trainer](:Category:_Trainers.md "wikilink").

## Comments

[Rogues](:Category:_Rogues.md "wikilink") may also find this guildmaster
by [practicing](Practice.md "wikilink") [rogue
lore](Rogue_Lore.md "wikilink") and then following thief marks that they
will detect around [Midgaard](:Category:_Midgaard.md "wikilink").

Note if you go in without rogue lore he will blackjack you. It is
possible to be killed in this manner.
