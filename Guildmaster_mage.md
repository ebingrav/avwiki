*Even though your guildmaster looks old and tired, you can clearly see
the vast amount of knowledge she possesses. She is wearing fine magic
clothing, and you notice that she is surrounded by a blue shimmering
aura.*

**Keywords:** guildmaster, arch, mage.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[trainer](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").

Comments
--------

[Mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may also find this guildmaster by
[casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") [detect magic](Detect_Magic "wikilink") upon
themselves and then following ethereal floating arrows that they will
detect around [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink").
