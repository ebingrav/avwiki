*This is one of the demonic followers of the Daemon
[Snikt](/wiki/Snikt "wikilink"). You realize that whenever your guildmaster
moves, you fail to notice it: the way of the true
[thief](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink"). He is dressed in poor clothing,
having the appearance of a beggar.*

**Keywords:** master, thief, guildmaster.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[trainer](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
