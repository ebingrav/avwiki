*Everyone [dies](/wiki/Death "wikilink") once in a while, but there's not
always someone available to [summon](/wiki/Summon "wikilink") you to a corpse.
Gurney is a high-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") answer to this problem. Once
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"), you can [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") Gurney
to [summon](/wiki/Summon "wikilink") a
[player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") to you that is badly hurt.
For example, to Gurney a [player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
named 'Caveman' to you, type: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") gurney caveman.
The beauty of this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is
that it bypasses many magical restraints that a [summon
spell](/wiki/Summon "wikilink") would not. There is also no
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") limitation on who you can gurney except for
the same one you have with [summon](/wiki/Summon "wikilink"). Please note that
you cannot gurney a [player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") from a
[cursed room](/wiki/Cursed_Rooms "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'gurney'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3ACharacters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is invaluable when helping
killed groupmates or others to [retrieve their gear from their
corpses](/wiki/Corpse_Retrieval "wikilink").
