*The hag is dressed in rags, has long tangled matted gray hair, and is
very old. The hag tells you 'I see you enter a fire and fight a great
battle with [a minion of hell](/wiki/Flaming_Beast_From_Hell "wikilink"), and
you die horribly!' With that the hag breaks into an ugly fit of
laughter.*

**Keywords:** Gleda, hag.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 28.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[cleric](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink"),
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Thieving_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [curse](/wiki/Curse "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
