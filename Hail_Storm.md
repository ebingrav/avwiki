*All [Stormlord](/wiki/Category%3A_Stormlords "wikilink")
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") manipulate weather in a very
localized manner. They can call forth a blizzard in the middle of a
summer meadow, or a shower of rain while miles underground. They must be
sustained, and will end early if the caster is struck, moved, or enters
a command. Hail storm calls forth weather of the same name, pelting all
the [Stormlord](/wiki/Category%3A_Stormlords "wikilink")'s enemies with shards
of ice. This affect may linger briefly after the
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") ends, but will not do any damage
while the [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") is not directly controlling it.*

Prerequisite(s): ?.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'hail storm'.
