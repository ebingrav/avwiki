*This piglike creature is gray and green skinned with coarse hair
covering its body. He is stooped over, but looks like he could hold its
own in a fight.*

**Keywords:** hairy, orc.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 9.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
