Comments
--------

A half-elven rogue is involved in [Elven
Foothills](/wiki/Category%3A_Elven_Foothills "wikilink")' [Gargoyle Bracer
Quest](/wiki/Gargoyle_Bracer_Quest "wikilink").

His [coat](/wiki/Long_Brown_Coat "wikilink") is a
[container](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink").

Directions
----------

**[Area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"):** [Elven
Foothills](/wiki/Category%3A_Elven_Foothills "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Elven_Foothills_Map "wikilink")).  
**[Room(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"):** A shaded house
([cursed](/wiki/Cursed_Rooms "wikilink"),
[private](/wiki/Private_Rooms "wikilink")).  
Walking route from [Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"): 3e, 2n, 3e, 9n, w, u, 2s, w,
s, \[e\].  
Walking route from [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"): 14n, 8w, 3n, 6w, 4n, w,
u, 2s, w, s, \[e\].  
Walking route from [Nyad](/wiki/Nyad "wikilink"): 2n, u, 2n, \[e\].  
Although this mob [wanders](/wiki/Wandering_Mobs "wikilink"), it normally
respawns inside "A shaded house" (see walking directions above) with a
closed door. Note that, because its "house" (room) is both
[cursed](/wiki/Cursed_Rooms "wikilink") and
[private](/wiki/Private_Rooms "wikilink"), you will not be able to enter its
house to kill it; instead, you will need to open the door to its house
and then wait patiently for it to emerge before you can kill it. Also
note that, because a half-elven rouge [sneaks](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink"), you
will see no message when it exits its house and, therefore, you will
need to use [commands](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") like
[look](/wiki/Look "wikilink") or [scan](Scan "wikilink") to notice when it has
moved.
