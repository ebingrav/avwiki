This short quest serves to reset the previously begotten halfling
alignment back to neutral toward both parties - halflings and half-orcs
in [Mountain Tunnels](/wiki/Category%3AMountain_Tunnels "wikilink"),
[Eragora](/wiki/Category%3AEragora "wikilink"). This is done so that the
player will be free to complete the [Mountain Adventurer
Quest](/wiki/Mountain_Adventurer_Quest "wikilink"), which is otherwise blocked
by the previously started [Halfling Detective
Quest](/wiki/Halfling_Detective_Quest "wikilink").

-   [Madak](/wiki/Madak "wikilink") (pp [Halfling
    Spy](/wiki/Halfling_Spy "wikilink"), 2s) will require your services,
    namely to get rid of [Sou f'Fle](/wiki/Sou_f'Fle "wikilink"), a slayer
    of half-orcs.
-   Teleport to [Mezzo](/wiki/Mezzo "wikilink") (aggie), e, dispose of Sou
    f'Fle and retrieve his [Necklace Of Half-Orken Teeth And
    Ears](/wiki/Necklace_Of_Half-Orken_Teeth_And_Ears "wikilink"). Watch out,
    he's not a pushover and wields a poisoned axe.
-   Teleport to [Halfling Spy](/wiki/Halfling_Spy "wikilink"), 2s, present
    the necklace.
