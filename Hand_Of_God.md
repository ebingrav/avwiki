*HOG is an acronym for Hand of God. HOG drastically boosts melee
[damage](/wiki/Damage "wikilink"). Your
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") is otherwise unaltered.
The [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") is available only during
Avatar's famed [hog-a-thons](/wiki/Hogathons "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [hog](/wiki/Hog "wikilink").

Please note that this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") cannot be
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") and may be activated *only* during
[hogathons](/wiki/Hogathons "wikilink").

See also [Hog](/wiki/Hog "wikilink"), [Hogathons](/wiki/Hogathons "wikilink").
