*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") inflicts damage on the
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). Its [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") on
your [slist](/wiki/Slist "wikilink") gives an idea of the damage it produces.*

Prerequisite(s): [Cause Critical](/wiki/Cause_Critical "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'harm'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This single-target damage [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
fourth of four that [clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") between [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 1 and
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 50. Because
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") specialize in
[casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") defensive rather than offensive spells, such
offensive spells as this one are not often useful but
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") may still find it worth
[learning](/wiki/Practice "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of many other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
at [hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") but will find it practically
useless.

See also [Harm Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Harm_Gear "wikilink").
