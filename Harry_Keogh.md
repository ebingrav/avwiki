*Harry Keogh is a tall dark man. As with all Wamphyri his eyes gleam
yellow, but there is no menace in them. He is wearing [a long black
coat](Long_Black_Coat.md "wikilink") and [a wide brimmed black
hat](Wide_Brimmed_Black_Hat.md "wikilink"). In his hand is [a scythe
made of the purest silver](Sharp_Silver_Scythe.md "wikilink"). He has
completely conquered the Wamphyri within him. He will not harm you, but
never cross him or his.*
