*Made of bleached white linen and embroidered in white silk with the
symbol of [Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink"), this headscarf is used by
[cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") and chirugeon to keep their
hair out of their eyes when taking care of their patients.*

[`Armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 6.`  
`Modifies `[`mana`](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink")` by 15 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`wisdom`](/wiki/Wisdom "wikilink")` by 2 continuous.`
