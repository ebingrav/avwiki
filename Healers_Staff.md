*This simple wooden staff is engraved with healing symbols and runes
from many [races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink").*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 8 to 27 (average 17).`  
`Modifies `[`armor`
`class`](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")` by -8 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`wisdom`](/wiki/Wisdom "wikilink")` by 2 continuous.`  
`'`[`protection` `evil`](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink")`' continuous.`
