*On first glance the shape of this
[weapon](:Category:_Melee_Weapons "wikilink") seems odd, but as you take
it into your hands you realize that the angle between hilt and blade
allows for the swing to hit the target at perfect time without having to
crank the wrist. The Kukri has short thick blade of steel, sharp on one
side, which is fitted into a carved wooden hilt. What comes as a
surprise though, is that it should be found here: there was surely no
peaceful transition of ownership.*
