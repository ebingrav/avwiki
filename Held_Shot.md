*Using this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), an
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") holds their shot one round,
aiming carefully so that their shot the following
[round](/wiki/Rounds "wikilink") will be that much more damaging.*

Prerequisite(s): [Fleeing Shot](/wiki/Fleeing_Shot "wikilink").

Syntax: [held](/wiki/Held "wikilink").
