Comments
--------

Wearing this helmet will make you [blind](/wiki/Blindness "wikilink"). Once
you're blind the only ways to remove it are by [dying](/wiki/Death "wikilink")
or by having someone cast [Deception](/wiki/Deception "wikilink") on you in
the [Arena](/wiki/Category%3AArena "wikilink").

Directions
----------

It is held by a spectre but the one you port to might not have it.
Wander around a look until you find the one that does.
