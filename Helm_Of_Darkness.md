## Comments

Wearing this helmet will make you [blind](Blindness.md "wikilink")! This
problem may be fixed either by (1) [dying](Death.md "wikilink") or by
(2) having someone cast [Deception](Deception "wikilink") on you in the
[Arena](:Category:_Avatar_Arena.md "wikilink").
