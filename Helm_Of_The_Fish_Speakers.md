` Modifies ac by -10 continuous`  
` type boat, extra flags glow hum nolocate`

Slot: Head  
Level: 23  
No Base AC (type boat)  
Weight: 5  
Mob: Myrmillo  
Area: [Senex Operis](/wiki/Category%3A_Senex_Operis "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Ruins_Of_Keresh_Map "wikilink"))  
This mob stays with the other gladiator spirits, inside the arena. To
get there, head north from the entrance to [Senex
Operis](/wiki/Category%3ASenex_Operis "wikilink") until you see a guard. From
here there should be a hidden door to the north. Open it and go through,
and then search for a mob with the short desc:  
(Translucent) A Myrmillo stands here, in a fish-shaped helmet  
Be careful, like the other gladiators he's armed, so he can hit hard for
his level

Port spectre gladiator, track Myrm, kill Myrm
