1. Look at the [Far Hunter](/wiki/Far_Hunter "wikilink") before you depart to
the [Rim](:Caregory:Rim "wikilink").  
2. Look at him again once you arrive, to reiterate what he wants.  
<spoiler> 3. Find the [storm wyrm
Oeheliu](/wiki/Storm_Wyrm_Oeheliu "wikilink") and eliminate it. It hunts
beyond the Fog through several mazes.  
4. Return with proof.  
5. Go to the same place that accepts all other dragon scale/skull
pieces.  
</spoiler> 6. Receive [Heroic Dragonscale
Slippers](/wiki/Heroic_Dragonscale_Slippers "wikilink")

As all Rim involvements, this one is tough to solo. Be VERY careful,
have insured gear and the morgue enabled.
