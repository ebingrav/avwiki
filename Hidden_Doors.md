Some AVATAR MUD [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") are not obvious to
players while they are closed; players may only notice such doors by
performing certain actions, such as by either [looking](/wiki/Look "wikilink")
or attempting to move in these [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink")'
respective directions.
