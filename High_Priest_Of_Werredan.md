*A tall man, and broad of shoulders, [Werredan](/wiki/Werredan "wikilink")'s
High [Priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") is no stranger to hard
work. His bearing shows that he was once a
[warrior](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink"). He strides proudly through
[the temple](/wiki/Category%3A_Temple_Of_Midgaard "wikilink"), ever ready to
serve his [god](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Werredan, high, priest.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 35.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink").
