*Elegant. Charming. Powerful. All those words describe the [High
Elf](/wiki/High_Elves "wikilink") who serves as High Priestess to
[Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink") the
[Goddess](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") of Healing. She
spent many years wandering the planes before coming back to
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") and taking on the job of
chief priestess to [Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink")'s new temple.*

**Keywords:** high, priestess, tulsith.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 35.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
