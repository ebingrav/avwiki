*Suraine became the youngest ever Sister of Kra when just twelve years
of age. Since then she has grown in influence until when the last
patriarch retired, she became the youngest high priestess of
[Kra](/wiki/Kra "wikilink"). Power corrupts or so it is said and her exposure
to power came young and has left its mark on her...*
