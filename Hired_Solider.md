*The soldier's uniform bears no mark. He tries to survey everything and
everyone in sight. Even on the relaxed Gorn's Anchor, he seems
especially alert.*

**Keywords:** soldier  
**Level:** 43-44  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** good  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink"), [detect
hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink")

### Comments

A Hired Soldier, [an Officer of Lord
Audis](/wiki/Officer_Of_Lord_Audis "wikilink"), [a Professional
Warrior](/wiki/Professional_Warrior "wikilink"), [a Paid
Mercenary](/wiki/Paid_Mercenary "wikilink"), and [a Seasoned
Sailor](/wiki/Seasoned_Sailor "wikilink") are the most easily accessible xp
mobs in Gorn's Anchor. Two of each (except for sailors, of which there
are four) patrol the dock.
