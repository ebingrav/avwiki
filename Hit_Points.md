*Hit points are arguably the most important statistic in a fight. If you
reach 0 hp, you go unconscious. When you hit -10 hit points, you die.
You'll lose fewer hp if your '[armor class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")' is
lower (which is better), and if certain damage-reducing spells are
affecting you.*

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may
[wear](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") [gear to increase their hit
points](/wiki/Category%3A_Hit-Point_Gear "wikilink").
