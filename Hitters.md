*The term hitter is used to define all members of a group who are not
[tanking](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink") or [healing](/wiki/Healers "wikilink"). The role
of the hitter (or hitters) in the group is to inflict damage onto the
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), and as such hitters can be of any
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") and will usually wear [equipment
to maximize their damage](/wiki/Category%3A_Hit_Gear "wikilink") to the mob.
In contrast the role of the [healer](/wiki/Healers "wikilink") is to keep the
[tank](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink") and group healthy, with any damage they inflict
a bonus. The distinction between hitter and [healer](/wiki/Healers "wikilink")
means that a [cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") is not necessarily
a [healer](/wiki/Healers "wikilink") in a group, nor are characters of other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") precluded from the role of
[healer](/wiki/Healers "wikilink").*
