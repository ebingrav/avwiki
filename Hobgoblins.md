Goblins who have been imbued with dark magic of an unknown origin,
Hobgoblins are the stuff of children's nightmares. While only slightly
larger than their lesser kin, they are more ferocious and bloodthirsty
than races ten times their size.

Hobgoblins have RACIAL-EVIL, RACIAL-BERSERK, and RACIAL-FRENZY

Hobgoblins are only available to Goblins via the remort command.
