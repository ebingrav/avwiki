*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") gives the target detect
[hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [evil](/wiki/Detect_Evil "wikilink"),
[invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink") and [magic](/wiki/Detect_Magic "wikilink"),
as well as [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Detect Evil](/wiki/Detect_Evil "wikilink"), [Detect
Magic](/wiki/Detect_Magic "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'holy sight'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is somewhat useful. Some
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") and
[priests](Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") it as
part of a "full
[spellup](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")".
Some [hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") prefer Holy Sight to
[Heighten Senses](/wiki/Heighten_Senses "wikilink") in seeing hidden and/or
invisible [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") or
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink").
