*Rather like an oversized machette, the blade of this sword flares
greatly towards the tip, but then curves back on itself viciously to
form a savage hook. You are not sure which would cause more damage, the
blade going in, or out.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 11 to 27 (average 19).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`
