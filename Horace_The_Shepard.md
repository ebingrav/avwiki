*[Centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") are considered by many to be one of the
[wisest](/wiki/Wisdom "wikilink") [races](Category%3A_Races "wikilink")
around, usually choosing a nomadic, solitary life, only gathering with
other [centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") occasionally over their long
lifetimes. Horace chose to settle for a time, at the request of the King
of [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"), as
[Centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") are known to bring success to endeavors
involving growth and creation.*

**Keywords:** Horace, centaur, Shepard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
