## Comments

The mob is a shopkeep. You can buy the gear off of him for 16-90 gold
coins depending on level.

This item varies wildly in level, like all items sold by shopkeepers. If
you kill [Baz](Baz_The_Blacksmith.md "wikilink") he'll repop with
different level horseshoes for sale.
