<b>Slot:</b> Held  
<b>Armor Class:</b> 1-3  
<b>Bonuses:</b> +1 Hitroll, +1 Damage  
<b>Weight:</b> 3  
<b>Level:</b> 11 \[I've seen it as low as 5. It changes every reboot, I
think.\]  
<b>Mob:</b> [Baz the Blacksmith](/wiki/Baz_the_Blacksmith "wikilink")  
The mob is a shopkeep. You can buy the gear off of him for ~100 coins.

Don't port Baz because it will take you to an aggressive mob and you
will probably die.
