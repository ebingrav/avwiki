*An elderly [high elf](/wiki/High_Elves "wikilink") stands here leaning on
[his staff](/wiki/Healer's_Staff "wikilink"). He has made the climb to
[lord](/wiki/Lord "wikilink") twice, first as an [elven](Elves "wikilink")
[cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink"), then as a
[high-elf](/wiki/High_Elves "wikilink"). Both times he has given it up to
return to the lower realms in a new form. This time, after learning the
ways of [the priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink"), he has eschewed
battle in any form and remained at [the
hospice](/wiki/Category%3A_Tul-Sith's_Hospice "wikilink") as a teacher. Now he
is the director of [the
hospice](/wiki/Category%3A_Tul-Sith's_Hospice "wikilink"), overseeing the care
and learning of his flock.*

**Keywords:** hospice, director, high, elf, priest.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 25.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[cleric](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink"),
[healer](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [protection
evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
