*The huge black [demon](Demons.md "wikilink") doesn't seem at all
[aggressive](Aggressive_Mobs.md "wikilink"), though you get the sense
that he could be if you were to try to get past him. He wears [a small,
heart-shaped amulet](Heart-Shaped_Amulet.md "wikilink") around his
neck.*
