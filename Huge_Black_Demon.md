*The huge black [demon](/wiki/Demons "wikilink") doesn't seem at all
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"), though you get the sense that
he could be if you were to try to get past him. He wears [a small,
heart-shaped amulet](/wiki/Heart-Shaped_Amulet "wikilink") around his neck.*

**Keywords:** huge, black, demon.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[protection evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink").

Comments
--------

This mob, previously level 70, is now barely hero level. The reasons
aren't exactly clear. He still attacks outlaws who enter his room.
