Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is used in [Caves of Ancient
Evil](/wiki/Category%3A_Caves_Of_Ancient_Evil "wikilink")'s [Seven-League
Boots Quest](/wiki/Seven-League_Boots_Quest "wikilink"). It
[unlocks](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") [a door](Category%3A_Doors "wikilink")
between A Huge Door and The Wizard's Quarters.
