This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") is used in [Caves of Ancient
Evil](:Category:_Caves_Of_Ancient_Evil.md "wikilink")'s [Seven-League
Boots Quest](Seven-League_Boots_Quest.md "wikilink"). It
[unlocks](Unlock.md "wikilink") [a door](:Category:_Doors.md "wikilink")
between A Huge Door and The Wizard's Quarters.
