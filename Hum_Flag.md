This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") doesn't do anything
useful. All [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") with it will be
seen as (Humming). This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") may
be added via a positive
[divine](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") "presence" while
[enchanting](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink").
