Comments
--------

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must pass a locked
[door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") to reach this humble pilgrim; to do
this, they will need to either [bash](/wiki/Bashdoor "wikilink") it open or
[unlock](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") it using its respective
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may obtain its
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") by either [buying](Buy "wikilink") [a
mine pass level one](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One "wikilink") from [the
receptionist](/wiki/Receptionist "wikilink") or completing [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Mine Pass Level One
Quest](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One_Quest "wikilink").

A humble pilgrim is involved in [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Flask of Marlots Ol' Special
Quest](/wiki/Flask_Of_Marlots_Ol'_Special_Quest "wikilink"), its [Legacy of
King Vorin Quest](/wiki/Legacy_Of_King_Vorin_Quest "wikilink"), and its [Set
of Three Loaded Dice Quest](/wiki/Set_Of_Three_Loaded_Dice_Quest "wikilink").
