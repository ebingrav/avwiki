*A young [human](/wiki/Humans "wikilink") male hunter watches you intently; he
keeps his spear close in case you decide to cause trouble. The hunters
of [Icewolf](/wiki/Category%3A_Icewolf "wikilink") not only provide the town
with food but also with protection.*

**Keywords:** hunter, male.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 43.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** none.  
**Affects:** none.
