*A young female [gargoyle](/wiki/Gargoyles "wikilink") watches you intently;
she keeps her spear close in case you decide to cause trouble. The
hunters of [Icewolf](/wiki/Category%3A_Icewolf "wikilink") not only provide
the town with food but also with protection.*

**Keywords:** hunter, gargoyle, female.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 46.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.
