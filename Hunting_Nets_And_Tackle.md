*This star-shaped net is fashioned from very heavy ropes, and trimmed at
the edges with a series of weights and hooks. It looks as if it has
undergone years of mending and adaptation as its owner has used it to
hunt various prey.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 18 to 55 (average 36).`  
`Modifies strength by 2 continuous`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 13 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** piles, nets, tackle.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 50-52.
