*As one of the working caste of iadh, this creature's arms end with wide
spade-like blades for digging. It slinks along the tunnel, barely in
sight.*

**Keywords:** insectoid, creature, iadh, drone  
**Level:** 45  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** bad  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3AMob_Types "wikilink"):**
[wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink"),
[scavenger](/wiki/Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink")  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink"),
[sneak](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink")

### Comments

Psions should be frugal with their use of [dancing
weapon](/wiki/Dancing_Weapon "wikilink"), as these mobs scavenge, flee, and
sneak.

Twenty iadh drones populate the colony section of Gorn's Anchor. This
section is cursed, and has a one-way entrance.
[Teleporting](/wiki/Teleport "wikilink"), [portaling](Portal "wikilink"), or
fighting one's way through the colony are the only ways out.
