**Level:** 46  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** satanic  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3AMob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),

[invisible](/wiki/Invis "wikilink")

### Comments

Two iadh sanctum guards are paired with two [iadh
assassins](/wiki/Iadh_Assassin "wikilink") in a cursed room with a locked
exit. The key is carried by one of the assassins.
