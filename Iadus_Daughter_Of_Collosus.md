*Born from the same terrible magicks that created
[Collosus](/wiki/Collosus "wikilink"), Iadus fears nothing in the valley
except her father and [the liche, Azric](/wiki/Power_Liche_Azric "wikilink").
Many times has she done battle with
[Azric](/wiki/Power_Liche_Azric "wikilink") yet she can never hurt it, though
its magical attacks do little to harm her. From mortals she has little
to fear, for how can you hurt living rock?*

**Keywords:** iadus.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 85.  
**Scan Description:** Iadus stares at you, death in her eyes.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**

invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
