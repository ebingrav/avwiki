This skill allows you to create ice arrows, bolts and stones and uses
mana to fletch them.

Prerequisite(s): [Fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink"), [Ice
Lance](/wiki/Ice_Lance "wikilink")

Syntax: [fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink")
&lt;[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink")/[bolts](Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink")/[stones](Category%3A_Stones "wikilink")&gt;
ice.

The primary damage ammunition for lord archer types.

This ammunition does not survive being used.
