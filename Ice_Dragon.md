*A huge, white reptilian creature, covered in ice, bears it's fangs and
looks intent on eating you alive.*

**Keywords:** ice, dragon.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 90.  
**Scan Description:** An ice dragon hisses at you, forked tongue dancing
twixt his teeth.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** [fond of the taste of
corpses](/wiki/Corpse-Eating_Mobs "wikilink"),
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Category%3A_Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink"),
[agressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink"),
[protection](/wiki/Protection_Good "wikilink").

Give [a white dragon scale](/wiki/White_Dragon_Scale "wikilink") to
[Jake](/wiki/Jake_The_Thorn-Slayer "wikilink") for a cats eye gemstone, worth
25000 gold.

[Skin](/wiki/Skin_Corpse "wikilink") the dragon's corpse to make [an ice scale
bracer](/wiki/Ice_Scale_Bracer "wikilink").
