*Demonstrating a mastery of the natural elements, one may magically
shape the water vapor in air into a massive, chilling icicle. The bonds
that hold this lance of ice together are weak, but they will last long
enough for the [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") to thrust the icy spear at one
target with a devastating, unblockable attack.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'ice lance'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [ranger](/wiki/Category%3A_Rangers "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is practically never used by
non-[rangers](/wiki/Category%3A_Rangers "wikilink").
