Ice Seed can refer to:

-   [Ice Seed (Ice Naga)](/wiki/Ice_Seed_(Ice_Naga) "wikilink") The ice seed
    that spawns on one of several [Ice Nagas](/wiki/Ice_Naga "wikilink").
-   [Ice Seed (Grandmother Ent)](/wiki/Ice_Seed_(Grandmother_Ent) "wikilink")
    The Ice Naga seed after it has been given to [Grandmother
    Ent](/wiki/Grandmother_Ent "wikilink").
-   [Ice Seed (Kroska)](/wiki/Ice_Seed_(Kroska) "wikilink") The ice seed that
    spawns on the ground next to [Kroska](/wiki/Kroska "wikilink").

  
All 3 of the seeds are found in
[Battleground](/wiki/Category%3ABattleground "wikilink") and are used for the
[Storm Brew Quest](/wiki/Storm_Brew_Quest "wikilink") to get a [Storm
Brew](/wiki/Storm_Brew "wikilink").
