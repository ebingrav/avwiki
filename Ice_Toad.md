*A huge amphibian, the ice toad's skin is pale blue-white that is
partially transparent, allowing you a good view of all the yummy toad
innards concealed beneath it.*

**Keywords:** ice, toad.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 36.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink"), [frost
breath](/wiki/Breathing_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

An ice toad wanders within the *Ice cave* rooms of the area and won't
wander outside of them.
