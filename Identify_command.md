[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may use this
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") to pay certain
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") to
[identify](/wiki/Identify_(spell) "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") for them. See
[Identifiers](/wiki/Category%3A_Identifiers "wikilink") for more information
about these [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink").

Syntax: identify &lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.  
Syntax: identify &lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;
appraise.
