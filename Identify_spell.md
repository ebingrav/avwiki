*What does it do? It gives you information about the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), of course.
[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"), what it is, and more can be seen
with this spell.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'identify'
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.

This spell is invaluable to
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"). It provides very detailed
information about [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), more than
may be obtained using [commands](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") like
[examine](/wiki/Examine "wikilink"). See
[Identifiers](/wiki/Category%3A_Identifiers "wikilink") for some
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") who will identify
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") for you for a price.

See also [Identify Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Identify_Gear "wikilink").
