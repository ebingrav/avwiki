*The Illuminati [mage](:Category:_Mages.md "wikilink") has had runes
burned into his body to aid in his
[spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink") [casting](Cast.md "wikilink").
Although his personal combat [spells](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink")
are not more than the [mages](:Category:_Mages.md "wikilink") of this
world, he has knowledge of arcane subjects no
[wizard](:Category:_Wizards.md "wikilink") of this world has dared to
think about in many millennia.*
