*The Illuminati [mage](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") has had runes
burned into his body to aid in his
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") [casting](Cast "wikilink").
Although his personal combat [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") are
not more than the [mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") of this world,
he has knowledge of arcane subjects no
[wizard](/wiki/Category%3A_Wizards "wikilink") of this world has dared to
think about in many millennia.*

**Keywords:** Illuminati, Mage.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 45.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[mage](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
