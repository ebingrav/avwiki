*The Illuminati Primi is one of the five members who lead the forces of
darkness on his world. He is here to check on the progress of the
takeover of this world and to aid where he can.*

**Keywords:** Primi.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 45.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** none.  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
