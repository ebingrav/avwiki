*An illusory shield is a projected image of the caster. Enemies can be
tricked into attacking the illusion instead of the
[psion](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink"), although the disguise only
works for a single hit.*

Prerequisite(s): [Displacement](/wiki/Displacement "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'illusory shield'.
