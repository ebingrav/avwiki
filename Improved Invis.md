Syntax: cast 'improved invis'

This spell improves on the classic 'invis' spell by allowing the caster
to remain invisible during combat. Unlike normal invisibility, the
strength and concentration required to cast it make it impossible to
cast upon on another.

of nowhere. Our archers tried to shoot back, but we could not see the
mage. The flames just kept coming and then the laughter began. I'll
