*With this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), you shall
regen your physical energy even quicker than before. Like [Enhanced
Vitality](/wiki/Enhanced_Vitality "wikilink"), however, you must be
[sleeping](/wiki/Sleep_(command) "wikilink") and it will only increase the
speed up to a certain point.*

Prerequisites: [Enhanced Vitality](/wiki/Enhanced_Vitality "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), just like its
predecessor [Enhanced Vitality](/wiki/Enhanced_Vitality "wikilink"), is rather
beneficial to [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") as it increases their
[move-point](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink") (?) regeneration rates.
