*On your [score](/wiki/Score "wikilink") sheet you may have noticed an
"incarnation" statistic. This number, which for most players is 1,
increases every time a player undergoes the [remort](/wiki/Remort "wikilink")
process and becomes a new [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Remort_Races "wikilink") or
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Remort_Classes "wikilink").*

*Much like the vanity popular among some [Humans](/wiki/Humans "wikilink") of
appending a numeral to their surname to indicate that they are among the
elite who have maintained a name through several generations, the
incarnation statistic is largely ornamental.*
