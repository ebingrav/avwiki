*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") enables the target
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") to see heat in the dark.
For some [races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Racial_Infravision "wikilink") is [an innate racial
ability](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink"). These
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink"), such as the
[drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink"), can then see in the dark. But, there may be a
price to pay, so these [races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") should
watch [their sensitivity to light in bright
rooms](/wiki/Racial_Nosun "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'infravision'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

See also [Racial Infravision](/wiki/Racial_Infravision "wikilink").

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in [Holy
Sight](/wiki/Holy_Sight "wikilink").

See also [Gear That Applies
'Infravision'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Infravision' "wikilink")
and [Infravision Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Infravision_Gear "wikilink").
