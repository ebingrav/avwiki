[hafted](/wiki/Category%3AHafted_Weapons "wikilink"). Since this weapon does
[piercing damage](/wiki/Category%3AMelee_Weapons_That_Pierce "wikilink") it is
[sharpenable](/wiki/Sharpen_Weapon "wikilink"). The [heavy spear,
'Ocean'](/wiki/Heavy_Spear,_"Ocean" "wikilink") in contrast is 15dr/10hr
weight 24.
