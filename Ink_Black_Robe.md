Comments
--------

Requires [Remove Curse](/wiki/Remove_Curse "wikilink") if you want to be able
to take it off, and fading if good aligned characters want to use it,
but otherwise a good upgrade from the [Chamorro
Ringmail](/wiki/Chamorro_Ringmail "wikilink"), as long as you remember not to
wear it when levelling.
