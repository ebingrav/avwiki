*The innocence [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") stops combat, much
like the [calm](/wiki/Calm "wikilink") or [awe](Awe "wikilink")
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), but is [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
directly on one ally. Most enemies attacking the target of this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") will feel compelled to spare the
innocent. Unlike [calm](/wiki/Calm "wikilink") or [awe](Awe "wikilink"), there
is no unpleasant side-effect that can worsen performance in future
combat.*

Prerequisite(s): [Absolve](/wiki/Absolve "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'innocence'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
