*As you look at the coin you notice how beautifully made the incision
is. It depicts a pick and hammer with a crown above... the royal symbol
of Domi. The coin is no bigger than a marble, yet the engravement is so
detailed you cannot get your eyes off it. You wonder if the coin is just
decorative or has a use.*

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks the chest to the
[Poignard Of Yashan](/wiki/Poignard_Of_Yashan "wikilink").

Directions
----------

**[Area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"):** [Duergar
Stronghold](/wiki/Category%3A_Duergar_Stronghold "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Duergar_Stronghold_Map "wikilink"))  
**[Room(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"):** Standing next to the
duergar throne.  
The coin is in the throne in the room; the throne acts as a chest. This
is also the same room [King Domi](/wiki/King_Domi "wikilink") is in. See [King
Domi](/wiki/King_Domi "wikilink") for more information.
