*[Psions](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") may now venture forth
briefly to see what resides in the next
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").
[Doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") do not bar the path of the spirit
as they get some insight into what is one
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") away.*

Prerequisites: [Minds Eye](/wiki/Minds_Eye "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'insight' <direction>.
