*This difficult-to-master lore grants
[Assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink") a SLIM chance to outright
kill an enemy when using [murder](/wiki/Murder "wikilink"),
[backstab](/wiki/Backstab "wikilink") or higher
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") equivalents with a poisoned
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Aimed Shot](/wiki/Aimed_Shot "wikilink"), [Assassin
Sight](/wiki/Assassin_Sight "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
