*While performing an Inspirating Dance, all allies in the same
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") who are awake and
[monitoring](/wiki/Monitor "wikilink") you gain [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink")
whenever you successfully [dodge](/wiki/Dodge "wikilink"). As with all
[Bladedancer](/wiki/Category%3A_Bladedancers "wikilink")
[stances](/wiki/Stance "wikilink"), this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") costs
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") to activate, and only functions if the
user is [wielding](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") identical
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Demonstrate](/wiki/Demonstrate "wikilink"), [Sublime
Grace](/wiki/Sublime_Grace "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](/wiki/Stance "wikilink") inspiring dance.
