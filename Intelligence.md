*This [attribute](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistics "wikilink") is as important to
a [mage](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") or
[cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") as [Wisdom](Wisdom "wikilink").
Higher intelligence results in more [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink")
gained per level, and allows all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") to
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") [skills and
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") with fewer
[practices](/wiki/Practice_Points "wikilink"). Also, higher intelligence
speeds regeneration of [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") when healing, and
increases damage from [spells](/wiki/Category%3ASpells "wikilink").*
