*When an [aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") enemy goes for a
[Bodyguard](/wiki/Category%3A_Bodyguards "wikilink")'s master, they'll often
find they never make it. A
[Bodyguard](/wiki/Category%3A_Bodyguards "wikilink") with this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") will automatically leap in front
of any foe he sees about to attack his current monitor target. The
[Bodyguard](/wiki/Category%3A_Bodyguards "wikilink") must have
[alertness](/wiki/Alertness "wikilink") active, and must be awake and not
presently fighting to be able to intercede. Foes who start combat with
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), or who use special sneaky
attacks can outfox an interceding
[Bodyguard](/wiki/Category%3A_Bodyguards "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Watchful Eye](/wiki/Watchful_Eye "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
