*The next time the target takes damage that would be fatal, they are
healed for 10 times that damage, and intervention ends. This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
[EXHAUSTED](/wiki/Exhaustion "wikilink") for a short time when
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Saving Grace](/wiki/Saving_Grace "wikilink"), [Via
Elimo](/wiki/Via_Elimo "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'intervention'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
