*The scrolls show plans for the Fae invasion of the Gith outpost in*  
*the Astral Domain. From the looks of things, the Githyanki don't*  
*stand a chance.*  
Type: Ticket  
Level: 125  
Weight: 2  
Flags: None  
Mob: [Southern Advisor](/wiki/Southern_Advisor "wikilink")  
Area: [Citadel Arcadia](/wiki/Category%3ACitadel_Arcadia "wikilink")  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

This item is used to reach the [Astral
Dragon](/wiki/Astral_Dragon "wikilink"), who carries the [Inferno
Stone](/wiki/Inferno_Stone "wikilink"). See [Gear In Astral
Invasion](/wiki/Category%3AGear_In_Astral_Invasion "wikilink") for more info
on how it is used.

This item loads on the [Southern Advisor](/wiki/Southern_Advisor "wikilink")
in [Citadel Arcadia](/wiki/Category%3ACitadel_Arcadia "wikilink"). It is
typically acquired through a small detour on a [Ruling
Glyph](/wiki/Ruling_Glyph "wikilink") run. See [Gear In Citadel
Arcadia](/wiki/Category%3AGear_In_Citadel_Arcadia "wikilink") for more
information on how to obtain this item.
