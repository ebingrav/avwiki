*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") decreases your
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") by the Grace of the
[Gods](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink"). It gives a bonus of
-100 [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"). It does NOT make you invincible.*

Prerequisite(s): [Holy Aura](/wiki/Holy_Aura "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'invincibility'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is fourth of four
[armor-class-boosting
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")
that both [clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") and
[priests](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") between [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 1 and
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 50.
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), except for
[druids](/wiki/Category%3A_Druids "wikilink") and (at [lord](Lord "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink"))
[paladins](/wiki/Category%3A_Paladins "wikilink"), will never be able to
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it. It is included in
[Aegis](/wiki/Aegis "wikilink") but *not* in [Awen](Awen "wikilink"). Its
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") bonus may be modified by its
[caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")'s choice of [worship](Worship "wikilink").
