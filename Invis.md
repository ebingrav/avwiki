*The INVIS [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") makes the target
character invisible. Invisible
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") will become visible when
they attack. NOTE: AVATAR [immortals](/wiki/Category%3A_Immortals "wikilink")
frown on [casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") invisibility on
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") used for
[training](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink") or
[healing](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink"). Be warned that attempting to
do so may result in swift retribution!*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'invis'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

See also [Racial Invis](/wiki/Racial_Invis "wikilink").

This is a highly valuable
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[clases](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") (who can) to learn. Invis is
one of three invaluable stealth
[skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), along with
[Sneak](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink") and [Move Hidden](Move_Hidden "wikilink"). It
is superfluous for [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
affected by [Mask](/wiki/Mask "wikilink"). Because
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") cease to be invisible
when they attack [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"),
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") normally wear gear like
[rings of the rat](/wiki/Ring_Of_The_Rat "wikilink") to provide invisibility
during runs, as it would be terribly wasteful to both time and
[mana](Mana_Points "wikilink") to re-[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") Invis after
every fight; however, like [Magic Light](/wiki/Magic_Light "wikilink"), Invis
may be highly useful during [corpse
retrivals](/wiki/Corpse_Retrieval "wikilink").

See also [Gear That Applies
'Invis'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Invis' "wikilink") and [Invis
Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Invis_Gear "wikilink").
