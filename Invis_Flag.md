This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") requires
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") to be
[affected](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") by [Detect
Invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink") (or by something comparable) in order to
see [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that have it.
