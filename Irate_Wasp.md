*Emotionless and blank, it's easy to forget that these beasts behave
entirely according to instinct. Unfortunately for you, this wasp's
instinct is to rip your skin off.*

**Keywords:** irate, wasp.  
**Scan Description:** An irate wasp turns to face you, and lunges!  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 65.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** Demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[assassin](/wiki/Assassin_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [Flying](/wiki/Flying "wikilink"), [detect
hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect

Comments
--------

This is an [EHA](/wiki/Category%3A_Elite_Hero_Areas "wikilink") mob, so
general rules regarding level 65 mobs do not apply.

The stinger that this mob wields cannot be
[decepted](/wiki/Deception "wikilink"), and disappears when the mob is killed.

This mob is an assassin, and will try to stab you. The stinger is
poisonous.
