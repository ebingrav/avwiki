*This creature is 12 feet tall. It resembles a normal stone
[gargoyle](/wiki/Gargoyles "wikilink"), but covered in iron scales and with
numerous spikes protruding from its body. Its eyes gleam a malignant
red, and flames lick around the edges of its mouth.*

**Keywords:** iron, gargoyle.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 40.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[fire-breathing](/wiki/Breathing_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
