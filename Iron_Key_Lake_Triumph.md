*This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is made of iron, with what
looks like iron vines wrapped around the shaft. At the head of the
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is the ancient symbol of the former
[demon](/wiki/Demons "wikilink") god Glasya.*

**Keyword(s):** key.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 29-31.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 0 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):**
[evil](/wiki/Evil_Flag "wikilink"), [magic](Magic_Flag "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks the door between A
trench at the bottom of the lake and Inside the iron bunker.
