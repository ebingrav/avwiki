*IRON SKIN transforms the caster's skin into a substance as hard as
iron. This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is a [BIOCENTRIC
Psionic Spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Biocentric_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). It
can only be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on others if the Caster first
masters the art of [BIO EMPATHY](/wiki/Bio_Empathy "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Calcify Flesh](/wiki/Calcify_Flesh "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'iron skin'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
