*Jackal, the head guard, is tall and sinewy, with a long mane of
jet-black hair and a confident grin. He stands purposefully in the
center of the white pattern. On his body is [armor made of strange black
metal](/wiki/Carburized_Steel_Plate_Armor "wikilink"), and in each of his
hands is [an immense sword](/wiki/Sword_Of_The_Survivor "wikilink"). On his
right wrist is [a strange white tattoo](/wiki/Survivor_Tattoo "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Jackal, head, guard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 75.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
