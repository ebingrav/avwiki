*Jackal, the head guard, is tall and sinewy, with a long mane of
jet-black hair and a confident grin. He stands purposefully in the
center of the white pattern. On his body is [armor made of strange black
metal](Carburized_Steel_Plate_Armor.md "wikilink"), and in each of his
hands is [an immense sword](Sword_Of_The_Survivor.md "wikilink"). On his
right wrist is [a strange white tattoo](Survivor_Tattoo.md "wikilink").*
