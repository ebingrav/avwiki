*Short brown fur covers the jackalope. Powerful hind legs propel the
jackalope across the prairie. Small, 2 prong antelope horns extend from
its head, giving it the appearance of a
[wizard](:Category:_Wizards.md "wikilink")'s experiment gone wrong.
Always on edge, the critter won't hesitate to defend it's territory.*
