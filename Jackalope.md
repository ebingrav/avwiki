*Short brown fur covers the jackalope. Powerful hind legs propel the
jackalope across the prairie. Small, 2 prong antelope horns extend from
its head, giving it the appearance of a
[wizard](/wiki/Category%3A_Wizards "wikilink")'s experiment gone wrong. Always
on edge, the critter won't hesitate to defend it's territory.*

**Keywords:** jackalope.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 13.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
