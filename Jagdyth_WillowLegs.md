*Jagdyth WillowLegs wants to be a
[warrior](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink"), but her legs are so long and
slender she often trips over them. Practicing with her
[sling](/wiki/Category%3A_Slings "wikilink") in the middens, shooting at rats,
she has not managed to kill one yet.*

**Keywords:** Jagdyth, WillowLegs, centaur.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 11.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
