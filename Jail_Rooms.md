[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") are where [capturing
mobs](/wiki/Capturing_Mobs "wikilink") will take
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") whom they capture and
where [townguards](/wiki/Townguard_Mobs "wikilink") will take
[outlawed](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink")
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") whom they capture.
