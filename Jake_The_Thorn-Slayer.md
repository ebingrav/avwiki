*Middle aged and past his prime, Jake still has some of the
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") he used to kill the
[dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") Thorn. He owns this shop and takes pride in
his ongoing [quest](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink") for those
willing. Some [dragons](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") exist that need killing.
[Heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") are made with such acts but seldom
are rewarded for their efforts. If you can give some [proof of your
kill](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink"), and [give](Give "wikilink") it to
Jake, he will reward you with [an
item](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quest_Rewards "wikilink") of possible use.*

**Keywords:** Jake, bartender.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
