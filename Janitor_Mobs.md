[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will pick up any [drink
containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Drink_Containers "wikilink"), any [trash
items](/wiki/Category%3A_Trash "wikilink"), and any other
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") worth up to 10 gold that they
see lying on the ground around them.

See also [Scavenging Mobs](/wiki/Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink").
