Quick and Easy Way
------------------

-   Portal [Tysiln San](/wiki/Tysiln_San "wikilink")
-   Go sesdw3n4en and kill the demons, one of them has the [Black Broken
    Cross](/wiki/Black_Broken_Cross "wikilink") (Aggies in path, 3 aggies in
    gear room)
-   Portal to [Horinos](/wiki/Horinos "wikilink") and give him the cross to
    get the [Jeweled Cross](/wiki/Jeweled_Cross "wikilink")
