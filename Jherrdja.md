*Neither man nor beast, this female chimera represents her brothers and
sisters. She is strong in spirit and conviction, but is a devout
pacifist.*

## Diplomatic Corps

Jherrdja represents [Centaurs](Centaurs "wikilink") in the Diplomatic
Corps.
