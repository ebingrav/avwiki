*Jim has traveled the East Road for 12 years doing this business. Every
year he comes back to [this field](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink") and
stays a tiny bit longer than last time. He will soon have to go to
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink") to pick up supplies.*

**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** (?).

[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").
