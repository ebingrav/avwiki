*Like all objects made by [goblin](Goblins "wikilink") hands, this
[key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") is made crudely, without any love of
this work. It is hard to believe that it can actually
[open](Unlock "wikilink") anything because of its crudeness. However,
[the goblin who held it](Goblin_Jailer "wikilink") clutched it tightly,
so it must be of some value, to someone.*
