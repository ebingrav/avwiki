## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") unlocks the gate at the south end
of [Solace](:Category:Town_Of_Solace "wikilink"). The gate is naturally
open, so it isn't very useful. One could lock the gate and confuse
everyone, but that's not very nice.
