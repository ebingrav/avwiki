## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") is used in [Mt.
Durr](:Category:_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Legacy of King Vorin
Quest](Legacy_Of_King_Vorin_Quest "wikilink"); it
[unlocks](Unlock "wikilink") the door between The Gateway to the Spirit
Realm and The Dwarven Hall of the Dead.
