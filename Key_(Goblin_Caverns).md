*Like all objects made by [goblin](Goblins.md "wikilink") hands, this
[key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") is made crudely, without any love
of this work. It is hard to believe that it can actually
[open](Unlock.md "wikilink") anything because of its crudeness. However,
[the goblin who held it](Goblin_Jailer.md "wikilink") clutched it
tightly, so it must be of some value, to someone.*

## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") unlocks the door between A
Torture Chamber and A Cramped Goblin Prison Cell.
