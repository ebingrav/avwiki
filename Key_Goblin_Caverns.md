*Like all objects made by [goblin](/wiki/Goblins "wikilink") hands, this
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is made crudely, without any love of
this work. It is hard to believe that it can actually
[open](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") anything because of its crudeness. However,
[the goblin who held it](/wiki/Goblin_Jailer "wikilink") clutched it tightly,
so it must be of some value, to someone.*

**Keyword(s):** goblin, key, crude.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 5-7.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <held>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 2 lbs.

[anti-good](/wiki/Anti-Good_Flag "wikilink"),
[anti-neutral](/wiki/Anti-Neutral_Flag "wikilink"),
[evil](/wiki/Evil_Flag "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks the door between A
Torture Chamber and A Cramped Goblin Prison Cell.
