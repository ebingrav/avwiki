## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") is used in [Elven
Foothills](:Category:_Elven_Foothills.md "wikilink")' [Gargoyle Bracer
Quest](Gargoyle_Bracer_Quest.md "wikilink"); it unlocks unlocks a glass
display case in A crumbling hallway in [Sarphyre's
Court](:Category:_Sarphyre's_Court.md "wikilink"); this case contains [a
Fae warding glyph](Fae_Warding_Glyph.md "wikilink"). Note that this case
is trapped and will shoot darts when opened.
