## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") is used in [Elven
Foothills](:Category:_Elven_Foothills "wikilink")' [Gargoyle Bracer
Quest](Gargoyle_Bracer_Quest "wikilink"); it unlocks unlocks a glass
display case in A crumbling hallway in [Sarphyre's
Court](:Category:_Sarphyre's_Court "wikilink"); this case contains [a
Fae warding glyph](Fae_Warding_Glyph "wikilink"). Note that this case is
trapped and will shoot darts when opened.
