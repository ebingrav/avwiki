Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is used in [Elven
Foothills](/wiki/Category%3A_Elven_Foothills "wikilink")' [Gargoyle Bracer
Quest](/wiki/Gargoyle_Bracer_Quest "wikilink"); it unlocks unlocks a glass
display case in A crumbling hallway in [Sarphyre's
Court](/wiki/Category%3A_Sarphyre's_Court "wikilink"); this case contains [a
Fae warding glyph](/wiki/Fae_Warding_Glyph "wikilink"). Note that this case is
trapped and will shoot darts when opened.
