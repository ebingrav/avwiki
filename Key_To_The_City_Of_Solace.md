Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks the gate at the south
end of [Solace](/wiki/Category%3ATown_Of_Solace "wikilink"). The gate is
naturally open, so it isn't very useful. One could lock the gate and
confuse everyone, but that's not very nice.
