and out of phase.*

**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is used in [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Legacy of King Vorin
Quest](/wiki/Legacy_Of_King_Vorin_Quest "wikilink"); it
[unlocks](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") the door between The Gateway to the Spirit
Realm and The Dwarven Hall of the Dead.
