*This simple [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") is made of a strong
silvery medal. It radiates an aura of blessings. The sigil of
[Tul-Sith](Tul-Sith.md "wikilink") is engraved into the body of the
[key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink"). It would be impossible to mistake
that it belongs somehow to [Tul-Sith](Tul-Sith.md "wikilink")'s Temple.*

**Keyword(s):** tul-sith, key.  
**[Level(s)](Object_Level.md "wikilink"):** 18-19.  
**[Type](:Category:_Object_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](Object_Slots.md "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](Object_Quality.md "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](Object_Weight.md "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](:Category:_Object_Flags.md "wikilink"):**
[glow](Glow_Flag.md "wikilink"), [good](Good_Flag.md "wikilink"),
[hum](Hum_Flag.md "wikilink"), [nodrop](NoDrop_Flag.md "wikilink").  

## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") unlocks the door east of The
Office of the Secretary to the Temple of Tul-Sith, which leads to
Tul-Sith's Treasure and Vault.
