*This [spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink") conjures an image of the
locked item's [key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink"), provided that the
[key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") isn't blocked by
[dark](Dark_Flag "wikilink") magic. The keyword can be a locked
[item](:Category:_Objects "wikilink"), a
[door](:Category:_Doors "wikilink") name, or even a direction.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'keyfinder' <keyword>.
