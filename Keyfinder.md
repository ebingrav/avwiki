*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") conjures an image of the
locked item's [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink"), provided that the
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") isn't blocked by
[dark](/wiki/Dark_Flag "wikilink") magic. The keyword can be a locked
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), a
[door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") name, or even a direction.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'keyfinder' <keyword>.
