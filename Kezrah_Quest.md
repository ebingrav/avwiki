To learn about the quest, read the description of the [Silver
Captain](/wiki/Silver_Captain "wikilink").

-   Find the [Water-Damaged Scroll](/wiki/Water-Damaged_Scroll "wikilink") in
    the area.
-   Give the [Water-Damaged Scroll](/wiki/Water-Damaged_Scroll "wikilink") to
    the [Silver Captain](/wiki/Silver_Captain "wikilink").

He gives you the [Silver Elixir](/wiki/Silver_Elixir "wikilink").
