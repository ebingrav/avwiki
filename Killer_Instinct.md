*At the height of their psionic training, [Black Circle
Initiates](/wiki/Category%3A_Black_Circle_Initiates "wikilink") can line up
mid-round [vital shots](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink") faster than normal.*

Prerequisite(s): [Sense Weakness](/wiki/Sense_Weakness "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
