Comments
--------

Casts [overconfidence](/wiki/Overconfidence "wikilink") and
[scramble](/wiki/Scramble "wikilink") and [brainstorm](Brainstorm "wikilink")

Killl'gher is guarded by three demons ([Tkachuk](/wiki/Tkachuk "wikilink")
level 65, [Forsberg](/wiki/Forsberg "wikilink") level 65, and
[Fleury](/wiki/Fleury "wikilink") level 63 \[hockey, eh?\]) (note: Tkachuk
sees invis and is aggie. The others are not aggie and will not assist on
heros, at least with sneak and move hidden.)

All 4 demons have [Sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
