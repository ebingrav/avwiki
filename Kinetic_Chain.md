*Tired of constantly picking up dropped
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink"), a
[psionicist](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") skilled in
[telekinesis](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
developed this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") to keep them
always near him. Whilst the [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
active, it will capture any [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") you
lose your grip on, returning it to your
[inventory](/wiki/Inventory "wikilink"). Each such capture will drain a little
of the caster's [mana points](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink"). This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") will be
[exhausted](/wiki/Exhaustion "wikilink") for a short time when
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Fetch](/wiki/Fetch "wikilink"),
[Levitation](/wiki/Levitation "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'kinetic chain'.

See also: [Kinetic Weapon Control](/wiki/Kinetic_Weapon_Control "wikilink")
