*The largest [duergar](/wiki/Duergar "wikilink") you have ever seen, King
Domi's hard face stares at you and his hands reach towards his weapons.
Obviously not someone to mess with, his scarred features show he has
been in many battles before, winning all.*

**Keywords:** King, Domi, duergar.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 27.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
