*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") reveals the
[alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") of the target
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Detect Alignment](/wiki/Detect_Alignment "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'know alignment'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is practically never used.
