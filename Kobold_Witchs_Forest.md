*This ugly creature reminds you somewhat of a rat with its long tail and
big pointy ears. He's small but ferocious looking.*

**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.

[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Category%3A_Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink").

Directions
----------

**[Area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"):** [The Witch's
Forest](/wiki/Category%3A_Witch's_Forest "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Witch's_Forest_Map "wikilink")).  
The Kobold [wanders](/wiki/Wandering_Mobs "wikilink").  
The Kobold is portable. However, there are many other kobolds in other
areas, so teleporting directly to the Kobold is not recommended.
