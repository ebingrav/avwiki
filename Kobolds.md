"Vermin" is the word most other races use to describe the scheming
Kobolds, and not just because of their rat-like faces. Conniving and
opportunistic, Kobolds can be surprisingly intelligent, but possess the
wisdom of an anthill; many of them are convinced magic simply does not
exist.

Kobolds can only remort into Goblins, and should only be selected by
players who are REALLY looking for a challenge.
