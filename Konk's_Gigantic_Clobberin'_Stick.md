*This stick, which apparently used to belong to someone named
[Konk](Konk.md "wikilink"), seems heavy enough that only the strongest
of [warriors](:Category:_Warriors.md "wikilink") could ever hope to
[wield](Wield.md "wikilink") it.*

[`Damage`](Melee_Weapon_Values.md "wikilink")` is 9-10 to 23-24 (average 16-17).`  
`Modifies `[`damage`` ``roll`](Damage_Roll.md "wikilink")` by 8 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** stick, konks, gigantic, clobbering.  
**[Level(s)](Object_Level.md "wikilink"):** 19-21.  
**[Type](:Category:_Object_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[weapon](:Category:_Melee_Weapons.md "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](Object_Slots.md "wikilink"):** <wielded>.  
**[Quality](Object_Quality.md "wikilink"):** 50 hps.  
**[Weight](Object_Weight.md "wikilink"):** 52 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](:Category:_Object_Flags.md "wikilink"):** none.  

## Comments

This [weapon](:Category:_Melee_Weapons.md "wikilink") is so
[heavy](Object_Weight.md "wikilink") that
[characters](:Category:_Characters.md "wikilink") must have at least 28
[strength](Strength.md "wikilink") to [wield](Wield.md "wikilink") it
(i.e. Giants, Dragons and Golems). Does great smash damage!
