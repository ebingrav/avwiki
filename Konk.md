*Amusingly, this nervous [giant](/wiki/Giants "wikilink") looks as if he would
believe whatever answer you gave him. He appears to be very much at home
in the woods. His clothing seems to be of [elvish](/wiki/Elves "wikilink")
style, curiously, and from [his
weapon](/wiki/Konk's_Gigantic_Clobberin'_Stick "wikilink"), you gather that
his name must be Konk. Konk appears to be waiting in this thicket for
something--or for someone other than you--and, judging from all of the
trampled underbrush about him, he has been doing so for some time now.
He looks a bit uncomfortable as his stomach grumbles loudly; he seems to
be terribly hungry.*

**Keywords:** giant, bodyguard, Konk, nervous, hungry.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.

Comments
--------

Konk is [Centaur Hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink")'s only
[portable](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") [mob](Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink").

Because Konk [stands](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink") in a
"[safe](/wiki/Safe_Rooms "wikilink")" room,
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who want [his
stick](/wiki/Konk's_Gigantic_Clobberin'_Stick "wikilink") may not obtain it by
[killing](/wiki/Kill "wikilink") him; however, he will give one to those who
complete a simple [ticket quest](/wiki/Category%3A_Ticket_Quests "wikilink").
See [Konk's Gigantic Clobberin' Stick
Quest](/wiki/Konk's_Gigantic_Clobberin'_Stick_Quest "wikilink") for more
information on this matter.
