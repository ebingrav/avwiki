*This stick, which apparently used to belong to someone named
[Konk](/wiki/Konk "wikilink"), seems heavy enough that only the strongest of
[warriors](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") could ever hope to
[wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") it.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 9-10 to 23-24 (average 16-17).`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 8 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** stick, konks, gigantic, clobbering.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 19-21.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <wielded>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 50 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 52 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

This [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink") is so
[heavy](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink") that
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must have at least 28
[strength](Strength "wikilink") to [wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") it.
