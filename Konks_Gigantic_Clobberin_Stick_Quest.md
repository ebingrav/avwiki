*Poor [Konk](/wiki/Konk "wikilink") has grown terribly hungry while waiting
(in vain) in a thicket for his beloved master, Kemenvarno, to return.
Fortunately, his [centaur](/wiki/Centaur "wikilink") hosts have just prepared
[a heap of baked potatoes](/wiki/Heap_Of_Baked_Potatoes "wikilink") for him to
eat. He would be very grateful to receive them...*

From [Konk](/wiki/Konk "wikilink"):

-   Go \[s\], 2s, 4w, s.

<!-- -->

-   [Get](/wiki/Get "wikilink") [a heap of baked
    potatoes](/wiki/Heap_Of_Baked_Potatoes "wikilink") from ground.

<!-- -->

-   Go n, 4e, 3n.

<!-- -->

-   [Give](/wiki/Give "wikilink") [a heap of baked
    potatoes](/wiki/Heap_Of_Baked_Potatoes "wikilink") to
    [Konk](/wiki/Konk "wikilink") and receive [Konk's Gigantic Clobberin'
    Stick](/wiki/Konk's_Gigantic_Clobberin'_Stick "wikilink").
