*With a vague red skin and an Orcish build this creature is smaller than
all other Orcs. His eyes are fierce and teeth half rotten. In his hands
he holds some kind of slingshot weapon.*

**Keywords:** Kwikua, orc.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 80.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** neutral.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink"), [detect
hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").

Comments
--------

The mob can hit hard (fusilier!), depending on your Eragora spells, and
will throw [a pile of rotten fruit](/wiki/Pile_of_Rotten_Fruit "wikilink"). If
you are a small caster, surge very high and bring him down before he
endangers you.
