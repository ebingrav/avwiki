*This Kzinti wears no armor, but carries an impressive looking crossbow.
His sight is keen, and he walks along the citadel walls, watching for
trouble. You can never be too careful, even in the middle of the
desert.*

**Keywords:** kzinti, sentry.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 41.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"),
[hide](/wiki/Hide "wikilink").
