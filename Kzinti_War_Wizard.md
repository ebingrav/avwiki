*This tall wizard has a scarred face, apparently from great burns. An
accident with a fireball, perhaps, but it only adds to his fire for
conquest. His explosive magicks have lead to many Kzinti victories, and
he hopes someday to add another notch to his belt.*

**Keywords:** kzinti, war, wizard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 65.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink"),
[aggie](/wiki/Aggressive "wikilink"), [hide](Hide "wikilink"), [detect
hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink").

Directions
----------

**[Area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"):** [Kzinti
Outpost](/wiki/Category%3A_Kzinti_Outpost "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Kzinti_Outpost_Map "wikilink")).  

Walking route from [Kzinti weaponsmith](/wiki/Kzinti_Weaponsmith "wikilink"):
s, 2e, 2n, \[n\], n, u, \[s}, s, e, n u.  
To get past the first locked door you must kill the 2 [Kzinti
assassins](/wiki/Kzinti_Assassin "wikilink") for [a glowing kzinti
insignia](a_Glowing_Kzinti_Insignia "wikilink"). To get past the second
locked door you must kill [The Kzinti field
lieutenant](/wiki/Kzinti_Field_Lieutenant "wikilink") for the [a special
Kzinti insignia](a_Special_Kzinti_Insignia "wikilink"). The path to the
War Wizard contain numerous [aggie](/wiki/Aggressive "wikilink") mobs.

The Kzinti war wizard is not portable.
