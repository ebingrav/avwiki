*At first glance, the weaponsmith is very well built, yet slender and
agile. Assuming it's a male, you are slightly surprised when your second
glance reveals a more feminine figure. Regardless, she
[wields](Wield.md "wikilink") [one of the wicked kzinti
claws](Kzinti_War_Claw.md "wikilink") with great skill, and strikes
consistent blows against [a column of stone](Whetstone.md "wikilink") to
test [the weapon](Kzinti_War_Claw.md "wikilink")'s strength. To your
dismay, [the stone](Whetstone.md "wikilink") is turning out to be the
weaker of the two.*
