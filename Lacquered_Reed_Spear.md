*This spear is made of tightly woven reeds of different thicknesses and
covered with a shiny black lacquer that makes it harder than any
material you have ever seen before.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 7 to 24 (average 15).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 4 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`dexterity`](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** reed spear.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <wielded>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 50 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 7 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

This piece of gear can also be found on various mobs of different
levels. The lowest level this item can be found is level 20, on the
[Gate Guard](/wiki/Gate_Guard "wikilink"). The highest it can be found is on
hero mobs, level 51.
