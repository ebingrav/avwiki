*Made entirely from ordinary leaves that have been stitched together,
and then carefully lacquered with some extremely hard but flexible
resin, this vest is surprisingly light. However, you would suspect that
it not especially durable.*

`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous`  
`Modifies `[`armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")` by -5 continuous.`

Comments
--------

This item is not the best gear for tanking, or for hitting, but it
provides a combination of both, so may be useful for soloing. It is also
extremely light, which may be useful for races with low
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink").
