*The Lady Karen looks unearthly beautiful. She wears [a silky white
leather dress](White_Leather_Dress.md "wikilink") that fails to cover
her body completely. You cannot help but stare. She is wearing [a
gauntlet](Wamphyri_War_Gauntlet.md "wikilink") on her right hand that
looks like a jumble of razors and hooks. She will not kill you on sight
for [Harry](Harry_Keogh.md "wikilink") has helped her control her
Wamphyri desires. However, to attack her is to ask for death.*
