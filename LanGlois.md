*LanGlois has just recently inherited her father's armor shop. She works
at tanning the alligator hides and weaving the special moss, found in
the area, into a strong but light weight
[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** LanGlois, woman, armourer.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[shopkeeper](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
