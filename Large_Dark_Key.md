Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks the chest in Werredan's
Treasure and Vault. It also unlocks the door north of Before the Alter
of Werredan, which leads to the passageway to the second floor of
Werredan's Temple.
