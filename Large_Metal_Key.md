*This large [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink"), made of shiny metal,
must [unlock](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") something unusual. It looks too large
to open a [door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink"). Wonder what it
[unlocks](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink")?*

**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks the strongbox in
Tul-Sith's Treasure and Vault. Oddly, it also unlocks the door west of
The Vestry of the Temple of Werredan, which is a room on the path from
the first floor of Werredan's Temple to the second floor of Werredan's
Temple.
