Modifies AC by -25 continuous  
Bases: 10-12  
Levels: 50-52  
Weight: 1  
Area: [House Of Beasts](/wiki/House_Of_Beasts "wikilink")  
Mob: [Beast Lemans](/wiki/Beast_Lemans "wikilink")  

To obtain the lemans family seal, portal to a
[Carnataur](/wiki/Carnataur "wikilink") or [Ursataur](Ursataur "wikilink").
This will take you to the [House of Beasts](/wiki/House_of_Beasts "wikilink").
Once there, you may track [Lemans](/wiki/Beast_LeMans "wikilink") to search
him out. He will be in an alcove that opens to the south. His private
chamber is behind a closed door to the west, which you have to "pull
candelabra" to open.
