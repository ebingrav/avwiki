*Leadership is a [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")...not
a given right. Some guilds pride themselves on their leadership, and
some do not bother to learn more than the basics. The more
[skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") you know, and the
[smarter](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink") you are, the greater the group you
can lead.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is first of six
leadership skills that [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
may potentially [learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"), dependent upon their
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), at either
[lowmort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink") or
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink"). See also [Leading
Ability](/wiki/Leading_Ability "wikilink").
