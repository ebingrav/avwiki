`Modifies hit roll by 5 continuous.`

Levels: 50-52  
Base: 10-12  
Area: [Bloodsong Island](/wiki/Category%3A_Bloodsong_Island "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Bloodsong_Island_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: [Ticket Quests](/wiki/Category%3ATicket_Quests "wikilink"), give items to
[Nenba](/wiki/Nenba_The_Archer "wikilink")  

PP [Nenba](/wiki/Nenba_The_Archer "wikilink") and start searching the area for
tickets. For more specific info see [Leather Of Crecy
Quest](/wiki/Leather_Of_Crecy_Quest "wikilink"). The underground area is
no-spell, and if you die to Gorre, your corpse is moved to Nenba.
