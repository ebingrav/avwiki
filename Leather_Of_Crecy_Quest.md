Teleport to [Nenba](/wiki/Nenba_The_Archer "wikilink") to reach [Bloodsong
Island.](/wiki/Category%3ABloodsong_Island "wikilink") Skin the corpses of the
4 bloodlusters then get the paper from [Gorre The
Slaughterkhan](/wiki/Gorre_The_Slaughterkhan "wikilink"). Then give the paper
to [Nenba](/wiki/Nenba_The_Archer "wikilink") and he will make it for you.
