You need to get the head and shaft of Vorin's war axe. One piece of it
is on [Nesin](/wiki/Nesin "wikilink"), who hangs out in [Mt
Durr](/wiki/Category%3AMt_Durr "wikilink"), right by the entrance to [The
Great Divide](/wiki/Category%3AGreat_Divide "wikilink").  
The other piece of the axe is held by [Garm](/wiki/Garm "wikilink"), a
ferocious beast at the bottom of the mines of Mt. Durr. To get there,
start out by buying a level 1 mine pass from the mining guild. Once
you're on the first level of the mine you can kill a pilgrim to get the
level 2 mine pass. On level 2 you can kill a dwarf named Pick for the
level 3 mine pass. Garm is on level 3.  
Once you have both pieces of the axe, you need to give them to the shade
of [Queen Ruahn](/wiki/Queen_Ruahn "wikilink"), who was Vorin's wife. She's in
the dwarven spirit realm, behind a locked door. The key is on the spirit
guard, who is in the same room as the locked door. After you give her
both pieces, she'll give you the restored axe, and open up a portal to
the ghost of the king. Go in the portal, give the king back his axe, and
he'll give you the [Legacy Of King
Vorin](/wiki/Legacy_Of_King_Vorin "wikilink").

This may help you get around Mt. Durr: [Mt Durr
Map](/wiki/Mt_Durr_Map "wikilink")
