*The leper begs pitifully, "Please, help me follow the sweet music! Help
me get to the paradise at the end of the alley! I crawl a little closer
every night, but I can't get there fast enough! You MUST help me!" When
it becomes apparent you have no intention of helping the man, he crawls
at you, slobbering and screaming, "You want to keep it all for yourself!
I'll KILL you!"*
