Get the first shot on the Inferno stone mob (called first shard), the
Lodestone Mob, and the Sceptre mob. Keep in mind that Sceptre and Lode
are reboot only. You need to get the first shot in so the shards spawn.
Don't be afraid of dying since you're about to evolve anyways!
