*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") represents added accuracy,
not in terms of hitting one's opponent, but rather of hitting specific
vital areas. Once a [rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") has learned
this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), he can aim [vital
shots](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink") at any of the specific body parts it can
hit. Aiming is harder than a random [vital shot](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink"),
but high [dex](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink") will help. You can currently hit:
head, heart, lungs, kneecap, and eye.*

Prerequisites: [rogue lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink"), [Vital
Shot](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink").

Syntax: [vital](/wiki/Vital "wikilink")
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt; <body-part>.  
Syntax: [vs](Vs "wikilink") &lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;
<body-part>.
