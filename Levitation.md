*Often confused for a parlor trick or a basic
[mage](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink")'s conjuring trick, the use of
psionics to levitate is unique in that it actually creates a
[telekinetic pull](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
to separate the target from the ground by a few feet. Flying bestows
several advantages, such as reduced [movement
costs](/wiki/Movement_Costs "wikilink"), and it makes it easy to traverse
[airborne](/wiki/Air_Terrain "wikilink") or [watery](Water_Terrain "wikilink")
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"). Since this is a [TELEKINETIC
spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), it can
easily be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on others.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'levitation'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

[Psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") may find this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") rather useful at
[lowmort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") but usually [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
[Fly](/wiki/Fly "wikilink") instead of it at
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink"). This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is superfluous for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") affected by
[Fly](/wiki/Fly "wikilink"). See [Flying Flag](Flying_Flag "wikilink") for
further information.
