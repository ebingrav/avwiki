*This is what appears to be a simple black uniform, well starched, made
to fit. Embroidered on the left chest is the symbol of the Guard of
[Werredan](/wiki/Werredan "wikilink"). On the epaulets, single bars of
blackened steel indicate this uniform belongs to a
[lieutenant](/wiki/Lieutenant "wikilink") of the guard.*

[`Armor` `Class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 5.`  
`Modifies `[`armor`
`class`](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")` by -3 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`strength`](/wiki/Strength "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`
