*The guineafowl egg has long been believed to have mythical healing
properties. They are rare and ferociously defended by the mother
gunieafowl. This particular egg is small and a uniform powder blue. It
gives off a light glow of the same blue. The resulting halo almost makes
those who look at it feel warm and welcome. It is almost begging to be
picked up...*
