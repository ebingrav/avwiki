*This [crossbow](/wiki/Category%3A_Light_Crossbows "wikilink") was constructed
of the best woods and metals as only a [Centaur](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink")
would. It's light and lovingly engraved with images of horses. It is
silent but deadly.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Missile_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 11 to 34 (average 22).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 15 continuous.`
