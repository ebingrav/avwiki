[Light](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have one
[value](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **Light Duration:** This value determines how many
    [tics](/wiki/Tics "wikilink") this [light](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink")
    will last before burning out, which may be some finite number (as
    for most [lights](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink") that are
    [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") 1-10, including those created by
    [casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") '[magic light](Magic_Light "wikilink")')
    or infinite.

See also [Lights](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink").
