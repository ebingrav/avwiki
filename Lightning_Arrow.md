This skill allows you to create lightning arrows, bolts and stones and
uses mana to fletch them.

Prerequisite(s): [Fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink"), [Chain
Lightning](/wiki/Chain_Lightning "wikilink")

Syntax: [fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink")
&lt;[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink")/[bolts](Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink")/[stones](Category%3A_Stones "wikilink")&gt;
lightning.

The secondary damage ammunition for lord archer types.

Does less damage than [Ice Arrow](/wiki/Ice_Arrow "wikilink") however stuns
the mob putting them in lag which can be useful against certain types of
lord mobs.

This ammunition does not survive being used.
