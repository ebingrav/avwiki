`Modifies Mana by 5 continuous`  
`Modifies Wisdom by 1 continuous`

Slot: About Body  
Base: 1-3  
Level: 6  
Weight: 5  
Area: [Dwarven Day Care](/wiki/Category%3A_Dwarven_Day_Care "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Meadow_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: [The nanny](/wiki/The_nanny "wikilink")  
Go to the [Dwarven Day Care](/wiki/Category%3ADwarven_Day_Care "wikilink"),
find the nanny (she wanders) and kill her to get the linen robe. While
you're there you might want to pick up some [Pink Ice
Ring](/wiki/Pink_Ice_Ring "wikilink"). Keep in mind that the mobs in this area
steal, and many [assist](assist "wikilink").
