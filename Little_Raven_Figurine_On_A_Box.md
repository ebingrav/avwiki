*This figurine is very delicate and well carved. It was carved from a
black stone, the color of jet. The eyes have little rubies and twinkle
in such a way they almost appear alive.*

## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") unlocks a chest in the shape
of a raven in Sssanth's Hidden Hut.
