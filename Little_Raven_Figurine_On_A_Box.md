*This figurine is very delicate and well carved. It was carved from a
black stone, the color of jet. The eyes have little rubies and twinkle
in such a way they almost appear alive.*

**Keyword(s):** raven, figurine, key.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks a chest in the shape of
a raven in Sssanth's Hidden Hut.
