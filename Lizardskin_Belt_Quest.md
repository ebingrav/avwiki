Find the [Mated Pair Of Cave
Lizards](/wiki/Mated_Pair_Of_Cave_Lizards "wikilink"), kill it and skin it. It
will create [a pair of cave lizard
skins](/wiki/Pair_Of_Cave_Lizard_Skins "wikilink"). Give this to
[Jon](/wiki/Jon "wikilink") and his will give you a [ lizardskin
belt](/wiki/Lizardskin_Belt "wikilink").
