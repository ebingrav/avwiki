Find the [hunting pack of cave
lizards](/wiki/Hunting_Pack_Of_Cave_Lizards "wikilink"), kill it and skin it.
It will create [a stack of cave lizard
skins](/wiki/Stack_Of_Cave_Lizard_Skins "wikilink"). Give this to
[Rojer](/wiki/Rojer "wikilink") and his will give you a [ lizardskin
Jerkin](/wiki/Lizardskin_Jerkin "wikilink").
