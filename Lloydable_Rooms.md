[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") allow players within them to
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") certain spells that they cannot
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") elsewhere.
