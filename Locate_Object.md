*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") reveals the location of all
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") with the given name. When
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") as [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'locate object' my
<name>, locate object will only find
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that you own (ie, are bound to
you).*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'locate object'
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") can be highly
useful to [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") in finding
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") that they want to obtain or that
they have misplaced.

See also [Nolocate Flag](/wiki/Nolocate_Flag "wikilink").
