Some AVATAR MUD [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") are lockable. Such
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") are usually closed and locked each
time that their [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") is "reset". Players
may move beyond closed locked [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") by
using at least one of four techniques, which are as follows:

1) Players who [find](/wiki/Keyfinder "wikilink"), obtain, and carry a closed
locked [door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink")'s associated
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") may [unlock](Unlock "wikilink") it
and then [open](/wiki/Open "wikilink") it. This technique will not work for
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") that have no associated
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").

2) Players who know how to [pick locks](/wiki/Pick_Lock "wikilink") may
[pick](/wiki/Pick "wikilink") a closed locked
[door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink")'s lock and then
[open](/wiki/Open "wikilink") it. This technique will not work for
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") that are
[pickproof](/wiki/Pickproof_Doors "wikilink").

3) Players who know how to [bash doors](/wiki/Bashdoor "wikilink") may bash a
closed locked [door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") open. This technique
will not work for [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") that are
[bashproof](/wiki/Bashproof_Doors "wikilink").

4) Players who can magically [pass doors](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink") may move
through closed locked [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") without
interacting with them. This technique will not work for
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") that are
[passproof](/wiki/Passproof_Doors "wikilink").
