*Lolin Longbeard is the newest deputy which may explain why he gets the
dirtiest jobs, like cleaning the graffiti off the walls here. [Jeremiah
Longbeard](/wiki/Jeremiah_Longbeard "wikilink") knows who did this but he has
no proof, so he has posted Lolin here hoping the Left Eye Gang will slip
up.*
