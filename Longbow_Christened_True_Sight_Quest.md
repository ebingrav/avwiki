*[A twisted hunter](/wiki/Twisted_Hunter "wikilink") would like to make [a
nice new bow](/wiki/Longbow_Christened_'True_Sight' "wikilink") but needs [a
suitable string](/wiki/Long_Black_Tentacle_String "wikilink") to do so.
Fortunately, he has found [a dangerous
mob](/wiki/Thin_Black_Tentacle "wikilink") that will provide him with [such a
string](/wiki/Long_Black_Tentacle_String "wikilink"). He would be very
grateful to receive some assistance...*
