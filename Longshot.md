*With a high, arcing shot, an [archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink")
can hit a foe who is in the next [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").
More often than not, the victim will move out to attack its assailant;
however, some victims may instead run away or simply stay put and dodge
future volleys. [Short bows](/wiki/Category%3A_Short_Bows "wikilink") and
[light crossbows](/wiki/Category%3A_Light_Crossbows "wikilink") cannot perform
a longshot.*

Prerequisite(s): [Held Shot](/wiki/Held_Shot "wikilink").

Syntax: longshot <direction>
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.  
Syntax: [ls](/wiki/Ls "wikilink") <direction>
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
