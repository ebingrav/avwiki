This term normally refers to [getting](/wiki/Get "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") from a corpse.

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may automatically loot
corpses of any [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") that they
[kill](/wiki/Kill "wikilink") using [AutoLoot](AutoLoot "wikilink").
