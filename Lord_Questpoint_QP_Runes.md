Since October 15, 2011, certain gear runs contain mobs that have runes
that can be traded into Prenethia (at Thorngate, teleable) for
questpoints. These runes are reboot only. Since then they have been
reshuffled to provide both XP and QP, depending on the difficulty of the
run in question.

**10 Questpoints**

-   Despair - Githyanki Warlock ([Silver
    Chestplate](/wiki/Silver_Chestplate "wikilink"))
-   Destruction - Githyanki Warlock
-   Enslavement - Githyanki Warlock
-   Fatigue - The Burning Phoenix ([Phoenix-Feather
    Greaves](/wiki/Phoenix-Feather_Greaves "wikilink"))
-   Regeneration - The Burning Phoenix

**5 Questpoints**

-   Disease - Imix, lord of the fire elementals. ([Sceptre of Blazing
    Fury](/wiki/Sceptre_of_Blazing_Fury "wikilink"))
-   Fire - Imix, lord of the fire elementals
-   Insanity - [Orius The Windprince](/wiki/Orius_The_Windprince "wikilink")
    ([Gold Belly Chain](/wiki/Gold_Belly_Chain "wikilink"))
-   Pain - [Orius The Windprince](/wiki/Orius_The_Windprince "wikilink")

**400 XP**

-   Chaos - statue of Anthraxus ([Stone Wings](/wiki/Stone_Wings "wikilink"))
-   Obfuscation - statue of Anthraxus

**300 XP**

-   Darkness - Sun Guardian ([Black Circle
    Master](/wiki/Black_Circle_Master "wikilink"), enter house)
-   Drought - Sun Guardian
-   Fear - Sun Guardian
-   Charm - an elemental consort ([Elemental Earth
    Ring](/wiki/Elemental_Earth_Ring "wikilink"))
-   Entropy - an elemental consort

**250 XP**

-   Power - Finn (detour on [Ruling Glyph](/wiki/Ruling_Glyph "wikilink") run)
-   Wrath - Finn

**200 XP**

-   Influence - Emissary of the mind flayers ([Githzerai
    Keep](/wiki/Category%3AGithzerai_Keep "wikilink")
-   Corruption - Emissary of the mind flayers

**100 XP**

-   Misfortune - [Lord Gith](/wiki/Lord_Gith "wikilink") ([Crown Of Wire And
    Broken Glass](/wiki/Crown_Of_Wire_And_Broken_Glass "wikilink"))
-   Blood - [Lord Gith](/wiki/Lord_Gith "wikilink")
-   Silence - [Lord Gith](/wiki/Lord_Gith "wikilink")

    Water](/wiki/Category%3APlane_Of_Water "wikilink"))
-   Ice - [Ice Efreet](/wiki/Ice_Efreet "wikilink")
-   Vengeance - [Ice Efreet](/wiki/Ice_Efreet "wikilink")
