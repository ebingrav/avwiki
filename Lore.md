*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") allows you to
see basic information about [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
which you [examine](/wiki/Examine "wikilink"). This essential but primitive
knowledge of [equipment](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") is the first in a
series of [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") that
augment your intuitive understanding of
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [examine](/wiki/Examine "wikilink")
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.

This is a useful [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") to
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink").
