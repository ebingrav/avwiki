*Lurch's Adder is evil objectified into a slim but powerful dagger.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 7-10 to 21-24 (average 15-16).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 4 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 4 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** dagger, adder, lurch.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 15.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <wielded>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 50 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 5 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** glow, hum.  

Comments
--------

Note that this being a lowmort item - it is creatable with LOWMORT
perfect gems. Those being impossible to obtain during regular gameplay,
you will have to use hero level ones.

For an item that is essentially a
[Shadowfang](/wiki/Dagger_"Shadowfang" "wikilink") clone with 2 extra HR,
accessible 2 levels earlier, the price is VERY steep. Buyer beware.

Directions
----------

`HELP MANIFEST`  
`Example: to create the object "Lurch's Adder", you would first`  
`embed a dagger with:`  
`         2 perfect diamonds`  
`         1 perfect ruby`  
`         1 perfect emerald`  
`         1 perfect amethyst`  
`To manifest the dagger, type:`  
`cast manifest dagger "Lurch's Adder"`
