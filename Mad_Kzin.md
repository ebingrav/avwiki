The Mad Kzin has the Token of Insanity and The Head of Jubal the
Benevolent (Note: Make links later).

Portal to Elven Demonologist (elf dem) in the Flipside Altar in the
Temple of Avatar. Move as follows:

s,s,{d},s,s,e,d,w,w,s

The Mad Kzin is listed by another reference as a level 40 mob, but is
very possibly (note: author's opinion only) at least 45 strength.

The books, just like all books in the Temple Library are aggie; however
they cannot see movehidden.
