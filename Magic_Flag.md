This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") prevents
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that have it from being
[transmuted](/wiki/Transmute "wikilink"). It may be added via either
[enchanting](/wiki/Enchanting "wikilink") or the
[Transmute](/wiki/Transmute "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").

See also [Transmute](/wiki/Transmute "wikilink").
