*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") creates a ball of light
which you can [hold](/wiki/Hold "wikilink") as a [light
source](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink"). It isn't much, and it certainly
won't last forever, but it beats fumbling around in the dark, especially
if there are things there that plan to eat you.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'magic light'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") can be quite handy for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who can not yet obtain
or [hold](/wiki/Hold "wikilink") a permanent [light
source](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink") (such as an [orb of faerie
magic](/wiki/Orb_Of_Faerie_Magic "wikilink")) and who do not wish to
[buy](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") temporary ones. It can also be handy for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who
[die](/wiki/Death "wikilink") in [dark places](Dark_Rooms "wikilink") after
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") ?? (when [Tul-Sith](Tul-Sith "wikilink") quits
giving [lights](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink") to dead players) and then
try to [retrieve their gear](/wiki/Corpse_Retrieval "wikilink") on their own.
