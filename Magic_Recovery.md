*An advanced form of [spell recovery](/wiki/Spell_Recovery "wikilink"), this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") enables the user to
recoup some of their wasted [magical energy](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink")
when a [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is fumbled.*

Prerequisite(s): [Spell Recovery](/wiki/Spell_Recovery "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") can be rather
useful to [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") that are both
of spellcasting [classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") (like
[psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink"),
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink"), and
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink")) and also of
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") that are relatively prone to
[spellcasting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") failure (like
[giants](/wiki/Giants "wikilink"), [goblins](Goblins "wikilink"), and
[kobolds](/wiki/Kobolds "wikilink")) by reducing
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") wasted by their failed
[casts](/wiki/Cast "wikilink").
