*Crackling and whizzing around the room, this magical presence never
finds a moment of rest as it searches for something else to maim. It is
made of a complete absence of color... literally. Its body is
translucent, like a ghost's, but even more bewilderingly, everything
directly behind the sphere is awash in a dull gray - even the otherwise
deep blue flickering torches lining the corridor.*
