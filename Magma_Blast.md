*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") will channel a flow of
magma directly into one [opponent](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), heavily
damaging them. All basic attack and area spells are more powerful for
in-class users who do not have to fear a counterattack thanks to [a
group-mate tanking the target](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Fireball](/wiki/Fireball "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'magma blast'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This single-target damage [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
eighth of ten that [mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") between [level](Level "wikilink") 1 and
[level](Level "wikilink") 50. [Mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may
find it useful until they [learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink")
[Desiccate](/wiki/Desiccate "wikilink") or when they are low on
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of many other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") may
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it at [hero](Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") but will find it fairly useless to
them.
