''Mana is the power for all magic spells. Each spell costs a certain
amount of mana; for most spells, this cost goes down as the player
increases in [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"). Also, the player gains more mana
per [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") if [intelligence](Intelligence "wikilink")
is high.

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may
[wear](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") [gear to increase their mana
points](/wiki/Category%3A_Mana_Gear "wikilink").
