''[Objects](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink") can be rematrixed and
recreated when they have been placed under a
[spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink"), with the proper ingredients.
Once the correct recipe of perfect gemstones has been
[embedded](Embed.md "wikilink") into an
[object](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink"), that
[object](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink") can be manifested into a
different, unique [item](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink").

Prerequisite(s): [Gem Lore](Gem_Lore.md "wikilink"), [Magic
Lore](Magic_Lore.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast.md "wikilink") 'manifest'
\<[object](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink")\> "<recipe name>"

Example: To create the [object](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink")
"[Lurch's Adder](Lurch's_Adder "wikilink")", you would first
[embed](Embed.md "wikilink") a dagger with two perfect diamonds, one
perfect ruby, one perfect emerald, and one perfect amethyst, and you
would then type *[cast](Cast.md "wikilink") 'manifest' dagger "[Lurch's
Adder](Lurch's_Adder "wikilink")"*.

See also [Manifest Gear](:Category:_Manifest_Gear.md "wikilink").
