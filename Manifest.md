''[Objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") can be rematrixed and
recreated when they have been placed under a
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), with the proper ingredients.
Once the correct recipe of perfect gemstones has been
[embedded](/wiki/Embed "wikilink") into an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), that
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") can be manifested into a
different, unique [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). NOTE: Be
warned that the gemstones [embedded](/wiki/Embed "wikilink") into
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") disappear once you [log
off](/wiki/Quit "wikilink"). Please also note that you cannot manifest items
that have been [etched](/wiki/Etch "wikilink").

Prerequisite(s): [Gem Lore](/wiki/Gem_Lore "wikilink"), [Magic
Lore](/wiki/Magic_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'manifest'
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt; "<recipe name>"

Example: To create the [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") "Lurch's
Adder", you would first [embed](/wiki/Embed "wikilink") a dagger with two
perfect diamonds, one perfect ruby, one perfect emerald, and one perfect
amethyst, and you would then type *[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'manifest'
dagger "Lurch's Adder"*.
