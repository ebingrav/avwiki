*A keen awareness of one's environment allows some to derive more than
the usual benefits from reading maps. After one hones the
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") of map lore, subtle distinctions
in [terrain](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink") suddenly show their true
value, and and it becomes possible to see whenever one crosses a border
between [areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"). An added benefit of the
increased attention to detail this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") imparts is the ability to read
[markings](/wiki/Markings "wikilink") left in a room with the
[woodcraft](/wiki/Woodcraft "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"); however, knowledge of map lore
alone won't help one seek out those [marks](/wiki/Markings "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Lore](/wiki/Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
