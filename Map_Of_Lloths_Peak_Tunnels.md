Comments
--------

Despite being a [scroll-type item](/wiki/Category%3AScrolls "wikilink"), the
scroll does not contain a spell. It is essentially used as a
[trash](/wiki/Category%3ATrash "wikilink") item.  
This map is obtained by giving the [Shiny Brass
Bauble](/wiki/Shiny_Brass_Bauble "wikilink") to the [Mother
Harpy](/wiki/Mother_Harpy "wikilink").  
This map is a list of directions to reach various mobs in the area.
However, the starting point of the directions does not appear to be
given. From the [Mother Harpy](/wiki/Mother_Harpy "wikilink"), the starting
point is e, d, s, d, w.  
Each symbol can be interpreted thusly:  
|^ - north  
|. - south  
|&gt; - east  
|&lt; - west  
/\\ - up  
\\/ - down  
The directions lead to the [Drider Queen](/wiki/Drider_Queen "wikilink")
(Maker/M'karr), [Sarphyre's
Court](/wiki/Category%3ASarphyre's_Court "wikilink") (Sarphyre/Srr'fier),
[Drider Witch](/wiki/Drider_Witch "wikilink") (Nyad Farel), respectively.  
Note that the directions to the [Drider Witch](/wiki/Drider_Witch "wikilink")
has an error in it. The down should be an up.
