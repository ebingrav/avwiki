## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") is used in [Mt.
Durr](:Category:_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Flask of Marlots Ol' Special
Quest](Flask_Of_Marlots_Ol'_Special_Quest "wikilink") and its [Set of
Three Loaded Dice Quest](Set_Of_Three_Loaded_Dice_Quest "wikilink"); it
[unlocks](Unlock "wikilink") the curtain between Marlot's and Out back
at Marlot's.
