Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is used in [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Flask of Marlots Ol' Special
Quest](/wiki/Flask_Of_Marlots_Ol'_Special_Quest "wikilink") and its [Set of
Three Loaded Dice Quest](/wiki/Set_Of_Three_Loaded_Dice_Quest "wikilink"); it
[unlocks](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") the curtain between Marlot's and Out back
at Marlot's.
