*Diana is a beautiful, powerfully built woman. She wears platinum armor
for three reasons. The first is to show she is strong enough to
withstand the [weight](Object_Weight "wikilink"). The second is to show
her rank of office. The third is that platinum is the hardest metal she
can find. She is willing to defend [Ofcol](:Category:_Ofcol "wikilink")
to her death.*

**Keywords:** Marshall, Diana.  
**[Level](Level "wikilink"):** 33.  
**[Alignment](Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [protection
evil](Protection_Evil "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
