*Diana is a beautiful, powerfully built woman. She wears platinum armor
for three reasons. The first is to show she is strong enough to
withstand the [weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"). The second is to show
her rank of office. The third is that platinum is the hardest metal she
can find. She is willing to defend [Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink")
to her death.*

**Keywords:** Marshall, Diana.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 33.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [protection
evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
