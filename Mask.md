*By subtly altering one's psionic traces, one can become essentially
invisible to the untrained eye. Due to the nature of this
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink"), it is impossible to mask
another's life signs.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'mask'.

[Psionicists](:Category:_Psionicists "wikilink") may find this
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink") rather useful at
[lowmort](:Category:_Lowmort "wikilink")
[tier](:Category:_Tiers "wikilink") but usually [cast](Cast "wikilink")
[Invis](Invis "wikilink") instead of it at
[hero](:Category:_Hero "wikilink") [tier](:Category:_Tiers "wikilink").
It is superfluous for [characters](:Category:_Characters "wikilink")
affected by [Invis](Invis "wikilink").
