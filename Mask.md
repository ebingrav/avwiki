*By subtly altering one's psionic traces, one can become essentially
invisible to the untrained eye. Due to the nature of this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), it is impossible to mask
another's life signs.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'mask'.

[Psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") may find this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") rather useful at
[lowmort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink") but usually [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
[Invis](/wiki/Invis "wikilink") instead of it at
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[tier](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiers "wikilink"). It is superfluous for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") affected by
[Invis](/wiki/Invis "wikilink").
