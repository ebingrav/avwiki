*This [spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink") improves the [to-hit
roll](Hit_Roll "wikilink") and [saving throw versus
spell](Saving_Throw "wikilink") of all those in the
[room](:Category:_Rooms "wikilink"). The amount of improvement increases
with the [caster](Cast "wikilink")'s [level](Level "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Bless](Bless "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'mass bless'.

This [spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink") is practically never used.
