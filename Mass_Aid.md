*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") costs 100
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink"), but cures all in the room of
[blindness](/wiki/Cure_Blindness "wikilink"), [poison](/wiki/Cure_Poison "wikilink")
and most importantly, [PLAGUE](/wiki/Cure_Disease "wikilink")!!!*

Prerequisite(s): [Cure Disease](/wiki/Cure_Disease "wikilink"), [Cure
Poison](/wiki/Cure_Poison "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'mass aid'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is useful on occasion,
especially when trying to cure multiple
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of
[plague](/wiki/Plague "wikilink").
