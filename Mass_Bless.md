*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") improves the [to-hit
roll](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink") and [saving throw versus
spell](/wiki/Saving_Throw "wikilink") of all those in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). The amount of improvement
increases with the [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")'s
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Bless](/wiki/Bless "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'mass bless'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is practically never used.
