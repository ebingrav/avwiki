*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is equivalent to
[divinity](/wiki/Divinity "wikilink") but upon everyone within the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Divinity](/wiki/Divinity "wikilink"), [Mass
Heal](/wiki/Mass_Heal "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'mass divinity'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used.
[Brandishes](/wiki/Category%3A_Brandishes "wikilink") are normally used rather
than [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") to heal mutiple
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") simultaneously.
