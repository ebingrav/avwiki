*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") [casts](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
[heal](/wiki/Heal_(spell) "wikilink") on everyone in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") as well as
[refresh](/wiki/Refresh "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Heal](/wiki/Heal_(spell) "wikilink"), [Mass Cure
Critical](/wiki/Mass_Cure_Critical "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'mass heal'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used.
[Brandishes](/wiki/Category%3A_Brandishes "wikilink") are normally used rather
than [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") to heal mutiple
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") simultaneously.
