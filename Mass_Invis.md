This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") renders all
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") in the caster's group,
including the caster, [invisible](/wiki/Invis_Flag "wikilink").''

Prerequisite(s): [Invis](/wiki/Invis "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'mass invis'.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used.
See [Invis Flag](/wiki/Invis_Flag "wikilink") for more information about
[invisibility](/wiki/Invis_Flag "wikilink").
