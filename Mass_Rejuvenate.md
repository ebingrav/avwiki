[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").*

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'mass rejuvenate'.

Prerequisite(s): [Mass Invigorate](/wiki/Mass_Invigorate "wikilink"),
[Rejuvenate](/wiki/Rejuvenate "wikilink").
