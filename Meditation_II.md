*This allows for additional regeneration of
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") while
[sleeping](/wiki/Sleep_(command) "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Meditation](/wiki/Meditation "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), like its
predecessor [Meditation](/wiki/Meditation "wikilink"), is very valuable to
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of
[spellcasting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") like
[psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink"),
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink"), and
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink"), as it greatly reduces time
needed for them to fully regenerate their
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink").
