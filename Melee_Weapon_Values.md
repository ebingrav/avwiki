[Melee-weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have five
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **Sharpness:** This value determines how many times this [melee
    weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink") has been
    [sharpened](/wiki/Sharpen "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **Damage, Minimum:** This value determines this [melee
    weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink")'s minimum possible
    damage to those it hits.

<!-- -->

-   **Damage, Maximum:** This value determines this [melee
    weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink")'s maximum possible
    damage to those it hits.

<!-- -->

-   **General Type:** This value determines which general type this
    [melee weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink") is, which may
    be [chain](/wiki/Category%3A_Chain_Weapons "wikilink"),
    [concealable](/wiki/Category%3A_Concealable_Weapons "wikilink"),
    [double](/wiki/Category%3A_Double_Weapons "wikilink"),
    [ethereal](/wiki/Category%3A_Ethereal_Weapons "wikilink"),
    [hafted](/wiki/Category%3A_Hafted_Weapons "wikilink"), [standard
    one-handed](/wiki/Category%3A_Standard_One-Handed_Weapons "wikilink"),
    [standard
    one-or-two-handed](/wiki/Category%3A_Standard_One-Or-Two-Handed_Weapons "wikilink"),
    or [standard
    two-handed](/wiki/Category%3A_Standard_Two-Handed_Weapons "wikilink").
    Follow these links for more information about each general type.

<!-- -->

-   **Damage Type:** This value determines which type of damage this
    [melee weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink") does, which may
    be [biting](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Bite "wikilink"),
    [blasting](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Blast "wikilink"),
    [chopping](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Chop "wikilink"),
    [clawing](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Claw "wikilink"),
    [crushing](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Crush "wikilink"),
    [hitting](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Hit "wikilink"),
    [piercing](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Pierce "wikilink"),
    [pounding](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Pound "wikilink"),
    [puncturing](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Puncture "wikilink"),
    [slashing](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Slash "wikilink"),
    [slicing](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Slice "wikilink"),
    [smacking](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Smack "wikilink"),
    [stabbing](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Stab "wikilink"), or
    [whipping](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Whip "wikilink"). Follow
    these links for more information about each damage type.

See also [Melee Weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").
