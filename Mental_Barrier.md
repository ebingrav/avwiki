*This is a protective discipline which relies on psionics. MENTAL
BARRIER creates a mobile
[telekinetic](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") wall
between the caster and his foes. As this is a
[telekinetic](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
effect, it can be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on anybody.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'mental barrier'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in
[Fortitudes](/wiki/Fortitudes "wikilink").
