Prerequisites: [Tutor
Other](http://avatar.melanarchy.info/index.php?title=Tutor_Other)  
Syntax: None \[Automatic Skill\]

Once you've learned this teaching skill, you will be able to teach
skills and spells like a guildmaster up to a certain level. Like a
guildmaster, you only know what your guild offers and the more teaching
skills you know, the higher the level of skills and spells you'll be
able to teach.
