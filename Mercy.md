*Mercy is one of those 'after-the-fact'
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") -- it's only used when it's
already too late for someone. This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") takes you to the newest corpse of
the [player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). It has the regular
constraints which you cannot bypass like a
[cursed](/wiki/Cursed_Rooms "wikilink"), [private](Private_Rooms "wikilink")
or [solitary](/wiki/Solitary_Rooms "wikilink")
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). Once there, you can
[summon](/wiki/Summon "wikilink") / [gurney](Gurney "wikilink") / whatever the
poor victim to their equipment.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'mercy'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is quite useful, especially
for swiftly returning to one's own corpse after
[dying](/wiki/Death "wikilink").
