Comments
--------

Millament is invisible to heroes but does not aggie. This mob is a
necromancer and will raise (non-player) corpses, so unless you want to
fight [Tryystania The DracoLich](/wiki/Tryystania_The_DracoLich "wikilink")'s
corpse, autosac will be helpful.

The room just above Millament is uncursed.
