*A mental master need not rely on mere eyeballs to view the world! With
this [spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink") in place, the
[Psion](:Category:_Psionicists.md "wikilink") becomes immune to
[blindness](Blindness.md "wikilink"), and can even see normally while
[sleeping](Sleep_(command).md "wikilink")! The downside is that
[mana](Mana_Points.md "wikilink") will regenerate slightly slower. While
affected, a [psion](:Category:_Psionicists.md "wikilink") can use their
mental powers to [scan](Scan.md "wikilink") into adjacent
[rooms](:Category:_Rooms.md "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Danger Sense](Danger_Sense.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast.md "wikilink") 'minds eye'.
