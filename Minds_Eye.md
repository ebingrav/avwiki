*A mental master need not rely on mere eyeballs to view the world! With
this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") in place, the
[Psion](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") becomes immune to
[blindness](/wiki/Blindness "wikilink"), and can even see normally while
[sleeping](/wiki/Sleep_(command) "wikilink")! The downside is that
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") will regenerate slightly slower. While
affected, a [psion](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") can use their
mental powers to [scan](/wiki/Scan "wikilink") into adjacent
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Danger Sense](/wiki/Danger_Sense "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'minds eye'.
