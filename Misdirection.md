''Misdirection does the same thing as [slink](/wiki/Slink "wikilink"), but
works on multiple opponents and has a slight chance of causing pursuers
to completely lose track of their targets altogether. You must be
actively sneaking to use this [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"),
and it will work on foes who are using the
[Alertness](/wiki/Alertness "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink").

Prerequisite(s): [Rogue Lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
