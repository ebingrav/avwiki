[Missile-weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have four
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **General Type:** This value determines which general type this
    [missile weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink") is, which
    may be [compound bow](/wiki/Category%3A_Compound_Bows "wikilink"),
    [dartgun](/wiki/Category%3A_Dartguns "wikilink"),
    [gun](/wiki/Category%3A_Guns "wikilink"), [heavy
    crossbow](/wiki/Category%3A_Heavy_Crossbows "wikilink"), [light
    crossbow](/wiki/Category%3A_Light_Crossbows "wikilink"), [long
    bow](/wiki/Category%3A_Long_Bows "wikilink"), [short
    bow](/wiki/Category%3A_Short_Bows "wikilink"), or
    [sling](/wiki/Category%3A_Slings "wikilink"). Follow these links for more
    information about each [general
    type](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **Damage, Minimum:** This value determines this [missile
    weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink")'s minimum possible
    damage to those it hits.

<!-- -->

-   **Damage, Maximum:** This value determines this [missile
    weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink")'s maximum possible
    damage to those it hits.

<!-- -->

-   **Draw Strength:** This value determines what
    [strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") one must have in order to
    [wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") this [missile
    weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink").

See also [Missile Weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Missile_Weapons "wikilink").
