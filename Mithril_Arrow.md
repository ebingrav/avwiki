This skill allows you to create mithril arrows, bolts and stones.

Prerequisite(s): [Fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink"), [Piercing
Arrow](/wiki/Piercing_Arrow "wikilink")

Syntax: [fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink")
&lt;[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink")/[bolts](Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink")/[stones](Category%3A_Stones "wikilink")&gt;
mithril.

Does less damage than [Ice Arrow](/wiki/Ice_Arrow "wikilink") and [Lightning
Arrow](/wiki/Lightning_Arrow "wikilink").

This ammunition does survive being used and maybe looted after killing
the mob.
