[Money](/wiki/Category%3A_Money "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have one
[value](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **Gold Value:** This value determines how much [Gold in
    Hand](/wiki/Gold_In_Hand "wikilink") this
    [money](/wiki/Category%3A_Money "wikilink") will yield to
    [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who pick it up,
    assuming that they can [carry](/wiki/Carrying_Capacity "wikilink") its
    entire weight; whatever potion they can't
    [carry](/wiki/Carrying_Capacity "wikilink") will automatically drop on
    the ground.

See also [Money](/wiki/Category%3A_Money "wikilink").
