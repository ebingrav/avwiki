[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will attack any players that
they see who are not fellow [monks](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink").

[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") may be of
[monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink")
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") without being "monk mobs."
