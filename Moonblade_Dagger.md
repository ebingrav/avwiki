` Modifies hit roll by 3 continuous.`  
` Modifies damage roll by 3 continuous.`

Slot: Weapon  
Level: 15-17  
Weight: 3  
Damage Range: 8/9 - 20/21  
Type: One-handed Piercing  
Mob: None. (in a chest)  
Area: [Deephome](/wiki/Category%3A_Deephome "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Deephome_Map "wikilink"))  
Find [Vernon](/wiki/Vernon "wikilink"), the mayor of
[Deephome](/wiki/Category%3ADeephome "wikilink"). He's portable, and Deephome
isn't that long of a walk from other lowlevel areas. Kill him, get his
key. go n u \[e\] and you'll find a chest. Inside it is the dagger.
