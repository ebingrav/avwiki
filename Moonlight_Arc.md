` Modifies hit roll by 21 continuous.`

Level: 51?  
Max: 69-77?  
Flags: anti-good  
Mob: [Priestess Queen](/wiki/Priestess_Queen "wikilink")  
Area: [Idolatry Of Lloth](/wiki/Category%3AIdolatry_Of_Lloth "wikilink")  

Before you try to get this piece of gear, you would be wise to study the
map of [The Idolatry Of Lloth!](/wiki/Category%3AIdolatry_Of_Lloth "wikilink")

PP "drid hunt". This will take you to the portion of the area labelled
"In the web" on the map.

From here head to the northwest corner, where you will find a hole
leading down. Go all the way down until you're on the base floor of the
area. Now venture into the dangerous Golden Web, which is in the
southeast corner of the floor.

You need to let yourself get pummeled by [Lloth-ra
Knights](/wiki/Lloth-ra_Knight "wikilink"). Instead of killing you, they will
get you to 5hp and capture you, dragging you to the [Priestess
Queen](/wiki/Priestess_Queen "wikilink"). Note that these knights won't drag
lords, so don't bother trying to get this item with your lord alt. Also
note that some mobs in the Golden Web won't drag you, but will kill you
outright instead. If this happens to you, please list which mobs do and
do not drag here.

Here are some rumors about the [Priestess
Queen](/wiki/Priestess_Queen "wikilink"). Remove the question marks, or edit
the list, as these rumors are proved or disproved.

-   Her hitpoints don't regen after she's been injured?
-   She doesn't wield her bow
-   She's aggie
-   She can be blackjacked
-   If she kills you, she's nice enough to drop your corpse off in the
    room labelled "Arachling nest" on the map

The [Priestess Queen](/wiki/Priestess_Queen "wikilink") herself isn't too
tough, and if it's true that she doesn't wield her bow, she doesn't hit
very hard either. Maybe a surged disintegrate will kill her in one shot.
Maybe you'll be able to survive by quaffing in full ac until you're
healthy. Maybe she's even blackjackable. Edit this page with strategies
that work for you.

Once you kill her, you might as well camp her room for a pop or two.
Note that the room is noquit, so you can't be -too- abusive in your
camping ;)
