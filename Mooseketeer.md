*This smallish moose man has been giving two sets of simple orders. The
Chief has ordered him to make sure the Chieftess doesn't sneak away. The
Chieftess has ordered him to make sure the Chief didn't sneak away.*

*He's afraid he's failed at both, and now just wants a drink before the
world ends.*
