## Comments

This piece of gear is nicely enchantable (no longer true), if you do it
right you can get b12-6 or better
[lemans](LeMans_Family_Seal.md "wikilink") equivalents... At around
level 35-40 or so.  
The same goes for [Tattered Kite
Shield](Tattered_Kite_Shield "wikilink"), but those aren't as easy to
obtain in large quantities.

Whenever Cyrtpea repops, her merchandise will be of a different level. A
higher level pearl will have a higher base ac. So if you're aiming for
lots of enchants for a hero level piece, kill her until she sells pearls
at level 15, the only level at which the pearls (occasionally) pop with
base 5.
