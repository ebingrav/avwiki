*Move hidden "[hides](/wiki/Hide_Flag "wikilink")" the player, much like the
[invis](/wiki/Invis "wikilink") [spell](Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").
[Players](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") and
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") must have [detect
hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink") to see the character.
[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") may also be affected by move
hidden.*

Prerequisite(s): [Hide](/wiki/Hide "wikilink"), [Sneak](Sneak "wikilink").

Syntax: move hidden.

See also [Racial Movehidden](/wiki/Racial_Movehidden "wikilink") and [Gear
That Applies 'Move
Hidden'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Move_Hidden' "wikilink").
