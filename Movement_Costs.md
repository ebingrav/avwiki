[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must spend a certain
amount of [move points](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink") to move (non-magically)
from one [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") to another. This amount
spent depends upon the [terrain type](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink")
of the [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") that they enter.
