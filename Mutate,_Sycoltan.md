*At long last, the [drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink")
[sorcerer](/wiki/Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink") himself... though you
wouldn't be able to tell under normal circumstances. It seems the
sorcerer has been attempting to empower himself using [the
dragon](/wiki/Forgotten_Dragon,_Zikthro "wikilink")'s soul and energy. It has
fully mutated his body into something not even closely resembling a
[drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink"). It looks as if it is a
[gargoyle](/wiki/Gargoyles "wikilink"), chimera, [dragon](Dragons "wikilink"),
and xorn spawning of evil. Grotesque, indeed. He doesn't look too happy
at your intrusion, either.*

**Keywords:** drow, sorcerer, mutate.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 50.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[multi-breather](/wiki/Breathing_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"), [very
aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [fly](Fly "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").

Comments
--------

The mutate, Sycoltan is involved in [The Forgotten
One](/wiki/Category%3A_Forgotten_One "wikilink")'s [Sceptre of Black Light
Quest](/wiki/Sceptre_Of_Black_Light_Quest "wikilink").

His clothing seems to have [inventory flags](/wiki/Inventory_Flag "wikilink")
and thus cannot be obtained.
