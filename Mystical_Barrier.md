*Once affected with a mystical barrier, damage upon you from
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is reduced slightly. It was
considered a good idea by several [magi](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink")
in the southern regions of Yult'Nea where the mosquitoes were casting
tiny fireballs to help cook their meal before feasting on
[magi](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") flesh. A spell repellent was
quickly produced. The causes of spell innovation are always
interesting.*

Prerequisite(s): [Shield](/wiki/Shield "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'mystical barrier'.

[Hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") may find it worthwhile to include
this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") when
[buffing](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")
themselves fully, especially if they are expecting to fight
[spellcasting mobs](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink"). This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")'s duration is relatively low,
just like that of [Sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink") and
[Frenzy](/wiki/Frenzy "wikilink").
