In any case, lucky adventurers will end up with [Namure's Leather
Collar](/wiki/Namure's_Leather_Collar "wikilink"). Happy questing!

**Hints:**  
A large or properly prepared hero obtain the required ticket by
themselves.  
Characters in the upper 40s are recommended to employ a well-rounded
group, especially one with a knack for [invisibility](/wiki/Invis "wikilink").
Be sure to avoid any [flying](/wiki/Fly "wikilink") enemies, as they will
especially cause trouble for smaller fry. Haha. Another fisherman joke.
