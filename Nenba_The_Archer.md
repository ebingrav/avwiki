*Nenba looks up and says, "I came to [this
island](/wiki/Category%3A_Bloodsong_Island "wikilink") because I'd worked up a
great new plan for [some armor for
archery](/wiki/Leather_Of_Crecy "wikilink"), and it had to be made out of
especially rugged leather. But my [invisibility](/wiki/Invis "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") wore off and they caught me
hunting them, so they smashed up all my equipment and stole my design
plans. As a final indignity, they tossed me out here." "If only someone
could give me back my designs, and bring enough good leather to make
[some armor](/wiki/Leather_Of_Crecy "wikilink"), I'd show those bellowing
brutes a thing or two!" She shakes her little fist at the island in
annoyance.*

**Keywords:** Nenba, archer, half-elf.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 60.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
