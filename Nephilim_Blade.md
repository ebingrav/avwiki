Directions
----------

To get it, the [Ethereal Symbol](/wiki/Ethereal_Symbol "wikilink") of [Saint
Manuel](/wiki/Saint_Manuel "wikilink") must be obtained from his corpse. He
resides at the heart of the glacier in the [Frozen
Wastes](/wiki/Category%3AFrozen_Wastes "wikilink"), adjacent to the [Frozen
Demon Tourach](/wiki/Frozen_Demon_Tourach "wikilink"). Take the
[symbol](/wiki/Ethereal_Symbol "wikilink") to the bottom of the [Downward
Spiral](/wiki/Category%3ADownward_Spiral "wikilink") (pp
[Mudman](/wiki/Mudman "wikilink")), and trade the
[symbol](/wiki/Ethereal_Symbol "wikilink") for the blade.
