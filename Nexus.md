''Nexus opens a gate in the fabric of reality, to join two distant
points in space. NEXUS is two-way and requires a destination (or
"target"). This can be either a
[player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or a
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") (not an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") or
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink")). A [nexus](/wiki/Nexuses "wikilink")
will stay open longer for higher-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink")
[players](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"), but will eventually
collapse. After the [nexus](/wiki/Nexuses "wikilink") is open, step in by
typing "[enter](Enter "wikilink") [nexus](/wiki/Nexuses "wikilink")". Since it
is two-way, the [nexus](/wiki/Nexuses "wikilink") will also be in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") you have traveled into.'

Prerequisite(s): [Portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'nexus'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")/[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is not nearly as valuable as
[Portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") but there are still occasions when having a
"two-way portal" may be convenient. See [Nexuses](/wiki/Nexuses "wikilink")
for more information about these
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") created by this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").
