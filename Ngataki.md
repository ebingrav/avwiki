*The [Tuataur](/wiki/Tuataurs "wikilink") hovers a few inches above the water
in a meditative position. His third eye is the only one open, and it
glares at you for intruding upon his solitude.*

**Keywords:** Ngataki, tuataur.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[trainer](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
