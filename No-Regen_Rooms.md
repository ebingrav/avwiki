[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") prevent players within them
from regenerating [hit points](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink"), [mana
points](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink"), and [move
points](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink").
