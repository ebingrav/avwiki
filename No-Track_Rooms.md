[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") prevent players from
[tracking](/wiki/Track "wikilink") any [mobs](Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")
within them.
