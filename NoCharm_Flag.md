An [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") with this
[flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") makes its
[wearer](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") immune to both [Charm
Person](/wiki/Charm_Person "wikilink") and [Invis](Invis "wikilink")
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). Certain
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") [wear](/wiki/Wear "wikilink")
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") with this
[flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") but
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may not normally obtain
it from them. [Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") [Invis](Invis "wikilink") on
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") wearing such
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") will immediately be both
[outlawed](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink") and [jailed](Jail_Rooms "wikilink").
