This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") prevents
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") from
[dropping](/wiki/Drop "wikilink") [objects](Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
that have it. It can be removed via the [Remove
Curse](/wiki/Remove_Curse "wikilink") [spell](Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").

See also [Remove Curse](/wiki/Remove_Curse "wikilink").
