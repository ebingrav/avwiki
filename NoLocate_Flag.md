This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") prevents
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that have it from being listed
by the [Locate Object](/wiki/Locate_Object "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").

See also [Locate Object](/wiki/Locate_Object "wikilink").
