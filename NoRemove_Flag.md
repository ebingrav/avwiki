This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") prevents
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") from
[removing](/wiki/Remove "wikilink") [objects](Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
that they are [wearing](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") that have it. It can be removed
via the [Remove Curse](/wiki/Remove_Curse "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").

See also [Remove Curse](/wiki/Remove_Curse "wikilink").
