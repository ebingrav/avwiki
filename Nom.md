*This is Nom. He's the eternal keeper of the Tree of Knowledge.
Throughout time he's been here and could teach you a thing or two. He
can HEAL you. If you type '[heal](/wiki/Heal_(command) "wikilink")', you can
peruse his pricing for this service. He also sells
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") of vital importance to those who
are in need. To see those, type '[list](/wiki/List "wikilink")' and perhaps
[haggle](/wiki/Haggle "wikilink") with him. Feel welcome to stay awhile.*

**Keywords:** Nom, shaman.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[healer](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").

Comments
--------

Located at the very center of [The
Meadow](/wiki/Category%3A_Meadow "wikilink"), Nom is the eternal keeper of the
Tree of Knowledge. He is the default [recall](/wiki/Recall "wikilink") point
for [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
[levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 1-4. Also, he sells
[bandages](/wiki/Some_Bandages "wikilink"), [wax
candles](/wiki/Wax_Candle "wikilink"), and overpriced [identify
scrolls](/wiki/Identify_Scroll "wikilink"). If you type
'[heal](/wiki/Heal_(command) "wikilink")' in his presence you can get a list
of [healing
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Heal_Characters "wikilink")
that he offers, for a price.
