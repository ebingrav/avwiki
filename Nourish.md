*Nourish will feed and slake the thirst of all in the
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). As a highly advanced form of
healing magic, it can only be [learned](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") by a select
few.*

Prerequisite(s): [Create Food](/wiki/Create_Food "wikilink"), [Create
Water](/wiki/Create_Water "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'nourish'.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") has no practical
value at present, as [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") no
longer suffer from hunger and thirst.
