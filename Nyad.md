*Cousin to the dryad, the aquatic Nyad takes the form of a stunning
[elven](/wiki/Elves "wikilink") woman with long green hair. She notices you
watching her and says, "In the name of the [High
Sylph](/wiki/High_Sylph "wikilink") I require your assistance. If you would
prove yourself trustworthy, return to me my wreath of water lilies. It
was stolen by one of the [elves](/wiki/Elves "wikilink") in these foothills. I
worry that it may fall into the claws of the driders within the Volcano,
who could use it as a magical focus to destroy me."*

**Keywords:** nyad, female, form.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [protection evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
