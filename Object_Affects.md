Each [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") has a set of affects
(usually none but sometimes one and, rarely, a few) that it applies to
any [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") who [wear](Wear "wikilink") it until
it is [removed](/wiki/Remove "wikilink"). These affects may include:

-   Altering one or more of their
    [statistics](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistics "wikilink"), namely
    [con](/wiki/Constitution "wikilink"), [dex](Dexterity "wikilink"),
    [int](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink"), [str](Strength "wikilink"), and
    [wis](/wiki/Wisdom "wikilink"). [Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") that
    improves [character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
    [stats](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistics "wikilink") may be found under
    [Statistic Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistic_Gear "wikilink") (which
    includes [Level Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Level_Gear "wikilink")).

<!-- -->

-   Altering their [armor class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"), [saving
    throw](/wiki/Saving_Throw "wikilink"), [hit roll](Hit_Roll "wikilink"),
    [damage roll](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink"), [maximum hit
    points](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink"), [maximum mana
    points](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink"), and/or [maximum move
    points](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink"). [Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink")
    that improves such qualities may be found under [Tank
    Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Tank_Gear "wikilink"), [Saving-Throw
    Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Saving-Throw_Gear "wikilink"), [Archer
    Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Archer_Gear "wikilink"), [Hit
    Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Hit_Gear "wikilink"), [Hit-Point
    Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Hit-Point_Gear "wikilink"), [Mana
    Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Mana_Gear "wikilink"), and [Move-Point
    Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Move-Point_Gear "wikilink"), respectively (more
    or less).

<!-- -->

-   [Affecting](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") them with one or more of a limited
    set of [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") and
    [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").
    [Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") that applies such
    [skill/spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
    [affects](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") may be found under [Gear That Applies
    Skills &
    Spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink").

Note that some [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") supposedly affects
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), [level](Level "wikilink"),
[sex](/wiki/Sex "wikilink"), [age](Age "wikilink"), height, weight,
[gold](/wiki/Gold_In_Hand "wikilink"), and/or
[experience](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink"); however, such
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") will *not* actually affect these
qualities.
