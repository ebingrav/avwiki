Each [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") pops with a certain level
or, most often, within a certain range of levels. When a
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") respawns with
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") on it, their levels will each
be between one less and one more than its [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink");
[ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink") is a notable exception
to this rule, as it will always be of one specific level determined by
its [warhead type](/wiki/Category%3A_Warhead_Types "wikilink").

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may
[save](Save "wikilink") with [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
up to 7 levels above their [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink").

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may
[wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") [weapons](Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") up
to 5 levels above their [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink").

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may
[wear](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") other [gear](Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") up to
3 levels above their [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink").

A piece of [armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink")'s level determines its
[base armor class](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink").
