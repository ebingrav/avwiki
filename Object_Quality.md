Each [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") has a specific "quality"
analagous to a [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")' [hit
points](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink"), including both current and maximum
amounts that are initially equal.
[Objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") may lose their current hit
points by various means, most often by being damaged by certain
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") during combat, but, unless they
are destroyed, they may be either [smithed](/wiki/Smith "wikilink") or
[repaired](/wiki/Repair "wikilink"). Also, some
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") give
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
[flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") that protect them from
certain kinds of [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") damage.
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may see an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")'s quality by
[identifying](/wiki/Identify "wikilink") it; also, all those who
[practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") both [armor lore](Armor_Lore "wikilink")
and [weapon lore](/wiki/Weapon_Lore "wikilink") may see roughly how damaged
most [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") are by toggling
[condition](/wiki/Condition "wikilink") on and then looking at them.
