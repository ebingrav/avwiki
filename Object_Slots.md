Each [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") has a defined set of
"slots" in which it may be [worn](/wiki/Wear "wikilink").
[Objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that are either
[lights](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink"),
[armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink"),
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink"),
[ammunition](/wiki/Category%3A_Ammunition "wikilink"),
[staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink"), or
[wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink") normally may be
[worn](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") while [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
of other [types](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink") normally may not,
although there are various exceptions. Usually,
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") may be [worn](/wiki/Wear "wikilink")
in only one slot, if any, although, once again, there are various
exceptions. Slots in which [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") may (or
may not) be worn are as follows:
