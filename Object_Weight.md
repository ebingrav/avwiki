Each [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") has a specific weight,
which is significant because each
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") has a certain [carrying
capacity](/wiki/Carrying_Capacity "wikilink") that limits how much total
weight he/she/it may carry at one time. An
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")'s weight may also affect its
capacity to be used as a [telekinetic
weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), as well
as how much damage it will do if and when it is used as such.
