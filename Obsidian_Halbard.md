*This halbard is crafted from a huge piece of obsidian. Honed to a fine
edge and strengthed by magic, this polearm bears the name of "Skull
Splitter."*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 18-20 to 51-52 (average 34-36).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 11 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 13 continuous.`
