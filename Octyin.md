*Dressed in the finest clothes his stealing minion's hands could bring
him, Octyin folds his hands in his lap and gazes at you, pondering why
you are here. On his head is [a dazzling
crown](/wiki/Encrusted_Jewel_Crown "wikilink"), complete with rare, precious
gems stolen from [the drows](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") and other unfortunate
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") alike.*

**Keywords:** Octyin, ogre.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 41.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [protection evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
