*This nameless guard has to be stoic to perform his duties. He has sworn
to guard [Octyin](/wiki/Octyin "wikilink") to the death. In his left hand he
grips [a bronze spear](/wiki/Bronze_Spear "wikilink") tightly, body rigid and
set for battle.*

**Keywords:** ogre, guard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 39.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
