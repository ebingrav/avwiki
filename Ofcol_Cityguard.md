*This guard is surely a trained and skilled fighter. He is fully armed
and armored, and he uses his equipment with a practiced precision.
Luckily for you, this guard will only use his deadly skills for the good
of the citizens of Ofcol.*

**Keywords:** cityguard, guard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 18.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink") (some),
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Category%3A_Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink") (some).  
**Affects:** [protection evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink").
