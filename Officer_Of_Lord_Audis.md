### Comments

An Officer of Lord Audis, [a Hired Soldier](/wiki/Hired_Solider "wikilink"),
[a Professional Warrior](/wiki/Professional_Warrior "wikilink"), [a Paid
Mercenary](/wiki/Paid_Mercenary "wikilink"), and [a Seasoned

mobs in Gorn's Anchor. Two of each (except for sailors, of which there
are four) patrol the dock.
