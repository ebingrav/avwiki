*This guard's duty is clear: kill anyone that escapes the other guard's
wrath. He conceals himself until it is too late for the unlucky victim.
Scanning the stairs for moving shadows and listening for footsteps, he
waits to kill once more.*

**Keywords:** ogre, guard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 37.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [hidden](Hide "wikilink").

Comments
--------

There are two different guards in the room, each with a different
weapon. The one with the dagger is
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"), the other is not. However, the
other may assist, depending on the player's level.
