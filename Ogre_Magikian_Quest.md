The start of this quest can be found by reading the [Ogre
Magikian](/wiki/Ogre_Magikian "wikilink")'s description.

-   Kill the [Archaeologist](/wiki/Archaeologist "wikilink") in [Kingdom Of
    The Willows](/wiki/Category%3AKingdom_Of_The_Willows "wikilink") and
    obtain the [Unusual Stone](/wiki/Unusual_Stone "wikilink").
-   Give the [Unusual Stone](/wiki/Unusual_Stone "wikilink") to the [Ogre
    Magikian](/wiki/Ogre_Magikian "wikilink").

The prize is a [Small Flesh-Bound
Book](/wiki/Small_Flesh-Bound_Book "wikilink").
