Ogres are huge humanoids, with ugly tempers and voracious appetites.
They are fond of treasure, and are often employed as mercenaries by evil
races in particular. They tend to have yellowish hides, are covered with
wart-like bumps, have black teeth and talons, and purplish eyes with
white pupils. Ogres are exceptionally strong and hardy, but are known to
be extremely dim-witted and lacking in wisdom. They are excellent in
melee combat, but are extremely limited when they attempt to utilize
magic.

They also have a natural ability to cut fine filets out of corpses.
