A dwarven mage is rare enough, but a beardless dwarf? Unheard of. As the
mystic stirs a kettle of a sweet smelling dark tea you notice his hands
are gloved in some kind of silk which shimmers as he stirs the tea.
Something about the gleam in the eye of the mystic cries out in warning
to you. This is a being of power.

The old mystic is using: <worn on hands> a pair of unblemished silken
gloves

Perfect match for a lv26 mage at 199hp
