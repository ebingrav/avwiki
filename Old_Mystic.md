*A [dwarven](Dwarves "wikilink") [mage](:Category:_Mages "wikilink") is
rare enough, but a beardless [dwarf](Dwarves "wikilink")? Unheard of. As
the mystic stirs a kettle of a sweet smelling dark tea you notice his
hands are gloved in some kind of silk which shimmers as he stirs the
tea. Something about the gleam in the eye of the mystic cries out in
warning to you. This is a being of power.*

**Keywords:** dwarf, mystic, old, wrinkled, beardless.  
**[Level](Level "wikilink"):** 25.  
**[Alignment](Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[mage](Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").
