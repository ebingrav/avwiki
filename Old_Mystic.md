*A [dwarven](Dwarves.md "wikilink")
[mage](:Category:_Mages.md "wikilink") is rare enough, but a beardless
[dwarf](Dwarves.md "wikilink")? Unheard of. As the mystic stirs a kettle
of a sweet smelling dark tea you notice his hands are gloved in some
kind of silk which shimmers as he stirs the tea. Something about the
gleam in the eye of the mystic cries out in warning to you. This is a
being of power.*

**Keywords:** dwarf, mystic, old, wrinkled, beardless.  
**[Level](Level.md "wikilink"):** 25.  
**[Alignment](Alignment.md "wikilink"):** apathetic.

[mage](Spellcasting_Mobs.md "wikilink"),
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs.md "wikilink").
