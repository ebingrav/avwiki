*Intertwining bands of diamond and obsidian form a ring.*  
Modifies armor class by -40 continuous.

`Modifies damage roll by 6 continuous.`  
`Modifies hit roll by 6 continuous.`  
`Modifies hp by 200 continuous.`  
`Modifies mana by 200 continuous.`

Keywords: bands diamond obsidian once-sundered ring  
Flags: glow magic bless  
Weight: 1  
Plane: [Thorngate](/wiki/Category%3AThorngate "wikilink")  
Area: [Rietta's Wonders](/wiki/Category%3ARietta's_Wonders "wikilink")  
Mob: [Lost Spirit Of Pascilon](/wiki/Lost_Spirit_Of_Pascilon "wikilink")
(ticket quest)  
To get this item you need to collect two pieces of gear from
[Noctopia](/wiki/Category%3ANoctopia "wikilink"). Both are called [Half Of The
Sundered Ring](/wiki/Half_Of_The_Sundered_Ring "wikilink").

-   Get [one half](/wiki/Half_Of_The_Sundered_Ring_(Duke) "wikilink") from
    [Duke Malafont](/wiki/Duke_Malafont "wikilink"). See [Gear In Obsidian
    Arena; Floor](/wiki/Category%3AGear_In_Obsidian_Arena;_Floor "wikilink")
    for more information on how that's done.
-   Get [the other half](/wiki/Half_Of_The_Sundered_Ring_(Queen) "wikilink")
    from [Dark Queen
    Zydarielle](/wiki/Dark_Queen_Zydarielle_(Noctopia) "wikilink"). See [Gear
    In Obsidian Arena;
    Stands](/wiki/Category%3AGear_In_Obsidian_Arena;_Stands "wikilink").
-   Give both pieces to [Garrow](/wiki/Garrow "wikilink"):

`You give half of the Sundered Ring to Garrow.`  
`Garrow takes your half of the Sundered Ring.`  
`Garrow nods appreciatively, "This is some special stuff right here.`  
`I can fix it, but, if this is what I think it is, you're gonna need`  
`something extra. Some way to infuse it with holy power like it`  
`used to have, maybe sacrifice some similarly divine artifact."`  
  
`"Or I could be wrong and it could just be another ring. If so...`  
`well, I know a fence, maybe..." `  
[`Garrow`](/wiki/Garrow "wikilink")` gives you the `[`Once-Sundered`
`Ring`](/wiki/Once-Sundered_Ring_(ticket) "wikilink")`!`

-   Give [Once-Sundered
    Ring (ticket)](/wiki/Once-Sundered_Ring_(ticket) "wikilink") and [Girth Of
    The Most
    Holy](http://avatar.melanarchy.info/index.php/Girth_Of_The_Most_Holy)
    to [Lost Spirit Of Pascilon](/wiki/Lost_Spirit_Of_Pascilon "wikilink"):

`You give the Once-Sundered Ring to The lost spirit of Pascilon.`  
`The lost spirit of Pascilon takes your the Girth of the Most Holy.`  
`Pascilon smiles, and seems to glow even brighter; "I never thought I'd see`  
`this ring again. It will never be quite what it was, of course, but I`  
`believe I can use the Holy Power of this girth to restore some of its former`  
`glory. Wear it with pride, friend."`  
[`The` `lost` `spirit` `of`
`Pascilon`](/wiki/Lost_Spirit_Of_Pascilon "wikilink")` gives you the `**`Once-Sundered`
`Ring`**`!`

Note that the ring is bound to the person who
[Pascilon](/wiki/Lost_Spirit_Of_Pascilon "wikilink") gives it to.
