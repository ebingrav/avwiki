*Open Hand allows you to improve your 'weaponless' attacks. This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is automatic once
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"). Where average martial artists leave
off, [monks](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") develop further by devoting
entirely to the open handed attack.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
