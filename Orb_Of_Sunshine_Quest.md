to learn why his fighters have been mysteriously disappearing
one-by-one. Unknown to him, a vital (though tragic) [piece of
evidence](/wiki/Tangle_Of_Bloody_Remains "wikilink") lies hidden somewhere in
[his hollow](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Hollow "wikilink"). He would be very
grateful to receive it...*
