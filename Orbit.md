*When [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"), the
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") uses their mental powers
to
[telekinetically](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")
control the target [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). The caster
places the [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") into orbit around
their body, and can thereby gain any magical
[effects](/wiki/Object_Affects "wikilink") the
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") can grant. Only one such
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") can be placed into orbit at any
given time. Unfortunately, the process of charging the
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") with psionic power causes the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") to destabilize and eventually
disappear. At the end of the [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")'s
duration, the orbiting [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") will be
lost forever. Objects with certain qualities and
[types](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink") are not valid candidates
for this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). There are no
reimbursements for [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that you lose
with this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")...*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'orbit'
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.
