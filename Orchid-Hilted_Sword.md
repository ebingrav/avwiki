*This sword is both durable and elegant. The long blade is tempered by
years of work, but is nothing compared to the hilt, which is ornately
shaped into the form and color of a Black Orchid.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 15-16 to 43 (average 29).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage`
`roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`hp`](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink")` by 20 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`strength`](/wiki/Strength "wikilink")` by 3 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`wisdom`](/wiki/Wisdom "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`
