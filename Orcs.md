Orcs are cruel and brutal humanoids that tend to live in tribal
societies ruled by force. They respect only strength, and are fierce
fighters. Their features are often described as "pig-like" in nature,
but they vary greatly in appearance with the exception that they are all
ugly to grotesque. Over the years, orcs have always tried to bring out
the most destructive and warlike qualities within their race, for they
consider these traits to be noble marks of the Gods' favor. Following
their own tribal customs, they consider the flesh of most of their
enemies to be quite a delicacy.

Orcs are slightly stronger and faster than humans, and are a lot hardier
as well. They are generally stupid in nature, and while there are orcish
shamans, it is rare to see an Orcish spellcaster.

Orcs all have the innate ability to see in the dark, for it is usually
in darkness when their ambushes and raids on friendlier races occur.
These fearsome creatures often go berserk in battle, and enter what can
often be described a frenzy-like state. Once they have destroyed their
enemies, they will often butcher the carcasses for food.
