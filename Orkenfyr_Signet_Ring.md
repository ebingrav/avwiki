Comments
--------

A bit weaker than the [Ring Of Purity](/wiki/Ring_Of_Purity "wikilink"), yet
at the same time carrying the full AC bonus of [Ring Of
Truth](/wiki/Ring_Of_Truth "wikilink"), this ring should be used both in
standard tank sets and in mixed sets for [tanking in hit
gear](/wiki/Tanking_In_Hit_Gear "wikilink"). The high hit roll bonus also
makes it an ideal archer ring, especially for soloing/tanking archers.
