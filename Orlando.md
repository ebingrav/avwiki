*Orlando is dressed in the black bodysuit that tends to be the uniform
amongst [assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink"). He obviously
wasn't happy as a bard, and has made a small career change. You think
that you preferred him as a bard.*

**Keywords:** Orlando, bard, assassin.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 15.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[poisonous](/wiki/Biting_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"), [wimpy](Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink"),
[sneak](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink").
