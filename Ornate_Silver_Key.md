*This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") bears the symbol of
[Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink") on one side and the personal sigil of
[Tul-Sith's high priestess](/wiki/High_Priestess_Of_Tul-Sith "wikilink") on
the other.*

**Keyword(s):** silver, ornate, key.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 29-31, 34-36.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks a series of doors. It
opens the door south of The Office of the Secretary to the Temple of
Tul-Sith, east of Entry Chamber to the High Priestess of Tul-Sith's
Quarters, south of The Office of the High Priestess of Tul-Sith, and up
from Private Quarters of the High Priestess of Tul-Sith.
