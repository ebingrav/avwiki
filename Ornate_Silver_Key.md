*This [key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") bears the symbol of
[Tul-Sith](Tul-Sith "wikilink") on one side and the personal sigil of
[Tul-Sith's high priestess](High_Priestess_Of_Tul-Sith "wikilink") on
the other.*

**Keyword(s):** silver, ornate, key.  
**[Level(s)](Object_Level "wikilink"):** 29-31, 34-36.  
**[Type](:Category:_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](:Category:_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") unlocks a series of doors. It
opens the door south of The Office of the Secretary to the Temple of
Tul-Sith, east of Entry Chamber to the High Priestess of Tul-Sith's
Quarters, south of The Office of the High Priestess of Tul-Sith, and up
from Private Quarters of the High Priestess of Tul-Sith.
