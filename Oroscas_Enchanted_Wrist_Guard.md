Modifies ac by -15 continuous  
Bases: 10-12  
Levels: 51-52  
Weight: 2  
Area: [Midgaard](/wiki/Midgaard "wikilink")  
Mob: [Count Orosca](/wiki/Count_Orosca "wikilink") ([Ticket
Quest](/wiki/Ticket_Quest "wikilink"))  

To get this gear you need to complete a ticket quest. All of the tickets
are antique emerald jewelry, and they're located in the various [Secrets
of Avatar](/wiki/Secrets_of_Avatar "wikilink")

The tickets are:  
[Antique Emerald Bracelet](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Bracelet "wikilink")  
[Antique Emerald Tiara](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Tiara "wikilink")  
[Antique Emerald Brooch](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Brooch "wikilink")  
[Antique Emerald Earring](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Earring "wikilink")  
[Antique Emerald Necklace](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Necklace "wikilink")  
Once you have them all, go to [Count Orosca](/wiki/Count_Orosca "wikilink"), a
portable mob in Midgaard, and give one of the tickets to him.
