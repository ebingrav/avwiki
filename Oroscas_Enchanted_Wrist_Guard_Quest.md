The five [tickets](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink") you need to collect
are:  
\* [Antique Emerald Bracelet](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Bracelet "wikilink")  
\* [Antique Emerald Tiara](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Tiara "wikilink")  
\* [Antique Emerald Brooch](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Brooch "wikilink")  
\* [Antique Emerald Earring](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Earring "wikilink")  
\* [Antique Emerald Necklace](/wiki/Antique_Emerald_Necklace "wikilink")  
Each [ticket](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink")'s individual page has
instructions on how to retrieve it.

Once you have them all, go to [Count Orosca](/wiki/Count_Orosca "wikilink"), a
portable mob in [Midgaard Fair](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard_Fair "wikilink"),
and give one of the tickets to him.
