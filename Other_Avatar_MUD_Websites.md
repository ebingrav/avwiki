[Aejase](http://aejase.webhop.net) is a versatile
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") search engine originally developed
by Khazaad. It disappeared from the Internet for a while (and became
somewhat out-of-date as a result) but returned in 2005 under new
management by Rien. Players may submit additions and corrections to this
site to help maximize its usefulness.

[Another Avatar MUD
Site](http://gishcam.tripod.com/gaming/avatar/index.html), by Sheba,
contains lots of useful information, including some
[race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink")-[class](Category%3A_Classes "wikilink")
data, detailed [worship data](/wiki/Category%3A_Religion "wikilink"), a
[scroll](/wiki/Category%3A_Scrolls "wikilink") library, player birthdays, and
much more.

[Avatar Picture Page](http://avatar.7thfire.com/), hosted by
[Debonair](/wiki/Debonair "wikilink"), shows photographs of many Avatar MUD
players in real life. This site also has forums for both discussion and
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") auctions.

[Outland.org](http://www.outland.org/) is Avatar MUD's official site and
is a great source of weekly news reports, information about scheduled
[quests](/wiki/Quests "wikilink"), official help files, and so forth.

[Sandgroper's Avatar MUD
Site](http://www.geocities.com/sandgroper_chimbu) provides good
descriptions for most [lowmort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink")
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"), including walking directions to
them and useful [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") found in them. It is
also the original home of the "Stat Tables," which may now be found
[here](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistics "wikilink") on this site, as well, revised
and expanded.

[Wiseman's Avatar race comparison
chart](http://users.tpg.com.au/wyvaud/avatar_races/races.html) is a
great place to visit before creating a new
[alt](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"), as it consists of a handy
sortable table of statistics for all of Avatar's different
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink").

If you know of another good Avatar website, feel free to write up a
description of it here.
