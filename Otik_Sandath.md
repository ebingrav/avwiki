*Otik is an older man with a gift for cooking. Otik whispers, "A magical
[staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink")! I bet it was forged by
[demons](/wiki/Demons "wikilink") from the terrible Darken Wood. There are
terrible mysteries in that place, there are."*

**Keywords:** Otik, Sandath, bartender.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 14.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[shopkeeper](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
