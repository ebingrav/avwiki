`Modifies armor class by -18 continuous.`  
`Modifies damage roll by 2 continuous.`  
`Modifies hit roll by 1 continuous.`

Bases: 10-12  
Levels: 50-52  
Weight: 8  
Area: [Lake of Tears](/wiki/Category%3A_Lake_Of_Tears "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Lake_Of_Tears_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: [Ouroburos](/wiki/Ouroburos "wikilink")  
Directions: Lake of Tears is west of Icewolf. Once you are in the area
you can track ouroborus. There are 3 ouroborus', 1 in each of 3 rooms.
The ouroborus in the last room has the tail. Warning, these are very
large mobs.
