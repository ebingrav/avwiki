*Apparently bathing is not a part of the Outsiders' routine. This little
[rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") is covered in dirt, his hair is
thick and matted, and his clothes reek like the Royal Stables after a
batch of bad hay.*

**Keywords:** Outsider, rogue, thief.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 14.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[thief](/wiki/Thieving_Mobs "wikilink"), [wimpy](Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"),
[sneak](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink").
