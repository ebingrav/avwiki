*The pack rat is a large rodent, with long whiskers. Mottled brownish
grey fur covers its slim body. Its plaintive face seems to be begging
for some new and exciting wonder to see.*

**Keywords:** pack, rat.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 22.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Category%3A_Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink"),
[poisonous](/wiki/Biting_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
