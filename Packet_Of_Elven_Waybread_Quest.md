The first part you should have [water
breathing](/wiki/Water_Breathing "wikilink") and [detect
invisible](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink") on.

-   Portal to [a pangolin](/wiki/Pangolin "wikilink").
-   Go 2nwn.
-   Get the [small arrow](/wiki/Small_Arrow "wikilink") from the bubble.

**Warning:** The following will take you to the [Transfigured
Forest](/wiki/Category%3ATransfigured_Forest "wikilink"). This place is highly
aggie and dangerous.

-   Portal to [stone trickster](/wiki/Stone_Trickster "wikilink").
-   Go ess.
-   Pull bloom.
-   Go 2sesese3sd2e.
-   Give to the [seasoned ranger](/wiki/Seasoned_Ranger "wikilink"). He can
    detect move, sneak, and invisible so no need to go visible. He will
    give you a [Packet Of Elven
    Waybread](/wiki/Packet_Of_Elven_Waybread "wikilink").
