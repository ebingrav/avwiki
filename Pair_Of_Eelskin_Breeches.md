*The property of the Lhadvar's [Chief
Fisherman](/wiki/Lhadvar_Fisherman "wikilink"), these eelskin breeches were
made to protect him from the water as he caught fish to feed the hungry
tribe.*

`Modifies `[`moves`](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink")` by -10 continuous.`
