Directions
----------

You can find this area in the [ruins of
Reveria](/wiki/Category%3ARuins_Of_Reveria "wikilink").
[Teleport](/wiki/Teleport "wikilink") [drow spectre](Drow_Spectre "wikilink")
and go s, 3w, 2n, 2w, n to enter Secrets of the Shrine. Once you make
your way down to the lower level go e, e, n to find mob or track. Mob is
invis/hidden and aggies. Area is cursed - be careful.
