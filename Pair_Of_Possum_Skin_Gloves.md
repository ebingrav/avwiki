*The thick fur of the Opossum was used to make these gloves. It is
surprising that the fur was left on the outside, but considering the
scaly nature of the animals living in this area, it adds extra
protection.*

[`Armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 4-6.`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 4 continuous.`  
`Facilitates the archery skill `[`fleeing`
`shot`](/wiki/Fleeing_Shot "wikilink")`.`
