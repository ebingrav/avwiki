Comments
--------

Unenchanted, this item should be 5 point improvement over an unenchanted
[Legacy Of King Vorin](/wiki/Legacy_Of_King_Vorin "wikilink"), assuming best
base AC is used, which effectively makes it identical (though the Legacy
is lighter, but of poorer quality). A well enchanted Legacy, or even
[Bronze Snake Armbands](/wiki/Bronze_Snake_Armbands "wikilink") *could* end up
better.

Directions
----------

To get this piece of gear one must explore the [Hamlet of
Kreigstadt](/wiki/Category%3A_Hamlet_Of_Kreigstadt "wikilink"). Certain
watchman who patrol the area are equipped with the bracer. Ulrich may be
used as a portal point, and one can simply head north to begin their
search. The [Robber Baron](/wiki/Robber_Baron "wikilink"), who is portable and
aggie, also carries a steel bracer.
