carved into a crystal clear pommel. The hilt is wrapped in white leather
that somehow always appears to be clean.*

[`Damage`](Melee_Weapon_Values.md "wikilink")` is 8-11 to 27-31 (average 17-21).`

## Comments

This weapon grants [Protection Evil](Protection_Evil.md "wikilink"),
which is a rather powerful defensive boost. This can be quite useful as
tank gear if one does not have access to [Protection
Evil](Protection_Evil.md "wikilink").
