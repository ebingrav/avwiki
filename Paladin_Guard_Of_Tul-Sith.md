*This young [paladin](/wiki/Category%3A_Paladins "wikilink") has only recently
finished his training. He was sent by the Captain from [Tul-Sith's
hospice](/wiki/Category%3A_Tul-Sith's_Hospice "wikilink") to be one of the
guards of the temple. He stands proud in his new uniform, and is
vigilant in his duties. Sadly, he thinks he left a portrait of his
mother back at [the
hospice](/wiki/Category%3A_Tul-Sith's_Hospice "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** guard, TulSith, paladin.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 25.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [protection evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink").
