*This simple but elegant uniform is made of the whitest silk. Nothing is
too good for the leader of [Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink")'s
[paladin](/wiki/Category%3A_Paladins "wikilink") guards. Embroidered in white
on white on the cuffs are circles bearing
[Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink")'s emblem. On the left risk is an oak
cluster that indicates the rank this leader bears.*

[`Armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 6-7.`

`class`](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")` by -3 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`wisdom`](/wiki/Wisdom "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`
