*This shortsword bears the emblem of [Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink")
carved into a crystal clear pommel. The hilt is wrapped in white leather
that somehow always appears to be clean.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 8-11 to 27-31 (average 17-21).`  
`'`[`protection` `evil`](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink")`' continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** shortsword, paladin, sword.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 24-28.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <wielded>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 50 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 9 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):**
[anti-evil](/wiki/Anti-Evil_Flag "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

This weapon grants [Protection Evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"), which
is a rather powerful defensive boost. This can be quite useful as tank
gear if one does not have access to [Protection
Evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink").
