*Named for a plant rumored to have Miraculous properties, this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") clears poisons, diseases and
other physical ailments. It may need to be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
multiple times to be fully effective.*

Prerequisite(s): [Pure Touch](/wiki/Pure_Touch "wikilink"), [Via
Elimo](/wiki/Via_Elimo "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'panacea'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
