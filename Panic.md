*While this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") lasts, the
panic-striken victim will fall and injure themself if they attempt to
[flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink"). Panic then ends. Panic is
[exhausted](/wiki/Exhaustion "wikilink") for a short time when
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Dread](/wiki/Dread "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'panic'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
