*Learning this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") will allow you the
chance to fend off incoming attacks, avoiding any damage that would've
been received from them. You must be [wielding](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") to use this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"). It is automatic once
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
