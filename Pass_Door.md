*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") enables the caster to pass
through most closed [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'pass door'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rather useful to all
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). It is superfluous to
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") affected by [Ectoplasmic
Form](/wiki/Ectoplasmic_Form "wikilink"). See [Translucent
Flag](/wiki/Translucent_Flag "wikilink") for more information about its
effects.

See also [Gear That Applies 'Pass
Door'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Pass_Door' "wikilink") and [Pass
Door Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Pass_Door_Gear "wikilink").
