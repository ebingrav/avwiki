Some AVATAR MUD [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") are passproof.
When such [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") are closed, players may
not magically [pass](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink") through them.
