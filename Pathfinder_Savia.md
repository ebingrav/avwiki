*Savia is one of the foremost pathfinders amongst the
[dwarves](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink"). Daughter of a trapper, she spent many of
her formative years on the trail with her father hunting game and
dressing skins. She honed her skills working with
[elvish](/wiki/Elves "wikilink") pathfinders and although not as good as an
[elf](/wiki/Elves "wikilink"), she is without peer amongst
[dwarven](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") pathfinders.*

**Keywords:** Pathfinder, Savia.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 24.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[mage](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
