*[Bodyguards](/wiki/Category%3A_Bodyguards "wikilink") really hate it when you
attack their masters. With this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"),
they gain a [damage](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink") bonus along with the
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") penalty commonly associated with
[rescue](/wiki/Rescue "wikilink"), but only when [rescuing](Rescue "wikilink")
their current [monitor](/wiki/Monitor "wikilink") target from genuine danger.*

Prerequisite(s): [Intercede](/wiki/Intercede "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
