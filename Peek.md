*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") shows you a
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")'s inventory when you look
at a [character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). Use of the
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is automatic once you have
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") it. You can now toggle its affect by
using the [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: peek.
