*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") enables
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") to
[pick](/wiki/Pick "wikilink") locked
[containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") or
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [pick](/wiki/Pick "wikilink")
&lt;[container](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink")/direction&gt;.
