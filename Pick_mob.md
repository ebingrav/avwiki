*Known as 'Pick', short for 'First Pick', an honorary title given to the
premiere miner in [Mt Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink"). Fast of
arm, with hawk-like eyes able to spot a flicker of durrite at 50 paces
and never short of a kind word for his colleagues, Pick remains a most
popular [dwarf](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Pick, powerful, dwarf, miner.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 23.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.

Comments
--------

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must pass two locked
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") to reach Pick; to do this, they
will need to either [bash](/wiki/Bashdoor "wikilink") these
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") open or
[unlock](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") them using their respective
[keys](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may obtain their
[keys](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") by doing the following: (1) either
[buy](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") [a mine pass level
one](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One "wikilink") from [the
receptionist](/wiki/Receptionist "wikilink") or complete [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Mine Pass Level One
Quest](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One_Quest "wikilink") and (2) get [a mine pass
level two](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_Two "wikilink") from [a humble
pilgrim](/wiki/Humble_Pilgrim "wikilink").

Pick is involved in [Mt. Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Legacy
of King Vorin Quest](/wiki/Legacy_Of_King_Vorin_Quest "wikilink").
