Some AVATAR MUD [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") are pickproof.
When such [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") are both closed and
[locked](/wiki/Lockable_Doors "wikilink"), players may not
[pick](/wiki/Pick_Lock "wikilink") their locks.
