[Pill](/wiki/Category%3A_Pills "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have four
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **[Spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") Level:** This value
    determines what level this [pill](/wiki/Category%3A_Pills "wikilink")'s
    one, two, or three [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") will be
    when they are [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **[Spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") [Cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
    (3):** These three values determine which one, two, or three
    [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") this
    [pill](/wiki/Category%3A_Pills "wikilink") will [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on
    those who [eat](/wiki/Eat "wikilink") it.

See also [Pills](/wiki/Category%3A_Pills "wikilink").
