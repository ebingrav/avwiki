*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") lowers the victim's
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") and damages them each tick. It spreads
from one [character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") in a
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") to others, having the same effect
on them as the original [victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'plague'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used, especially
as it spreads so easily to both friend and foe alike.

See also [Gear That Applies
'Plague'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Plague' "wikilink").
