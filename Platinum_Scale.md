*This must be what the Sun Cabal were trying to retrieve from the Astral
Plane.*  
*It's probably a good thing Ramahdon never got his hands on this.*  
Type: Ticket  
Level: 125  
Flags: magic  
Weight: 3  
Mob: [Sygerion](/wiki/Sygerion "wikilink")  
Area: [Memory Lane](/wiki/Category%3AMemory_Lane "wikilink")  

------------------------------------------------------------------------

Note that this gear is reboot only.

This item loads on [Sygerion](/wiki/Sygerion "wikilink"), who is in the last
gear room of the [Memory Lane](/wiki/Category%3AMemory_Lane "wikilink") run.
See [Gear In Memory Lane](/wiki/Category%3AGear_In_Memory_Lane "wikilink") for
more information on how to retrieve this item.

The scale is used to gain access to the [Sun
Cabal](/wiki/Category%3ASun_Cabal "wikilink"), where the [Shield Of
Lords](/wiki/Shield_Of_Lords "wikilink") can be acquired. To reach that area
give this item to [Ramahdon](/wiki/Ramahdon "wikilink") in the
[Summoning](http://avatar.melanarchy.info?title=Category:Summoning) on
[Midgaardia](/wiki/Category%3AMidgaardia "wikilink"). See [Gear In Sun
Cabal](/wiki/Category%3AGear_In_Sun_Cabal "wikilink") for more information on
how this item is used.
