*With this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") you can
[wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") equal [weighted](Object_Weight "wikilink")
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") in both hands up to a certain
[weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"). ([Weight](Object_Weight "wikilink")
is determined by how much [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") and how
many poise-like [skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") you have.)*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
