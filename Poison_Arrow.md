This [skill](:Category:_Skills_And_Spells.md "wikilink") allows the
[archer](:Category:_Archers.md "wikilink") to create [poison
arrows](:Category:_Poison_Warheads.md "wikilink"). You will need
progressively higher [level](Object_Level.md "wikilink") [fletching
kits](Fletching_Tools_And_Materials.md "wikilink") to make the higher
[level](Object_Level.md "wikilink")
[arrow](:Category:_Arrows.md "wikilink") items. If making [poison
arrows](Poison_Arrows.md "wikilink"), you must have a [poison
item](:Category:_Poisons.md "wikilink") in your inventory, and specify
it in the [command](:Category:_Commands.md "wikilink") line. The poison
arrow skill will also inform you about the nature of a [poison
item](:Category:_Poisons.md "wikilink") when
[examining](Examine.md "wikilink") it.

Prerequisite(s): [Fletch](Fletch.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [fletch](Fletch.md "wikilink")
\<[arrows](:Category:_Arrows.md "wikilink")/[bolts](:Category:_Bolts.md "wikilink")/[stones](:Category:_Stones.md "wikilink")\>
poison \<[poison-object](:Category:_Poisons.md "wikilink")\>.
