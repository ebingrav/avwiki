This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") allows the
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") to create [poison
arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Poison_Warheads "wikilink"). You will need
progressively higher [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") [fletching
kits](/wiki/Fletching_Tools_And_Materials "wikilink") to make the higher
[level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") [arrow](Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink")
items. If making [poison arrows](/wiki/Poison_Arrows "wikilink"), you must
have a [poison item](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") in your inventory,
and specify it in the [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") line.
The poison arrow skill will also inform you about the nature of a
[poison item](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") when
[examining](/wiki/Examine "wikilink") it.

Prerequisite(s): [Fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink").

Syntax: [fletch](/wiki/Fletch "wikilink")
&lt;[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink")/[bolts](Category%3A_Bolts "wikilink")/[stones](Category%3A_Stones "wikilink")&gt;
poison &lt;[poison-object](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink")&gt;.
