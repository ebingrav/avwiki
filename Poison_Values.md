[Poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have two
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **[Poison Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink"):** This value
    determines this [poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink")'s
    [type](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink"), which may be either
    [poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poison "wikilink"),
    [toxin](/wiki/Category%3A_Toxin "wikilink"),
    [biotoxin](/wiki/Category%3A_Biotoxin "wikilink"),
    [virus](/wiki/Category%3A_Virus "wikilink"),
    [venom](/wiki/Category%3A_Venom "wikilink"), or
    [plague](/wiki/Category%3A_Plague "wikilink") (or some other
    [type](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") not found in Midgaardia).
    Follow these links for more information about each [poison
    type](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **Doses:** This value determines this
    [poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink")'s number of doses.

See also [Poisons](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink").
