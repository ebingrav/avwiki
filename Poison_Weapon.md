*[Poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") is the stock and trade of any
good [Assassin](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink"). This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") allows them to modify a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") to carry
[poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink"), or to
re-[poison](/wiki/Poison_(command) "wikilink") a poisoned
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") which has lost its bite.
Required for the job are a poisoning tool kit and a [poison
item](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink"). The [poison
item](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") will determine how many doses of
what kind of [poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") are imbued. Only
piercing [weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") can be
[poisoned](/wiki/Poison_(command) "wikilink"), and a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") cannot be
[sharpened](/wiki/Sharpen_Weapon "wikilink") once it has been modified to
carry [poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink"). A high quality tool kit
and high [dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink") and
[intelligence](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink") will make this task much easier.
There is a chance of lasting damage to the
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") if you bungle this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"). There is no guarantee the
[poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") will infect the target on every
hit, or even every [round](/wiki/Rounds "wikilink"). Especially damaging
attacks such as [backstab](/wiki/Backstab "wikilink") or
[murder](/wiki/Murder "wikilink") are much more likely to transfer
[poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") than a normal hit. Once
poisoned, the victim will experience [hp](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") drain
and various negative affects. To remove the
[poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") from a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink"), type
[POISON](/wiki/Poison_(command) "wikilink")
&lt;[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")&gt; REMOVE.*

Prerequisite(s): [Rogue Lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink"), [Weapon
Lore](/wiki/Weapon_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: [poison](/wiki/Poison_(command) "wikilink")
&lt;[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")&gt;
&lt;[poison-object](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink")&gt;.  
Syntax: [poison](/wiki/Poison_(command) "wikilink")
&lt;[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")&gt; remove.
