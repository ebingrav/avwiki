*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") reduces the
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") of the
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") by two, as well as reducing the
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")'s regeneration rate.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'poison'
&lt;[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is practically never used.

See also [Gear That Applies
'Poison'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Poison' "wikilink") and [Poison
Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Poison_Gear "wikilink").
