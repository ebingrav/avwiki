## Comments

Note that the poison in this weapon is likely to pour out upon
equipping, since [Poison Weapon](Poison_Weapon "wikilink") isn't learned
until level 42 for [rogues](:Category:_Rogues.md "wikilink"),
[assassins](:Category:_Assassins.md "wikilink"), or [Black Circle
Initiates](:Category:_Black_Circle_Initiates.md "wikilink").
