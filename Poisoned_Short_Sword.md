*A short sword with a green ichor dripping off the blade lies here.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 9 to 18 (average 13).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 4 continuous.`

[anti-good](/wiki/Anti-Good_Flag "wikilink"), [evil](Evil_Flag "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

Note that the poison in this weapon is likely to pour out upon
equipping, since [Poison Weapon](/wiki/Poison_Weapon "wikilink") isn't learned
until level 42 for [rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink"),
[assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink"), or [Black Circle
Initiates](/wiki/Category%3A_Black_Circle_Initiates "wikilink").
