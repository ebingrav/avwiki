[Poisoned-weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisoned_Weapons "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have four
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **[Poison Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink"):** This value
    determines which [type of poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink")
    this [poisoned
    weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisoned_Weapons "wikilink") carries.

<!-- -->

-   **Damage, Minimum:** This value determines this [poisoned
    weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisoned_Weapons "wikilink")'s minimum possible
    damage to those it hits.

<!-- -->

-   **Damage, Maximum:** This value determines this [poisoned
    weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisoned_Weapons "wikilink")'s maximum possible
    damage to those it hits.

<!-- -->

-   **[Poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") Doses:** This value
    determines how many doses of
    [poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") this [poisoned
    weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisoned_Weapons "wikilink") carries.

See also [Poisoned Weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisoned_Weapons "wikilink").
