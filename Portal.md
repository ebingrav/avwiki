*The Portal [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") opens a one-way gate
in the fabric of reality, by joining the locations of the
[caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") and target. The target can be either a
[player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or a
[mobile](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), but not an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") or
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). A [portal](/wiki/Portals "wikilink")
will stay open longer for higher-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink")
[players](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"), but it will eventually
collapse. After the [portal](/wiki/Portals "wikilink") has opened, step
through to the destination by typing "[enter](/wiki/Enter "wikilink")
[portal](/wiki/Portals "wikilink")". Portal carries a risk of damage to
[hp](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") and/or [mana](Mana_Points "wikilink"). Enter
unknown [portals](/wiki/Portals "wikilink") with caution. It is your
responsibility to know which [portal](/wiki/Portals "wikilink") you
[enter](/wiki/Enter "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'portal'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")/[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is invaluable to
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), especially when running in
groups. When portaling, target keywords should be chosen carefully as
some are shared by multiple [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink").
[Scry](/wiki/Scry "wikilink") may be used to verify where a certain
combination of keywords will (currently) lead; this destination may
change over time, however, as [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") move
and die and respawn. See [Portals](/wiki/Portals "wikilink") for more
information about these [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")
created by this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").
