` Level 21 spells of: 'heal'.`

Object 'potion dew' type potion, extra flags glow magic. Weight 3, value
5000, level 27. Object Quality (1 / 1 hps)

Mob: [Young Minotaur](/wiki/Young_Minotaur "wikilink")  
Area: [Paradise Point](/wiki/Category%3A_Paradise_Point "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Paradise_Point_Map "wikilink"))  
The Young Minotaurs wander so track when you get to them or scan around.

From Sol: 3e, 13s, 4w, 5n,w,s. The mob is portable, but sometimes you'll
end up in [Bloodsong Island](/wiki/Category%3ABloodsong_Island "wikilink").
[Docile Minotaur](/wiki/Docile_Minotaur "wikilink") is a safer pp to the area.
All of the young minotaurs have a potion.  
be careful in some of the rooms as you will be fired upon and it really
hurts!  
There are also some biggish wandering aggies in the area, so it's a good
idea to remember to set your recall if you're small.
