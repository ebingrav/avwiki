## Comments

Although this [mob](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink") is portable, at least
one [mob](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink") in [Bloodsong
Island](:Category:_Bloodsong_Island.md "wikilink") uses these same
keywords; thus, it is safer to use keywords [docile
minotaur](Docile_Minotaur_Female.md "wikilink") instead. Also, beware of
both (1) larger [aggressive](Aggressive_Mobs.md "wikilink")
[minotaurs](Minotaurs.md "wikilink") that may wander among these young
ones ([Territorial Minotaur](Territorial_Minotaur "wikilink")) and (2)
certain [mobs](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink") that will snipe at
[characters](:Category:_Characters.md "wikilink") in certain
[rooms](:Category:_Rooms.md "wikilink"), which may be very painful or
even deadly!

See also [Suggested Healing
Gear](Suggested_Spellcasting_Gear_#Suggested_Healing_Gear.md "wikilink").
