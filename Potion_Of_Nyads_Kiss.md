Level: 20-21  
Weight: 1  
Type: Quaff  
Effect: Water Breathing, Cure Light x2  
Area: [Elven Foothills](/wiki/Category%3A_Elven_Foothills "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Elven_Foothills_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: None (on the ground).  
  
Port [Baron Sarph](/wiki/Baron_Amier_Sarphyre "wikilink"), go 3e, u, w, s, u,
n, look around for [Half-Elven Rogue](/wiki/Half-Elven_Rogue "wikilink") and
kill it to get [Key To Sarphyre's
Display](/wiki/Key_To_Sarphyre's_Display "wikilink").  
Then go back to [Baron Sarphyre](/wiki/Baron_Amier_Sarphyre "wikilink") and go
2e, 2n to get to the case, open it with the
[key](/wiki/Key_To_Sarphyre's_Display "wikilink") and get the [Fae Warding
Glyph](/wiki/Fae_Warding_Glyph "wikilink") from it.  
  
Then port [Nyad](/wiki/Nyad "wikilink") or go 2s, e, u, w, s, u, s, d, 2s to
get to [Nyad](/wiki/Nyad "wikilink"), give her the
[Glyph](/wiki/Fae_Warding_Glyph "wikilink") and you will be transported to a
room which has the Nyad's Kiss on the ground. You may as well Grab the
[Nyad Hair](/wiki/Lock_Of_Long_White_Nyad_Hair "wikilink") while you're here
and complete the [Gargoyle Bracer Quest](/wiki/Gargoyle_Bracer "wikilink")
while you're here.

The [Wreath Of Water Lillies](/wiki/Wreath_Of_Water_Lillies "wikilink") can be
given to [Nyad](/wiki/Nyad "wikilink") as well, and this is technically part
of this quest too. However, it will not directly help you complete it.
