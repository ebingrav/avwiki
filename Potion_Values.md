[Potion](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have four
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **[Spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") Level:** This value
    determines what level this
    [potion](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink")'s one, two, or three
    [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") will be when they are
    [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **[Spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") [Cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
    (3):** These three values determine which one, two, or three
    [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") this
    [potion](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink") will
    [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on those who [quaff](Quaff "wikilink") it.

See also [Potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink").
