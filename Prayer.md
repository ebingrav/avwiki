*With prayer, a [worshipper](/wiki/Worship "wikilink") may seek a blessing or
submit to a curse dependent upon the needs of their
[God](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink"). The results of a
prayer are varied and only your particular [god or
goddess](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") knows the reasons
for each.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'prayer'.
