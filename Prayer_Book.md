*The book is filled with short prayers to Dyah Yuyun Utami, the Goddess
of Light. As you quickly glance through them you read some of them:
'Goddess of Light whom I adore, Protect my heart from pain and terror. I
give you my love, now you give me health and warmth inside my heart's
core.' Hmmm... lets see what else this thing can do!*

`Level 2 spells of: '`[`dispel` `magic`](/wiki/Dispel_Magic "wikilink")`'.`
