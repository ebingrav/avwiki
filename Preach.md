*When Preaching, a [Priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink")'s magic can
reach the masses. The [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") takes
single-target [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), and
[casts](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") them on all the members of the
[Priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink")'s group. Various other
[skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") are required to Preach more
powerful [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), though they all use
the Preach [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink").*

Prerequisites: [Magic Lore](/wiki/Magic_Lore "wikilink"), [Via
Pax](/wiki/Via_Pax "wikilink").

Syntax: preach &lt;[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")&gt;.
