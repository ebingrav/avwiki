*When Preaching, a [Priest](:Category:_Priests "wikilink")'s magic can
reach the masses. The [command](:Category:_Commands "wikilink") takes
single-target [spells](:Category:_Spells "wikilink"), and
[casts](Cast "wikilink") them on all the members of the
[Priest](:Category:_Priests "wikilink")'s group. Various other
[skills](:Category:_Skills "wikilink") are required to Preach more
powerful [spells](:Category:_Spells "wikilink"), though they all use the
Preach [command](:Category:_Commands "wikilink").*

Prerequisites: [Magic Lore](Magic_Lore "wikilink"), [Via
Pax](Via_Pax "wikilink").

Syntax: preach \<[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink")\>.
