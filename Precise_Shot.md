*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") represents the
[Fusilier](/wiki/Category%3A_Fusiliers "wikilink")'s specialization with
[slings](/wiki/Category%3A_Slings "wikilink") and [thrown
weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Throwing_Weapons "wikilink"). It increases damage
and accuracy with both, but only against larger enemies.*

Prerequisite(s): [Target Leading](/wiki/Target_Leading "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
