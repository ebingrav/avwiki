*When engaged in direct combat, a
[Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") with this Rite will hit
before his enemy during regular melee rounds. It is not effective
against other [players](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"), nor does it
work for "between round" attacks such as those from
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"),[skills](Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"),
or from being unexpectedly attacked.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
