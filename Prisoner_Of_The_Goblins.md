*Pity is the first emotion that reaches you as you glance at this
hapless adventurer. While passing this mountain, he probably
inadvertently entered [these
caverns](/wiki/Category%3A_Goblin_Caverns "wikilink") searching for a
shortcut. As you move closer to examine him, he manages to whisper to
you, 'Please... bring me... [the key](/wiki/Crude_Key "wikilink")... you...
will be... rewarded... well...' As you hear this, your purse appears
smaller and smaller.*

**Keywords:** adventurer, prisoner.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 4.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
