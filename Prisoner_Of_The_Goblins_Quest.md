From [frightened goblin](/wiki/Frightened_Goblin "wikilink"):

-   Find [A goblin jailer](/wiki/Goblin_Jailer "wikilink") and dispose of him.
-   [Get](/wiki/Get "wikilink") [a small, crude
    key](/wiki/Small,_Crude_Key "wikilink") from his corpse.
-   Find [A prisoner of the goblins](/wiki/Prisoner_Of_The_Goblins "wikilink")
    and give it to him in order to begin the quest proper.

You can lead the prisoner to:

1.  Safety at the [Meadow](/wiki/Category%3AMeadow "wikilink") entrance, and
    receive 300 gold,
2.  Locate the key to *The Treasure Room Of The Great Goblin* (it's on
    one of the roaming guards) where the prisoner will reward you with a
    [silver beacon](/wiki/Silver_Beacon "wikilink"),
3.  The room before the [Valley Of The
    Dogs](/wiki/Category%3AValley_Of_The_Dogs "wikilink"), reward is a
    [mithril rod](/wiki/Mithril_Rod "wikilink"),
4.  The exit onto the River Tunnels, and then all the way to the *A
    Watery Pit* and receive a ... somewhat less satisfactory conclusion.
