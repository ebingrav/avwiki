[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") prevent both players and
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") from entering them while two players
and/or [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") are already within them.

See also [Solitary Rooms](/wiki/Solitary_Rooms "wikilink").
