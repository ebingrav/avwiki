[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") are affected by either
[Protection Evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink") or [Protection
Good](/wiki/Protection_Good "wikilink"), depending on their alignment.
