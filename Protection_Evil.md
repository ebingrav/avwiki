*Protection Evil reduces the damage taken from any attack from an evil
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") by 25%, and only works on the
[caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"). Protection Evil can be used in conjunction
with [Sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Detect Alignment](/wiki/Detect_Alignment "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'protection evil'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in both
[Aegis](/wiki/Aegis "wikilink") and [Awen](Awen "wikilink"). See also
[Protection Good](/wiki/Protection_Good "wikilink").

See also [Gear That Applies 'Protection
Evil'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Protection_Evil' "wikilink") and
[Protection Evil Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Protection_Evil_Gear "wikilink").
