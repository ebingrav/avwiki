*Protection Good reduces the damage taken from any attack from an good
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") by 25%, and only works on the
[caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"). Protection Good can be used in conjunction
with [Sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Detect Alignment](/wiki/Detect_Alignment "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'protection good'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in
[Awen](/wiki/Awen "wikilink") but *not* in [Aegis](Aegis "wikilink"). See also
[Protection Evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink").

See also [Gear That Applies Protection
Good](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_Protection_Good "wikilink") and
[Protection Good Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Protection_Good_Gear "wikilink").
