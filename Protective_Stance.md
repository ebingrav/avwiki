*Great defenders protect the weak. While in Protective Stance, you will
never fail a [rescue](/wiki/Rescue "wikilink") attempt.*

Prerequisite(s): [Rescue](/wiki/Rescue "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](/wiki/Stance "wikilink") protective.

See also [Relentless Stance](/wiki/Relentless_Stance "wikilink") and [Surefoot
Stance](/wiki/Surefoot_Stance "wikilink").
