*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") does minimal physical
damage, but against enemies with spellcasting abilities (and not merely
[natural abilities](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") similar to
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), such as
[dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") [breath](Racial_Breath "wikilink")) it will
also drain [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") and transfer it to the
caster.*

Prerequisite(s): [Psychic Drain](/wiki/Psychic_Drain "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'psyphon'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
