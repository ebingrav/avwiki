*The touch of a [Priest](:Category:_Priests "wikilink") is pure enough
to burn away poisons and diseases in the target. It may need to be
[cast](Cast "wikilink") multiple times to be fully effective.*

Prerequisite(s): [Via Pax](Via_Pax "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'pure touch'
\<[character](:Category:_Characters "wikilink")\>.
