*The touch of a [Priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") is pure enough
to burn away poisons and diseases in the target. It may need to be
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") multiple times to be fully effective.*

Prerequisite(s): [Via Pax](/wiki/Via_Pax "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'pure touch'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
