*The body is a temple, and with this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), the caster can keep that temple
tidy, becoming immune to disease. Since this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") requires a constant light flow of
Divine Power, the caster will regenerate [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink")
slightly slower.*

Prerequisite(s): [Mass Aid](/wiki/Mass_Aid "wikilink"),
[Nourish](/wiki/Nourish "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'purity'.
