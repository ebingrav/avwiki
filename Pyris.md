*Slain by [sprite](/wiki/Sprites "wikilink")
[wizards](/wiki/Category%3A_Wizards "wikilink") centuries ago, the spirit of
Pyris still haunts [Westwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood "wikilink"). It
prefers to take the form of an ancient red [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink"),
but is never completely solid. As in life, it has a taste for unicorn,
and will still slaughter any it encounters.*

**Keywords:** Pyris, draconic, phantasm.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 18.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink"), [wimpy](Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [fly](/wiki/Fly "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink"), [invis](Invis "wikilink").
