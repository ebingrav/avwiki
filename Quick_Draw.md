*All [archers](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") have to keep one hand
free to operate and reload their
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink").
[Fusiliers](/wiki/Category%3A_Fusiliers "wikilink") with the quick draw
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") can use this empty hand to
rapidly draw and throw a [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") from
their belts (inventory) between shots.*

Prerequisite(s): [Precise Shot](/wiki/Precise_Shot "wikilink").

Syntax: [throw](/wiki/Throw "wikilink")
