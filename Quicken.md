*QUICKEN is a toggle [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") which,
when turned on, decreases your [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
lag. You will be able to [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") more
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") per [round](Rounds "wikilink"),
but your [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") cost will also increase in
proportion to the speed at which you set quicken.*

Prerequisite(s): [Magic Lore](/wiki/Magic_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: quicken on.  
Syntax: quicken off.  
Syntax: quicken <speed:1-9>.  
See also [Augment](/wiki/Augment "wikilink") and [Surge](Surge "wikilink").
