*Quickstrike allows the [Rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") to use
the [vital shot](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") much more quickly and
efficiently, even in the middle of combat. When the
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") is executed mid-round, the
[Rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") will aim for a vital area. If not
interrupted, the [Rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink")'s next hit will
be a [vital shot](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink"), doing more damage than normal.
Since the element of surprise is not incorporated, this will do less
damage than [vital shot](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink") would if used to
initiate combat. Also, despite the [lethal aim](/wiki/Lethal_Aim "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), the
[Rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") cannot specify a vital region to
hit.*

Prerequisite(s): [Assassin Sight](/wiki/Assassin_Sight "wikilink").

Syntax: [vital](/wiki/Vital "wikilink")
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
