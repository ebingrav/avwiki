*To see the [racial abilities](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") intrinsic
to a specific [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink"), simply type racial
&lt;[race abbreviation](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink")&gt;. To use a
[racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") which is not automatic, type
racial &lt;[racialname](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink")&gt;.*

Syntax: racial &lt;[race-abbr](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink")&gt;.  
Syntax: racial &lt;[racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink")&gt;.  
Syntax: racial &lt;[racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink")&gt;
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.  
See also [Racials](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink").
