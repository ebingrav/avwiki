*The Curse of [Quixoltan](/wiki/Quixoltan "wikilink") causes the corpses of
[Draconians](/wiki/Draconians "wikilink") to decay almost immediately after
they [die](/wiki/Death "wikilink"). The corpses cannot be
[embalmed](/wiki/Embalm "wikilink") or preserved in any way by mortals.*

The corpse rots and the contents spill onto the ground on the next tic.
