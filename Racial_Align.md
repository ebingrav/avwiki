*Races with a strong connection to the elements can attempt to align
themselves to a specific element to gain access to additional abilities.
The available elements are fire, air, water and earth. You may not align
to more than one element at once.*

Syntax: racial align <element>

This racial is EXHAUSTED for a time when used.

The abilities granted by racial-align can be observed by typing the
racial of the appropriate elemental type. Align water and align earth
are most often used, though air is uniquely capable of disabling pesky
caster mobs.

Fire Elementals are capable of the following:
[burn](/wiki/Racial_Burn "wikilink") [fireaura](Racial_Fireaura "wikilink")
[firering](/wiki/Racial_Firering "wikilink")
[powerthrust](/wiki/Racial_Powerthrust "wikilink")

Water Elementals are capable of the following:
[swim](/wiki/Racial_Swim "wikilink") [liquify](Racial_Liquify "wikilink")
[airdome](/wiki/Racial_Airdome "wikilink") [sweep](Racial_Sweep "wikilink")
[aquatic](/wiki/Racial_Aquatic "wikilink")

Earth Elementals are capable of the following:
[armorstone](/wiki/Racial_Armorstone "wikilink")
[lithify](/wiki/Racial_Lithify "wikilink")
[shelter](/wiki/Racial_Shelter "wikilink")
[earthtremor](/wiki/Racial_Earthtremor "wikilink")

Air Elementals are capable of the following:
[dodge](/wiki/Racial_Dodge "wikilink")
[evaporate](/wiki/Racial_Evaporate "wikilink")
[suffocate](/wiki/Racial_Suffocate "wikilink")
