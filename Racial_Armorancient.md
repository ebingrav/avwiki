*These racials offer different levels of protection from damage.  
*These racials are automatic, they cannot be initiated by any commands.

Armorancient is better than [Racial
Armorstone](/wiki/Racial_Armorstone "wikilink") and worse than [Racial
Armorsteel](/wiki/Racial_Armorsteel "wikilink").

[Dragons](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") and [Golems](Golems "wikilink") possess
this level of racial armor.
