*A [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") prone to berserking has the
potential to become enraged when hurt, raising [damage
roll](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink") at the expense of [armor
class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"). Several different levels of rage are
possible, depending on how frequently the
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") is hurt. The
[calm](/wiki/Calm "wikilink") and [awe](Awe "wikilink")
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") can slow the effects of berserk,
and the [frenzy](/wiki/Frenzy "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") will exacerbate them.*

See also [Berserk](/wiki/Berserk "wikilink").
