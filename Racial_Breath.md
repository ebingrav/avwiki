*This is a breath attack that [Dragons](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") may use. It
can only be used once before having to rest. Typing 'racial breath' with
no target attacks a [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") you are already
in combat with.*

Syntax: racial breath &lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
