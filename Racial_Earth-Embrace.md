*A [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") with earth-embrace has lived
very long and has been blessed by the earth with an uncanny ability to
remember what they have learned, even when facing the harsh reality of
[death](/wiki/Death "wikilink"). While automatic, this earthen [racial
ability](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") treats
[death](/wiki/Death "wikilink") as if you had 1/10th the
[TNL](/wiki/TNL "wikilink"), thus reducing the experience penalty when
[dying](/wiki/Death "wikilink").*
