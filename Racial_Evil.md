*A [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") with this curse is innately
evil, and their [alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") will tend to edge in
that direction. It is possible for extremely well behaved evil
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") to achieve neutrality, and even
good [alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"), but it's going to take a lot
more fighting evil than it normally would.*
