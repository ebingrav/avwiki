*This is similar to the [fly](/wiki/Fly "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), but it can't be used on another.
The use of [racial skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") causes
fatigue, and a need for rest. You can shorten the need of rest by
landing earlier.*

Syntax: racial fly.
