*This [racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") puts the
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") into a frenzy, raising
its [hit](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink") and [damroll](Damage_Roll "wikilink") and
raising (worsening) the [character's](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"). Racial frenzy affects your
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"), i.e., it is not
targetable on others. The racial ability has a 'fatigue factor' that
prevents it being used right after it wears off.*

Syntax: racial frenzy.

See also [Frenzy](/wiki/Frenzy "wikilink").
