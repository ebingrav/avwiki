*This [racial ability](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") functions exactly
like the [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") for which it is named.
Like most [racials](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink"), it requires rest
between uses.*

Syntax: racial hide.

See also [Hide](/wiki/Hide "wikilink").
