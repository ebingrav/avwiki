*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is much like the
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink")
[Infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink"), which allows seeing heat sources
in the dark. It is \*not\* [night vision](/wiki/Nightvision "wikilink"), but
it is automatic.*

See also [Infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
