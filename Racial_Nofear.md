*[Races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") with the nofear
[racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") are not affected by fear
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), but also refuse to
[flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") once combat is already engaged.*
