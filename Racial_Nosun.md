*All [races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") have certain attributes, and
a number of the underground [races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") are
affected by NOSUN, which means they should not be outdoors on a sunny
day without expecting consequences. The sun will not damage the
underground dwellers but will prevent regeneration and will tell you
that you are smoking. You will not be harmed by the sun in Limbo or on
the Cloud or if you are [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 10 or lower.*
