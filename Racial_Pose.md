*This [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") is available to all
players and produces random actions based upon one's
[race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink"). The actions are drawn from a
library of potential actions and generated in multiple colors for your
entertainment.*

Syntax: racial pose.  
Syntax: rpose.  
See also [Rpose](/wiki/Rpose "wikilink").
