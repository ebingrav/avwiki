*Stealth is two-fold: A stealthy [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") is
less likely to be attacked by [aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink")
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") when a less-stealthy target also
presents itself. A stealthy [race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") will
also succeed more often with [rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink")
[skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") such as
[blackjack](/wiki/Blackjack "wikilink"), [backstab](Backstab "wikilink") and
[sneak attack](/wiki/Sneak_Attack "wikilink"). Some examples of stealthy
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") include [Drow](Drow "wikilink"),
[Halfling](/wiki/Halflings "wikilink") and [Demonseed](Demonseeds "wikilink").
Some examples of non-stealthy [races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink")
include [Giant](/wiki/Giants "wikilink"), [Troglodyte](Troglodytes "wikilink")
and [Troll](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink").*
