*This innate psychic ability allows [Tuataurs](/wiki/Tuataurs "wikilink") to
learn information about enemies (and friends) simply by
[looking](/wiki/Look "wikilink") at them. The information is similar to the
output of the [bioscan](/wiki/Bioscan "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").*
