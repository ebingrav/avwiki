*Certain creatures who have adapted to living in or near the sea are
intrinsically comfortable in [water](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink"). The
[racial ability](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") to swim grants a number
of bonuses to players while they are in the
[water](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink"); most notable is an increased
resistance to drowning.*
