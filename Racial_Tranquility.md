*This [racial ability](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") grants virtual
immunity to most forms of mental manipulation. Although still prone to
psionic damage, the user will be mostly unaffected by
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") such as
[overconfidence](/wiki/Overconfidence "wikilink") and
[frenzy](/wiki/Frenzy "wikilink").*
