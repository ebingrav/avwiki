*This [racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") is an instinct to
[flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") when hurt. This is not a guaranteed
[flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink"), but panic can set in once you get hurt enough
and even if your actual [wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy "wikilink") is set low, you may
still run away.*
