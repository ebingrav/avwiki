*This Rite reduces the odds of berserking from damage inflicted by any
source other than the [scar](/wiki/Scar "wikilink")
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"). This applies to both
[racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") and
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") abilities.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
