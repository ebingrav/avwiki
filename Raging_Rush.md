*Raging Rush increases the
[Berserker](:Category:_Berserkers "wikilink")'s
[hp](Hit_Points "wikilink") regeneration rate while under berserk
effects. This applies to both [racial](:Category:_Races "wikilink") and
[class](:Category:_Classes "wikilink") abilities. The power of Raging
Rush requires that the [berserker](:Category:_Berserkers "wikilink") be
awake and able to channel the energy of their berserk state. A
[Berserker](:Category:_Berserkers "wikilink") will not enjoy the
benefits of the Raging Rush while asleep. Raging Rush will not function
if the [Berserker](:Category:_Berserkers "wikilink") has better than
-750 [AC](Armor_Class "wikilink") at [hero](:Category:_Hero "wikilink"),
or -1250 [AC](Armor_Class "wikilink") at
[Lord](:Category:Lord "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
