*Raging Rush increases the
[Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink")'s
[hp](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") regeneration rate while under berserk
effects. This applies to both [racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") and
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") abilities. The power of Raging
Rush requires that the [berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") be
awake and able to channel the energy of their berserk state. A
[Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") will not enjoy the
benefits of the Raging Rush while asleep. Raging Rush will not function
if the [Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") has better than
-750 [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") at
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink"), or -1250
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") at [Lord](Lord "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
