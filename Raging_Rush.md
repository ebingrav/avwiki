*Raging Rush increases the
[Berserker](:Category:_Berserkers.md "wikilink")'s
[hp](Hit_Points.md "wikilink") regeneration rate while under berserk
effects. This applies to both [racial](:Category:_Races.md "wikilink")
and [class](:Category:_Classes.md "wikilink") abilities. The power of
Raging Rush requires that the
[berserker](:Category:_Berserkers.md "wikilink") be awake and able to
channel the energy of their berserk state. A
[Berserker](:Category:_Berserkers.md "wikilink") will not enjoy the
benefits of the Raging Rush while asleep. Raging Rush will not function
if the [Berserker](:Category:_Berserkers.md "wikilink") has better than
-750 [AC](Armor_Class.md "wikilink") at
[hero](:Category:_Hero.md "wikilink"), or -1250
[AC](Armor_Class.md "wikilink") at
[Lord](:Category:Lord.md "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
