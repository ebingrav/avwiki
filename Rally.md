*Having a [Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") on the front
lines can effectively rally the rest of the troops. When leading, a
[Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") can use this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") to grant his group-mates, and
himself, a boost to [hitroll](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Lead Party](/wiki/Lead_Party "wikilink").

Syntax: rally.
