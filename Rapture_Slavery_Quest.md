This quest confers the [Eragora - The Abolisher
Insignia](/wiki/Eragora_-_The_Abolisher_Insignia "wikilink") or [Eragora -
Slave Hunter Insignia](/wiki/Eragora_-_Slave_Hunter_Insignia "wikilink"), and
is located within [Rapture](/wiki/Category%3ARapture "wikilink") on
[Eragora](/wiki/Category%3AEragora "wikilink").

To start, talk to [Grek'gor](/wiki/Grek'gor "wikilink") and/or [Barol
Kleft](/wiki/Barol_Kleft "wikilink") and pick a side.

Rewards for this quest are gear or (lots of) money, depending on the
side chosen.
