*A mask, is barely the way to describe this work of art. Feathers come
from the skin base, arranged as a raven head. The beak is a light orange
and yellow wood stained with the blood. You are unsure of how this
beautiful mask is to stay, but with the strangeness of everything else,
you know it will stay on.*
