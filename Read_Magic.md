*The symbols, glyphs, runes... call them what you will, of magic are
hard to understand by the average adventurer on the run. In fact,
they're impossible to understand unless you know how to read it. Once
you learn how to read magic, the biggest benefit is that you will then
be able to [recite](/wiki/Recite "wikilink")
[scrolls](/wiki/Category%3A_Scrolls "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Magic Lore](/wiki/Magic_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).

Spellcasters and even non-spellcasters may find rare occasion to use
this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), but they will
probably find most other
[skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") far more useful to
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") than this one.
