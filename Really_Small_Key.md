*You hold the [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") on your hand, and
marvel at how such a small item could survive being carried on [such a
huge guard](/wiki/Sentinel "wikilink").*

**Keyword(s):** key, tiny.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 39-41.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks a door between Sentinel
Watch and Entrance to the Cavern.
