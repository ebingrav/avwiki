*CLASS REBUILD: With this [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"),
certain [classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") can change their
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") to another. They are effectively
rebuilt from the ground up and will have to re-[train](/wiki/Train "wikilink")
and re-[practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink")
[skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") as a "new"
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). Those
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") which qualify due to being
[grandfathered](/wiki/Category%3A_Grandfathered_Classes "wikilink") can
rebuild into an equivalent [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink").*

*RACE EVOLUTION: With this [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"),
certain [races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") can evolve from one
[race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") into another. They maintain their
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") and are remade into their new image. Like
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), this will require
[retraining](/wiki/Train "wikilink") and [repracticing](/wiki/Practice "wikilink").
This is a serious decision and those who use the rebuild
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") cannot go back to their old
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"). Rebuilds are final.*

Syntax: rebuild <password>
