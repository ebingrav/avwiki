recipe. It reads as follows:*  
*"To make an [Invitation to
Imagination](/wiki/Invitation_To_Imagination "wikilink"), combine [the skin of
one chameleon](/wiki/Skin_Of_A_Chameleon "wikilink"), [a vial of
quicksilver](/wiki/Vial_Of_Quicksilver "wikilink"), and [some grain
contaminated by ergot](/wiki/Ergot_Contaminated_Rye "wikilink")."*
