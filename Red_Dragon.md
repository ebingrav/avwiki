*The red [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") stares at you angrily, as it wakes
when you arrive. It says 'Oh, [human](/wiki/Humans "wikilink"), YOU woke me
up... Hsss...' while it moves closer. 'Can one NEVER sleep in peace...
Hsss...*'

**Keywords:** red, dragon.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 20.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[fire-breather](/wiki/Breathing_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").

Comments
--------

Be very careful when [teleporting](/wiki/Teleport "wikilink") to this
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), as there are other portable red
dragons in Midgaardia that are *much* bigger and nastier than this one;
it is much safer to walk to this [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") from
[Diana](/wiki/Marshall_Diana "wikilink") (see Directions below).

Its [sharpened bone](/wiki/Sharpened_Bone "wikilink") is a
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") that unlocks a gnawed body beside it.
This body contains some [gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink") (some of
which is good, though very heavy, [statistic
gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistic_Gear "wikilink")), namely: [a pair of
silvery boots](/wiki/Pair_Of_Silvery_Boots "wikilink"), [a pair of silvery
gloves](/wiki/Pair_Of_Silvery_Gloves "wikilink"), [the shield of the
rose](/wiki/Shield_Of_The_Rose "wikilink"), [a silvery breast
plate](/wiki/Silvery_Breast_Plate "wikilink"), [a silvery
helmet](/wiki/Silvery_Helmet "wikilink"), [some silvery arm
plates](/wiki/Some_Silvery_Arm_Plates "wikilink"), and [some silvery leg
plates](/wiki/Some_Silvery_Leg_Plates "wikilink").

If you find replacements to any of the red dragon/silvery gear, please
note them on the piece of gear that was replaced, and on the appropriate
"suggested gear" lists
