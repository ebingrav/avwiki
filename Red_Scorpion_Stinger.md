*A red scorpion's stinger is here, dripping with poison.*

`Carries roughly 60 `[`doses`](/wiki/Poison_Values "wikilink")` of `[`biotoxin`](Category%3A_Biotoxin "wikilink")`, a strong `[`poison`](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink")` which which lowers overall health.`  
`This should be used on no less than a level 35 weapon.`

**Keyword(s):** red scorpion stinger.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 42.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 3 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 2 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

Not all giant scorpions in the area have the stinger.
