*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") refreshes the [movement
points](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink") of a
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'refresh'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rather useful for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink").

See also [Refresh Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Refresh_Gear "wikilink").
