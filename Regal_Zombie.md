this corpse rose as a zombie under the influence of the red stone. Even
in undeath, the zombie appears regal.*

[undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
