*The Regeneration [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") will speed
hit-point healing at the cost of mana regeneration. Mana regen is cut to
1/3, and [hp](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") regen is doubled. REGENERATION is a
[BIOCENTRIC Psionic
Spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Biocentric_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). It can only
be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on others if the Caster first masters the art
of [BIO EMPATHY](/wiki/Bio_Empathy "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'regeneration'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
