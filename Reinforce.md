*[Psions](:Category:_Psionicists.md "wikilink") can train their minds to
reinforce an [item](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink")'s structure,
increasing the amount of damage it can normally take without being
destroyed. This can only be done once to an
[item](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink"). Beware a miscalculation, for
the [item](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink") may actually get weaker or
destroyed through too much or too little mental energy. In any case, an
increase in [level](Object_Level.md "wikilink") of 1 or 2 will occur.
NOTE: [containers](:Category:_Containers.md "wikilink") destroyed also
destroy all contents.*

Prerequisite(s): [Transmute](Transmute.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast.md "wikilink") 'reinforce'
\<[object](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink")\>.

See also [Reinforced Flag](Reinforced_Flag.md "wikilink").

This will increase the items hit points by 25%.
