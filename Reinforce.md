*[Psions](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") can train their minds to
reinforce an [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")'s structure,
increasing the amount of damage it can normally take without being
destroyed. This can only be done once to an
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). Beware a miscalculation, for the
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") may actually get weaker or
destroyed through too much or too little mental energy. In any case, an
increase in [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") of 1 or 2 will occur. NOTE:
[containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") destroyed also destroy
all contents.*

Prerequisite(s): [Transmute](/wiki/Transmute "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'reinforce'
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.

See also [Reinforced Flag](/wiki/Reinforced_Flag "wikilink").
