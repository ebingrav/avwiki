An [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") with this
[flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") will have higher-than-normal
[hit points](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"). It can be added via the
[Reinforce](/wiki/Reinforce "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").

See also [Reinforce](/wiki/Reinforce "wikilink").
