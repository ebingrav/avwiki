*The relentless attacker breaks down any defense. While in relentless
stance, your current target cannot be [rescued](Rescue.md "wikilink")
from you unless the rescuer is using [protective
stance](Protective_Stance_.md "wikilink"). This
[stance](Stance.md "wikilink") ends early if you are
[rescued](Rescue.md "wikilink"), if you [flee](Flee.md "wikilink"), or
if you are the target of combat-ending
[spells](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink") such as
[calm](Calm.md "wikilink") or [innocence](Innocence.md "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Rescue](Rescue.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](Stance.md "wikilink") relentless.

See also [Protective Stance](Protective_Stance.md "wikilink") and
[Surefoot Stance](Surefoot_Stance.md "wikilink").
