*The relentless attacker breaks down any defense. While in relentless
stance, your current target cannot be [rescued](/wiki/Rescue "wikilink") from
you unless the rescuer is using [protective
stance](/wiki/Protective_Stance "wikilink"). This [stance](/wiki/Stance "wikilink")
ends early if you are [rescued](/wiki/Rescue "wikilink"), if you
[flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink"), or if you are the target of combat-ending
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") such as [calm](Calm "wikilink")
or [innocence](/wiki/Innocence "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Rescue](/wiki/Rescue "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](/wiki/Stance "wikilink") relentless.

See also [Protective Stance](/wiki/Protective_Stance "wikilink") and [Surefoot
Stance](/wiki/Surefoot_Stance "wikilink").
