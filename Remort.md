*The remort process is how some of AVATAR's experimental or powerful
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") and
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") are accessed. The user will be
reset to a [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 2
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of the new
[race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink"), and their current
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), or a [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink")
2 [character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of the new
[class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), and either
[human](/wiki/Humans "wikilink") or their old
[race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink"), depending on how high
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") they were when they remorted. The purpose of
this is to give people a chance to try something different. After you
remort to another [class](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"), the remort
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") requirement goes up by 100
[Lord](/wiki/Lord "wikilink") [levels](Sublevel "wikilink"). Be sure to read
the appropriate helps and make absolutely certain you want to remort
before using the [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"). It is
irreversible.*

Syntax: remort <password>
&lt;[race-abbr](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink")&gt;.
