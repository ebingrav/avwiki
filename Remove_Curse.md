*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") removes a
[curse](/wiki/Curse "wikilink") from a
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Curse](/wiki/Curse "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'remove curse'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rather valuable for all
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").
