*Repairing an [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") damaged in battle
yourself is a convenient way to save the cost of paying a
[smith](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink"). There is a drawback to repairing
something yourself, rather than having an expert do it, however. Being
an amateur, every time you repair something, you create small flaws that
weaken the overall strength of that
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). Certain
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") who have a natural affinity for or
with metals and other materials are able to repair an object without the
risk of damaging it. NOTE: There are
[shops](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink") that will do the same thing, but
for a price.*

Prerequisite(s): [Armor Lore](/wiki/Armor_Lore "wikilink"), [Weapon
Lore](/wiki/Weapon_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: repair &lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.
