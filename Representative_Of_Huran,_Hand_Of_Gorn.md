*In an unprecedented move, [Huran](/wiki/Huran,_A_Hand_Of_Gorn "wikilink") has
sent one of his [monks](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") to visit [this
temple honoring Shizaga](/wiki/Category%3A_Shizaga's_Temple "wikilink").
Though the visitor is tight-lipped about why he has been sent, he has
been quick to point out that no tricks of [Shizaga](/wiki/Shizaga "wikilink")
will get any information out of him regarding [the monk's own mysterious
temple](/wiki/Category%3A_Temple_Of_Gorn "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** representative, Huran, Gorn.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 36.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
