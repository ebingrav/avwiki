*The Broo Leader wears a mish-mash of armor, seemingly held together
with dirt, sticks, and other things too disgusting to mention. This
combination of man and goat forces the other Broos to attack with a
series of grunts, clicks, and brute force.*

**Keywords:** Broo, Leader.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 14.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Thieving_Mobs "wikilink"),
[poisonous](/wiki/Biting_Mobs "wikilink"), [wimpy](Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").

Comments
--------

Note that the [broo shaman](/wiki/Diseased_Broo_Shaman "wikilink") sacrifices
corpses. If you're after the [leader's
gear](/wiki/Tattered_Kite_Shield "wikilink") you should kill any shamans in
the room first. Additionally, other Broos tend to grab corpses, which
can also be inconvenient.
