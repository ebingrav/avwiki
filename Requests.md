General Requests
----------------

**Request:** An area of the set up specifically for general lowmort
help: i.e., good places to level, tips on soloing various classes, and
other useful things that are hard to learn without someone pointing out
(i.e., monk's toss skill makes the mob retarget to the highest person on
the group's order, even if the battle has already started).

*Update:* Category pages have just been added for all AVATAR
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"). I think that these pages
would be good places to post tips on how to play each class most
effectively. To help get things started and to provide some preliminary
ideas for both format and content on these new class pages, I did a
little preliminary work on [Rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink"),
[BCIs](/wiki/Category%3A_Black_Circle_Initiates "wikilink"), and
[Assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink"). The categories [Lowmort
Levels 1-10](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_Levels_1-10 "wikilink") through
[Hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") would probably be great places to
put information about getting through these levels/tiers most
effectively. The categories [Lowmort 1-10
Areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_1-10_Areas "wikilink") through [Hero
Areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero_Areas "wikilink") would be great to post
information about recommended areas to gain experience and such. Again,
to help get things started, I added some suggestions on areas at these
levels for others to revise and expand upon.

*Update:* Hurrah! [Clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") and
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") now have plenty of information
posted about how to play their
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") most effectively from
[levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 1 to 51(999). I hope to see these pages
further revised, expanded, and corrected (as needed), and generally made
increasingly useful over time. Perhaps they may also serve as examples
for users interested in contributing similar information about other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"). Also, every [skill and
spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") from
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 1 to 51(999) now has its own page, though many
of these are still blank for now. A lot of structure has been put into
place but much work still remains to be done in fitting information into
it. Warnings about [tossing](/wiki/Toss "wikilink"), for example, might be
handy to post on [Monks](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") under Tanking, on
[Tanks](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink") (since other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") may use
[Toss](/wiki/Toss "wikilink") while [tanking](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink"), as well),
and/or on [Toss](/wiki/Toss "wikilink").

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Request:** We need more work done on
[areas](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"). Each area should have, as a bare
minimum, a portal point and walking directions. Extra information about
experience or tips on how to run there would be welcome as well. Take a
look at the areas in [Incomplete](/wiki/Category%3A_Incomplete "wikilink") if
you would like to help.

*Update:* Every [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") in Avatar MUD from
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 1 to 51 now has its own category, except for
most of those created for 2005's
[lowmort](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort "wikilink") [area-building
contest](/wiki/Area-Building_Contests "wikilink"). There is still much work to
be done, however.

*Update:* Every normal [area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") listed here
now has walking directions from at least one [recall](/wiki/Recall "wikilink")
point, as well as keywords for at least one [portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink")
point within it where possible (or, in some cases, walking directions
from a nearby [portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point instead). Much work
still remains to be done on other sections, though.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Request:** It'd be nice if there were a little navigation 'menu'
(perhaps in the top-right corner ala wikipedia?) of the specific level
hit/tank/mana gear pages that would allow you to move vertically
(up/down in level) instead of just horizontally (11-20 managear to 11-20
hitgear). i.e. move directly from [Lowmort 11-20 Hit
Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_11-20_Hit_Gear "wikilink") to [Lowmort 31-40
Hit Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Lowmort_31-40_Hit_Gear "wikilink").

*Update:* [Waite](:User:_Waite "wikilink") made a wonderful start on
this today, it seems.

*Update:* We now have (I hope) some pretty nice gear link menus.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Request:** Since we've got all of these great maps, we could start
adding a link to them anytime we link to an area. Like for an item if we
have a line like:  
Area: [Sarphyre's Court](/wiki/Category%3ASarphyre's_Court "wikilink")  
Then we may as well make it:  
Area: [Sarphyre's Court](/wiki/Category%3ASarphyre's_Court "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Sarphyre's_Court_Map "wikilink"))  
Seeing as there are tons of items that need changing, it'll probably
take a group effort, so I figured I'd mention it here.

*Update:* Most, if not all, area links now have a convenient (Map) link
beside them.
