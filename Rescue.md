*RESCUE is a [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") which, when
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"), allows you to intervene in a battle by
redirecting any opponents' attacks from the target
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") onto yourself. This can
be used to help a badly hurt group member who is being attacked, but be
careful, since you'll end up taking all the subsequent damage yourself.
Typing RESCUE with no target will rescue your current
[monitor](/wiki/Monitor "wikilink") target. Each time you must refocus your
attention to rescue a friend, your momentary loss of concentration has
an unpleasant affect upon your [armor class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"). Be
warned, you would-be heroes. These penalties add up quickly.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: rescue &lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
