[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") prevent
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of certain
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") and/or
[alignments](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") from entering them.
