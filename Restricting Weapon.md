*A restricting weapon saps an enemy's mental strength, leaving them
tired and lethargic. This slows their movements in combat, reducing the
likelihood that they will land multiple blows per combat round.*

Prerequisite(s): [Disabling Weapon](Disabling_Weapon "wikilink")

Syntax: [cast](cast "wikilink") 'restricting weapon' (self only)
