*In the process of restringing a [bow](/wiki/Category%3A_Bows "wikilink"), an
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") tweaks it to suit his personal
shooting style. As [bow](/wiki/Category%3A_Bows "wikilink") tightness
increases, the minimum damage of the bow will go up, as will the
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") required to use the bow, or DRAW
STRENGTH. Crossbows can be restrung, but the results are generally not
as dramatic as a restringing a bow. There is a chance of permanently
ruining a [bow item](/wiki/Category%3A_Bows "wikilink") with this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Archery Lore](/wiki/Archery_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: restring &lt;[bow/crossbow](/wiki/Category%3A_Bows "wikilink")&gt;
&lt;tighter/looser&gt;.
