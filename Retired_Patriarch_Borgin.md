*Patriarch Borgin was head of the order of Durr for over sixty years
before*  
*the advancing years took their toll. Now he spends his days tending
his*  
*fine garden, conversing with friends and parishioners and writing
his*  
*memoirs.*  
Borgin is one of the higher level mobs in [Mt
Durr](/wiki/Category%3AMt_Durr "wikilink") and wears no equipment. As such,
the only real value in fighting him is for the XP, which is pretty good.
He does cast spells from time to time, so you can sometimes get a
surprise out of him or he can hit a group member at the back.

His main value comes from his position as a handy portal point for those
seeking access to the [Duergar
Stronghold](/wiki/Category%3ADuergar_Stronghold "wikilink") or any of the
areas which lie beyond it.
