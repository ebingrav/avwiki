*After a successful [parry](/wiki/Parry "wikilink"), a master of riposte will
automatically attempt a counter attack. Although other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") can learn this later, it will
work less well for them based on how far removed they are from
[warriors](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink"). IE- fairly well for
[rangers](/wiki/Category%3A_Rangers "wikilink"), worse for
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") and god-awful for
[monks](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") who aren't used to weapon-based
[skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Parry](/wiki/Parry "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
