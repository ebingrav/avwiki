*Robarn has been a Longbeard for three years now and has become a decent
officer of the peace in that time. Initially
[Jeremiah](/wiki/Jeremiah_Longbeard "wikilink") was reluctant to employ him as
he tended to be lazy as a youth, but he's matured into a fine young
[dwarf](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Robarn, Longbeard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 21.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
