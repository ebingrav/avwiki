*These fine linen robes were custom made for [the High Priest of
Werredan](/wiki/High_Priest_Of_Werredan "wikilink"). The weave is so fine, it
almost feels like silk. They are made of purest black, with divine
symbols embroidered black on black with the occasional bit of darkest
gray.*

`Modifies `[`mana`](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink")` by 10 continuous.`
