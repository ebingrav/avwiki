*The rockworm is a disgusting creature. Thick folds of tough flesh
encircle the creature, letting it bore its way through even the most
solid of rock. Thin flat spots near the head apparently double for eyes.
The creature writhes around pitifully.*

**Keywords:** rockworm.  
**[Level](Level "wikilink"):** 24.  
**[Alignment](Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").
