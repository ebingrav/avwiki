very upset. In his hands he carries what appears to be [a coil of
blackened wire](Coiled_Loop_Of_Wire "wikilink"). You blink, and then
reach for your throat as he loops [the
wire](Coiled_Loop_Of_Wire "wikilink") over your head!*
