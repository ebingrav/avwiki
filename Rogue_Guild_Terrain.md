Curiously, this is an actual [terrain
type](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink").

Players must [spend](/wiki/Movement_Costs "wikilink") six [move
points](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink") to enter (non-magically) a
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") with this [terrain
type](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink").

[Citizens](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink") will not enter
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") with this [terrain
type](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink").

[Rogue guild guards](/wiki/Guild_Guard_Mobs "wikilink") will operate in
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") with this [terrain
type](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink").

See also [Dump Rooms](/wiki/Dump_Rooms "wikilink").
