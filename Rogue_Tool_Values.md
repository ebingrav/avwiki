[Rogue-tool](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have three
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **[Usage Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink"):** This value
    determines which function(s) this [rogue
    tool](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink") may perform, which may be
    to [blackjack mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Blackjacks "wikilink"), to [disarm
    traps](/wiki/Category%3A_Trap-Disarming_Kits "wikilink"), to [pick
    chests](/wiki/Category%3A_Chest_Picks "wikilink"), to [pick
    doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Door_Picks "wikilink"), and/or to [poison
    weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisoning_Kits "wikilink"). Most [rogue
    tools](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink") have only one function.
    Follow these links for more information about each [rogue-tool
    type](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **Uses Number:** This value determines how many times this [rogue
    tool](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink") may yet be used before
    being consumed.

<!-- -->

-   **Quality:** This value (which is different from [Object
    Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink")) determines how good this [rogue
    tool](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink") is as a percentage. When
    [examined](/wiki/Examine "wikilink"), a [rogue
    tool](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink")'s quality value will be
    expressed as an adjective according to the table shown at right.

See also [Rogue Tools](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink").
