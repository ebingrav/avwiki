*Dressed totally in black, this [halfling](/wiki/Halflings "wikilink") seems
quite upset that you have found his ambush spot. Looking at him
carefully, you notice that his belt is loose. "So that's what I heard",
you muse. He bares his teeth, the white looking out of place, and
prepares to sheath [his blade](/wiki/Serrated_Dagger "wikilink") in your
breast.*

**Keywords:** rogue, assassin.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 35.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [hide](Hide "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
