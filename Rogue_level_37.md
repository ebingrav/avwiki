*Dressed totally in black, this [halfling](/wiki/Halflings "wikilink") looks
very upset. In his hands he carries what appears to be [a coil of
blackened wire](/wiki/Coiled_Loop_Of_Wire "wikilink"). You blink, and then
reach for your throat as he loops [the
wire](/wiki/Coiled_Loop_Of_Wire "wikilink") over your head!*

**Keywords:** rogue, assassin.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 37.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[assassin](/wiki/Assassin_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [hide](Hide "wikilink").
