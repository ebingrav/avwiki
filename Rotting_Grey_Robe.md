## Comments

Apart from the penalty to strength, this robe also gives permenant
plague. As its mana and AC are identical to both the [Pitch-Black
Robe](Pitch-Black_Robe "wikilink") and the [Red Velvet
Cape](Red_Velvet_Cape "wikilink"), it is much better to go for one of
those - not to mention a lot easier to get either of them.
