Different weapons accept the same [Runes](/wiki/Rune "wikilink") differently.
Even two different copies of the same item might react to
[Runes](/wiki/Rune "wikilink") differently.

However, any weapon that accepts [Runes](/wiki/Rune "wikilink") will accept
them in one of ten ways. Which set of runes any weapon accepts is
randomly determined when the item pops. So, to determine what
[Runes](/wiki/Rune "wikilink") will have a desired affect on any given weapon,
first delve the item a couple times to see which set of runes you're
dealing with. Once you figure out which one of the ten sets of
[Runes](/wiki/Rune "wikilink") this weapon is accepting, you can safely
[Rune](/wiki/Rune "wikilink") the item without fear of accidentally applying
the wrong [Rune](/wiki/Rune "wikilink").
