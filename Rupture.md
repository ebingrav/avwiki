*Rupture does severe mental damage to the target. If your target is
attacking YOU, you do extra damage but you take a penalty to your
[INT](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink") for a short period of time. If your
target is non-[intelligent](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink"), you do reduced
damage.*

Prerequisite(s): [Ultrablast](/wiki/Ultrablast "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'rupture'
