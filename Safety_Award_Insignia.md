This insignia is gained by completing [Safety Award
Quest](/wiki/Safety_Award_Quest "wikilink") in [Exploration
Outpost](/wiki/Category%3AExploration_Outpost "wikilink") on
[Eragora](/wiki/Category%3AEragora "wikilink").

This insignia grants a boost of +5% to all XP gained, and lasts one
week.
