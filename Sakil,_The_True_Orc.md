*Wearing stylish clothes that don't fit, the [orc](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink") has
a slightly different look to him than the rest of
[Orkenhome](/wiki/Category%3A_Orkenhome "wikilink")'s residents. The eyes set
deep into the twisten [Orken](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink") face don't seem to show
the same [intelligence](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink") and compassion that the
rest of the citizens posses. That's because Sakil is a true
[orc](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink") who has found the perfect place to blend in and
commit all manner of atrocities.*

**Keywords:** Sakil, true, orc.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 32.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[assassin](/wiki/Assassin_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink").
