Comments
--------

Saliri is involved in [Caves of Ancient
Evil](/wiki/Category%3A_Caves_Of_Ancient_Evil "wikilink")'s [Seven-League
Boots Quest](/wiki/Seven-League_Boots_Quest "wikilink").

[A huge iron key](/wiki/Huge_Iron_Key "wikilink") from [An iron
gargoyle](/wiki/Iron_Gargoyle "wikilink") opens the
[door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") to Saliri's
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").

East of Saliri behind a closed door is a room where some gold/gemstones
pop on the floor and the [Silk Lined Cloak](/wiki/Silk_Lined_Cloak "wikilink")
pop.
