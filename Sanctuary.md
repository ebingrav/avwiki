*The Sanctuary [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") reduces the damage
taken by the [character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") from any
attack by one half. Can be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on any
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). Sanctuary can be used in
conjunction with either [Protection Evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink") or
[Protection Good](/wiki/Protection_Good "wikilink") or both.*

Prerequisite(s): [Protection Evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"),
[Protection Good](/wiki/Protection_Good "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'sanctuary'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in
[Awen](/wiki/Awen "wikilink") but *not* in [Aegis](Aegis "wikilink"). Like
[Frenzy](/wiki/Frenzy "wikilink"), Sanctuary has a relatively short duration
compared with other [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink").

See also [Gear That Applies
Sanctuary](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_Sanctuary "wikilink") and
[Sanctuary Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Sanctuary_Gear "wikilink").
