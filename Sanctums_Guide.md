*A spectral fellow in full black tie, the maitre d' welcomes you to the
respite that is [Sanctum](/wiki/Category%3A_Sanctum "wikilink"). You can walk
around or [purchase](/wiki/Buy "wikilink") a brochure. Lastly, he asks you to
wipe your feet.*

**Keywords:** Sanctum, Guide.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** (?).  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision "wikilink"),
[protection evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
