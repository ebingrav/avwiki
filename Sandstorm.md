*Sandstorm creates a sweeping gust of sand which pelts your
[opponents](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") into oblivion.
[Opponents](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") are also blinded. This is an
area spell.*

Prerequisite(s): [Control Weather](/wiki/Control_Weather "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'sandstorm'.

Some [hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") [tanks](Tanks "wikilink") may
find this [ranger](/wiki/Category%3A_Rangers "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") useful for initiating combat with
multiple [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") simultaneously.

See also [Sandstorm Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Sandstorm_Gear "wikilink").
