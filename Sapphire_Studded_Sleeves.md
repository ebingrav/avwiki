## Comments

To obtain this item, you need to know either [Pick
Lock](Pick_Lock "wikilink"), or have the [Piece Of Grape
Candy](Piece_Of_Grape_Candy "wikilink"). The boots are in the yellow
bear.

This is an easily fodderable item for low level heroes and is not so
difficult to enchant. Enchanting it three times with a brill in the mix
(taking it -15ac bonus) makes it equal to an [Aura Of Elemental
Strength](Aura_Of_Elemental_Strength "wikilink"), and better for those
looking for some extra mana while in tank gear - making it a really nice
piece for solo casters.
