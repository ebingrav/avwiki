*This golden ring displays an intricate insignia of [Grande Baron Amier
Sarphyre](/wiki/Baron_Amier_Sarphyre "wikilink"), self-appointed ruler of
Midgaardia's [sprites](/wiki/Sprites "wikilink"), and identifies its wearer as
one of his agents. It seems to be rather valuable and you sense that it
has been blessed by [spritely](/wiki/Sprites "wikilink") magic.*

`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`hp`](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`mana`](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink")` by 10 continuous.`
