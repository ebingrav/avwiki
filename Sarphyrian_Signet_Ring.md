*This golden ring displays an intricate insignia of [Grande Baron Amier
Sarphyre](Baron_Amier_Sarphyre.md "wikilink"), self-appointed ruler of
Midgaardia's [sprites](Sprites.md "wikilink"), and identifies its wearer
as one of his agents. It seems to be rather valuable and you sense that
it has been blessed by [spritely](Sprites.md "wikilink") magic.*

`Modifies `[`hp`](Hit_Points.md "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`
