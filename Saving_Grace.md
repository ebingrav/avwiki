*With a prayer for help, the especially sensitive individual can receive
a divine gift of clarity that grants a deeper understanding of the
battlefield. With this gift, it becomes possible to change one's
[monitor](/wiki/Monitor "wikilink") focus. However, there is a price; by
paying more attention to others' well-being, the caster loses some
defensive capability.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'saving grace'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rather useful for
[hero](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink")
[cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") [healers](Healers "wikilink").
