*This [sagely](/wiki/Category%3A_Sages "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") increases the
[intelligence](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink") of the [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
for a duration.*

Prerequisite(s): [Lore](/wiki/Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'savvy'.

This is a somewhat useful
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") for all
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") (who can) to
[learn](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"). It increases its
[caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")'s [intelligence](Intelligence "wikilink") by
4, though not more than 3 above its [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")'s maximum
[trainable](/wiki/Train "wikilink") [intelligence](Intelligence "wikilink").
