*A scatha is a type of sword originally made at a time when points were
difficult to create. The scatha is fairly basic, simply a double-edged
sword with a wide, razor sharp tip.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 7-10 to 22-26 (average 14-18).`  
`Modifies `[`armor`
`class`](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")` by -5 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 2 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** Scatha, sword.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 21-23.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <wielded>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 50 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 8 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):**
[magic](/wiki/Magic_Flag "wikilink"), [nodrop](NoDrop_Flag "wikilink").
