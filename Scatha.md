*A scatha is a type of sword originally made at a time when points were
difficult to create. The scatha is fairly basic, simply a double-edged
sword with a wide, razor sharp tip.*

[`Damage`](Melee_Weapon_Values.md "wikilink")` is 7-10 to 22-26 (average 14-18).`  
`Modifies `[`armor`` ``class`](Armor_Class.md "wikilink")` by -5 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage`` ``roll`](Damage_Roll.md "wikilink")` by 2 continuous.`
