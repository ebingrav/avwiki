*Upon executing this [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"), the
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink")'s next shot will be a loose
flurry of scarcely-aimed [arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink"). The
more in the [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") at the time, the
greater the odds these [arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink") will find
useful targets. Due to the looseness with which they are fired, each
individual arrow will do less damage than it would normally.
[Archers](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") can specify the number of
[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink") they wish to fire, up to a limit
determined by [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") and
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") percentage. If the
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") is in direct combat,
scattershot will only hit the direct opponent and the maximum number of
[arrows](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink") will be reduced.*

Prerequisite(s): [Longshot](/wiki/Longshot "wikilink").

Syntax: scattershot <number>.
