*The sceptre consists of a metal shaft with a round globe atop it.
Within the globe beats the heart of
[Sycoltan](/wiki/Mutate,_Sycoltan "wikilink"), the evil
[drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink") [sorcerer](Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink").*

`It has an unknown `[`duration`](/wiki/Light_Values "wikilink")`.`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 4 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 4 continuous.`
