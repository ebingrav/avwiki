*[Dwarven](Dwarves "wikilink") children can be a handful, marauding
about the classroom brandishing [toy axes](Toy_Axe "wikilink") and the
like. Enter Schoolmistress Krila. No one is sure of her age, though
everyone in [Mt Durr](:Category:_Mt_Durr "wikilink") remembers being
taught by her, even parents and grandparents. With a quick stare she can
reduce the common room at [Bomli](Bomli "wikilink")'s to silence. This
is not a lady to mess with, but for all that, the children seem
genuinely fond of her.*

**Keywords:** Krila, Schoolmistress, teacher, stern.  
**[Level](Level "wikilink"):** 21.  
**[Alignment](Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
