*[Dwarven](Dwarves.md "wikilink") children can be a handful, marauding
about the classroom brandishing [toy axes](Toy_Axe.md "wikilink") and
the like. Enter Schoolmistress Krila. No one is sure of her age, though
everyone in [Mt Durr](:Category:_Mt_Durr.md "wikilink") remembers being
taught by her, even parents and grandparents. With a quick stare she can
reduce the common room at [Bomli](Bomli.md "wikilink")'s to silence.
This is not a lady to mess with, but for all that, the children seem
genuinely fond of her.*

**Keywords:** Krila, Schoolmistress, teacher, stern.  
**[Level](Level.md "wikilink"):** 21.  
**[Alignment](Alignment.md "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs.md "wikilink").
