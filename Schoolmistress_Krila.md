*[Dwarven](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") children can be a handful, marauding
about the classroom brandishing [toy axes](/wiki/Toy_Axe "wikilink") and the
like. Enter Schoolmistress Krila. No one is sure of her age, though
everyone in [Mt Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink") remembers being
taught by her, even parents and grandparents. With a quick stare she can
reduce the common room at [Bomli](/wiki/Bomli "wikilink")'s to silence. This
is not a lady to mess with, but for all that, the children seem
genuinely fond of her.*

**Keywords:** Krila, Schoolmistress, teacher, stern.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 21.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
