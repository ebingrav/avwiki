*Score shows you detailed information about your
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). This is the screen that
monitors your progress in the game, and you should refer to it
frequently. As you gain [levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink"), you get more
information.*

*At [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 10, you get a numeric value for your
[alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink") (-1000 to 1000).*

*At [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 15, you can see your
[hitroll](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink") and [damroll](Damage_Roll "wikilink").*

*At [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 25, you can see a numeric value for your
[AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") (lower is better.)*
