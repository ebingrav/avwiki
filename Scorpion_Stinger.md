A scorpion's stinger is here, dripping with poison. This item seems to
be poison. It weighs approximately 2 lbs. This item cannot be worn. It
carries roughly 79 doses of toxin, a moderate poison which has a slowing
affect. This should be used on no less than a level 20 weapon.
