*Using this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), an
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") essentially
[scans](/wiki/Scan "wikilink") two [rooms](Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") away
in a particular direction.*

Prerequisite(s): [Map Lore](/wiki/Map_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: scout <direction>.
