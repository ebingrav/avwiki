*Scramble disrupts the higher mental functions of a target by scrambling
their concentration. It causes no damage, and actually very little pain,
but it renders its victims almost incapable of
[casting](Cast.md "wikilink") magical
[spells](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink"), and also has a negative
affect on [intelligence](Intelligence.md "wikilink"). NOTE: Scramble
will not keep [monsters](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink") from using
[innate physical abilities](:Category:_Racials.md "wikilink"), such as
fire [breath](Breathing_Mobs.md "wikilink") or
[poison](Biting_Mobs.md "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Magic Lore](Magic_Lore.md "wikilink"),
[PsiSting](PsiSting.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast.md "wikilink") 'scramble'
\<[mob](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink")\>.
