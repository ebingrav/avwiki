*Scramble disrupts the higher mental functions of a target by scrambling
their concentration. It causes no damage, and actually very little pain,
but it renders its victims almost incapable of
[casting](Cast "wikilink") magical
[spells](:Category:_Spells "wikilink"), and also has a negative affect
on [intelligence](Intelligence "wikilink"). NOTE: Scramble will not keep
[monsters](:Category:_Mobs "wikilink") from using [innate physical
abilities](:Category:_Racials "wikilink"), such as fire
[breath](Breathing_Mobs "wikilink") or
[poison](Biting_Mobs "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Magic Lore](Magic_Lore "wikilink"),
[PsiSting](PsiSting "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'scramble'
\<[mob](:Category:_Mobs "wikilink")\>.
