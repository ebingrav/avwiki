*Scramble disrupts the higher mental functions of a target by scrambling
their concentration. It causes no damage, and actually very little pain,
but it renders its victims almost incapable of
[casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") magical
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), and also has a negative affect
on [intelligence](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink"). NOTE: Scramble will not keep
[monsters](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") from using [innate physical
abilities](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink"), such as fire
[breath](/wiki/Breathing_Mobs "wikilink") or
[poison](/wiki/Biting_Mobs "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Magic Lore](/wiki/Magic_Lore "wikilink"),
[PsiSting](/wiki/PsiSting "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'scramble'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
