*Scry allows you peek into a [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink")
without actually going into it. You can peer in on either
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") or
[players](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Locate Object](/wiki/Locate_Object "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'scry'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")/[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is rather
valuable, especially as it allows
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") to look into a
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") before transporting themselves
there via [Teleport](/wiki/Teleport "wikilink") or [Portal](Portal "wikilink")
or [Nexus](/wiki/Nexus "wikilink").
