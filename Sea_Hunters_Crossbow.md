*This crossbow, beneath its peeling blue paint, is a sturdy contraption
built for hunting seamonsters and other dangerous beasts. A heavy metal
eyelet on the bottom of the frame lets a strong wire connect the bow to
the bolt, so that snared prey can easily be hauled onto the boat.*

`Damage is 25-70.`  
`Modifies strength by 2 continuous`  
`Modifies hit roll by 19 continuous`
