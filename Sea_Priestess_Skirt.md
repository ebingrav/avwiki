Comments
--------

Result of the [Sea Priestess' Skirt
Quest](/wiki/Sea_Priestess'_Skirt_Quest "wikilink") done primarily in the
[Rim](/wiki/Category%3ARim "wikilink"), ending in [Gorn's
Anchor](/wiki/Category%3AGorn's_Anchor "wikilink").

If you receive this item while having a [Far Hunter Tattoo
Insignia](/wiki/Far_Hunter_Tattoo_Insignia "wikilink"), the skirt is better:

`Faintly increases resistance to water continuous [10%]`  
`Modifies hp by 11 continuous`  
`Modifies mana by 32 continuous`

It will also be bound.
