*The seal [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") mends rips in the
fabric of space, sealing [portals](/wiki/Portals "wikilink") or
[nexuses](/wiki/Nexuses "wikilink") shut.*

Prerequisite(s): [Portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'seal'
&lt;[portal](/wiki/Portals "wikilink")/[nexus](/wiki/Nexuses "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is midly useful for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") wanting to quickly
eliminate unwanted [player](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")-made
[portals](/wiki/Portals "wikilink") or [nexuses](/wiki/Nexuses "wikilink") for one
reason or another, rather than letting them disappear naturally after a
period of time.
