This quest takes the player through three different areas, and involves
no difficult combat.

1.  Murder the winged courier who roams the
    [Airways](/wiki/Category%3AAirways "wikilink") (*A winged courier whizzes
    through the atmosphere.*) and steal the sealed letter. To teleport
    use the keywords *messenger sprite*.
2.  Take it to [High Father Linneas
    Murr](/wiki/High_Father_Linneas_Murr "wikilink") in [Abbaye Du
    Livre](/wiki/Category%3AAbbaye_Du_Livre "wikilink"). To reach him, walk
    19n, 3e, 3n, 3e, n, 2u, 4n, e, n, 3e, 2n, \[n\], 3e, \[s\] from
    [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"). He is also teleportable. Once you
    present the letter you will receive another letter.
3.  From Aelmon walk 20s, 7e into [Lord Audis'
    Villa](/wiki/Category%3ALord_Audis'_Villa "wikilink"), but do not enter
    the house proper since it has big guards and will turn you into an
    [Outlaw](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink"). Instead give the letter to
    [Portho](/wiki/Portho "wikilink") who is sitting outside the house. Portho
    is also teleportable.
4.  Receive a [Topaz](/wiki/Topaz "wikilink") in reward.
