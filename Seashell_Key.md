*This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") has a very unique shape - it
looks like seashell made of lightweight metal.*

**Keyword(s):** seashell, key.  
**[Level(s)](Object_Level.md "wikilink"):** 10.  
**[Type](:Category:_Object_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](Object_Slots.md "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](Object_Quality.md "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](Object_Weight.md "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](:Category:_Object_Flags.md "wikilink"):** none.  

## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") is used in [The River
Folk](:Category:_River_Folk.md "wikilink")'s [River Folk Treasure
Quest](River_Folk_Treasure_Quest.md "wikilink"); it unlocks an ancient
wooden seachest in An Underwater Cave.
