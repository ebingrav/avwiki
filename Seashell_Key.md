*This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") has a very unique shape - it
looks like seashell made of lightweight metal.*

**Keyword(s):** seashell, key.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 10.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is used in [The River
Folk](/wiki/Category%3A_River_Folk "wikilink")'s [River Folk Treasure
Quest](/wiki/River_Folk_Treasure_Quest "wikilink"); it unlocks an ancient
wooden seachest in An Underwater Cave.
