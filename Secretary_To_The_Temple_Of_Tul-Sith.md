*This young [half-elf](/wiki/Half-Elves "wikilink") woman is barely past her
childhood. This is her first assignment. She was always good with
numbers, more than [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). As a result
she serves her [Goddess](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") by
keeping the place running smoothly.*
