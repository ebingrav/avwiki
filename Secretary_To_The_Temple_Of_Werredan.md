*Standing tall and proud this young
[priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") and clerk serves his
[God](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") not with a sword, but
with numbers, yes numbers. He peers over his legers and keeps the books
balanced, ordering supplies and making sure the guards are all paid on
time.*

**Keywords:** secretary, priest, werredan.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 30.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
