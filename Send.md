`Syntax: c 'send' `<target>` `<plane>  
`        c 'send' steve outland`  
`Prerequisites: planeshift, salvation`

*The Send ritual is used to shift another person to another plane. The
target must signal willingness by grouping with all 3 casters required
to complete the spell, and by resting. The ritual works from any type of
room or any plane where Lords can cast Lord level spells, and any plane
is a valid target. In all ways except mana cost, it is as if the target
cast planeshift themselves.*

*The ritual can only be learned by those with some skill at magic and it
takes three casters to complete. Both the initiating and final caster
must supply a valid destination plane for the ritual to be successful.*

*Lastly, it is rumored that three casters can send a person who has lost
his connection to the realm of AVATAR even if he is not resting.*

## Comments

Note that the final caster - who completes the ritual - will be lagged
as if they had planeshifted themselves.
