Syntax: c 'send' <target> <plane>  
c 'send' steve outland  
Prerequisites: [planeshift](/wiki/Planeshift "wikilink"),
[salvation](/wiki/Salvation "wikilink")

The Send ritual is used to shift another person to another plane. The
target must signal willingness by grouping with all 3 casters required
to complete the spell, and by resting. The ritual works from any type of
room or any plane where Lords can cast Lord level spells, and any plane
is a valid target. In all ways except mana cost, it is as if the target
cast planeshift themselves.

The ritual must be started by a Mage, Sorcerer or Psion, and takes three
casters to complete. Only the final caster needs to input the
destination plane.
