*The Black Circle can use their psionic powers to locate weak points in
their enemies. Once this [spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink") is
successfully [cast](Cast.md "wikilink") on an
[enemy](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink"), all [vital
shots](Vital_Shot.md "wikilink") made against that
[enemy](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink") by any [Black Circle
Initiate](:Category:_Black_Circle_Initiates.md "wikilink") will do more
damage.*

Prerequisite(s): [Bio Empathy](Bio_Empathy.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast.md "wikilink") 'sense weakness'
\<[mob](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink")\>.
