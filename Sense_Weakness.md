*The Black Circle can use their psionic powers to locate weak points in
their enemies. Once this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
successfully [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on an
[enemy](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"), all [vital
shots](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink") made against that
[enemy](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") by any [Black Circle
Initiate](/wiki/Category%3A_Black_Circle_Initiates "wikilink") will do more
damage.*

Prerequisite(s): [Bio Empathy](/wiki/Bio_Empathy "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'sense weakness'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
