Perform the following:

-   c tele [Septarch Kyxial](/wiki/Septarch_Kyxial "wikilink")
-   puzzle kyxial
-   answer the two questions
-   c tele huge troll (or 2.huge troll)
-   e:d:s
-   get all blanket
-   c tele odevar merchant
-   give blanket merchant
-   c tele kyxial
-   give rustleaf kyxial
-   receive [Rod Of Blue Crystal](/wiki/Rod_Of_Blue_Crystal "wikilink")
    ([lightning bolt](/wiki/Lightning_Bolt "wikilink") wand)
-   receive insignia ['Sept Nik'Avrym
    Problem-Solver'](/wiki/Sept_Nik'Avrym_Insignia "wikilink")

*Note*: The quest is repeatable once per week.
