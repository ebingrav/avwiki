*Chant allows [priests](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") to
[preach](/wiki/Preach "wikilink") any movement-related or [curative
spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Heal_Characters "wikilink")
(example: [pass door](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink"), [pure
touch](/wiki/Pure_Touch "wikilink")).*

Prerequisite(s): [Preach](/wiki/Preach "wikilink").

Syntax: [preach](/wiki/Preach "wikilink")
&lt;[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")&gt;.
