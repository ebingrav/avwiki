*When [Finar](/wiki/Clan_Chief_Finar "wikilink") was a lad he often ventured
along [the Mountain path](/wiki/Category%3A_Highways/Great_Wall "wikilink") to
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink") with
[Voinar](/wiki/Clan_Chief_Voinar "wikilink") to drink and gamble. They had a
foolproof way to win - loaded dice - and set about trying to swindle
[Ofcol](/wiki/Category%3A_Ofcol "wikilink")'s finest. Of course they failed
but [Finar](/wiki/Clan_Chief_Finar "wikilink") kept his dice as a memento of
times gone. Now they are yours.*
