may get [a mine pass level one](Mine_Pass_Level_One.md "wikilink") by
completing [Mt. Durr](:Category:_Mt_Durr.md "wikilink")'s [Mine Pass
Level One Quest](Mine_Pass_Level_One_Quest.md "wikilink") rather than by
buying it.*
