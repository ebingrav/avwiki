*Note that those who know [bashdoor](/wiki/Bashdoor "wikilink") may instead,
from [Finar](/wiki/Clan_Chief_Finar "wikilink"), go \[e\], {s}, \[d\], {d}, n,
w, {n}, using [bashdoor](/wiki/Bashdoor "wikilink") to open all locked
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink"), after which they may
[get](/wiki/Get "wikilink") [a basket of cavern
truffles](/wiki/Basket_Of_Cavern_Truffles "wikilink") from ground and then
continue as instructed from that point below. Also,
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who prefer to do so may
get [a mine pass level one](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One "wikilink") by
completing [Mt. Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Mine Pass Level
One Quest](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One_Quest "wikilink") rather than by buying
it.*
