*The Mystagogue was the leader of [this once lively
shrine](:Category:_Enchantment_Of_The_Shades "wikilink"). At one point
in time, he was unchallenged in the area in combat. Excelling in both
magical and physical challenges, he is nowhere near the level he had
once attained. However, he is still plenty powerful to dispose of you!*

**Keywords:** shade, Mystagogue.  
**[Level](Level "wikilink"):** 30.  
**[Alignment](Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[mage](Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[undead](Undead_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [fly](Fly "wikilink"), [invis](Invis "wikilink"), [pass
door](Pass_Door "wikilink").
