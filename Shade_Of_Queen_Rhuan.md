*Among the shades that drift about the Halls of the Dead, a female
[dwarf](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") of regal bearing seems to watch your every
move. This is Queen Rhuan, who with her husband [King
Vorin](/wiki/Shade_Of_King_Vorin "wikilink") united the Dwarven Kingdom
against a massive [Troll](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink") invasion hundreds of years
ago. After [Vorin](/wiki/Shade_Of_King_Vorin "wikilink")'s untimely death,
Rhuan ascended the throne, ruling with kindness and justice for over 150
years. It is said that whenever the spirit of Rhuan walks the Halls of
the Dead, the [Dwarves](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") will soon face great
danger.*

Comments
--------

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") will need to get [the
key to the spirit realm](/wiki/Key_To_The_Spirit_Realm "wikilink") from [a
spirit guard](/wiki/Spirit_Guard "wikilink") to [unlock](Unlock "wikilink")
the door to the [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") where this
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") is.

The shade of Queen Rhuan is involved in [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Legacy of King Vorin
Quest](/wiki/Legacy_Of_King_Vorin_Quest "wikilink").
