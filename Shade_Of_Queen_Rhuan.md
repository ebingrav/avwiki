*Among the shades that drift about the Halls of the Dead, a female
[dwarf](Dwarves.md "wikilink") of regal bearing seems to watch your
every move. This is Queen Rhuan, who with her husband [King
Vorin](Shade_Of_King_Vorin.md "wikilink") united the Dwarven Kingdom
against a massive [Troll](Trolls.md "wikilink") invasion hundreds of
years ago. After [Vorin](Shade_Of_King_Vorin.md "wikilink")'s untimely
death, Rhuan ascended the throne, ruling with kindness and justice for
over 150 years. It is said that whenever the spirit of Rhuan walks the
Halls of the Dead, the [Dwarves](Dwarves.md "wikilink") will soon face
great danger.*

## Comments

[Characters](:Category:_Characters.md "wikilink") will need to get [the
key to the spirit realm](Key_To_The_Spirit_Realm.md "wikilink") from [a
spirit guard](Spirit_Guard.md "wikilink") to

[room](:Category:_Rooms.md "wikilink") where this
[mob](:Category:_Mobs.md "wikilink") is.

The shade of Queen Rhuan is involved in [Mt.
Durr](:Category:_Mt_Durr.md "wikilink")'s [Legacy of King Vorin
Quest](Legacy_Of_King_Vorin_Quest.md "wikilink").
