[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") prevent
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") within them,
specifically [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") that have
"[nosun](/wiki/Racial_Nosun "wikilink")"
[racials](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink"), from being affected
negatively by any sunlight that may shine upon them.
