[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"), unless attacked first, will
both "clone" and attack some non-[lord-tier](/wiki/Lord "wikilink"),
non-incapacitated player around them. When shapeshifting to clone some
player, these [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") will take upon
themselves that player's name, [sex](/wiki/Sex "wikilink"),
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"), [hit points](Hit_Points "wikilink"), [armor
class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"), [saving throw](Saving_Throw "wikilink"),
[hitroll](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink"), [Damroll](Damage_Roll "wikilink"),
[alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"), and [spell
affects](/wiki/Affects "wikilink").
