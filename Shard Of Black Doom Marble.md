## Comments

Although this is a weapon, it is used in the held slot. Hence, it can be
enchanted with [Enchant Weapon](Enchant_Weapon "wikilink") to give even
more [hitroll](Hit_Roll "wikilink")/[damroll](Damage_Roll "wikilink") as
long as the level does not go above 54. Also, since one does not attack
with it, the base weapon values are not important. It also can be runed
(See: [Rune](Rune "wikilink")) for -15ac.

The highest a shard can go, naturally, is to 12/12.
