This is a mini-quest in [Exploration
Outpost](/wiki/Category%3AExploration_Outpost "wikilink") on
[Eragora](/wiki/Category%3AEragora "wikilink").

-   Once on Eragora, venture into the water portion of the area by
    teleporting to and following [Mayflower](/wiki/Mayflower "wikilink").
-   Do downward and run the area, and kill all ulexite-shelled turtles.
    Some of them will skin for shards of ulexite.
-   Give one to [Mozlin Goblestone](/wiki/Mozlin_Goblestone "wikilink") who is
    e, n of [Ginormous Dingersen](/wiki/Ginormous_Dingersen "wikilink"), and
    he will cast atheist imm-level [Steel
    Skeleton](/wiki/Steel_Skeleton "wikilink") on you. (Imm-level spells last
    156 ticks).

Being treasure, the shard can also be sold at jewelers for a hefty
price.
