*One of the fundamental tenets of the psionic discipline is the idea
that all are connected. This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
harnesses that link by transferring some of the caster's
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") to a companion for a while. Provided
that the caster has enough [strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") left, this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") can be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on
more than one target; however, it can only be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
one time on any [character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'share strength'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
