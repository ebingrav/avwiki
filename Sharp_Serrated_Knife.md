*This knife is sharpened at the tip but serrated along the edge to make
the [skinning](/wiki/Skin "wikilink") of prey easier.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 6 to 18 (average 12).`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`
