*This short sword is of the type favored by many
[assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink"). Taking a few practice
swings you see how it could be so deadly.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 15-16 to 47-48 (average 31-32).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 7 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 10 continuous.`

**Keyword(s):** short, sword.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 50-52.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <wielded>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 100 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 4 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

There are 3 sneaky pirates wandering in the lower portion of Redtooth's
Isle.
