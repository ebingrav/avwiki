*By sharpening your [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink"), you
increase the [minimum damage](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink") that it
does, thus increasing the overall average damage. The more you sharpen a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink"), the less your [minimum
damage](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink") will go up. There are also many
things that can happen to your [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")
while you are sharpening it. If you get lucky, one of your sharpening
attempts may increase the [maximum
damage](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink") on your
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") as well as the [minimum
damage](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink"). If you try to sharpen it too
much, the [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") may shatter and be
lost in a scattering of metal shards. If you slip errantly with your
[whetstone](/wiki/Whetstone "wikilink"), you may end up
[dulling](/wiki/Dulling "wikilink") your
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") and setting its [minimum
damage](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink") back to the original number.
[Dulling](/wiki/Dulling "wikilink") may also remove
[enchants](/wiki/Enchant_Weapon "wikilink") that have been placed upon the
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") prior to it being sharpened.
You can get a decent idea of a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")'s condition by
[examining](/wiki/Examine "wikilink") or
[identifying](/wiki/Identify_(spell) "wikilink") it, which will allow you to
determine how many times you could realistically sharpen a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") before reaching its peak
sharpness. You will, of course, need a whetstone to sharpen your
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") with.*

Prerequisite(s): [Weapon Lore](/wiki/Weapon_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: [sharpen](/wiki/Sharpen "wikilink")
&lt;[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")&gt;.
