*Shen is large for a [halfling](/wiki/Halflings "wikilink"), but spear bald.
In place of hair, he has a jagged black pattern tattooed on his scalp.
Planning a future raid, Shen is content for now to observe the
[giants](/wiki/Giants "wikilink") in the valley below, merely taking notes of
their movements and only occassionally putting a rock between their eyes
from his treetop roost.*

**Keywords:** Shen, halfling.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[trainer](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
