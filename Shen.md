*Shen is large for a [halfling](Halflings "wikilink"), but spear bald.
In place of hair, he has a jagged black pattern tattooed on his scalp.
Planning a future raid, Shen is content for now to observe the
[giants](Giants "wikilink") in the valley below, merely taking notes of
their movements and only occassionally putting a rock between their eyes
from his treetop roost.*

**Keywords:** Shen, halfling.
