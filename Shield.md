*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") [protects the
target](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")
by altering their [Armor Class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"). Shield provides
20 points of [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"). This can be
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") upon yourself or someone else if the
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'shield'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in
[Foci](Foci "wikilink"). Its [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") bonus may be
modified by its [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")'s choice of
[worship](/wiki/Worship "wikilink").

See also [Shield Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Shield_Gear "wikilink").
