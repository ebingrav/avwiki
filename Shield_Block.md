*What good is a shield? Why, to stop things from hitting you, of course.
Shield Block does this automatically (once
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink")). If a blow is deflected by Shield
Block, it does no damage. A high [dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink") helps
in getting the shield into position to block. Also, chances are
partially [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink")-dependant: the lower the mob's
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") (or the higher your
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink")), the better chance you have of deflecting the
shot. Some shields contain magic spells which will discharge against the
attacker after a successful shield block.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
