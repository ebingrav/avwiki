The [Shield Of The Tree](/wiki/Shield_Of_The_Tree "wikilink") is a difficult
quest in the Transfigured Forest. It is very hard to solo parts of the
quest and will require several players or several alts with different
alignments/fighting styles.

You will need eight different items before you are ready to obtain the
shield.

Three can be found scattered around the area, in no fixed location.

<spoiler> Retrieve the following items:

-   [clump of stone](/wiki/Clump_Of_Stone "wikilink")
-   [clump of leaves](/wiki/Clump_Of_Leaves "wikilink")
-   [clump of ice](/wiki/Clump_Of_Ice "wikilink")

These are scattered around on several mobs. The ice is in the tree on a
[Giant Creature Of Ice](/wiki/Giant_Creature_Of_Ice "wikilink"), the leaves on
a [Giant-Shaped Swirl Of
Leaves](/wiki/Giant-Shaped_Swirl_Of_Leaves "wikilink"), and the stone on a
[Trunk Of Stone](/wiki/Trunk_Of_Stone "wikilink") near the entrance. There are
two of each mob. Kill both for repop. </spoiler>

You will also need four items which will generally require four
different characters to gain.

<spoiler> You will also need to get one of <b>each</b> of the mana
items:

-   [Gnarled Branch Of Age](/wiki/Gnarled_Branch_Of_Age "wikilink")
-   [Swirl Of Fumes](/wiki/Swirl_Of_Fumes "wikilink")
-   [Grey Orb Of Stone](/wiki/Grey_Orb_Of_Stone "wikilink")
-   [Crackling Sphere Of
    Lightning](/wiki/Crackling_Sphere_Of_Lightning "wikilink")

Since each of these require a certain type of player to get you will
need a mix of good/melee, good/caster, evil/melee, evil/caster.
</spoiler>

After you have all these items get started by getting the
[root](/wiki/Glowing_Root "wikilink") in the secret tunnel (hidden west exit
in the shortcut to Ent). Give it to the [Heavily Scarred
Ent](/wiki/Heavily_Scarred_Ent "wikilink") to begin the quest.

<spoiler> He will give you a new [glowing
root](/wiki/Glowing_Root_(Transformed) "wikilink"). You will then give the new
root to the [Vortex Of Souls](/wiki/Vortex_Of_Souls "wikilink") which will
take it and the four mana items and give you a [glowing
seed](/wiki/Glowing_Seed "wikilink"). Take the seed back to the scarred ent
and give it to him. He will take it and give you back [glowing
seed](/wiki/Glowing_Seed_(Transformed) "wikilink"). Give it and the three
clumps back to ent to receive the [Shield Of The
Tree](/wiki/Shield_Of_The_Tree "wikilink"). </spoiler>
