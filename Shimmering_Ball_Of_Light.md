Comments
--------

Non-permenant light source - lasts about a run, longer if you switch
gear a lot. The lower level ones can be found on the ordinary
[surveyors](/wiki/Deep_Gnome_Surveyor "wikilink"), but attacking them will
make you an outlaw.
