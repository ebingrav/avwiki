*The pike's blade combines a long cutting edge, a thick stabbing point
and a sturdy hook at the base. A chain is linked onto the blade, and
feeds through a loop on the corresponding gauntlets, so that the pike
may be thrown and retrieved.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 17 to 52 (average 34).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 12 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 12 continuous.`
