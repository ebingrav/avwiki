*Crafted from stout wood with small fossils embedded as ornaments, the
short spear appears as a short but still deadly spear.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 6-11 to 20-24 (average 13-17).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 3 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 3 continuous.`
