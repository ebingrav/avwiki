*It seems short enough for a [halfling](/wiki/Halflings "wikilink") or
[gnome](/wiki/Gnomes "wikilink") to use in one hand, but really it is intended
for throwing.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 11 to 34 (average 22).`  
`Modifies `[`armor`
`class`](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")` by -5 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`dexterity`](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink")` by 2 continuous.`
