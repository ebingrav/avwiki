*A [Shadowfist](/wiki/Category%3A_Shadowfists "wikilink")'s
[monk](/wiki/Category%3A_Monks "wikilink") training enables him to resist the
distractions of combat while lining up a [vital
shot](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink"). This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is automatic once trained, but is
not absolute.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
