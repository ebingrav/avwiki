*Based originally on the farmer's sickle, the Sica was a sturdy blade,
curved into a two-thirds circle, sharpened on both the inside circle and
the outside. Effective both in offense and defense, a master of the Sica
could disarm an opponent in short order.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 10 to 25 (average 17).`  
`Modifies `[`damage`
`roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 3 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 3 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`moves`](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink")` by -15 continuous.`
