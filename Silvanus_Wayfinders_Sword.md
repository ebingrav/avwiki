*This sword, judging from its [Faerie](/wiki/Faerie_Language "wikilink")
inscription, formerly belonged to one named [Silvanus
Wayfinder](/wiki/Centaur_Ranger "wikilink"). It appears to be of
[centaurish](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") make.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 7-9 to 22-26 (average 14-17).`

`Modifies `[`hp`](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`
