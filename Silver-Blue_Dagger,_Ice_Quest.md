This quest involves three ticket items and two areas. The mobs you
encounter are tough (levels 65-95, the strongest are sanced). Every mob
involved in the quest but the mother is aggie, too. Here goes:

-   From [Mourning Drow](/wiki/Mourning_Drow "wikilink"): go 3w n 2d n w s
    6w d.
-   Open wall, get [painting](/wiki/Small_Painting "wikilink") wall.
-   In [Category%3ANecropolis](/wiki/Category%3ANecropolis "wikilink"), find
    the [High Drow Mother](/wiki/High_Drow_Mother "wikilink") that, on scan,
    looks like this:

`Insane with loss, this mother searches for things she lost.`

-   Give her the picture. She will then give you [a note detailing the
    drider invasion](/wiki/Note_Detailing_The_Drider_Invasion "wikilink").
-   Tele back to [Mourning Drow](/wiki/Mourning_Drow "wikilink"). From here,
    walk 2w s 7w n w.
-   Give the note to the [Statue Of Veyah
    L'Aturii](/wiki/Statue_Of_Veyah_L'Aturii "wikilink"). She will give you
    [the battle commands for Veyah's
    commander](/wiki/Battle_Commands_For_Veyah's_Commander "wikilink").
-   Go e s 6e 2n 2d n w 7s 2d 2n w s.
-   Give the commands to the [General Commander For Veyah
    L'Aturii](/wiki/General_Commander_For_Veyah_L'Aturii "wikilink"). This
    will transport you to the
    [room](/wiki/The_Hidden_Room_Of_The_Necropolis_(Necropolis) "wikilink")
    where [the projection of Veyah
    L'Aturii](/wiki/Projection_Of_Veyah_L'Aturii "wikilink") is.
-   Kill Veyah and loot her corpse for [the silver-blue dagger,
    "Ice"](/wiki/Silver-Blue_Dagger,_"Ice" "wikilink"). She also drops a [lock
    of hair](/wiki/Lock_Of_Hair "wikilink"), be sure to pick it up for some
    quick cash.

The portal takes you back to [the start of the
area](/wiki/At_The_Cusp_Of_The_Necropolis_(Necropolis) "wikilink").

-   Tele to [Gremlin Bawanna](/wiki/Gremlin_Bawanna "wikilink") and walk s w s
    e 2u.
-   Give the hair to [the ancient elf](/wiki/Ancient_Elf "wikilink") for a
    [cats eye gemstone](/wiki/Cats_Eye_Gemstone "wikilink").
