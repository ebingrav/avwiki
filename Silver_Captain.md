*This [Lizard Man](Lizardmen.md "wikilink") has a proud and noble
bearing. He is reviewing a crude map of [the
swamp](:Category:_Crescent_Bog.md "wikilink"), as if directing his
troops to try and find something. He carries a roll call of the Brigade.
You notice all the names are checked, except for one. The Captain
notices your glance, and says, "Alas, one of our band seems to have
vanished. If you come upon a [Lizard Man](Lizardmen.md "wikilink") named
Kezrah, tell him we are looking for him."*

**Keywords:** lizard, man, silver, brigade, captain.

**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types.md "wikilink"):** none.
