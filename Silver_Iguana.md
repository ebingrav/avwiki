*A piece of arm jewelry is here, in the shape of a curled silver
iguana.*  
Modifies mana by 150 continuous. Slot: Arms  
Type: Treasure  
Flags: glow magic nolocate  
Plane: [Thorngate](/wiki/Category%3AThorngate "wikilink")  
Area: [Rietta's Wonders](/wiki/Category%3ARietta's_Wonders "wikilink")  
Mob: [Bask](/wiki/Bask "wikilink")  
To get this gear you need to get a [Lizard Man Challenge
Token](/wiki/Lizard_Man_Challenge_Token "wikilink"). This is a lvl 125 invis
ticket that loads on the ground in [Elven
Foothills](http://avatar.melanarchy.info/index.php?title=Category:Elven_Foothills).
To get it teleport to
[Nyad](http://avatar.melanarchy.info/index.php?title=Nyad) and go
2nuw2sedw.

Once you have the ticket go to [Rietta's
Wonders](/wiki/Category%3ARietta's_Wonders "wikilink") and find
[Bask](/wiki/Bask "wikilink"). Give him the token, then kill him, and loot the
gear. Note that the Silver Iguana is reboot only. Also this quest can
only be completed once per incarnation, similar to the
[Ultimoose](http://avatar.melanarchy.info/wiki/Ultimoose_Power).
