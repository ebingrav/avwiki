Ancient Drow](:Category:_Tower_Of_The_Ancient_Drow.md "wikilink"). The
key is needed to get the [Golden Key (Tower Of The Ancient
Drow)](Golden_Key_(Tower_Of_The_Ancient_Drow) "wikilink").
