Comments

Ancient Drow](/wiki/Category%3A_Tower_Of_The_Ancient_Drow "wikilink"). The key
is needed to get the [Golden Key (Tower Of The Ancient
Drow)](/wiki/Golden_Key_(Tower_Of_The_Ancient_Drow) "wikilink").
