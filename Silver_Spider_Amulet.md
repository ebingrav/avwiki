Comments
--------

Despite it being of type [armor](/wiki/Category%3A_Armor "wikilink"), the
amulet is in fact a [key](/wiki/Category%3AKeys "wikilink"). The amulet
unlocks the web blocking the western part of [Drow
City](/wiki/Category%3ADrow_City "wikilink").
