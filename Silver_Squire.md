*This [Lizard Man](/wiki/Lizardmen "wikilink") is gangly and thin, with big,
clumsy hands and feet. His tail is also fairly short.*

**Keywords:** lizard, man, silver, squire.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 27.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
