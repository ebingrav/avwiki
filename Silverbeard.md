*Silverbeard the Dragonbane is what his clan calls this stout
[dwarf](Dwarves.md "wikilink"). In his prime, he was caught in a white
[dragon](Dragons.md "wikilink")'s lair and barely escaped with his life.
The [dragon](Dragons.md "wikilink") didn't escape from his hammer,
however. He polishes a silver mug while he winks at you. Care for a
drink or something to eat?*

**Keywords:** Silverbeard.  
**[Level](Level.md "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](Alignment.md "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs.md "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](Detect_Hidden.md "wikilink"), [detect
invis](Detect_Invis.md "wikilink").
