*Silverbeard the Dragonbane is what his clan calls this stout
[dwarf](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink"). In his prime, he was caught in a white
[dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink")'s lair and barely escaped with his life.
The [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") didn't escape from his hammer, however.
He polishes a silver mug while he winks at you. Care for a drink or
something to eat?*

**Keywords:** Silverbeard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
