*This [longbow](/wiki/Category%3A_Long_Bows "wikilink") is simple but
effective, whether used for hunting in the woods or for slaying darklin
or other foes.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Missile_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 10-13 to 35-38 (average 23-25).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`
