*This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") is one of those that is
supposed to [unlock](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") any
[door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink"). But does it?*

**Keyword(s):** key, skeleton.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 24-26, 34-36.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks a series of doors. It
unlocks the door west of Before the Alter of Tul-Sith, and the door
south of An Alcove in the Temple of Tul-Sith.
