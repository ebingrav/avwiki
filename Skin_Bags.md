*A bag is here, made from the skin of (fill-in-the-blank)*.  
Capacity is 250 lbs. Type:
[Containers](/wiki/Category%3AContainers "wikilink")  
Level: 1  
Weight: 7  
Flags: [magic](/wiki/Magic_Flag "wikilink")  
Area: Any  
Mob: Any  

Have an [archer](/wiki/Category%3AArchers "wikilink") level 37 or higher skin
any random corpse (other than the ones that make specific [Skin
Items](/wiki/Category%3ASkin_Items "wikilink")). If you're lucky you'll end up
with a bag.
