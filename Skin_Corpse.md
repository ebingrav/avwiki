*With this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), an
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") can make an
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") from the body of a fallen enemy.
The particular [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") created is random;
the [archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") will attempt to make the
best possible [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") with the materials
available. An [archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") may fail entirely
at this task, yielding only an embarrassing stack of skin and entrails.
Some [monsters](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")' corpses will yield
[special treasures](/wiki/Category%3A_Skin_Items "wikilink") when skinned. A
[bowie knife](/wiki/Standard_Issue_Bowie_Knife "wikilink") is necessary to
skin a corpse, unless the user has the [racial
butcher](/wiki/Racial_Butcher "wikilink") ability.*

Prerequisite(s): [Armor Lore](/wiki/Armor_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: [skin](/wiki/Skin "wikilink") <corpse>.

See also [Skin Items](/wiki/Category%3A_Skin_Items "wikilink").
