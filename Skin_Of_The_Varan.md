`Object 'skin thick' type armor, extra flags noremove.`  
`Weight 15, value 693, level 35.`  
`Object Quality (100 / 100 hps)`  
`Armor class is 9.`  
`Modifies armor class by -30 continuous.`  
`Modifies moves by -50 continuous.`  
`Modifies strength by 2 continuous.`

Slot: On Body  
Level: 34  
Base: 7  
Weight: 15  
Area: [Island of Utami](/wiki/Category%3A_Island_Of_Utami "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Island_Of_Utami_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: [Ancient Varan](/wiki/Ancient_Varan "wikilink")  
  
Port [Ancient Varan](/wiki/Ancient_Varan "wikilink") and kill for Skin. Note
that the mob is sanced and slightly tricky for the level of item.

The item is cursed. Its keywords do not include 'Varan', but 'thick
skin'.
