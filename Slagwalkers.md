` Modifies save vs magic by -50 continuous`  
` Modifies armor class by -10 continuous`  
` Modifies hit roll by 4 continuous`

Slot: Feet  
Level: 40  
Base: 9  
Weight: 3  
Flags: none  
Mob: [Mother Slagslug](/wiki/Mother_Slagslug "wikilink")  
Area: [Drow Armory](/wiki/Category%3A_Drow_Armory "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Drow_Armory_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob is portable. Skin her corpse for the gear. The mobs in the area eat
corpses, so it is best to kill the rest of them in the room before
killing the mother.
