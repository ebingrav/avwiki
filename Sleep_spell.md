*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") puts its
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") to
[sleep](/wiki/Sleep_(command) "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'sleep'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is rarely used. Some
[heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") find it useful when helping
lower-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") players to [retrieve their gear from
their corpses](/wiki/Corpse_Retrieval "wikilink").

See also [Sleep Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Sleep_Gear "wikilink").
