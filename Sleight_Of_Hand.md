*With nimble fingers, a stealthy [rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink")
may take [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") unseen. Like
[peek](/wiki/Peek "wikilink"), this will be forever upon
[practicing](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"). If you don't want to hide your
movements from others, don't [practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") this. The
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is now toggleable with use of the
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"). Note: This is only effective
in [GETting](Get "wikilink") [objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink").*

Prerequisites: [Rogue Lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink"),
[Steal](/wiki/Steal "wikilink").

Syntax: [sleight](/wiki/Sleight "wikilink").
