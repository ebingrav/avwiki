*A corroded ring of some unrecognizable metal lies on the ground. It is
coated with green slime. Perhaps this once belonged to some hapless
adventurer who wandered into one dungeon too many.*

`Armor class is 2.`

## Comments

Not all green slimes in the area carry this ring. The slimes carry the
ring in their inventory, so you won't be able to see it by looking at
the slimes unless you have [Peek](Peek "wikilink"), or until you kill
the slime.
