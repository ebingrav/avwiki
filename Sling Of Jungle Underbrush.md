Duergar](Disemboweled_Duergar "wikilink") in [Morte
Vallta](:Category:Morte_Vallta "wikilink"). See the [Morte Vallta

[gia vul](Giant_Vulture "wikilink"). From there, walk 2d n 2w to get to
the Duergar.
