` Modifies hit roll by 17 continuous.`  
` Modifies dexterity by 2 continuous.`

Type: Sling  
Level: 50  
Max: 46  
Weight: 8  
Mob: [Disembodied Duergar](/wiki/Disembodied_Duergar "wikilink")  
Area: [Morte Vallta](/wiki/Category%3A_Morte_Vallta "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Morte_Vallta_Map "wikilink"))  
This piece of gear is located on the [Disembodied
Duergar](/wiki/Disembodied_Duergar "wikilink") in [Morte
Vallta](/wiki/Category%3AMorte_Vallta "wikilink"). See the [Morte Vallta
Map](/wiki/Morte_Vallta_Map "wikilink") for an idea of how to walk to him,
he's in the room labelled "tree of woe". Portal point to the area is
[gia vul](/wiki/Giant_Vulture "wikilink"). From there, walk 2d n w to get to
the Duergar.
