*This [Rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") refines the art of cowardice one
step further! If [sneaking](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink"), a master of the Slink
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") will be able to escape notice
when fleeing from combat, causing his pursuer much confusion, and a
delay in the chase. You must be actively [sneaking](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink") to
use this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), and it will not work on
[foes](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") who are using the
[Alertness](/wiki/Alertness "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Rogue Lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
