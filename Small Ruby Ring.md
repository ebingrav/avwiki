## Comments

There are two near identical versions of this ring. The first no longer
exists and was found on [Matron Mother
Stress](Matron_Mother_Stress "wikilink") in [Drow
City](:Category:_Drow_City "wikilink"). The second, which possesses a
slightly better AC, can be found in
[Tiureess](:Category:_Tiureess "wikilink") and can be obtained by doing
the [Septarch Envy Quest](Septarch_Envy_Quest "wikilink").
