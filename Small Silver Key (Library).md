*This small silver [key](:Category:_Keys "wikilink") bears the royal
Seal of [Midgaard](:Category:_Midgaard "wikilink"). It is quite small
and looks like it might be intended to [unlock](Unlock "wikilink")
either a small chest or a desk drawer. It is hard to tell.*

large oak desk in The Main Desk.
