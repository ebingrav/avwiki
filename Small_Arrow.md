*This small [arrow](/wiki/Category%3A_Arrows "wikilink") has been crafted to
an astonishing degree of perfection, even more surprising as it was
either meant for a child's [bow](/wiki/Category%3A_Bows "wikilink"), or as a
mere toy. The signs of the sableroix family, accompanied with an 'R'
have been carved into the gleaming shaft.*
