Comments
--------

This is the reward of the [Ogre Magikian
Quest](/wiki/Ogre_Magikian_Quest "wikilink").  
The book teaches [Demonic Advice](/wiki/Demonic_Advice "wikilink"). However,
[wizards](/wiki/Category%3AWizards "wikilink") can naturally practice this, so
this item is of little practical use.  
Although the description mentions this being a
[mage's](/wiki/Category%3AMages "wikilink") book, this is actually a
[spellbook](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellbooks "wikilink") for
[wizards](/wiki/Category%3AWizards "wikilink").

be warned that item gives you only 10% of the skill each time you use it
and it seems only up to 50%, i.e. 5 times from 0. you may experiment if
it will help once you spend one prac...
