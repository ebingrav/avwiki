shelf on the wall.*

**Keyword(s):** key, small, golden.

**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 2 lbs.

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") unlocks the door between The
quartermaster's keep and The treasure room; it also unlocks a small
treasure chest in The treasure room.
