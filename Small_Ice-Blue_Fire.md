## Comments

They are in a nospell room, and there's three of them. They hit pretty
hard for non-wielding mobs.

Kill all three of them, and the [Shattered Amulet Of Fire And
Ice](Shattered_Amulet_Of_Fire_And_Ice "wikilink") appears on the floor.
This is part of the [Smoldering Crown Of Dying Fire
Quest](Smoldering_Crown_Of_Dying_Fire_Quest "wikilink").
