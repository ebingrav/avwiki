Comments
--------

There are two near identical versions of this ring. The first no longer
exists and was found on [Matron Mother
Stress](/wiki/Matron_Mother_Stress "wikilink") in [Drow
City](/wiki/Category%3A_Drow_City "wikilink"). The second, which possesses a
slightly better AC, can be found in
[Tiureess](/wiki/Category%3A_Tiureess "wikilink") and can be obtained by doing
the [Septarch Envy Quest](/wiki/Septarch_Envy_Quest "wikilink").
