*This small silver [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink") bears the royal
Seal of [Midgaard](:Category:_Midgaard.md "wikilink"). It is quite small
and looks like it might be intended to [unlock](Unlock.md "wikilink")
either a small chest or a desk drawer. It is hard to tell.*

**Keyword(s):** key, silver.  
**[Level(s)](Object_Level.md "wikilink"):** 27-29.  
**[Type](:Category:_Object_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](Object_Slots.md "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](Object_Quality.md "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](Object_Weight.md "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](:Category:_Object_Flags.md "wikilink"):**
[anti-neutral](Anti-Neutral_Flag.md "wikilink").  

## Comments

This [key](:Category:_Keys.md "wikilink")
[unlocks](Unlock.md "wikilink") a large oak desk in The Main Desk.
