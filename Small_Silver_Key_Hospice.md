*This small delicate silver [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") bears the
mark of [Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink"). It probably unlocks a door
somewhere in [the Hospice](/wiki/Category%3A_Tul-Sith's_Hospice "wikilink").*

**Keyword(s):** silver, key.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 24-26.

[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.

[bless](/wiki/Bless_Flag "wikilink"), [glow](Glow_Flag "wikilink"),
[hum](/wiki/Hum_Flag "wikilink").

Priest's Quarters and The Shrine of Tul-Sith.
