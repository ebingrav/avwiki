*This small silver [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") bears the royal
Seal of [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"). It is quite small
and looks like it might be intended to [unlock](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink")
either a small chest or a desk drawer. It is hard to tell.*

**Keyword(s):** key, silver.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 27-29.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 5 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):**
[anti-neutral](/wiki/Anti-Neutral_Flag "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

This [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") [unlocks](/wiki/Unlock "wikilink") a
large oak desk in The Main Desk.
