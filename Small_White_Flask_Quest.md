From [Museum Curator](/wiki/Museum_Curator "wikilink") go \[s\]w. Get sandle
from the bush. Find the monked with the name "an annoyed monk". He has
no unique keyword so you will have to wander around to find him. When
you enter the room he is he will complain about his missing sandle.
Return it to him to receive a [Small White
Flask](/wiki/Small_White_Flask "wikilink").
