*A small wooden lockbox is here, with a combination lock.*

[`Capacity`](/wiki/Container_Values "wikilink")` is 25 lbs.`

**Keyword(s):** small, wooden, lockbox.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 0-52.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[container](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 1000 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 3 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):**
[dark](/wiki/Dark_Flag "wikilink"), [nolocate](Nolocate_Flag "wikilink").  

Comments
--------

These [containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") always spawn
[locked](/wiki/Container_Values "wikilink") but
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may unlock them either
by [breaking](/wiki/Break "wikilink") their locks using a
[wielded](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") [crushing
weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons_That_Crush "wikilink") or, for those
who have [learned](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") how to [pick
locks](/wiki/Pick_Lock "wikilink"), by [picking](Pick "wikilink") their
locks—either with difficulty by hand or, for those who have
[learned](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") [rogue lore](Rogue_Lore "wikilink"), with
ease while [holding](/wiki/Hold "wikilink") a [chest
pick](/wiki/Category%3A_Chest_Picks "wikilink").

Lockboxes also randomly spawn armed with one of serveral kinds of
[traps](/wiki/Container_Values "wikilink") that will spring when they are
[broken](/wiki/Break "wikilink") and/or [picked](Pick "wikilink") and/or
[opened](/wiki/Open "wikilink"), possibly with lethal consequences; however,
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who have
[learned](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") how to [disarm
traps](/wiki/Disarm_Traps "wikilink") may both [find](Inspect "wikilink") and
[dismantle](/wiki/Dismantle "wikilink") such
[traps](/wiki/Container_Values "wikilink") while [holding](/wiki/Hold "wikilink") a
[trap-disarming kit](/wiki/Category%3A_Trap-Disarming_Kits "wikilink").

Lockboxes practially always contain some small random amount of
[money](/wiki/Category%3A_Money "wikilink"). Some lockboxes also contain one
or (rarely) more random [gemstones](/wiki/Category%3A_Gemstones "wikilink")
and/or "[vials of
healing](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions_In_Lockboxes "wikilink")".
