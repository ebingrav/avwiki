*Although not the most sporting attack, walloping an enemy upside the
head with the handle of a [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") has
been known to make even the toughest of foes stagger. The
[heavier](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink") a
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") is, the more effective smashing
with it will be. You can only smash with
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") with normal handles, which
doesn't include [hafted](/wiki/Category%3A_Hafted_Weapons "wikilink"),
[chain](/wiki/Category%3A_Chain_Weapons "wikilink") or
[double-headed](/wiki/Category%3A_Double_Weapons "wikilink") weapons.*

Prerequisite(s): [Bash](/wiki/Bash "wikilink").

Syntax: smash &lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
