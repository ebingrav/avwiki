*Smedley Fanshaw came to [Durr Valley](:Category:_Mt_Durr.md "wikilink")
around the time that [Bomli](Bomli.md "wikilink") opened the Wayhouse.
Very little is known about Smedley's previous career, and he does not
speak of it. He takes evident delight in maintaining the Wayhouse and
tending its beautiful flower gardens, and many of the locals consult him
for gardening advice.*

**Keywords:** gnome, Smedley, Fanshaw, gardener.  
**[Level](Level.md "wikilink"):** 22.  
**[Alignment](Alignment.md "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs.md "wikilink").
