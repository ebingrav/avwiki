*Smedley Fanshaw came to [Durr Valley](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")
around the time that [Bomli](/wiki/Bomli "wikilink") opened the Wayhouse. Very
little is known about Smedley's previous career, and he does not speak
of it. He takes evident delight in maintaining the Wayhouse and tending
its beautiful flower gardens, and many of the locals consult him for
gardening advice.*

**Keywords:** gnome, Smedley, Fanshaw, gardener.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 22.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
