*Smith is the command to instruct a [smith
shopkeeper](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink") to
[repair](/wiki/Repair "wikilink") a damaged
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") for you. To find out the cost
required to fix your item, use [APPRAISE](/wiki/Appraise "wikilink"). Like
[shops](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink"), you can
[haggle](/wiki/Haggle "wikilink") the price as well as have different costs
for different [Smiths](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink"). If you don't have
enough [gold](/wiki/Gold_In_Hand "wikilink"), the
[Smith](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink") will [repair](/wiki/Repair "wikilink")
your [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") with whatever amount of
[gold](/wiki/Gold_In_Hand "wikilink") you DO have to a certain extent.*

Syntax: smith &lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.

See also [Smiths](/wiki/Category%3A_Smiths "wikilink").
