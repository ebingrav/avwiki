-   Find the [crumbling servitor](/wiki/Crumbling_Servitor "wikilink") in the
    northeast section (room Stables East).
-   Kill it. Randomly an [earth
    elemental](/wiki/Enraged_Earth_Elemental "wikilink") will be released.
-   Kill it and it will drop its [essence](/wiki/Earth_Essence "wikilink").
-   Take it to [Xohd](/wiki/Xohd "wikilink") by going w\[s\]s\[e\]ds.
-   Give it to him and receive a [smooth stone
    tablet](/wiki/Smooth_Stone_Tablet "wikilink").
