*The Sithiss snakemen were a puissant and sneaky force. Fighting
one-on-one was their strength and also their weakness. Defeating these
substantial beings would be a life-changing accomplishment.*

**Keywords:** snakeman, warrior.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 42.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** [very
aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[poisonous](/wiki/Biting_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink").

Comments
--------

Note that the snakemen can [tail](/wiki/Racial_Tail "wikilink"), and hence
stun you in combat.  
Only one of the snakeman warriors holds the crown. The others are
holding various junk items.  

Directions
----------

**[Area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"):** [Gem of
Destiny](/wiki/Category%3A_Gem_Of_Destiny "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Gem_Of_Destiny_Map "wikilink")).  
There are multiple snakemen warriors, and some
[wander](/wiki/Wandering_Mobs "wikilink"). The snakemen can be reached by
giving the [Key Shaped Sword](/wiki/Key_Shaped_Sword "wikilink") to the
[GateKeeper](/wiki/GateKeeper "wikilink").
