*If you successfully SNEAK, then you can move in and out of
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") without being noticed.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: sneak.

See also [Racial Sneak](/wiki/Racial_Sneak "wikilink") and [Gear That Applies
'Sneak'](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear_That_Applies_'Sneak' "wikilink").
