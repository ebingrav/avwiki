*The first step along the road to becoming a lethal Assassin is to
master the simple art of the sneak attack. Using the element of
surprise, a thief will be able to hit his foe harder in the first round
of combat. Any [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") can be used for
this attack, however, it will be less effective on enemies who are
especially alert, such as [aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink") or
[wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink") monsters and
[guards](/wiki/Townguard_Mobs "wikilink"). Surprise attack is affected by your
[race](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink")'s innate stealthiness. Large or
clumsy [races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") may be spotted moving in
for the kill.*

Prerequisite(s): [Sneak](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink").

Syntax: [surprise](/wiki/Surprise "wikilink")
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
