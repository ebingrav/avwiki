*Sniping is a combination of the [longshot](/wiki/Longshot "wikilink") and
[vital shot](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink") abilities that only
[assassins](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink") can master. Sniping is
hard, but can deliver considerable damage to enemies, as well as cripple
them from a safe distance. Note that specifying a region to hit is
optional. If none is specified, assume the
[assassin](/wiki/Category%3A_Assassins "wikilink") will aim for the vital area
at which he has the clearest shot. This is somewhat easier than choosing
to aim for a specific area.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: snipe <direction> &lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;
<region>.
