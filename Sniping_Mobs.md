[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"), except when fighting, will
periodically [longshot](/wiki/Longshot "wikilink") any players that they scan
in adjoining [rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").
