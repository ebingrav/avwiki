*Sol was once the pupil of [Aelmon](/wiki/Aelmon "wikilink"). He then heard
about the strange shaman known only as [Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink") and came to
study under him. What he found is a secret between the two but Sol
jumped at the chance to be near the man again.*

**Keywords:** Sol.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[healer](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").

Comments
--------

Sol is located in [Stonehall](/wiki/Category%3A_Stonehall "wikilink") and he
is the default [recall](/wiki/Recall "wikilink") point for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") between
[levels](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 5 and 14. He is virtually identical to
[Nom](/wiki/Nom "wikilink"), selling [bandages](/wiki/Some_Bandages "wikilink"),
[wax candles](/wiki/Wax_Candle "wikilink"), and [identify
scrolls](/wiki/Identify_Scroll "wikilink"). He also sells [healing
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Heal_Characters "wikilink")
if you type '[heal](/wiki/Heal_(command) "wikilink")' in his presence, and
will spell up players up to [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 10 with
[armor](/wiki/Armor_(spell) "wikilink") and [bless](Bless "wikilink").

Sol is involved in [Stonehall](/wiki/Category%3A_Stonehall "wikilink")'s [Old
Parchment Quest](/wiki/Old_Parchment_Quest "wikilink").
