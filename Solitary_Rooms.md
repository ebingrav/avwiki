[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Types "wikilink") prevent both players and
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") from entering them while one player
and/or [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") is already within them.

See also [Private Rooms](/wiki/Private_Rooms "wikilink").
