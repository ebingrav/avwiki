*The Soul Squid has 8 tentacles, each tipped with a glowing chitinous
point that looks extremely unpleasant. The squid seems to have got an
idea about embedding those points into you, something which you may want
to avoid...*

**Keywords:** soul, squid.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 26.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"), [very
aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").

Comments
--------

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must pass a locked
[door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") to reach this
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"); to do this, they will need to either
[bash](/wiki/Bashdoor "wikilink") it open or [unlock](Unlock "wikilink") it
using its respective [key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") may obtain its
[key](/wiki/Category%3A_Keys "wikilink") by either [buying](Buy "wikilink") [a
mine pass level one](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One "wikilink") from [the
receptionist](/wiki/Receptionist "wikilink") or completing [Mt.
Durr](/wiki/Category%3A_Mt_Durr "wikilink")'s [Mine Pass Level One
Quest](/wiki/Mine_Pass_Level_One_Quest "wikilink").

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") need [water
breathing](/wiki/Water_Breathing "wikilink") to reach this
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") without risking drowning. [Old
Brort](/wiki/Old_Brort "wikilink"), who stands nearby, has [a kettle of green
tea](/wiki/Kettle_Of_Green_Tea "wikilink") that can provide this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") when [quaffed](Quaff "wikilink").

[Skinning](/wiki/Skin "wikilink") the soul squid's corpse yields [severed
tentacle of a soul squid](/wiki/Severed_Tentacle_Of_A_Soul_Squid "wikilink").
