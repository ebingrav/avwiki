*An orb of deepest black split only by a huge mouth, the spawner is
responsible for all the denizens roaming the cavern. Locked within its
cave, it routinely spits out new monsters, which then slip through the
interlocking weave of stone.*

**Keywords:** spawner, orb.  
**[Level](Level.md "wikilink"):** 70.  
**[Alignment](Alignment.md "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](:Category:_Mob_Types.md "wikilink"):**
[kleptomaniac](Thieving_Mobs.md "wikilink"),
[necromancer](Corpse-Animating_Mobs.md "wikilink"),
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs.md "wikilink"), [very
aggressive](Aggressive_Mobs.md "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](Detect_Hidden.md "wikilink"),
[fly](Fly.md "wikilink"), [infravision](Infravision.md "wikilink").
