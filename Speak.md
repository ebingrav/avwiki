''SPEAK is used to either identify the
[languages](/wiki/Category%3A_Languages "wikilink") you currently know (type
SPEAK), or to change from one [tongue](/wiki/Category%3A_Languages "wikilink")
to another (type SPEAK [COMMON](/wiki/Common_Language "wikilink") for
example). Only those with that
[Language](/wiki/Category%3A_Languages "wikilink") ability or [an
Immortal](/wiki/Category%3A_Immortals "wikilink") can understand you, others
will only hear/see garbage if said (The only exception to this is
[COMMON](/wiki/Common_Language "wikilink"), which all players can understand).

Syntax: speak &lt;[language](/wiki/Category%3A_Languages "wikilink")&gt;.

This [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") only affects
communication via [*in-character*
channels](/wiki/Category%3A_In-Character_Channels "wikilink"); any
communication via [*out-of-character*
channels](/wiki/Category%3A_Out-of-Character_Channels "wikilink") will remain
entirely unaffected by it.

See also [Languages](/wiki/Category%3A_Languages "wikilink").
