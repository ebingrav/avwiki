*While mimicking the touch of a spectre,
[Shadowfists](/wiki/Category%3A_Shadowfists "wikilink") ignore their target's
attempts to [parry](/wiki/Parry "wikilink") or [shield
block](/wiki/Shield_Block "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Demonic Intent](/wiki/Demonic_Intent "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](/wiki/Stance "wikilink") spectral fang.

See also [Vampire Fang](/wiki/Vampire_Fang "wikilink").
