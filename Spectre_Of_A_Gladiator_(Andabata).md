*The Andabata fighting style requires both patience and savagry, and is
perhaps the most difficult of the gladitorial styles to master. At first
glance, it looks like there are no handicaps at all, until one notices
that his helmet is without eyeholes. Instead, they are forced to find
their opponent purely by sound.*
