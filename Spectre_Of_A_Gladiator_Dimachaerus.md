*The Dimachaerus form of gladitorial fighting appears to have been an
early precursor to the current [roguish](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink")
mercenary, a master of the piercing dagger.*

**Keywords:** spectre, gladiator, Dimachaerus.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 22.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [pass door](/wiki/Pass_Door "wikilink").

but only this one has keyword Dimachaerus.
