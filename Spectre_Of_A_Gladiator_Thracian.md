*A captured warrior from a now extinct clan, this man now ghost is all
that is left from a forgotten era. While his flesh looks nearly living,
his eyes are dead, like the owner of them had died long before he was
killed. The eyes of a man turned slave.*

**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 22.

[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
