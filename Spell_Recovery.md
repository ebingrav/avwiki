*This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") reduces the lag
you get when you lose concentration in [casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") a
spell. Use of this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is
automatic once [practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Magic Lore](/wiki/Magic_Lore "wikilink"),
[Meditation](/wiki/Meditation "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") can be rather
useful to [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") that are both
of spellcasting [classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") (like
[psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink"),
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink"), and
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink")) and also of
[races](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink") that are relatively prone to
[spellcasting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") failure (like
[giants](/wiki/Giants "wikilink"), [goblins](Goblins "wikilink"), and
[kobolds](/wiki/Kobolds "wikilink")). Especially when soloing, a quick
recovery from a failed [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") at a
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") may make a difference between killing
this [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") and being killed by it.
