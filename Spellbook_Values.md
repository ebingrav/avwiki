[Spellbook](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellbooks "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have four
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **[Spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") Taught:** This value
    determines which [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") this
    [spellbook](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellbooks "wikilink") will teach to those
    who read it.

<!-- -->

-   **Reading [Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** This value determines what
    [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink")
    [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") must reach before
    they may read this [spellbook](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellbooks "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **Training Gain:** This value determines what percentage this
    [spellbook](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellbooks "wikilink") will raise, each time
    it is read, its readers' adeptness at its
    [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") (see above) up to a certain
    limit (see below).

<!-- -->

-   **Training Limit:** This value determines what *maximum* percentage
    this [spellbook](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellbooks "wikilink") may raise its
    readers' adeptness at its [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
    (see above).

See also [Spellbooks](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellbooks "wikilink").
