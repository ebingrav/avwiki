[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will cast defensive and/or
offensive [spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") during combat. Such
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") have various subtypes; some cast
only [cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") spells, some cast only
[mage](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") spells, some cast only
[psonicist](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") spells (perhaps
[telekinetic
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Telekinetic_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") in
particular), some cast only [sorcerer](/wiki/Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink")
spells, and some cast only "undead" spells.
