*With a silent scream, a little black magic, and a whole lot of
mischief, a [Sorcerer](/wiki/Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink") can make a
whole crowd of people suddenly stop talking... literally!
[Cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") this little treasure and set it loose. Silence
never felt so bad...*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'sphere of silence'.
