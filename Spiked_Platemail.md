` Modifies armor class by -8 continuous.`

Slot: on body  
Base: 2-3  
Level: 10  
Weight: 30  
Mob: [Fizzdop](/wiki/Fizzdop "wikilink")  
Area: [Fizzdop's Lair](/wiki/Category%3A_Fizzdop's_Lair "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Fizzdop's_Lair_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob is portable. Area is off of the [White Stone
Road](/wiki/Category%3AWhite_Stone_Road "wikilink"), so it's not too far from
[Sol](/wiki/Sol "wikilink"). There are no exits from Fizzdrop's Lair itself,
so you must recall to get out.
