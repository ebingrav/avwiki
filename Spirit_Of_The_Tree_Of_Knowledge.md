Comments
--------

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") may
[practice](Practice "wikilink") their [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") 1-5
[skills and spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") with this
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). They will have to visit a different
[trainer](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink"), like
[Carl](/wiki/Carl "wikilink"), to [practice](Practice "wikilink")
higher-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") [skills and
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink").
