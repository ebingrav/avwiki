This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink") has the object
[spiritlinked](/wiki/Spiritlink "wikilink") to its owner. All damage done to
it will be transferred to its owner. This link is destroyed when it is
dropped or placed in a bag.
