*All [Stormlord](/wiki/Category%3A_Stormlords "wikilink")
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") manipulate weather in a very
localized manner. They can call forth a blizzard in the middle of a
summer meadow, or a shower of rain while miles underground. They must be
sustained, and will end early if the caster is struck, moved, or enters
a command. Spring rain provides a magical refeshing affect, granting
[mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") and [movement](Move_Points "wikilink") to
all in the [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). It also reduces all
fire damage. This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") affects ally
and foe alike, and sometimes lingers briefly after the
[Stormlord](/wiki/Category%3A_Stormlords "wikilink") stops controlling it.*

Prerequisite(s): ?.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'spring rain'.
