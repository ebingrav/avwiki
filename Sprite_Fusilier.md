*A [sprite](Sprites.md "wikilink")
[fusilier](:Category:_Fusiliers.md "wikilink") beams warmly at you.
"Fascinating," she exclaims, "have you examined these marks? No
[sprite](Sprites.md "wikilink") has laid eyes upon these glyphs in ages!
I'm sure that [the Baron](Baron_Amier_Sarphyre.md "wikilink") will be
intrigued to learn all about them. But I digress; let me introduce
myself..." This [sprite](Sprites.md "wikilink") then proudly identifies
herself as one of three [sprites](Sprites.md "wikilink") sent here by
[Baron Sarphyre](Baron_Amier_Sarphyre.md "wikilink") to help
[Silvanus](Centaur_Ranger.md "wikilink") in exterminating
[Westwood](:Category:_Westwood.md "wikilink")'s darklin infestation.
When [Silvanus](Centaur_Ranger.md "wikilink") had appealed to the [Oak
Circle](:Category:_Oak_Circle.md "wikilink")'s
[sprites](Sprites.md "wikilink") for aid, explaining to them how
[Westwood](:Category:_Westwood.md "wikilink")'s darklin were spreading
and would eventually threaten their home too, they had appealed
reluctantly in turn to [Baron
Sarphyre](Baron_Amier_Sarphyre.md "wikilink"), who was all too happy to
respond, as he hoped that it would help to "legitimize" his claims of
authority over them. "I don't want to bore you with
[sprite](Sprites.md "wikilink") politics, though," she adds, "and,
besides, we all have much more important things to concern ourselves
with now, like preparing ourselves to attack the darklin. May
[Roixa](Roixa.md "wikilink") be with you, my new friend."*
