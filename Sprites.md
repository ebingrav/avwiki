These faeries have long been regarded as the peaceful guardians of the
forest and respected by all. Their extreme magical nature and
intelligence counteracts their weakness in stature and stamina. In the
past their place has been solely the forest, but as the population has
grown, and settlements have dug into their homeland, some have found the
need to leave their homes and seek out goals...or revenge against those
who may have forced them out.

Sprites are extremely potent spellcasters. They can cast spells more
effectively than just about any other race found in this realm. They are
quick of wit, and extremely fast on their feet as well. They are
physically the weakest of the races in this realm however, and though
they may not get hit often, no damage they take can be considered to be
light.

Sprites all have the racial ability to dodge attacks, and have the
innate ability to fly as well. Sprites can be quite mischievous, and
have all developed the ability to turn invisible at will.

This race is only available through remort.
