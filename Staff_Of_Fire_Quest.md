-   Find the [scorched servitor](/wiki/Scorched_Servitor "wikilink") in the
    northeast section (room Stables East).
-   Kill it. Randomly an [fire
    elemental](/wiki/Furious_Fire_Elemental "wikilink") will be released.
-   Kill it and it will drop its [essence](/wiki/Fire_Essence "wikilink").
-   Take it to [Zash](/wiki/Zash "wikilink") by going w\[s\]3w2swdes.
-   Give it to him and receive a [staff of
    fire](/wiki/Staff_Of_Fire "wikilink").
