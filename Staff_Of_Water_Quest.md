-   Find the [rust servitor](/wiki/Rusty_Servitor "wikilink") in the northeast
    section (room Stables West).
-   Kill it. Randomly an [water
    elemental](/wiki/Wrathful_Water_Elemental "wikilink") will be released.
-   Kill it and it will drop its [essence](/wiki/Water_Essence "wikilink").
-   Take it to [Vymm](/wiki/Vymm "wikilink") by going \[s\]3w2swdn.
-   Give it to her and receive a [staff of
    water](/wiki/Staff_Of_Water "wikilink").
