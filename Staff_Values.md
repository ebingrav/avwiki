[Staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have four
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **[Spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") Level:** This value
    determines what level this [staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink")'s
    [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") will be when it is
    [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **[Spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") [Cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"):**
    This value determines which [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
    this [staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") will
    [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") when it is [zapped](Zap "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **Charges, Maximum:** This value determines how many times this
    [staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") may be
    [brandished](/wiki/Brandish "wikilink") *at most* before being consumed.
    It will decrease by one (unless it is larger than 10) each time this
    [staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") is
    [recharged](/wiki/Recharge "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **Charges, Current:** This value determines how many *remaining*
    times this [staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") may be
    [brandished](/wiki/Brandish "wikilink") before being consumed. It will
    initally (usually) equal this
    [staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink")'s maximum charges (above) but
    will decrease by one each time this
    [staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") is
    [brandished](/wiki/Brandish "wikilink"). It will be reset to equal this
    [staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink")'s *new* maximum charges each
    time this [staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") is
    [recharged](/wiki/Recharge "wikilink").

See also [Staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink").
