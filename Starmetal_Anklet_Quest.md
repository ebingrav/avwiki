General Directions
------------------

From [the memory of Tazimarc](/wiki/Memory_of_Tazimarc "wikilink"):

-   Kill [the memory of
    Tazimarc](/wiki/Memory_of_Tazimarc "wikilink") (nospell).

<!-- -->

-   Get [an acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(1_of_3) "wikilink") from its corpse.

From [the ghost of Thalimus](/wiki/Ghost_of_Thalimus "wikilink"):

-   Give [an acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(1_of_3) "wikilink") to [the ghost of
    Thalimus](/wiki/Ghost_of_Thalimus "wikilink") to receive [an
    acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(2_of_3) "wikilink").

From [the ghost of Abbot Temian](/wiki/Ghost_of_Abbot_Temian "wikilink"):

-   Give [an acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(2_of_3) "wikilink") to [the ghost of Abbot
    Temian](/wiki/Ghost_of_Abbot_Temian "wikilink") to receive [an
    acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(3_of_3) "wikilink").

From [Abbot Cassinus](/wiki/Abbot_Cassinus "wikilink"):

-   Give [an acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(3_of_3) "wikilink") to [Abbot
    Cassinus](/wiki/Abbot_Cassinus "wikilink") to receive [Starmetal
    Anklet](/wiki/Starmetal_Anklet "wikilink").

Step-by-step Detailed Directions
--------------------------------

From Aelmon:

-   Go 15e, \[s\] to get to the entrance of [Defender's
    Graveyard](/wiki/Category%3ADefender's_Graveyard "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Go 2s, w, 2s, e, n. (An alternative is 2s, e, 2s, w, n.)

<!-- -->

-   Kill [a twisted wood spirit](/wiki/Twisted_Wood_Spirit "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Enter hole that is created when the [twisted wood
    spirit](/wiki/Twisted_Wood_Spirit "wikilink") dies.

<!-- -->

-   Go s.

<!-- -->

-   Kill [an elemental guardian](/wiki/Elemental_Guardian "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Get [a jade rod](/wiki/Jade_Rod "wikilink") from its corpse.

<!-- -->

-   Unlock patina using [a jade rod](/wiki/Jade_Rod "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Open patina.

<!-- -->

-   Go d.

<!-- -->

-   Kill [the memory of
    Tazimarc](/wiki/Memory_of_Tazimarc "wikilink") (nospell).

<!-- -->

-   Get [an acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(1_of_3) "wikilink") from its corpse.

<!-- -->

-   Go u, n.

<!-- -->

-   Push boulder to get out.

<!-- -->

-   Go u, s, w, 2n, e. (An alternative is u, s, e, 2n, w.)

<!-- -->

-   Push eye.

<!-- -->

-   Go d.

<!-- -->

-   Give [an acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(1_of_3) "wikilink") to [the ghost of
    Thalimus](/wiki/Ghost_of_Thalimus "wikilink") to receive [an
    acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(2_of_3) "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Push lever to open the exit to get out.

<!-- -->

-   Go u, 3w, s.

<!-- -->

-   Enter tomb.

<!-- -->

-   Give [an acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(2_of_3) "wikilink") to [the ghost of Abbot
    Temian](/wiki/Ghost_of_Abbot_Temian "wikilink") to receive [an
    acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(3_of_3) "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Teleport to [Abbot Cassinus](/wiki/Abbot_Cassinus "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   Give [an acorn](/wiki/Acorn_(3_of_3) "wikilink") to [Abbot
    Cassinus](/wiki/Abbot_Cassinus "wikilink") to receive [Starmetal
    Anklet](/wiki/Starmetal_Anklet "wikilink").
