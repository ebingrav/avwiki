*An imposing statue of the mythical beast Cerberus stands here. Built
out of solid obsidian, this statue has been brought to life and set to
guard the [door](:Category:_Doors.md "wikilink") to [the veldri
leader](Eylidre,_Leader_Of_The_Veldri.md "wikilink"). Its three heads
glare at you and snarl, but make no motion to attack you.*
