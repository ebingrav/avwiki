*An imposing statue of the mythical beast Cerberus stands here. Built
out of solid obsidian, this statue has been brought to life and set to
guard the [door](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") to [the veldri
leader](/wiki/Eylidre,_Leader_Of_The_Veldri "wikilink"). Its three heads glare
at you and snarl, but make no motion to attack you.*

**Keywords:** statue, beast, Cerberus.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 40.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[fire-breathing](/wiki/Breathing_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").
