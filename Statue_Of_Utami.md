The statue is so lifelike you gaze in amazement. Never before have you
seen anyone as beautiful as the woman who this statue resembles.  
Dyah Yuyun Utami, Ancient Goddess of Love and Youth.  
She is shown here with her arms spread out before her, ready to embrace
you, in a long white gown that flows around her perfect body.
