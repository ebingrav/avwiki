*Steadfast [Berserkers](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") cannot be
deterred from finishing their battles. This Rite grants its Master
immunity to [calm](/wiki/Calm "wikilink"), [dread](Dread "wikilink") and
[awe](/wiki/Awe "wikilink"). Since it works on the enemy, rather than the
[Berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink"), the
[innocence](/wiki/Innocence "wikilink") [spell](Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
is unaffected.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
