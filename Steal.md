*STEAL attempts to steal coins, or an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), from a
[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). In order to STEAL successfully, you
must [practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") the appropriate
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"). You cannot steal an
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") if the
[mobile](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") is wearing it.*

Prerequisite(s): [Sneak](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink").

Syntax: steal coins &lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.  
Syntax: steal &lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
