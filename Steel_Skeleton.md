*STEEL SKELETON causes the caster's very bones to become as unbreakable
as steel. This technique is so advanced that only
[Psions](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") can ever learn it. This
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is a [BIOCENTRIC Psionic
Spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Biocentric_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). It can only
be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on others if the Caster first masters the art
of [BIO EMPATHY](/wiki/Bio_Empathy "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Iron Skin](/wiki/Iron_Skin "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'steel skeleton'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
