A long spear made of shining steel leans aginst the wall. Object 'spear
steel rip' type throwing weapon, extra flags none. Weight 10, value
1404, level 51. Object Quality (40 / 40 hps) It does 44 to 54 points of
damage.

`Modifies damage roll by 5 continuous.`  
`Modifies moves by 250 continuous.`

This item seems to be throwing weapon. It weighs approximately 10 lbs.
This item appears meant to be wielded as a weapon. It would make a good
melee weapon.

Carried by: [Survivor Guard](/wiki/Survivor_Guard "wikilink") (the one east of
Jackal)
