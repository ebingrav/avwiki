Comments
--------

Stein, as an [assassin mob](/wiki/Assassin_Mobs "wikilink"), will initiate
combat even against [sneaking](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink")
[hiding](/wiki/Hide "wikilink") [invisible](Invis "wikilink")
[heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink").

Furthermore, her "roommate," [Wielmann](/wiki/Wielmann "wikilink"), as a
[guild guard mob](/wiki/Guild_Guard_Mobs "wikilink"), will not only
[assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink") her in combat but will attempt to
[blackjack](/wiki/Blackjack "wikilink")
non-[rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") and then dump them
unconscious inside [Midgaard
Country](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard_Country "wikilink")'s barn.
