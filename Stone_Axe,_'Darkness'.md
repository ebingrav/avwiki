*This [weapon](:Category:_Melee_Weapons.md "wikilink") was definitely
not an apprentice's work: the haft of shiny steel is inlaid with veins
of durrite, appearing as if grown in a slow and careful process within
the depth of the earth, and the axe-head is a slice of black stone.
Where stone and steel join the thin bands of durrite which extend out
from the haft wrap themselves around the dark slab, which shows a
perfect reflection of everything around. Quite surprisingly the
mirror-image seems to get clearer and more pronounced in the places
where the original fades into shadows.*
