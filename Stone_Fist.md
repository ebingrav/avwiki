*A master of the Stone Fist technique can temporarily increase the
weight and hardness of their hands. The heaviness of their fists allows
their blows to rain down with more force, but it also causes them to
miss more frequently than they would otherwise.*

Syntax: cast 'stone fist'

This spell gives +2 damage roll, -2 hit roll, and modifies open hand
attacks with blunt damage.
