This quest starts at the [Midgaard Royal
Surveyor](/wiki/Midgaard_Royal_Surveyor "wikilink"). Read his description.

-   Kill the [Stone Serpent Of
    Keresh](/wiki/Stone_Serpent_Of_Keresh "wikilink") to get the [Stone
    Tail](/wiki/Stone_Tail "wikilink").
-   Give the [Stone Tail](/wiki/Stone_Tail "wikilink") to the [Midgaard Royal
    Surveyor](/wiki/Midgaard_Royal_Surveyor "wikilink").
-   Give the [Scale Of Stone](/wiki/Scale_Of_Stone "wikilink") to
    [Paige](/wiki/Paige "wikilink").
-   Give the [Letter Of Commendation](/wiki/Letter_Of_Commendation "wikilink")
    to the [Lord Of The Hunt](/wiki/Lord_Of_The_Hunt "wikilink").

The reward is a moonstone, worth 6000 gold.
