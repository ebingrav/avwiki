*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") [protects the
caster](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")
by altering their [Armor Class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink"). Stone Skin
provides 40 points of [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") change and can only
be [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on self.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'stone skin'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is included in
[Foci](Foci "wikilink"). Its [AC](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") bonus may be
modified by its [caster](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")'s choice of
[worship](/wiki/Worship "wikilink").

See also [Stone Skin Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Stone_Skin_Gear "wikilink").
