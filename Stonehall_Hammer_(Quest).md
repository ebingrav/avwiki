*The hammer has a white marble head, etched with decorative golden
runes. An ornate brass fixture attaches the it to a shiny handle,
perfectly balanced for one-handed use, but also long enough to be hafted
with both hands. The Stonehall Hammer is given by Silverbeard to only
the finest adventurers he encounters. While the soft stone doesn't make
a fantastic weapon, the hammer is more of a status symbol, and a sign of
hope to any who sees it.*
