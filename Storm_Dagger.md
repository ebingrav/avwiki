*Beautiful wisps of white and blue cover the handle of the dagger,
depicting a storm in all its rage. The white swirls together to form
clouds, while the blue provides the backdrop of the sky. In contrast to
this, the dagger's metal is covered in dried blood.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 11 to 32 (average 21).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 3 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 6 continuous.`
