*Strength is responsible for the [amount of weight a character can
carry](/wiki/Carrying_Capacity "wikilink"). A higher strength also increases
[hitroll](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink") & [damroll](Damage_Roll "wikilink"),
resulting in higher damage to the enemy.*
