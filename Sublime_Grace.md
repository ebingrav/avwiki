*The [Bladedancer](/wiki/Category%3A_Bladedancers "wikilink") fighting style
requires two identical [weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").
They must be the exact same item, not merely named the same or the same
[weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"). If these conditions are met, the
[Bladedancer](/wiki/Category%3A_Bladedancers "wikilink") with Sublime Grace
can use their offhand [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink")
just as effectively as their main one, in terms of the number of hit
attempts they gain per round. Also, because the
[Dancer](/wiki/Category%3A_Bladedancers "wikilink") isn't favoring either
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink"), they become harder to
[disarm](/wiki/Disarm "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Dual](/wiki/Dual "wikilink"), [Poise](Poise "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
