*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") summons a
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") from almost anywhere into
your [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").
[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who are fighting may not
be summoned. Be careful what you summon; it would be unwise to transport
high-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") [mobiles](Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")
into low-[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") [areas](Category%3A_Areas "wikilink")
or to move a common [portal](/wiki/Portal "wikilink") point.*

Prerequiste(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'summon'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")/[mob](Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is handy on occasion but is
not as useful as many others.
