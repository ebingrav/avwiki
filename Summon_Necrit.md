*You can summon a necrit familiar to float nearby. The necrit is a
creature sensitive to cursed places and can tell you if you stand within
a [no-recall](/wiki/Cursed_Rooms "wikilink"),
[no-quit](/wiki/No-Quit_Rooms "wikilink"), or
[anti-magic](/wiki/Anti-Magic_Rooms "wikilink")
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). You will see its report every time
you [look](/wiki/Look "wikilink"), as with
[exits](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Exits "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'summon necrit'.
