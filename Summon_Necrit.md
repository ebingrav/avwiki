*You can summon a necrit familiar to float nearby. The necrit is a
creature sensitive to cursed places and can tell you if you stand within
a [no-recall](Cursed_Rooms "wikilink"),
[no-quit](No-Quit_Rooms "wikilink"), or
[anti-magic](Anti-Magic_Rooms "wikilink")
[room](:Category:_Rooms "wikilink"). You will see its report every time
you [look](Look "wikilink"), as with
[exits](:Category:_Room_Exits "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'summon necrit'.
