*The surefoot will never fall. While in Surefoot Stance, you have a much
higher resistance to knockdown attacks. Surefoot Stance ends early if
you use an attack [skill](:Category:_Skills.md "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Bash](Bash.md "wikilink"), [Trip](Trip.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](Stance.md "wikilink") surefoot.

See also [Protective Stance](Protective_Stance.md "wikilink") and
[Relentless Stance](Relentless_Stance.md "wikilink").
