*The surefoot will never fall. While in Surefoot Stance, you have a much
higher resistance to knockdown attacks. Surefoot Stance ends early if
you use an attack [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Bash](/wiki/Bash "wikilink"), [Trip](Trip "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](/wiki/Stance "wikilink") surefoot.

See also [Protective Stance](/wiki/Protective_Stance "wikilink") and
[Relentless Stance](/wiki/Relentless_Stance "wikilink").
