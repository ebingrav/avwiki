*SURGE is a toggle [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") which,
when turned ON, increases the damage you can do with [combat
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Harm_Mobs "wikilink"). You
may also set the power level (1 - 5) at which you wish to surge (i.e.
the size of the damage modifier). However, this added energy comes at a
price - the [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink") required to
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") your [combat
spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Harm_Mobs "wikilink") will
increase in proportion to the power level at which you set surge. The
[maximum surge power level](/wiki/Surging_Ability "wikilink") is attainable by
[sorcerers](/wiki/Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink") and
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink"). Other
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") have a lower
[limit](/wiki/Surging_Ability "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Magic Lore](/wiki/Magic_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: surge on.  
Syntax: surge off.  
Syntax: surge <power:1-5>.  
See also [Augment](/wiki/Augment "wikilink") and
[Quicken](/wiki/Quicken "wikilink"), as well as [Surging
Ability](/wiki/Surging_Ability "wikilink").
