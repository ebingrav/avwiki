*As you study the blade of this impressive sword, you can read the words
"[Venghilrim](Venghilrim "wikilink")'s Doom" etched into it in small but
well crafted letters. It is said that a great
[knight](:Category:_Paladins "wikilink") took it upon himself to attempt
to slay the [dragon](Dragons "wikilink")
[Venghilrim](Venghilrim "wikilink"), and had a special blade forged for
the task. Unfortunately, the sword was never drawn, as the
[knight](:Category:_Paladins "wikilink") was snapped in half by the
[dragon](Dragons "wikilink") before he had a chance. The
[dragon](Dragons "wikilink") keeps this sword by him as a gloating
reminder to all who challenge him.*
