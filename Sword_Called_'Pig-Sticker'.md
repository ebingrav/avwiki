*This sword is [the captain of the frontier
guard](Frontier_Captain.md "wikilink")'s pride and joy. Once the
[orcs](Orcs.md "wikilink") began pestering [his
outpost](:Category:_Frontier_Outpost.md "wikilink"), he had this sword
fashioned and enchanted with money out of his own pocket. Given an
[orc](Orcs.md "wikilink")'s likeliness to a pig, he had the word
"Pig-Sticker" etched into the blade of this fine sword, and it has
spilled the blood of many pigs -- uhh, [orcs](Orcs.md "wikilink").*
