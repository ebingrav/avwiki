*This sword is [the captain of the frontier
guard](/wiki/Frontier_Captain "wikilink")'s pride and joy. Once the
[orcs](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink") began pestering [his
outpost](/wiki/Category%3A_Frontier_Outpost "wikilink"), he had this sword
fashioned and enchanted with money out of his own pocket. Given an
[orc](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink")'s likeliness to a pig, he had the word
"Pig-Sticker" etched into the blade of this fine sword, and it has
spilled the blood of many pigs -- uhh, [orcs](/wiki/Orcs "wikilink").*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 8 to 26 (average 17).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 2 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 4 continuous.`
