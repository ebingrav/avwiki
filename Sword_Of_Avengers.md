` Modifies strength by 2 continuous.`  
` Modifies constitution by 2 continuous.`  
` Modifies damage roll by 10 continuous.`  
` Modifies hit roll by 10 continuous.`

Flags: dark anti-good  
Level: 50-52  
Max: 45-50  
Weight: 13  
Weapon Type: One handed piercer  
Mob: [Sword Of Avengers (mob)](/wiki/Sword_Of_Avengers_(mob) "wikilink")  
Area: [Saint of the Blade
Temple](/wiki/Category%3A_Saint_Of_The_Blade_Temple "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Saint_Of_The_Blade_Temple_Map "wikilink"))  
To get to the [Saint Of The Blade
Temple](/wiki/Category%3ASaint_Of_The_Blade_Temple "wikilink") pp
[Ulrich](/wiki/Ulrich "wikilink") and go n, w, 4s, w

From there, go 2w, d, n, d, \[n\], {n} to get to the Sword. **Beware
aggie mobs that see through move hidden and invis**

Note that the final door is locked. The key is on [Archbishop
Morte](/wiki/Archbishop_Morte "wikilink"), a high level invis mob in the 2nd
to last room. Also note that sometimes there are [Dragoon
Lieutenants](/wiki/Dragoon_Lieutenants "wikilink") wandering the halls. They
are aggie and they stab, so low hp characters beware.
