*An elegant, double-bladed curved sword of shiny new blood mithril is
here.*

`Modifies damage roll by 40 continuous.`  
`Modifies hit roll by 20 continuous.`

Slot: Wield  
Weight: 3  
Flags: nolocate dull  
Plane: [Noctopia](/wiki/Category%3A_Noctopia "wikilink")  
Areas: [Shiftwatch Orb](/wiki/Category%3AShiftwatch_Orb "wikilink"), [Pits Of
Blood And Chain](/wiki/Category%3APits_Of_Blood_And_Chain "wikilink"), [Under
The Stars](/wiki/Category%3AUnder_The_Stars "wikilink"), [Obsidian Arena;
Floor](/wiki/Category%3AObsidian_Arena;_Floor "wikilink")

It is a double weapon which does slicing damage. Thus, it is
sharpenable.  
This weapon is obtained through a ticket quest. It requires:  
\* [Old Double-Curved Sword](/wiki/Old_Double-Curved_Sword "wikilink") from
[Yorimandil The Blind](/wiki/Yorimandil_The_Blind "wikilink") in either [Under
The Stars](/wiki/Category%3AUnder_The_Stars "wikilink") or [Obsidian Arena;
Floor](/wiki/Category%3AObsidian_Arena;_Floor "wikilink")  
\* [Blood-Mithril Shackle](/wiki/Blood-Mithril_Shackle "wikilink") from the
[Broken Oni](/wiki/Broken_Oni "wikilink") in [Pits Of Blood And
Chain](/wiki/Category%3APits_Of_Blood_And_Chain "wikilink")  
\* [Blood-Mithril Core](/wiki/Blood-Mithril_Core "wikilink") from the [Iron
Claw Sniper](/wiki/Iron_Claw_Sniper "wikilink") in [Shiftwatch
Orb](/wiki/Category%3AShiftwatch_Orb "wikilink").  
Turn in the Core to Greystone Boulderbuster.
