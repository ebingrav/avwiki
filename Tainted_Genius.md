*With agreements written with blood ages past,
[sorcerers](/wiki/Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink") have learned of an ability
to increase their learning through a bitter
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). With this tainted genius, your
[experience](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") is increased. Beware the
side-effects, however. Once your brilliance disappears, a taint remains.
This taint prevents you from [casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") certain
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") as well as penalizes you if you
die.*

Prerequisite(s): [Corruption](/wiki/Corruption "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'tainted genius'.

This spell doubles your experience gained for a short amount of time.
After that wears off you are subject to a variety of nasty status
effects. Anyone who knows more specifically what this spell does to you,
feel free to edit this page.
