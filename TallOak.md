*His branches somewhat barren, TallOak, the retired
[ent](Ents "wikilink") [ranger](:Category:_Rangers "wikilink"), has
joined the [centaur](Centaurs "wikilink") band to help protect its
charge. Old, even for an [ent](Ents "wikilink"), he can still contribute
and aid in the defense of [the
camp](:Category:_Centaur_Camp "wikilink"). No longer very mobile, he
sits on the path between the ruined village and the glade hoping to
protect [the secret](Baby_Unicorn "wikilink") the
[centaurs](Centaurs "wikilink") guard within.*

**Keywords:** TallOak, ent, ranger.
