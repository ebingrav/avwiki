*His branches somewhat barren, TallOak, the retired
[ent](/wiki/Ents "wikilink") [ranger](Category%3A_Rangers "wikilink"), has
joined the [centaur](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") band to help protect its
charge. Old, even for an [ent](/wiki/Ents "wikilink"), he can still contribute
and aid in the defense of [the
camp](/wiki/Category%3A_Centaur_Camp "wikilink"). No longer very mobile, he
sits on the path between the ruined village and the glade hoping to
protect [the secret](/wiki/Baby_Unicorn "wikilink") the
[centaurs](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink") guard within.*

**Keywords:** TallOak, ent, ranger.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 15.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
