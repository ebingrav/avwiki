*These bloody remains include most of two heads, minus their brains. One
head looks [somewhat reptilian](/wiki/Lizardmen "wikilink") while the other
(and fresher) head appears [centaurish](/wiki/Centaurs "wikilink"). The
mangled face of the latter seems a bit familiar to you, like someone
whom you passed or encountered a bit earlier.*

[ticket](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink").
