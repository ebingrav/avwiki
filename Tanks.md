*The term "tank" is used to define the member of a group (usually a
[warrior](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") or fighter type), who
initiates combat and attempts to absorb all of the
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")' damage. Typically a group will also
contain a [cleric/healer](/wiki/Healers "wikilink") type, as well as [extra
firepower](/wiki/Hitters "wikilink").*
