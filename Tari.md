*Tari is actually a [High Elf](/wiki/High_Elves "wikilink"), and they only
show their age when in the very last years of their long lives. Rumor
holds Tari is the last living being to have personally met
[Roixa](/wiki/Roixa "wikilink") before her accendancy to
[Godhood](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** tari, elderly, she-elf, elf.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[healer](/wiki/Category%3A_Healers "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[trainer](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
