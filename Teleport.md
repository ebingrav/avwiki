*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") takes you from your current
location to your target. The target must be a
[PC](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or a
[Mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink"). Kind of got to know where you are
going. This spell now carries a risk of damage to
[hp](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink")/[mana](Mana_Points "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'teleport'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")/[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is invaluable to
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink"). When teleporting, target
keywords should be chosen carefully; for example, more than one person
has typed *cast 'teleport' baron* intending to kill [Baron
Sarphyre](/wiki/Baron_Amier_Sarphyre "wikilink") only to die to the [Robber
Baron](/wiki/Robber_Baron "wikilink") instead, which could easily have been
prevented by typing *cast 'teleport' bar sar* instead.
[Scry](/wiki/Scry "wikilink") may be used to verify where a certain
combination of keywords will (currently) lead; this destination may
change over time, however, as [mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") move
and die and respawn.
