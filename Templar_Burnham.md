*The Order of Templars is famous for the hardiness of their
[warriors](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink"). Templar Burnham is no
exception. Held in his hand is [an exquisite Samurai
sword](/wiki/Emperor's_Blade "wikilink"), given to him by [an ancient
Shogun](/wiki/Shogun_Senjisama "wikilink"), in [a realm far
away](/wiki/Category%3A_Shogun "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Templar, Burnham.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 35.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink").
