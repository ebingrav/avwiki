*The temporal blade is a piece of masterful sorcery. It was forged from
the hardest of steel, and then tempered in pure spring water. Lightning
struck it on the peak of a mountain, imbibing it with the essence of
light.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 12 to 28 (average 20).`
