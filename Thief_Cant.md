*Those with [rogue lore](/wiki/Rogue_Lore "wikilink")
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") have the ability to
[speak](/wiki/Speak "wikilink") a [language](Category%3A_Languages "wikilink")
only understood by the [thieves](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") of the
realm. It works much like the other
[racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Races "wikilink")
[languages](/wiki/Category%3A_Languages "wikilink") [spoken](/wiki/Speak "wikilink")
and is accessed by the [SPEAK](/wiki/Speak "wikilink")
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"). Thief Cant is required when
talking to [shopkeepers](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink") selling
[rogue tools](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogue_Tools "wikilink").*
