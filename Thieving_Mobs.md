[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will steal gold carried by any
players that they see around them.

See also [Drifting Mobs](/wiki/Drifting_Mobs "wikilink") and [Sentinel
Mobs](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").
