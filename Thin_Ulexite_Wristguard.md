*A sliver of ulexite, this milky white wristguard reflects all light
translucently, offering a glimpse of the pulsing veins of the wearer.*

[`Armor` `Class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 10-12.`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 5 continous.`  
`It improves archery accuracy.`  
`It facilitates the archery skill `[`longshot`](/wiki/Longshot "wikilink")`.`

Comments
--------

It is questionable if this wrist is better than the [Ice Scale
Bracer](/wiki/Ice_Scale_Bracer "wikilink"), since archery is supposedly all
about accuracy, and the Ice Scale improves damage instead. More testing
is required for sure. However, longshot bonus is nice, but the wording
is unclear - does the wrist confer the skill, or just augments it?
