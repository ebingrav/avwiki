*With increased battle experience and training, a
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") gains an additional
attack per [round](/wiki/Rounds "wikilink") in melee. This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is automatic once
[practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Second Attack](/wiki/Second_Attack "wikilink").

Syntax: none.
