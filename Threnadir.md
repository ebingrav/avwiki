*Threnadir doesn't bother looking up from his book when he speaks.
Despite his apparent youthful appearance, he gives off an aura of
knowledge and experience. He keeps to the darker corners of the library,
and his overdeveloped subterranean eyes dart rapidly over the pages of
the tome in his hands.*
