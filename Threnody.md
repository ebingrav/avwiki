Syntax: cast threnody <target>  
Prerequisites: [ Eulogy](/wiki/Eulogy "wikilink")  
Participants: 15  
With a collective effort, you can summon forth a corpse across the
planes without a greater sacrifice like with eulogy. It can only be
attempted from within the safer planes of [
Midgaardia](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaardia "wikilink") and [
Thorngate](/wiki/Category%3A_Thorngate "wikilink"). (You can only threnody
corpses in Midgaardia from in Midgaardia.)

"It was unbelievable, I tell you...the way they were screeching together
over there. They're all crazy."

- a spectator during the dirge for Timothy the Fair.
