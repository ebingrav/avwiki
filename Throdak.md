*Here you can see a projection of a half-orc. The half-orc is clad in a
full suit of chain mail and he wields an impressive two handed axe. His
hair is braided and reached down to his waist. On face and neck you can
see tattoos of various celestial bodies, the most prominent being that
of a cresent moon. As you approach, he begins to speak...*

**Keywords:** throdak, half, orc, paladin, projection.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 95.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[trainer](/wiki/Category%3ATrainers "wikilink")  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [detect
hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink"),
[sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink")  

Comments
--------

Throdak is involved in the [Suntamer Quest](/wiki/Suntamer_Quest "wikilink").

Upon zapping him, he will equip the [Great Axe
"Suntamer"](/wiki/Great_Axe_"Suntamer" "wikilink"), [Celestial
Tattoos](/wiki/Celestial_Tattoos "wikilink") and [Paladin's
Chainmail](/wiki/Paladin's_Chainmail "wikilink").
