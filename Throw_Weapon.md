*This [command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") allows the user to
throw [weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") at their foes. In order
to use this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), you must have a
specialized [throwing weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Throwing_Weapons "wikilink")
readied; standard [weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") tend to be
awkward and unwieldy to throw. If you're looking for the
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") where you throw an opponent on
the ground, you're in the wrong place; for that skill, see
[TOSS](/wiki/Toss "wikilink"). [Throwing
weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Throwing_Weapons "wikilink") come in an incredible
variety, and some even have unique special abilities. You will need to
[wield](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") these items to throw them. You can only throw
from your offhand if both your [wielded](/wiki/Wield "wikilink")
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") and the projectile are light
enough, based on your level of prowess with the ambidexterity skills
([poise](/wiki/Poise "wikilink"), [balance](Balance "wikilink"), etc.)
[Examining](/wiki/Examine "wikilink") a [throwing
weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Throwing_Weapons "wikilink") with [weapon
lore](/wiki/Weapon_Lore "wikilink") will reveal its damage and special
attributes.*

Prerequisite(s): [Weapon Lore](/wiki/Weapon_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: [throw](/wiki/Throw "wikilink")
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
