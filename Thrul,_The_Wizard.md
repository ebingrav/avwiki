*This [wizard](:Category:_Wizards.md "wikilink") sits here reading a
[scroll](:Category:_Scrolls.md "wikilink"), thin aged hands still
holding it without a shake. Although not directly looking at you, you
are sure that you are unnoticed. He can purchase or
[identify](Identify_(command).md "wikilink") magical
[items](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink") for the right price, but his
teaching days are long behind him.*
