*This [wizard](/wiki/Category%3A_Wizards "wikilink") sits here reading a
[scroll](/wiki/Category%3A_Scrolls "wikilink"), thin aged hands still holding
it without a shake. Although not directly looking at you, you are sure
that you are unnoticed. He can purchase or
[identify](/wiki/Identify_(command) "wikilink") magical
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") for the right price, but his
teaching days are long behind him.*

**Keywords:** Thrul, wizard.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 51 or more.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[citizen](/wiki/Citizen_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").
