[Ticket](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink")-[type](Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink")
[objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") have four
[values](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Values "wikilink").

-   **Tickettaker:** This value determines which
    [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") will accept this
    [ticket](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   **Ticket Type:** This value determines what will happen to
    [characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") who give this
    [ticket](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink") to its taker (see above);
    they will either (1) be transported to a certain
    [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"), (2) receive a certain
    [object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), or (3) have a certain
    [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") [cast](Cast "wikilink")
    upon them.

<!-- -->

-   **Other (2):** What these two values are and do depend upon the
    previous value.

See also [Tickets](/wiki/Category%3A_Tickets "wikilink").
