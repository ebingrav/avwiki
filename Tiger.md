*With its orange-yellow skin and black stripes, the tiger is able to
move unnoticeably through its surroundings. It almost caught you by
surprise.*

**Keywords:** tiger.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 40.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").

Directions
