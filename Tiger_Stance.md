*Tiger pounces on the fallen. While in Tiger Stance, you automatically
gain an immediate free attack on any foe you successfully
[trip](/wiki/Trip "wikilink") or [toss](Toss "wikilink"). Tiger Stance ends
early if you [kick](/wiki/Kick "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Serpent Technique](/wiki/Serpent_Technique "wikilink"),
[Whirlwind Kick](/wiki/Whirlwind_Kick "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](/wiki/Stance "wikilink") tiger.

See also [Emu Stance](/wiki/Emu_Stance "wikilink").
