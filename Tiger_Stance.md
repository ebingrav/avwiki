*Tiger pounces on the fallen. While in Tiger Stance, you automatically
gain an immediate free attack on any foe you successfully
[trip](Trip "wikilink") or [toss](Toss "wikilink"). Tiger Stance ends
early if you [kick](Kick "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Serpent Technique](Serpent_Technique "wikilink"),
[Whirlwind Kick](Whirlwind_Kick "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](Stance "wikilink") tiger.
