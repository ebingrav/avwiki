*Those familiar with martial technique can use chronomancy to speed up a
fighter's attacks. When cast on a group-mate, time skip buys them an
extra [round](/wiki/Rounds "wikilink") of attacks. This spell is
[EXHAUSTED](/wiki/Exhaustion "wikilink") for a short time when
[cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Chaos Channel](/wiki/Chaos_Channel "wikilink"), [Third
Attack](/wiki/Third_Attack "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'time skip'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
