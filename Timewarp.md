*Warp time, my friend, and reap the double edged sword of faster
degeneration on things close to yourself... magic! Timewarp will make
your [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") duration decrease at twice
the normal speed. For now it is a [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
better kept secret so it is only possible to [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") on
oneself. NOTE: It doesn't affect the degeneration of
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") or
[racial](/wiki/Category%3A_Racials "wikilink") fatigues.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'timewarp'.
