*The edges of these claws are covered in jagged spikes, making them the
ideal tool for shredding flesh. Bloody bits of tissue hang from the base
of the claws, indicating that they were savagely ripped from the paws of
some poor beast.*  
Modifies armor class by -10 continuous.

`Modifies damage roll by 5 continuous.`

It is a one handed weapon which does clawing damage.  
Damage is 9 to 23 (average 16).  
Slot: Wield/Offhand  
Level: 20  
Weight: 15  
Flags: magic anti-good noremove  
Area: [Laboratory Of
Chalmus](/wiki/Category%3ALaboratory_Of_Chalmus "wikilink")
