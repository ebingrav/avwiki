` Modifies wisdom by -2 continuous.`  
` Modifies hit roll by 3 continuous.`  
` Modifies damage roll by 4 continuous.`

Slot: Held  
Level: 39-41  
Base: None (type treasure)  
Flags: magic nodrop noremove  
Mob: [Mad Kzin](/wiki/Mad_Kzin "wikilink")  
Area: [Flipside Altar](/wiki/Category%3A_Flipside_Altar "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Flipside_Altar_Map "wikilink"))  
The pp for this item is [Vineman](/wiki/Vineman "wikilink"). See the area's
article for walking directions there.  
If you use this item, make sure to remove it before levelling up, or you
might gain less pracs than you would normally. The [Mad
Kzin](/wiki/Mad_Kzin "wikilink") also holds the [Mummified Head Of Jubal The
Benevolent](/wiki/Mummified_Head_Of_Jubal_The_Benevolent "wikilink").
