*Tomli, son of [Bomli](/wiki/Bomli "wikilink"), works as a messenger boy. He
doesn't have time or breath to talk to you right now. He must have [an
urgent message](/wiki/Luverly_Hot_Pie "wikilink") to deliver.*

**Keywords:** Tomli, messenger.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 21.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** heroic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [sneak](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink").
