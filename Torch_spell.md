*Torch is quite similar to [BLAST BOLT](/wiki/Blast_Bolt "wikilink"), taking
the caster's [light source](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink") and exploding
it against the target's body. The use of an active [light
source](/wiki/Category%3A_Lights "wikilink") creates a far more spectacular
and deadly explosion than [detonate](/wiki/Detonate "wikilink") or [blast
bolt](/wiki/Blast_Bolt "wikilink") could produce. There are no reimbursements
for [items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") that you lose with this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). [Casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") to initiate combat will do no
damage to your opponent but will cause your enemy to attack in
retaliation.*

Prerequisite(s): [Detonate](/wiki/Detonate "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'torch'
