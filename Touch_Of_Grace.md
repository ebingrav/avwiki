*A [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") of benevolence, a touch of
grace calls upon the caster's chosen
[deity](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") to heal a weak mortal
soul in Midgaardia. Because of the spirit of the spell, the heal is done
anonymously.*

Prerequisite(s): [Saving Grace](/wiki/Saving_Grace "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'touch of grace'.
