This is a variable insignia that can be activated upon completion of
[Restoring Mark V (Part 2)
Quest](/wiki/Restoring_Mark_V_(Part_2)_Quest "wikilink") that takes place in
[Glyntaff Pass](/wiki/Category%3AGlyntaff_Pass "wikilink") and many other
locations.

Depending on the place of activation bonuses can vary. Remember not to
lose the weapon from [Restoring Mark V (Part 1)
Quest](/wiki/Restoring_Mark_V_(Part_1)_Quest "wikilink") as wielding it is
required when performing the activation.
