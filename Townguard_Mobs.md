[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will hunt and attack
[outlawed](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink")
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink"). They will immediately
arrest any [outlaws](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink") that they find in [safe
rooms](/wiki/Safe_Rooms "wikilink") without a fight and send these
[outlaws](/wiki/Outlaw_Flag "wikilink") to [jail](Jail_Rooms "wikilink") for
sentencing.
