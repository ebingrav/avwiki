*A gland is here, oozing malodorous toxins.*

It carries roughly 31 [doses](/wiki/Poison_Values "wikilink") of
[biotoxin](/wiki/Category%3A_Biotoxin "wikilink"), a strong
[poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink") which lowers overall health.
This should be used on no less than a level 35 weapon.

**Keyword(s):** a toxin gland.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** 52.  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[poison](/wiki/Category%3A_Poisons "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** none.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 6 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 4 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.
