*A gland is here, oozing malodorous toxins.*

It carries roughly 31 [doses](Poison_Values.md "wikilink") of
[biotoxin](:Category:_Biotoxin.md "wikilink"), a strong
[poison](:Category:_Poisons.md "wikilink") which lowers overall health.
This should be used on no less than a level 35 weapon.
