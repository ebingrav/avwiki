*With a careful eye and a keen focus on the surroundings, one may
discern the signs of someone's passing. The guidance gained by using the
track [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is sufficient to point one
in the right direction, most of the time. A successful track is more
likely if the [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") is highly trained.
Tracking takes time; delays are incurred whenever a person moves and has
to relocate the trail. The [bloodhound](/wiki/Bloodhound "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") can help speed the process.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: track
