*With this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") you can Transfer your
life into another. This is not an efficient process however, and even
less efficient if the target is at max [hits](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink").
The cost is high for using this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"),
not in [mana](/wiki/Mana_Points "wikilink"), but in YOUR life, and you will be
unable to stop the process, and will fall unconscious yourself!*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'transfer life'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is hardly ever used.
