*A translucent flag means that person is slightly harder to make out, a
little ethereal and without substance.*

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") or
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") with this flag may pass through all
closed [doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") except for those
[doors](/wiki/Category%3A_Doors "wikilink") that are
[passproof](/wiki/Passproof_Doors "wikilink"). Some
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") have this flag inherently. This flag
also results from [wearing](/wiki/Wear "wikilink") certain special pieces of
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink"), using certain [magical
objects](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellcasting_Gear "wikilink") like certain
[potions](/wiki/Category%3A_Potions "wikilink"), or being
[affected](/wiki/Affects "wikilink") by [Pass Door](Pass_Door "wikilink") or
[Ectoplasmic Form](/wiki/Ectoplasmic_Form "wikilink")
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink").
