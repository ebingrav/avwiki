*Over time, [Psionicists](/wiki/Category%3A_Psionicists "wikilink") have
learned many ways to change things on a molecular level. Transmute
doesn't turn lead into gold, but you can make a small change to an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")'s overall
[weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"). Transmute is not a perfect
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), and there is a small chance the
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") will be destroyed, or otherwise
permanently mangled. [Magic](/wiki/Magic_Flag "wikilink")
[items](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") are more or less impossible to
alter molecularly without causing enormous explosions of light and
power. This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") will not work on any
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") with a [magic
flag](/wiki/Magic_Flag "wikilink"). Note that transmute itself is a magical
process, so any [item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") can only be
transmuted once. NOTE: If successful, transmute will add 1 or 2
[levels](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") to an
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"). NOTE:
[Containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") destroyed also destroy
all contents.*

Prerequisite(s): [Steel Skeleton](/wiki/Steel_Skeleton "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'transmute'
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;
&lt;heavier/lighter&gt;.

See also [Magic Flag](/wiki/Magic_Flag "wikilink").
