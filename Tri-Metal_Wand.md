Directions
----------

When you first enter the Secret Shrine, go north close to the back of
the shrine (it's small), find the west wall and go south one from the
north wall. Have detect magic up and pull a lever to unlock the hidden
DOWN exit. From there, walk around, you'll see what looks like an Onyx
Golem. That's the Veldri Mage.
