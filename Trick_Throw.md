*While darting between a foe's legs, a
[Fusilier](/wiki/Category%3A_Fusiliers "wikilink")'s projectiles are extremely
hard to catch. This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") halves the
odds of an enemy's [catch weapon](/wiki/Catch_Weapon "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), but only if the
[Fusilier](/wiki/Category%3A_Fusiliers "wikilink") is actively using
[Underfoot](/wiki/Underfoot "wikilink") against them.*

Prerequisite(s): [Quick Draw](/wiki/Quick_Draw "wikilink"),
[Underfoot](/wiki/Underfoot "wikilink").

Syntax: [throw](/wiki/Throw "wikilink")
&lt;[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink")&gt;.
