*A trident is a three-pronged spear, each prong sharpened for maximum
slashing, slicing, and piercing action. The arms of the prongs are
reinforced, to be used in deflecting sword-blows, as well.*

[`Damage`](Melee_Weapon_Values.md "wikilink")` is 9 to 29 (average 19).`  
`Modifies `[`hit`` ``roll`](Hit_Roll.md "wikilink")` by 3 continuous.`

`Modifies `[`dexterity`](Dexterity.md "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`
