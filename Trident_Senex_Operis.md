*A trident is a three-pronged spear, each prong sharpened for maximum
slashing, slicing, and piercing action. The arms of the prongs are
reinforced, to be used in deflecting sword-blows, as well.*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 9 to 29 (average 19).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 3 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`constitution`](/wiki/Constitution "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`dexterity`](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`strength`](/wiki/Strength "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`
