*A successful use of the trip [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink")
means you will duck down, swing your legs around and knock your opponent
off their feet. A player may attempt to trip at any time. It can be used
to start a fight or in the middle of one. A tripped opponent will be
weakened as they are on the ground. Trip cannot be used against
[flying](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink") opponents as their legs are not on the
ground. Be careful however, as the [tank](/wiki/Tanks "wikilink") can be
switched after the opponent is tripped. TRIP is an auxiliary fighting
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") to weaken your opponent. All
that is required is that you must [practice](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") the
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") and that the opponent is not
[flying](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: trip &lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
