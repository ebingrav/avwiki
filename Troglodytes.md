Trogs are a reptilian race of humanoids that live in subterranean
places. They are quite strong, have above average reflexes, and are
extremely tough. They do however have a huge lack in mental capacity.
Because of these factors they tend to be ferocious warriors, but when
the rare spellcaster is found, they are not very adept at their magic.

Trogs can be identified by their reptilian features, by the nauseous
odor they emit in battle, and by their very repulsiveness. Troglodytes
are a subterranean race, and as such have developed the ability to see
well in the darkness of the underworld.

Troglodytes are capable of the following:  
[Infravision](/wiki/Racial_Infravision "wikilink")  
[ Butcher](/wiki/Racial_Butcher "wikilink")
