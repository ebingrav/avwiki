*This [troll](/wiki/Trolls "wikilink") is bigger than the rest outside, and is
probably their leader. Standing at least eight feet tall, he is quite
intimidating, and his eyes are black as night. A bit of drool falls from
his lip, as he looks you over for lunch.*

**Keywords:** troll, leader.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 34.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
