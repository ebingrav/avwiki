Trolls are generally found in rather uninhabited places, and vary
greatly in their physical appearance. What they do all seem to have in
common is that they are quite strong, extremely hardy, and have an
amazing capacity to heal damage quickly. While not too smart, not too
wise, and being shunned by most races, trolls are not so bad as people
might think, and can be quite a welcome addition to any group.

Trolls are protected by their leather-like skin, and can utilize their
claws to inflict damage in addition to using weapons. They shun direct
sunlight however, and lose their incredible capacity to heal damage when
the sun is upon them.

Trolls are only available via the remort command.
