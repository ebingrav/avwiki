Tul-Sith is the goddess of [wisdom](/wiki/Wisdom "wikilink") and health. She
welcomes [prayers](/wiki/Prayer "wikilink") but frowns upon sacrifices. She is
not a fan of combat, but she will bless her worshippers with quicker
healing and stronger bodies. Unlike [Gorn](/wiki/Gorn "wikilink"), Tul-Sith is
forgiving and merciful. Unlike [Bhyss](/wiki/Bhyss "wikilink"), Tul-Sith
cherishes life. Join the [clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") of
this age in humble prayer for the Goddess Tul-Sith and wish her
everlasting tranquility.
