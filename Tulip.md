*This [thief](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink"), affectionately known as
'Tulip' for his tendency to wilt under pressure, is the guild basher,
providing a strong arm where necessary and engaging in a bit of
extortion to boost guild coffers.*

**Keywords:** Tulip, strong, nervous, man, thief.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 7.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** none.
