*[Tulip](/wiki/Tulip "wikilink") got this tattoo by mistake. He was
celebrating his acceptance into the
[thieves](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") guild with a few friends when
he passed out after one too many firebreathers. When he awoke he found
to his surprise, he was sporting a fine tattoo of none other than a
tulip!*

[`Armor` `class`](/wiki/Armor_Values "wikilink")` is 1-2.`  
`Modifies `[`armor`
`class`](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink")` by -1 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`strength`](/wiki/Strength "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`
