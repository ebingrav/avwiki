Power](/wiki/Ultimoose_Power "wikilink"), combining this item with a [Mauve
Mollusc Mukluks](/wiki/Mauve_Mollusc_Mukluks "wikilink") will result in the
same HR/DR as the Ultimoose + [Fatewalkers](/wiki/Fatewalkers "wikilink"), but
with added bonuses.
