*[Demons](/wiki/Demons "wikilink") and piety don't mix either. When confronted
with the holy presence of this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"),
[Demons](/wiki/Demons "wikilink") will often [flee](Flee "wikilink") rather
than fight. This is an area spell.*

Prerequisite(s): [Turn Undead](/wiki/Turn_Undead "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'turn demon'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is hardly ever used.
