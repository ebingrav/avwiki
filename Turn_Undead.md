*The [undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink") and piety don't mix. When
confronted with the holy presence of this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"), the
[undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink") will often [flee](Flee "wikilink")
rather than fight. This is an area spell.*

Prerequisite(s): [Dispel Evil](/wiki/Dispel_Evil "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'turn undead'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is hardly ever used.

See also [Turn Undead Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Turn_Undead_Gear "wikilink").
