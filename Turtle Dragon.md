*This is a very big [dragon](Dragons "wikilink"). It looks more turtle
than [dragon](Dragons "wikilink") though. Its blue-green scales glisten
with moisture. Steam jets from its nostrils. It looks intently at you as
if to look you over before eating you.*

**Keywords:** turtle, dragon.  
**[Level](Level "wikilink"):** 15.  
**[Alignment](Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.

[aggressive](Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[gas-breather](Breathing_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").

[infravision](Infravision "wikilink").
