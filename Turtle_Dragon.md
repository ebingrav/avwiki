*This is a very big [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink"). It looks more turtle
than [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") though. Its blue-green scales glisten
with moisture. Steam jets from its nostrils. It looks intently at you as
if to look you over before eating you.*

**Keywords:** turtle, dragon.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 15.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[gas-breather](/wiki/Breathing_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"),
[infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
