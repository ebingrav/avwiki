To obtain the [Turtle Shell Amulet](/wiki/Turtle_Shell_Amulet "wikilink"),
kill the egg-laying tortuga. Give the egg to the wise tortuga who is one
south of Conian, who is hidden, for a ticket. Then find and kill the
tortuga matriarch. Careful, aggies along the way. Queen is biggish mob.
After you kill queen, skin corpse for Gold and Ivory shell Fragment.
Give Conian either ticket for the item.
