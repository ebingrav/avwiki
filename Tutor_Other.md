*Once you've learned this teaching skill, you will be able to teach
[skills and spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") like a
[guildmaster](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink") up to a certain
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"). Like a
[guildmaster](/wiki/Category%3A_Trainers "wikilink"), you only know what your
guild offers and the more teaching skills you
[know](Practice "wikilink"), the higher the [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink") of
[skills and spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") you'll be
able to teach.*

Prerequisite(s): [Demonstrate](/wiki/Demonstrate "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"), like its
predecessor [Demonstrate](/wiki/Demonstrate "wikilink"), is rarely used.
