*With a handle made of braided [gryphon](Griffons.md "wikilink") hide,
this dagger is one of a twin set, known as the Twined Daggers. The
slender blade is rumored to be of damascus make, strong and perfect for
piercing even the toughest [armor](:Category:_Armor.md "wikilink").*
