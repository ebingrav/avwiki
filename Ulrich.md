Comments
--------

Ulrich is a big nasty mob in [Hamlet of
Kreigstadt](/wiki/Category%3A_Hamlet_Of_Kreigstadt "wikilink").

Ulrich is a popular [shopkeeper](/wiki/Category%3A_Shopkeepers "wikilink") for
[heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") because he can buy all
[sorts](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink") of
[gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink"), unlike the
[armourer](/wiki/Armourer "wikilink") or [weaponsmith](Weaponsmith "wikilink")
of [Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard "wikilink"), who both specialize in a
certain [type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink") of
[equipment](/wiki/Category%3A_Gear "wikilink").

Ulrich is portable and not [aggie](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"). Because
of this, he serves as a useful portal point to many areas connected to
[Wildwood](/wiki/Category%3A_Wildwood "wikilink"), such as [Githzerai
Tower](/wiki/Category%3A_Githzerai_Tower "wikilink") and [Cavern of the
Fates](/wiki/Category%3A_Cavern_Of_The_Fates "wikilink").

Because of his [level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"), and the fact that he uses
spells midround, he is a popular practice mob for
[heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") who aspire to kill the [Ultimate
Dragon](/wiki/Ultimate_Dragon "wikilink"). However, despite Ulrich's
[size](/wiki/Level "wikilink"), he is not quite as big as the
[UD](/wiki/Ultimate_Dragon "wikilink").

[Heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") often intentionally die to Ulrich.
Sometimes this is done to clear [spells](/wiki/Affects "wikilink"), so the
rest of their group don't have to wait for them to tick off.
[Sorcerers](/wiki/Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink") who make use of [Tainted
Genius](/wiki/Tainted_Genius "wikilink") also intentionally kill themselves.
Ulrich is a good [mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") to die to because 1)
he's huge, 2) he's in an uncursed room, and 3) he's not
[aggie](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"), and so, assuming, you relog he
won't kill you again.

Because [heroes](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero "wikilink") love fighting Ulrich so
much, and because of the fact that he uses area spells, it is not a good
idea to [sleep](/wiki/Sleep_(command) "wikilink") in his
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"). If you're sleeping there and
someone starts a fight with him you may find yourself on the cloud
before you know it.
