`Modifies armor class by -10`  
`Modifies hit roll by 7 continuous.`

Bases: 10-12  
Levels: 50-51  
Weight: 3  
Area: [Hero Quest](/wiki/Category%3A_Hero_Quest "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Hero_Quest_Map "wikilink"))  
Mob: [Ultimate Dragon](/wiki/Ultimate_Dragon "wikilink")  
Once you hit hero 500 you can attempt the Ultimate Dragon quest. Ask an
Imm to set it up for you.

Note: its a skin item from the Dragons Corpse, and can only be made if
an archer-type kills the dragon.
