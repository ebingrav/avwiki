port to [Moose Guard](/wiki/Moose_Guard "wikilink"), 3s w 3s w u, get
[Mistletoe](/wiki/Mistletoe "wikilink"), d n 4w n w n w s, get [Green Man's
Mask](/wiki/Green_Man's_Mask "wikilink")  
port to moose guard, 3s 2e, give the mistletoe to
[Nostradomoose](/wiki/Nostradomoose "wikilink"). (this will transport you)  
find Nostradamoose again, give mask to him to be transported to a
[Nospell](/wiki/Anti-Magic_Rooms "wikilink") aggie zone.  
find [Wolf Pack Leader](/wiki/Wolf_Pack_Leader "wikilink") get his [Wolf's
Ear](/wiki/Wolf's_Ear "wikilink").  
find [Bull Moose](/wiki/Bull_Moose "wikilink") and give him the ear. This will
transport you to [Ultimoose](/wiki/Ultimoose "wikilink"), who is aggie. Kill
him and claim your [prize](/wiki/Ultimoose_Power "wikilink").
