''[Fusiliers](/wiki/Category%3A_Fusiliers "wikilink") are adept at using their
small size to confound larger enemies. This
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") acts like an enhanced
[dodge](/wiki/Dodge "wikilink") against enemies who are larger than the user.

Prerequisite(s): [Dodge](/wiki/Dodge "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
