Object 'gnome gnimh uniform academy' type armor, extra flags none.  
Weight 5, value 30, level 10.  
Object Quality (100 / 100 hps)  
Armor class is 3.  
Modifies armor class by -5 continuous.

`Modifies constitution by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies dexterity by 1 continuous.`

  
  
Portal Point: Cadet  
  
Walking directions: 3e2n3e5n2ws to entrance of [Village of
Gnimh](/wiki/Village_of_Gnimh "wikilink"). From there, kill all the cadets
until one gives you the right uniform. Not all the uniforms have
constitution and dexterity bonuses. Use identify spell or ident command
at [Ent](/wiki/Ent "wikilink") in [Stonehall](Stonehall "wikilink") to make
sure you have the right uniform. Or just equip the item and watch your
stats.
