*With a curse of wakefulness, you can prevent your enemy from
[resting](/wiki/Rest "wikilink") or [sleeping](Sleep_(command) "wikilink"). It
also seems immune to the [sleep](/wiki/Sleep_(spell) "wikilink")
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). A twisted kind of defense if
you're willing to curse yourself.*

Prerequisite(s): [Corruption](/wiki/Corruption "wikilink"), [Demonic
Intent](/wiki/Demonic_Intent "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'unrest'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
