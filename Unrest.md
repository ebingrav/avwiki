*With a curse of wakefulness, you can prevent your enemy from
[resting](Rest "wikilink") or [sleeping](Sleep_(command) "wikilink"). It
also seems immune to the [sleep](Sleep_(spell) "wikilink")
[spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink"). A twisted kind of defense if
you're willing to curse yourself.*

Prerequisite(s): [Corruption](Corruption "wikilink"), [Demonic
Intent](Demonic_Intent "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'unrest'
\<[mob](:Category:_Mobs "wikilink")\>.

## Comments

When a mob casts unrest on you, a priest can
[clarify](clarify "wikilink") you to put you at ease.
