*While using this [stance](/wiki/Stance "wikilink"), a
[Shadowfist](/wiki/Category%3A_Shadowfists "wikilink") will steal
[health](/wiki/Hit_Points "wikilink") from his target on critical hits and
vital strikes.*

Prerequisite(s): [Demonic Intent](/wiki/Demonic_Intent "wikilink").

Syntax: [stance](/wiki/Stance "wikilink") vampire fang.

See also [Spectral Fang](/wiki/Spectral_Fang "wikilink").

This stance is incredibly useful, as it rewards a large amount of hp for
use. Used in conjection with vital counter,
[shadowfists](/wiki/Category%3A_Shadowfists "wikilink") become incredibly
longlasting tanks, at least at hero.

Some have said it's better than [bloodlust](bloodlust "wikilink"), but
that remains unconfirmed.
