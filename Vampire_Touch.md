*With a kiss, a brush of a finger, or a bite on the neck, this
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") represents evil well. Draining
another life to gain your own, you truly belong among the
[undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink"), the vampires, and the unholy.*

Prerequisite(s): [Leech](/wiki/Leech "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'vampire touch'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.
