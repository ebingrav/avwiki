*This veldri was once a powerful mage before an accident seriously
wounded her, bringing her career to a halt. She fled to the shrine and
was taken in by the other veldri. Here, she studies tactics to use in
the upcoming battle with the duergar.*

**Keywords:** golem, veldri, mage.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 42.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[mage](/wiki/Spellcasting_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").

Comments
--------

One must [push](/wiki/Push "wikilink")/[pull](Pull "wikilink") a crest
(visible only to those [detecting magic](/wiki/Detect_Magic "wikilink")) in
order to open the trapdoor to [this
area](/wiki/Category%3A_Secrets_Of_The_Shrine "wikilink")'s lower level.
[Detect Magic](/wiki/Detect_Magic "wikilink") can be obatined by getting the
[Onyx Looking Glass](/wiki/Onyx_Looking_Glass "wikilink") from the [Drow
Outcast](/wiki/Drow_Outcast "wikilink").
