*Venghilrim is a young [dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink"), as far as
[dragon](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink") lifespans go. He has only lived a century
or so, but time means nothing to most [dragons](/wiki/Dragons "wikilink"). His
icey breath chills the lair around him, as a frosty steam pours from his
nostrils. He lounges about a pile of coins and treasures, the blackness
of his eyes looking right through you. At his feet lies [a glowing
sword](/wiki/Sword_Christened_'Venghilrim's_Doom' "wikilink") that he keeps
one talon on at all times. The other talons may soon be in your flesh.*

**Keywords:** Venghilrim, dragon.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 30.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink"),
[frost-breather](/wiki/Breathing_Mobs "wikilink"),
[kleptomaniac](/wiki/Category%3A_Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invis](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink"), [sanctuary](Sanctuary "wikilink").

Comments
--------

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") need both
[fly](/wiki/Fly "wikilink") and [water breathing](Water_Breathing "wikilink")
to reach Venghilrim.

Venghilrim's [room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") has no physical exits
and, so, [fleeing](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") from him is impossible.
