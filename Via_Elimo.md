*The Way of Perfection requires that a
[Priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") deal only with those of pure
intention. This discipline gives a boost in healing power to the
[Priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") who [casts](Cast "wikilink")
upon those of high [alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"), but a penalty to
the [Priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") who deigns to heal unwashed
evil heathens.*

Prerequisites: [Via Pax](/wiki/Via_Pax "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
