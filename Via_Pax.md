*A [Priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink")'s power comes from devotion
to the Way of Peace. The Via Pax discipline represents the first Step
along the Way. Once [practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"), the
[Priest](/wiki/Category%3A_Priests "wikilink") will be unable to initiate
combat, but, by the Grace of [Tul-Sith](/wiki/Tul-Sith "wikilink"), can
receive bonus [exp](/wiki/Experience_Points "wikilink") from healing under
certain circumstances.*

Prerequisite(s): [Meditation](/wiki/Meditation "wikilink").

Syntax: none (automatic).
