*[Sorcerers](/wiki/Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink"), being
[sorcerers](/wiki/Category%3A_Sorcerers "wikilink"), like to mar everything
around them. This is yet one more way to do so. By violating an
[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink"), you twist it away from the
goodness of the world and make it an artifact of evil.
[Casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") this [spell](Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")
does require heavy use of the cackle
[social](/wiki/Category%3A_Socials "wikilink"). It will raise the
[level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") of an
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Corruption](/wiki/Corruption "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'violation'
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt;.

See also [Dark Flag](/wiki/Dark_Flag "wikilink").
