*[Sorcerers](:Category:_Sorcerers.md "wikilink"), being
[sorcerers](:Category:_Sorcerers.md "wikilink"), like to mar everything
around them. This is yet one more way to do so. By violating an
[object](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink"), you twist it away from the
goodness of the world and make it an artifact of evil.
[Casting](Cast.md "wikilink") this
[spell](:Category:_Spells.md "wikilink") does require heavy use of the
cackle [social](:Category:_Socials.md "wikilink"). It will raise the
[level](Object_Level.md "wikilink") of an
[item](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Corruption](Corruption.md "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast.md "wikilink") 'violation'
\<[object](:Category:_Objects.md "wikilink")\>.

See also [Dark Flag](Dark_Flag.md "wikilink"). This spell will add the
Dark Flag to an item. An item which already has the Dark Flag cannot be
violated.
