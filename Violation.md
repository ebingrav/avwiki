*[Sorcerers](:Category:_Sorcerers "wikilink"), being
[sorcerers](:Category:_Sorcerers "wikilink"), like to mar everything
around them. This is yet one more way to do so. By violating an
[object](:Category:_Objects "wikilink"), you twist it away from the
goodness of the world and make it an artifact of evil.
[Casting](Cast "wikilink") this [spell](:Category:_Spells "wikilink")
does require heavy use of the cackle
[social](:Category:_Socials "wikilink"). It will raise the
[level](Object_Level "wikilink") of an
[item](:Category:_Objects "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Corruption](Corruption "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](Cast "wikilink") 'violation'
\<[object](:Category:_Objects "wikilink")\>.

See also [Dark Flag](Dark_Flag "wikilink"). This spell will add the Dark
Flag to an item. An item which already has the Dark Flag cannot be
violated.
