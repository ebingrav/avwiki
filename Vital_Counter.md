*Once [practiced](/wiki/Practice "wikilink"), this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") allows the
[Shadowfist](/wiki/Category%3A_Shadowfists "wikilink") to attempt a [vital
shot](/wiki/Vital_Shot "wikilink") when making a counterattack. This is
automatic as long as the
[Shadowfist](/wiki/Category%3A_Shadowfists "wikilink") is attempting a
standard or crush counter.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
