*Vymm is the citadel's mistress of water. As she dances you can't help
but be reminded of rivers flowing over rocks, seeking a path to the sea.
The blue silken master's robes follow her movements, rippling along
behind.*

**Keywords:** vymm, master, water  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 70.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [detect
invisible](/wiki/Detect_Invis "wikilink").

Comments
--------

Vymm will give you the [ Sparse and Circumstantial
Evidence](/wiki/Sparse_and_Circumstantial_Evidence "wikilink") if you give her
[ a phail of vile ichor](/wiki/Phial_of_Vile_Ichor "wikilink").

Give [water essence](/wiki/Water_Essence "wikilink") to Vymm to complete the
[Citadel Of Arcanists](/wiki/Category%3A_Citadel_Of_Arcanists "wikilink")'
[Staff of Water Quest](/wiki/Staff_Of_Water_Quest "wikilink").
