*If an opponent blocks a
[berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink")'s attack with
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") or shield, the
[berserker](/wiki/Category%3A_Berserkers "wikilink") gains an immediate extra
followup attack. Wail does not loop; the extra attack cannot itself
generate another one.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
