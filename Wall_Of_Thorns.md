Syntax: c 'wall of thorns'

<dir>
With a magical spell, a wall of thorns will grow temporarily to block an
exit so that it is impossible to pass unless ethereal.

Warning: Do not camp with this spell in Lord Areas. You can create a
temporary haven, but if you are able to regen/respell indefinitely, it
is a foregone conclusion that this spell will turn into Legend+.
