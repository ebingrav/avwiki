[Mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") of this
[type](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink") will wander randomly from one
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") to another through any unobstructed
(non-magical) [exits](/wiki/Category%3A_Room_Exits "wikilink") that they may
encounter. Such wandering is usually limited to a specific
[area](/wiki/Category%3A_Areas "wikilink") or [sector](Sectors "wikilink").

See also [Drifting Mobs](/wiki/Drifting_Mobs "wikilink") and [Sentinel
Mobs](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").
