*When using the wanderlust [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), the
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") will move faster from place to
place, but will consume more movement than usual doing so. Note that as
wanderlust is a walking [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), it is
not compatible with the [movement cost](/wiki/Movement_Costs "wikilink")
reduction granted by [flying](/wiki/Flying_Flag "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): [Farstride](/wiki/Farstride "wikilink").

Syntax: wanderlust.
