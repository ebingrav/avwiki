*When [Rallying](/wiki/Rally "wikilink"), a Master of the Warcry Rite will
grant his groupmates a [damage](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink") bonus in
addition to the [hitroll](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink") bonus.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: none (automatic).
