*A ward is a [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") which [protects an
item](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Enhance_Gear "wikilink") rather
than a person. There are five wards known to
[mages](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") and four of them can be attempted
with this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). These four are
specific to types of damage, meaning that a [lightning
ward](/wiki/Lightning-Ward_Flag "wikilink") will not protect an
[item](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink") from fire. [Caustic
Wards](/wiki/Caustic-Ward_Flag "wikilink") protect against damage from acid or
other corrosive spells. Although
[clerics](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink") protest, the idea of wards was
developed by a [mage](/wiki/Category%3A_Mages "wikilink") who envied the
[Sanctuary](/wiki/Sanctuary "wikilink") given by
[Gods](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") on a
[cleric](/wiki/Category%3A_Clerics "wikilink")'s behalf and is said to do
something similar for his highly-prized collection of treasures. NOTE:
Ward raises [level](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink") by 1 and may even fail
causing destruction. NOTE:
[Containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") destroyed through a
[miscast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") also destroy contents.*

Prerequisite(s): [Enchant Armor](/wiki/Enchant_Armor "wikilink"), [Enchant
Weapon](/wiki/Enchant_Weapon "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'ward'
&lt;[object](/wiki/Category%3A_Objects "wikilink")&gt; <ward-type>

Ward types are [lightning](/wiki/Lightning-Ward_Flag "wikilink"),
[fire](/wiki/Fire-Ward_Flag "wikilink"), [ice](Ice-Ward_Flag "wikilink"), and
[caustic](/wiki/Caustic-Ward_Flag "wikilink").

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is most useful both in
protecting [containers](/wiki/Category%3A_Containers "wikilink") and in
preventing [charged](/wiki/Charge_Shield "wikilink") shields from damaging
themselves.
