*With a cough, Warden Thornberry looks you over with skepticism. Warden
Thornberry says, "There's [a darklin boar](/wiki/Darklin_Boar "wikilink") in
[this forest](/wiki/Category%3A_Westwood_Outlook "wikilink") and I want it
dead." Warden Thornberry exclaims, "[Kill](/wiki/Kill "wikilink") it and I'll
give you [a medal](/wiki/Oak_Leaf_Medal "wikilink")!" With another cough and a
harrumph, he turns his back on you and looks out into the trees.*
