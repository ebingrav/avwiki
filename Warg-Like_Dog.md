Comments
--------

The [Orphanage](/wiki/Category%3AOrphanage "wikilink") (and the [Village of
Gnimh](/wiki/Category%3AVillage_Of_Gnimh "wikilink")) replaced an older area
called Warg Alley. In this
[grandfathered](/wiki/Category%3AGrandfathered "wikilink") area, there were
many wargs. This mob references that old area.
