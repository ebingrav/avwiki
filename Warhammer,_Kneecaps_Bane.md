*The fabled warhammer, Kneecap's bane, is huge by the standard of
[dwarven](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink")
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink"). Fully two feet long
(half as high as a [dwarf](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink")!) it can deliver a
fearful blow if only you can aim with it!*

[`Damage`](/wiki/Melee_Weapon_Values "wikilink")` is 12 to 31 (average 21).`  
`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 1 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage` `roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`
