Mobs of this special function will use combat skills including
[trip](/wiki/Trip "wikilink"), [bash](Bash "wikilink"),
[toss](/wiki/Toss "wikilink"), [smash](Smash "wikilink"),
[disarm](/wiki/Disarm "wikilink"), as well as the occasional Sprite-bashing
taunt.
