*During the second Giant-Ent war, Wartha sought to free
[Velkor](/wiki/Velkor "wikilink") from her stone body and make use of her
fabled battle abilities. The Gods discovered Wartha's plan and
imprisoned him in the rock beside her.*

**Keywords:** dwarf, wartha.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 66.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink"),
[aggressive](/wiki/Aggressive_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
