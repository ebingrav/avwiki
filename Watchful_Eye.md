*With the [alertness](/wiki/Alertness "wikilink")
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") active,
[Bodyguards](/wiki/Category%3A_Bodyguards "wikilink") with the watchful eye
ability can change [monitor](/wiki/Monitor "wikilink") targets midfight,
although there is a slight lag for doing so. They also keep close tabs
on their masters during combat, and gain success chance when rescuing
their [monitor](/wiki/Monitor "wikilink") targets if they're using
[alertness](/wiki/Alertness "wikilink").*

Prerequisites: [Alertness](/wiki/Alertness "wikilink").

Syntax: [monitor](/wiki/Monitor "wikilink")
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.
