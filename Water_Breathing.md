*You can cause yourself or another player to sprout gills and be able to
breathe water. With these gills, you can survive drowning in any
[water](/wiki/Water_Terrain "wikilink") [room](Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'water breathing'
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")&gt;.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is very useful for
[characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") of all
[classes](/wiki/Category%3A_Classes "wikilink") and is normally included in a
"full
[spellup](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells_That_Buff_Characters "wikilink")".

See also [Water Breathing
Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Water_Breathing_Gear "wikilink").
