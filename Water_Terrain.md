Water terrain actually encompasses a few [terrain
types](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink").
[Rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") with these [terrain
types](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink") will appear cyan to players.

Players must [spend](/wiki/Movement_Costs "wikilink") between one and six
[move points](/wiki/Move_Points "wikilink") to enter (non-magically)
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink") with these [terrain
types](/wiki/Category%3A_Terrains "wikilink").

Also, players must either be flying or carry a
[boat](/wiki/Category%3A_Boats "wikilink") to enter (non-magically) some such
[rooms](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink").
