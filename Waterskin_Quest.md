From [Odertha](/wiki/Odertha "wikilink"):

-   Go s, w, n, w, n.

<!-- -->

-   Find a tortured spirit ([man](/wiki/Tortured_Spirit_(man) "wikilink"),
    [woman](/wiki/Tortured_Spirit_(woman) "wikilink"), or
    [child](/wiki/Tortured_Spirit_(child) "wikilink")) that carries
    [ectoplasm](/wiki/Ectoplasm "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   [Kill](/wiki/Kill "wikilink") him/her.

<!-- -->

-   [Get](Get "wikilink") [ectoplasm](/wiki/Ectoplasm "wikilink") from
    his/her corpse.

<!-- -->

-   Return to [Odertha](/wiki/Odertha "wikilink").

<!-- -->

-   [Give](Give "wikilink") [ectoplasm](/wiki/Ectoplasm "wikilink") to
    [Odertha](/wiki/Odertha "wikilink") and receive
    [waterskin](/wiki/Waterskin "wikilink").
