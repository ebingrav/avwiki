*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") reduces the
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink") of the
[victim](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") by two points. It will not,
however, work more than once on a given target; the weakening is itself
a fairly weak spell, and it can only be performed once.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'weaken'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is practically
never used.

See also [Weaken Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Weaken_Gear "wikilink").
