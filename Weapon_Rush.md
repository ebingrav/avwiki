*When [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink"), your main
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") flies out of your hand and
attacks your current target once. The
[weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") MIGHT return to your hand when
the [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is complete. Or it MIGHT fall
to the ground, where it will be subject to
[scavengers](/wiki/Scavenging_Mobs "wikilink"),
[stompers](/wiki/Stomping_Mobs "wikilink"), and the infamous imp... There are
no reimbursements for [weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Weapons "wikilink") that you
lose with this [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink")...*

Prerequisite(s): [Torch](/wiki/Torch_(spell) "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'weapon rush'.
