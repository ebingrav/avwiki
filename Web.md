*The web [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") allows you to trap your
[enemy](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") in sticky webs, reducing their [hit
roll](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink"), [damage roll](Damage_Roll "wikilink"), and
[dexterity](/wiki/Dexterity "wikilink"). Also, the
[enemy](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") will be unable to
[flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") from battle. Fighting against the web causes
slight damage.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'web'
&lt;[mob](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

This [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink")'s best feature is
that it prevents [wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink")
[mobs](/wiki/Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink") from [fleeing](/wiki/Flee "wikilink");
however, due to its high rate of failure, it is rarely used. Other
[spells](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") like
[Overconfidence](/wiki/Overconfidence "wikilink") are far more effective by
comparison.
