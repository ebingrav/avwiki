short spear appears as a short but still deadly spear.*

`Modifies `[`hit` `roll`](/wiki/Hit_Roll "wikilink")` by 3 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`damage`
`roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 5 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`constitution`](/wiki/Constitution "wikilink")` by 2 continuous.`  
`Modifies `[`age`](/wiki/Age "wikilink")` by 50 continuous.`
