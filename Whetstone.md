*This whetstone looks like it could [sharpen](/wiki/Sharpen "wikilink") any
cutting/piercing [weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").*

`Is big enough to be used 30 more times.`

**Keyword(s):** whetstone.  
**[Level(s)](/wiki/Object_Level "wikilink"):** various (see chart below).  
**[Type](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Types "wikilink"):**
[trash](/wiki/Category%3A_Trash "wikilink").  
**[Slot(s)](/wiki/Object_Slots "wikilink"):** <held>.  
**[Quality](/wiki/Object_Quality "wikilink"):** 200 hps.  
**[Weight](/wiki/Object_Weight "wikilink"):** 1 lbs.  
**[Flag(s)](/wiki/Category%3A_Object_Flags "wikilink"):** none.  

Comments
--------

[Warriors](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") must use whetstones to
[sharpen](/wiki/Sharpen "wikilink")
[weapons](/wiki/Category%3A_Melee_Weapons "wikilink").

Data above is from the [Igecsoz](/wiki/Category%3A_Igecsoz "wikilink")
whetstone; other stones may or may not be different.
