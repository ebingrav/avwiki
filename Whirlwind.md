*The whirlwind attack is used in place of a regular round of attacks,
and hits all enemies that are presently fighting the user. It can only
be performed with a
[double-headed](/wiki/Category%3A_Double_Weapons "wikilink") or [chain
weapon](/wiki/Category%3A_Chain_Weapons "wikilink")
[wielded](/wiki/Wield "wikilink") in both hands.*

Prerequisite(s): [Double Wield](/wiki/Double_Wield "wikilink").

Syntax: whirlwind.
