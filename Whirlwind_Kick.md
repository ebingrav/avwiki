*After learning this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), your
mastery of [kick](/wiki/Kick "wikilink") will become so great as to
automatically attempt an additional [kick](/wiki/Kick "wikilink"). (Similar to
[third attack](/wiki/Third_Attack "wikilink") but for
[kick](/wiki/Kick "wikilink").)*

Prerequisite(s): [Scorpion Style](/wiki/Scorpion_Style "wikilink").

Syntax: [kick](/wiki/Kick "wikilink").
