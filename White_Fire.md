[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink"). They are utterly harmless,
except to any [Demons](/wiki/Demons "wikilink") present.*

Prerequisite(s): [Turn Demon](/wiki/Turn_Demon "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'white fire'.
