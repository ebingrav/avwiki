Comments
--------

This knight is stronger than the other three knights on the road, mainly
because it has a weapon.

The plate armor does not have any modifiers on it, but it is fairly
heavy. Some [psionicists](/wiki/Category%3APsionicists "wikilink") like to use
it with the [Dart](/wiki/Dart_(spell) "wikilink") spell.

The lance actually has a negative modifier on it (worses your [armor
class](/wiki/Armor_Class "wikilink") by 3), but it is a [hafted
weapon](/wiki/Category%3AHafted_Weapons "wikilink"), so feel free to try it
out.
