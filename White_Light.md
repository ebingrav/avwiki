*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") creates a flash of Holy
Light which burns any [undead](/wiki/Undead_Mobs "wikilink") in the room, but
is completely harmless to the innocent.*

Prerequisite(s): [Turn Undead](/wiki/Turn_Undead "wikilink").

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'white light'.
