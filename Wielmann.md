*Managing to look dapper in ragged clothing, this
[thief](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") has a calculating and
[intelligent](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink") look behind his eyes. He must be
one of the ringleaders.*

**Keywords:** Wielmann, man, clean, intelligent.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 17.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** demonic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):** [guild
guard](/wiki/Guild_Guard_Mobs "wikilink"),
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [detect hidden](/wiki/Detect_Hidden "wikilink"), [protection
evil](/wiki/Protection_Evil "wikilink"), [sneak](Sneak "wikilink").

Comments
--------

Wielmann's "roommate," [Stein](/wiki/Stein "wikilink"), as an [assassin
mob](/wiki/Assassin_Mobs "wikilink"), will initiate combat even against
[sneaking](/wiki/Sneak "wikilink") [hiding](Hide "wikilink")
[invisible](/wiki/Invis "wikilink") [heroes](Category%3A_Hero "wikilink").

Furthermore, Wielmann, as a [guild guard
mob](/wiki/Guild_Guard_Mobs "wikilink"), will not only
[assist](/wiki/Assistive_Mobs "wikilink") her in combat but will attempt to
[blackjack](/wiki/Blackjack "wikilink")
non-[rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") and then dump them
unconscious inside [Midgaard
Country](/wiki/Category%3A_Midgaard_Country "wikilink")'s barn.
