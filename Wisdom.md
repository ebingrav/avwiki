*Wisdom is the first [attribute](:Category:_Statistics "wikilink") any
new adventurer should [train](Train "wikilink"). High wis results in
more [practice sessions](Practice_Points "wikilink") per
[level](Level "wikilink"), which allows quicker
[training](Practice "wikilink") of other
[attributes](:Category:_Statistics "wikilink") and
[spells/skills](:Category:_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). Many players
choose to carry "[leveling gear](:Category:_Level_Gear "wikilink")"
which maximizes wisdom when leveling.*
