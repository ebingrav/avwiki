*Wisdom is the first [attribute](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistics "wikilink") any
new adventurer should [train](/wiki/Train "wikilink"). High wis results in
more [practice sessions](/wiki/Practice_Points "wikilink") per
[level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"), which allows quicker
[training](/wiki/Practice "wikilink") of other
[attributes](/wiki/Category%3A_Statistics "wikilink") and
[spells/skills](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). Many players
choose to carry "[leveling gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Level_Gear "wikilink")"
which maximizes wisdom when leveling.*
