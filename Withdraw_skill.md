*Withdraw represents the ability of a seasoned
[Rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") to [flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") from
combat in a controlled and calculated fashion. By simply providing a
direction when he uses the [flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink")
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink"), a
[Rogue](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") who has mastered Withdraw will
[flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") in the specified direction, instead of running
out randomly. [Rogues](/wiki/Category%3A_Rogues "wikilink") who wish to use
this [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") must be actively using the
[Alertness](/wiki/Alertness "wikilink") [skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink")
to do so, in order to scout out all possible escape routes. NOTE: You
must type out the full word "north" "south" "east" "west" "up" "down".
Partial typing, typos or any other mistake will be considered a random
[flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") attempt.*

Prerequisite(s): [Alertness](/wiki/Alertness "wikilink"),
[Misdirection](/wiki/Misdirection "wikilink").

Syntax: [flee](/wiki/Flee "wikilink") <direction>.
