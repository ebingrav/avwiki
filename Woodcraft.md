*Whether etched in stone or carved on tree bark,
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") [markings](/wiki/Markings "wikilink")
are messages generally overlooked by others. They can be as subtle as a
bent branch or a misplaced stone, yet they speak volumes to one who
knows how to read them. Using this
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink"), an
[archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink") can [mark](/wiki/Mark "wikilink") a
[room](/wiki/Category%3A_Rooms "wikilink"), leaving a message for his fellow
hunters. More importantly, any [archer](/wiki/Category%3A_Archers "wikilink")
can find their way to the [mark](/wiki/Markings "wikilink") using the
[seek](/wiki/Seek "wikilink") [command](Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") and
the mark's creator's name. If you have made more than one
[mark](/wiki/Markings "wikilink") in an [area](Category%3A_Areas "wikilink"),
those who attempt to [seek](/wiki/Seek "wikilink") them will only
[seek](/wiki/Seek "wikilink") the most recently created one.
[Marks](/wiki/Markings "wikilink") are to be considered public
[channels](/wiki/Category%3A_Channels "wikilink"), and the usual rules and
standards apply.*

Prerequisite(s): [Map Lore](/wiki/Map_Lore "wikilink").

Syntax: [mark](/wiki/Mark "wikilink") <message>.  
Syntax: [seek](/wiki/Seek "wikilink") &lt;mark creator's name&gt;.
