*This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") duplicates the built-in
[RECALL](/wiki/Recall "wikilink") ability without the [movement
cost](/wiki/Movement_Costs "wikilink") beyond the [casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink")
of the [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink"). An added
limitation of [casting](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") the
[spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills_And_Spells "wikilink") is that it must be
done while not fighting.*

Prerequisite(s): none.

Syntax: [cast](/wiki/Cast "wikilink") 'word of recall'.

This [spell](/wiki/Category%3A_Spells "wikilink") is hardly ever used.

See also [Word Of Recall
Gear](/wiki/Category%3A_Word_Of_Recall_Gear "wikilink").
