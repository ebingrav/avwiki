`Modifies damage roll by 3 continuous`  
`Modifies hit roll by 2 continuous`  
`Modifies strength by 2 continuous `

<b>Slot:</b> Body  
<b>Weight:</b> 5  
<b>Level:</b> 20  
<b>Armor Class:</b> 4  
<b>Area:</b> [Tortured Terrain](/wiki/Category%3A_Tortured_Terrain "wikilink")
([Map](/wiki/Tortured_Terrain_Map "wikilink"))  
Find Sario and kill him for the journal. Then go to
[Midgaard](/wiki/Category%3AMidgaard "wikilink") and find the Ex-Wayfinder
(portable) and give him the journal for the cloak. Ex-wayfinder can be
found somewhat east of Aelmon (northeast).

Sario hits suprisingly hard for the level of the gear..be careful.
