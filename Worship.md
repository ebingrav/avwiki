*This is a big step in your life. Unlike the whimsical religions of
other worlds, the choosing of a [God or
Goddess](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") for devotion becomes
permanent. The reason for this is that the
[gods](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") take an active role
once they have you within their flock and they will not [release your
soul](/wiki/Unworship "wikilink") once they have it. There are many perks and
negatives to worshipping a
[god](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink") on Avatar. You will
have to research, ask around, or take a guess as to whether or not a
religion is something you really wish to do. Some
[Gods](/wiki/Category%3A_Gods_And_Goddesses "wikilink"), like
[Bhyss](/wiki/Bhyss "wikilink"), have only a few benefits (although attractive
to some) and many small negatives. The addition of religion to your
life, however, will create a more unique experience for you. The choice
is yours.*
