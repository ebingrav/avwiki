`Modifies `[`damage`
`roll`](/wiki/Damage_Roll "wikilink")` by 11 continuous.`

`Damage is 19 to 47 (average 33).`

Comments
--------

Heavy 9/11 one-hand wield. Have to go past [a very aggresive
corpse-eater](/wiki/Corrupted_Guardian "wikilink") (level 85) to get to the
mob that has it, which is level 95.
