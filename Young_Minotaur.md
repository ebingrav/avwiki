*When young, [minotaurs](/wiki/Minotaurs "wikilink") look rather a lot like
ordinary cattle. They walk on all fours, and they keep their hands
clenched in fists.*

**Keywords:** minotaur, young.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 27.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[wimpy](/wiki/Wimpy_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
