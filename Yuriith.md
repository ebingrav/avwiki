*One of the Riithi working caste, this yuriith is strong of back and
small of brain. Smarter than a beast, it's lower than average
intelligence allows it to perform mundane and repetitive, even tedious,
tasks without complaint. Living in an underground environment where
light does not exist, the Riithi no longer have eyes, yet their
oversized ears more than make up for that seeming loss. This one is
digging through the loose stone and rock debris left from the
cataclysmic devastation of the ancient city.*
