*ZAP zaps a magical [wand](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink") at a target. If
the target is not specified, and you are fighting someone, then that
character is used for a target. You must [HOLD](/wiki/Hold "wikilink") a
[wand](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink") or a
[staff](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") before using ZAP. This
[command](/wiki/Category%3A_Commands "wikilink") uses up their magical object.
[Wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink") and
[staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink") have multiple charges. When a
[magical object](/wiki/Category%3A_Spellcasting_Gear "wikilink") has no more
charges, it will be consumed. One must be adept with the "[arcane
knowledge](/wiki/Arcane_Knowledge "wikilink")"
[skill](/wiki/Category%3A_Skills "wikilink") to be able to zap items.*

Syntax: zap
&lt;[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink")/[mob](Category%3A_Mobs "wikilink")&gt;.

See also [Arcane Knowledge](/wiki/Arcane_Knowledge "wikilink"),
[Staves](/wiki/Category%3A_Staves "wikilink"), and
[Wands](/wiki/Category%3A_Wands "wikilink").
