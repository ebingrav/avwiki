*Zashti was one of the first [drow](/wiki/Drow "wikilink")
[warriors](/wiki/Category%3A_Warriors "wikilink") chosen to lead the raid
against the [ogres](/wiki/Ogres "wikilink") in [this particular
cave](/wiki/Category%3A_Waterfall_Cave "wikilink"). His
[strength](/wiki/Strength "wikilink"), [size](Size "wikilink"), and [cunning
mind](/wiki/Intelligence "wikilink") made him an excellent choice to
assassinate [the Great Ogre](/wiki/Octyin "wikilink"). He hopes to be able to
retrieve the treasure stolen from [the High Drow
city](/wiki/Category%3A_Ruins_Of_Reveria "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Zashti, drow, warrior.  
**[Level](/wiki/Level "wikilink"):** 37.  
**[Alignment](/wiki/Alignment "wikilink"):** apathetic.  
**[Flags](/wiki/Category%3A_Mob_Types "wikilink"):**
[steadfast](/wiki/Sentinel_Mobs "wikilink").  
**Affects:** [infravision](/wiki/Infravision "wikilink").
