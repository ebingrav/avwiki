*Zashti was one of the first [drow](Drow.md "wikilink")
[warriors](:Category:_Warriors.md "wikilink") chosen to lead the raid
against the [ogres](Ogres.md "wikilink") in [this particular
cave](:Category:_Waterfall_Cave.md "wikilink"). His
[strength](Strength.md "wikilink"), [size](Size.md "wikilink"), and
[cunning mind](Intelligence.md "wikilink") made him an excellent choice
to assassinate [the Great Ogre](Octyin.md "wikilink"). He hopes to be
able to retrieve the treasure stolen from [the High Drow
city](:Category:_Ruins_Of_Reveria.md "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Zashti, drow, warrior.  
**[Level](Level.md "wikilink"):** 37.
