*Zashti was one of the first [drow](Drow "wikilink")
[warriors](:Category:_Warriors "wikilink") chosen to lead the raid
against the [ogres](Ogres "wikilink") in [this particular
cave](:Category:_Waterfall_Cave "wikilink"). His
[strength](Strength "wikilink"), [size](Size "wikilink"), and [cunning
mind](Intelligence "wikilink") made him an excellent choice to
assassinate [the Great Ogre](Octyin "wikilink"). He hopes to be able to
retrieve the treasure stolen from [the High Drow
city](:Category:_Ruins_Of_Reveria "wikilink").*

**Keywords:** Zashti, drow, warrior.  
**[Level](Level "wikilink"):** 37.
