*[Kra](/wiki/Kra "wikilink")'s influence is again on the up, the Sisters of
Kra and zealots like this [dwarf](/wiki/Dwarves "wikilink") help keep it so.
The zealot's eyes are unfocused and his personal hygiene is
questionable, so he is given a wide berth!*
