This [flag](/wiki/Category%3A_Character_Flags "wikilink") appears only during
[zombiefests](/wiki/Zombiefests "wikilink") and indicates that a
[character](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") is a zombie.

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") gain it either when an
[immortal](/wiki/Category%3A_Immortals "wikilink") grants it to them or when
they are killed by zombies.

[Characters](/wiki/Category%3A_Characters "wikilink") lose it either when they
are killed by non-zombies or when a [zombiefest](/wiki/Zombiefests "wikilink")
ends.

See also [Zombiefests](/wiki/Zombiefest "wikilink").
